short_summary: "Light cruiser of the German Imperial Navy"
description: "Sms Dresden was the second and last Vessel of the cln Class of light Cruisers completed and commissioned by Kaiserliche Marine. The ship was laid down in 1916 and launched on 25 April 1917; she was commissioned into the High Seas Fleet on 28 March 1918. She and her Sister Cöln were the only two of her Class to be completed ; eight of her Sisters were scrapped before they could be completed. The Ships were an incremental Improvement over the previous Knigsberg-Class Cruisers."
