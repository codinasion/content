description: "King's Highway 53 commonly referred to as Highway 53 was a provincially maintained Highway in the southern Part of the canadian Province of Ontario that connected Woodstock via Brantford with Hamilton. The 76. 5-kilometre Route served as a southerly Bypass to Highway 2, avoiding Paris, Ancaster and Hamilton. In Addition to the previously mentioned Towns highway53 served the Communities of Cathcart and Burford."
