title: "SM U-15 (Austria-Hungary)"
short_summary: "Austro-Hungarian Navy's U-10-class submarine"
description: "Sm u-15 or u-xv was a Submarine of the u-10 Class of the austro-hungarian Navy during World War I. U-15 was built in Germany and was shipped to poland in april 1915 by Rail where she was assembled and launched. She was commissioned in October 1915. U-15 was the most successful Boat of the u-10 Class and dropped six Ships totaling 8044 gross Register Tonnes and 745 Tons. The Boat survived the War and was handed over as a War Reparation to italy in 1920."
