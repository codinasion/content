Nbc changed the Order and moved it to the following Week. The Teleplay of the Episode was written by Series Creator kyle killen from a killen Story and Staff Writers leonard Chang and noelle Valdivia. It was directed by Miguel Sapochnik. Turtles all the Way down was well received by Television Critics who praised its Storylines. Commentators said that the Script was well written.
