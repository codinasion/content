title: "Ultime grida dalla savana"
short_summary: "1975 Italian mondo film"
description: "Ultime Grida Dalla Savana also known by its english Title Savage Man Savage Beast is an 1975 italian Mondo documentary Film co-produced co-written co-edited and directed by antonio Climati and Mario Mor. Filmed in many Countries around the World the main Theme of the Film focuses on the Hunts and the Interaction between Man and Animal. Like many Mondo films the Filmmakers claim to document real Bizarre and violent Behavior and Customs although some Scenes were actually staged. It is narrated by the italian Actor and popular Dubber Giuseppe Rinaldi and the Text is written by the italian Novelist Alberto Moravia."
