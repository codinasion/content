description: "Uss Constitution also known as old Ironsides is a three-masted wooden-hulled heavy Frigate of the united States navy. She is the world's oldest commissioned naval warship still afloat. She was launched in 1797 one of six original Frigates authorized for Construction by the 1794 naval Act and the third to be constructed. The Name Constitution was one of ten Names presented to President george Washington in March 1795 by Secretary of War Timothy pickering for the new Frigates. Joshua Humphreys designed the Frigates to be the Capital Ships of the young Navy and thus Constitution and her sister Ships were larger and more heavily armed and built than the standard Frigates of the. She was built in the Edmund hartt Shipyard in the north End of Boston Massachusetts. Her first Duties were to provide Protection for american Merchant Vessels during the quasi-war with France and to defeat the barbary Pirates in the first barbary War."
