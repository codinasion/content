description: "Teri Meri Kahaani is an indian Hindi-Language romantic Comedy by kunal Kohli in 2012. Shahid Kapoor and Priyanka Chopra Portray three Couples from three different Eras in a three-act Role Alongside. In 1910 Sargodha, they are two star-crossed Lovers during the british Raj ; in 1960 Mumbai, a popular Bollywood Actress and a struggling Musician ; and in 2012 London, two University Students."
