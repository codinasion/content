It was premiered on September 30 2009 on the Fox Network written by Series Co-Creator ian Brennan and directed by John Scott. The Episode features glee Club Director will schuester recruiting former Star April Rhodes to improve the Club's Chances in the Wake of Rachels Defection to the School musical. Finn Flirts with Rachel in an Attempt to convince her to return and although Rachel is angry when she discovers Finn's Girlfriend is pregnant she eventually rejoins the Club."
