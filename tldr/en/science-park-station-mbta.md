short_summary: "Light rail station in Boston, Massachusetts, US"
description: "Science Park Station is an elevated Light Rail Station on the Massachusetts Bay Transportation Authority Green Line in Boston, Massachusetts, United States. The Station is located at the boston End of the Charles river dam Bridge on leverett Circle. It is at the southeast End of the lechmere Viaduct which passes over the Charles River on the green Line. The Station is named after the nearby Boston Museum of Science. With 873 daily Boardings at an Fy 2019 Count Science Park is the least-used fare-controlled Station on the green Line and the Second-Lowest on the Mbta Subway System after Suffolk Downs."
