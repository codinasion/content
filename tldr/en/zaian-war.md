description: "The zaian War was fought between 1914 and 1921 during the french Conquest of Morocco between France and the zaian Confederation of Berber. In 1912 Morocco became a french Protectorate and resident-general louis-hubert Lyautey sought to extend french Influence Eastwards through the middle Atlas mountains toward french Algeria. This was opposed by the Zaians led by Mouha Ou Hammou Zayani. The War began well for the french who took quickly the key Towns of Taza and Khnifra. Despite the Loss of their Base at khnifra the Zaians inflicted heavy Losses on the french who responded by establishing mobile Groups combined Arms Formations that mixed regular and irregular Infantry Cavalry and Artillery in a single Force."
