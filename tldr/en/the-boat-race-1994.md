short_summary: "Oxford versus Cambridge rowing race"
description: "The 140th Boat Race took Place on March 26 1994. The annual Boat Race is a rowing Race that takes Place along the Thames between Crews from the Universities of Oxford and Cambridge. Cambridge won by 612 Lengths. The Race saw the first Competitors from Norway in the History of the Race, in Brothers Snorre and Sverke Lorgen. It was also the first Time both competing Coxes had previously won the Event."
