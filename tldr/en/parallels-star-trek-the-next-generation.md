description: "'Parallels' is the 11th Episode of the seventh Season of the american Science Fiction Television Series Star Trek: the next Generation, the 163rd overall. It was originally released by broadcast Syndication on 29 november 1993. The Episode was written by Brannon Braga and was directed by robert Wiemer. Executive Producer Jeri Taylor wrote its final Scene, but was uncredited."
