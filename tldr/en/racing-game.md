description: "Racing Games are a video Game Genre in which the Player participates in racing Competitions. They can be based on Anything from real-world racing Leagues to fantastical Settings. They are distributed along a Spectrum between more realistic Racing Simulations and more fantasy arcade-style Racing Games. Kart racing Games emerged in the 1990s as a popular Sub-Genre of the Latter. Racing Games can also fall under the Category of Sports Video Games."
