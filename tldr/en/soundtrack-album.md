title: "Soundtrack album"
short_summary: "Music recorded directly from the soundtrack of a piece of media"
description: "A Soundtrack Album is any Album that includes Music directly recorded from the Soundtrack of a specific Film or Television Show. The first such Album to be commercially released was the 1938 Walt Disney snow white and the seven Dwarfs soundtrack to the Film of the same Name. The first Soundtrack Album of a Film's orchestral Score was that for Alexander Korda's 1942 Film Rudyard Kipling's Jungle Book, composed by Miklós Rózsa."
