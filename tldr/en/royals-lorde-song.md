description: "'Royals' is the Debut single by New Zealand Singer-Songwriter Lorde, included in her Debut extended play the Love Club Ep and Debut Studio Album Pure Heroine. Lorde wrote the Song with Producer Joel little. 'Royals' is a minimalist Art Pop and Electropop Song with Influences of Hip Hop, R & B, and indie Pop. The Lyrics express Disapproval of the lavish Lifestyle presented by Pop and Hip Hop Artists in Songs and Music Videos making them appear like modern-day Royalty."
