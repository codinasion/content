description: "Star Control 3 is an Action Game developed by legend Entertainment and published by Accolade in 1996. The third Installment in the Star Control trilogy the Game was released in 1996 for Ms-Dos and in 1998 for Mac Os. The Story takes Place after Star Control Ii, beginning with a Disaster that disrupts superluminal Travel through Hyperspace. This leads the Player to investigate a new Quadrant of Space joined by allied Aliens from the previous Games."
