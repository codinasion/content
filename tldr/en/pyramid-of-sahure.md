title: "Pyramid of Sahure"
short_summary: "Fifth Dynasty Egyptian pyramid complex"
description: "The Pyramid of Sahure is a Pyramid complex built for the egyptian Pharaoh Sahure from the fifth Dynasty from the late 26th to 25th Century Bc. It introduced a Period of Pyramid Building by Sahure's Successors in Abusir on a Location previously used by Userkaf for his Sun Temple. The Site was first thoroughly excavated by Ludwig Borchardt between March 1907 and 1908 who wrote the standard Work Das grabdenkmal Des knigs Sahu-Re between 1910 and 1913."
