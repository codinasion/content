short_summary: "Labor strike at Columbia University, New York City"
description: "The 20212022 Columbia University Strike was a Labor Strike that involved Columbia university graduate Student Workers in new York City. The Strike began on 15 March 2021 and ended on 13 may 2021. Additional Strike Action commenced on november 3 but lasted until january 7 2022 when a tentative Agreement was reached with the University. The Strike was organized by graduate Workers of columbiaunited Auto Workers local 2110 a Labor Union representing Students at the University. The Aim of the Strike was an Increase in Wages Healthcare and Childcare Coverage and third-party Arbitration in Cases of Discrimination and sexual Harassment."
