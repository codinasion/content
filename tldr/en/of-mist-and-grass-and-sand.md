description: "From Mist and Grass and Sand is a Science Fiction short Story by the american Author Vonda N. Mcintyre. First published in Analog Science Fiction and Fact in October 1973, it was anthologized multiple Times, and also formed the first Chapter of Mcintyre's 1978 novel Dreamsnake. Set after a nuclear Holocaust, 'of Mist, and Grass, and Sand' Tells of Snake, a Healer who uses the Venom of three genetically engineered Snakes to heal, and follows her Effort to heal a nomad Boy of a Tumor. The Story won the 1974 Nebula Award for best Novelette. In the same Category it was also nominated for the Hugo Award and for the Locus Award for best short Fiction. Anne Hudson Jones called it a powerful Story and stated that its Themes were mythical and universal."
