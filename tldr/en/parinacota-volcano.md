title: "Parinacota (volcano)"
short_summary: "Volcano on the border of Chile and Bolivia"
description: "Parinacota Parina Quta or Parinaquta is a dormant Stratovolcano on the Border between Bolivia and Chile. Together with Pomerape it forms the Volcanic Chain nevados de payachata. The Summit is Part of the central volcanic Zone of the Andes its Elevation reaches 6380 Metres above Sea Level. The symmetric Cone is capped by a Summit Crater with Widths of 1 Km or 1000 Metres. On the southern slopes further down three parasitic Centres known as Ajata Cones lie. These cones have generated lava flows. The Volcano is surrounded by a Platform formed by lava Domes and andesitic Lava Flows."
