description: "Stocksbridge Park Steels Football Club is an english Association Football Club based in Stocksbridge, South Yorkshire. They currently play in the northern Premier League Division one East. The Club was founded in 1986 after a Merger between two Clubs and has a yellow and blue Home Kit. They play at bracken Moor. England international Jamie Vardy and Republic of Ireland international Scott Hogan have played for the Team."
