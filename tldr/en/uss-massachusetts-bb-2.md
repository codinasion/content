title: "USS Massachusetts (BB-2)"
short_summary: "Indiana-class battleship of the United States Navy"
description: "The uss Massachusetts was a Battleship in the Indiana Class prior to the Dreadnought and the second united States navy Ship comparable to foreign Battleships of its Time. Authorized in 1890, and commissioned six Years later, she was a small Battleship, though with heavy Armor and Ordnance. The Ship Class also pioneered the Use of an intermediate Battery. She was designed for coastal Defense and her Decks were therefore not safe from the large Waves in the open Ocean."
