description: "Tylopterella is a Genus of Eurypterid, a Group of extinct aquatic Arthropods. Only one Fossil of the single and type Species, T. Boylei has been discovered in elora Canada Deposits from the late Silurian Era. The Name of the Genus is composed of ancient greek Words meaning Knot and Meaning Wings. The Species name boylei Honors david Boyle who discovered the Specimen of Tylopterella."
