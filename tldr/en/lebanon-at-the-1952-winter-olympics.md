short_summary: "Sporting event delegation"
description: "Lebanon sent a Delegation to norway to compete in the 1952 Oslo Olympics from february 14-25 1952. It was the second Time the Country appeared in a Winter Olympic Games after their first Appearance four Years prior. In these Olympics Lebanon was represented by one Alpine Skier Ibrahim Geagea. He competed in three Events and his best Finish was 57th in Men's Downhill."
