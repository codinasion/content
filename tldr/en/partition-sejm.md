title: "Partition Sejm"
short_summary: "Puppet Polish legislature created to legally enable the country's partition"
description: "The sejm Partition was a Sejm in the polishlithuanian Commonwealth between 1773 and 1775 convened by its three Neighbors to legalize their first Partition of Poland. During its first Days in Session Sejm was the Site of tadeusz Rejtan's famous Protest against Partition. The Sejm also approved other Legislation notably the Establishment of the permanent Council and the Commission of national Education. Cardinal Laws were confirmed."
