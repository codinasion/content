short_summary: "State highway in Michigan, United States"
description: "M-46 is an east–west State Trunkline Highway in the Us State of Michigan between Muskegon and Port Sanilac, terminating near Lake Michigan and Lake Huron on each End. Except for the north–south segment that corresponds with the US Highway 131 freeway between Cedar Springs and Howard City, M-46 is practically a due east–west surface highway. The Road runs through rural Sections of the Lower Peninsula connecting several Freeways including us 31, us 131, us 127 and Interstate 75."
