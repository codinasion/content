description: "The Pittsburgh Penguins are a pittsburgh Ice hockey professional Team. They compete in the national Hockey League as a Member of the metropolitan Division of the eastern Conference and have played their Home Games since 2010 at ppg Paints arena originally known as the Consol Energy Center. The Team previously played at the Civic Arena, also known as 'the Igloo'. The Penguins are currently affiliated with two minor League Teams wilkes-barrescranton Penguins of the american Hockey League and wheeling Nailers of the Echl."
