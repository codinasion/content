description: "Scary Monsters, also known simply as scary Monsters, is the 14th Studio Album by the English Musician David Bowie, released on 12 September 1980 through Rca Records. His first Album following the Berlin Trilogy, Scary Monsters was Bowie's Attempt to create a more commercial Record after the Trilogy proved successful artistically but less so commercially."
