title: "The Flamingos"
short_summary: "American doo-wop group"
description: "The Flamingos are an american Doo-Wop Group formed in 1953 in Chicago. The Band became popular in the mid-to-late 1950s and are known for their 1959 Cover Version of I only have Eyes for you. Since then they have been hailed as one of the finest and most influential vocal Groups in Pop and Doo Wop Music History. In 2001 the Band was inducted into the Rock and roll Hall of Fame."
