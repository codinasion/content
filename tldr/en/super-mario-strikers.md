description: "Super Mario Strikers, known in Europe and Australia as Mario Smash Football, is a 2005 Sports video Game developed by next Level Games and published by Nintendo for the Gamecube. The Game's Developers had worked on Nhl Hitz Pro before Development of Strikers, which served as an Influence for the fast-paced and physical Nature of the Game. This was also the last Mario Game to be released in Japan and north America on the Gamecube."
