description: "Skyfall is a 2012 Spy Film and the Twenty-Third in the James Bond Series produced by Eon Productions. The Film is the third to star Daniel Craig as fictional Mi6 Agent James Bond and Features Javier Bardem as Raoul Silva, the Villain, with Judi Dench returning as M. Based on a Script by neal Purvis Robert Wade and John logan the Film has Bond investigating a Series of targeted Data Leaks and co-ordinated Attacks on Mi6 led by Silva. It sees the Return of two recurring Characters miss Moneypenny and Q after an Absence of two Films. Ralph Fiennes Brnice Marlohe and albert Finney are among the supporting Actors."
