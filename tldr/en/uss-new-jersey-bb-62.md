title: "USS New Jersey (BB-62)"
short_summary: "United States battleship and now museum ship"
description: "Uss New Jersey is an iowa-class Battleship, and was the second Ship of the United States Navy to be named after the U. S. State of New Jersey. She was often fondly called big J. New Jersey earned more Battle Stars for Combat Operations than the other three completed iowa-class Battleships and was the only us battleship used during vietnam War to provide Gunfire Support."
