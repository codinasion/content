description: "St Lawrence's Church is an anglican parish Church at Mereworth, Kent, United Kingdom. It is in the deanery of West Malling, the Diocese of Rochester and Province of Canterbury. The Church was built by John Fane in the mid-1740s the 7th Earl of Westmorland after his Removal of the Village's 12th Century Worship Site to allow for the Extension of Mereworth Castle."
