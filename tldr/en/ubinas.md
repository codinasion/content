title: "Ubinas"
short_summary: "Volcano in southern Peru"
description: "Ubinas is an active Stratovolcano in the Region of Moquegua in southern Peru located about 60 Km East of the City of Arequipa. Part of the central volcanic Zone of the Andes it rises 5672 Meters above Sea Level. The Volcano's Summit is cut by 1. 4-kilometre-wide and 150-metre-deep Caldera, which itself contains a smaller Crater. Ubinas is a steep upward Cone below the Summit with a prominent Notch on the southern Side. The lower Part of the Volcano is known as ubinas I and the steeper upper Part as ubinas Ii they represent different Stages of the Volcano's geological History."
