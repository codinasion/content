short_summary: "2011 single by Porcelain Black featuring Lil Wayne"
description: "'this is what Rock N' Roll Looks like' is the Debut single of american industrial Singer-Songwriter Porcelain Black, featuring american Rapper Lil Wayne. This Song was to serve as the Lead single from the Singer's Debut Studio Album, Mannequin Factory, which has now been cancelled. Produced by moroccan-swedish Producer Redone, who had originally discovered Black, 'this is what Rock N' Roll Looks like' is influenced by the Genre of Rock, lyrically stating how the Listener should be Proud of who they are. The Song was released on 29 March 2011 digitally in the united States and managed to chart at Number Forty-Four on the us billboard hot Dance Club Songs."
