description: "Yasunori Mitsuda is a japanese Composer and Musician. He is best known for his Work in video Games, primarily for the Chrono, Xeno, Shadow Hearts, and Inazuma Eleven Franchises, among various Others. In high School Mitsuda began composing Music for his own Games later attending a Music College in Tokyo. He was still a Student and received an Internship at Wolf Team's Game Studio."
