short_summary: "Radio station in Winchester, Virginia"
description: "Winc is a commercial Broadcast Radio Station licensed to Winchester, Virginia, United States. The Station has News Talk and Sports programming. Owned by colonial Radio Group of Williamsport Llc winc serves the northern Shenandoah Valley in Virginia. The Studios of the Station are located in Winchester while the Transmitter is situated South of the City in nearby Kernstown. In Addition to the standard Analog Transmission Winc is available Online."
