short_summary: "Dam in Shasta County, California"
description: "Spring Creek Debris Dam is an earthfill dam on Spring Creek, a tributary of the Sacramento River, in Shasta County in the U. S. State of California. Completed in 1963, the dam, maintained by the U. S. The Bureau of Reclamation Serves primarily to collect severe Acid Mine Drainage from the Iron Mountain Mine. The Dam Forms spring creek Reservoir less than 1 Mile long. Spring Creek and south Fork Spring Creek Flow from a 16-square-mile Watershed into the Reservoir. The Dam is directly upstream from the City of Keswick California and Keswick Reservoir. The Operations are Part of the Trinity River Division of the central Valley Project."
