short_summary: "1919–20 rebellions in Armenia"
description: "The muslim Uprisings in Kars and Sharurnakhichevan were a Series of local Muslim Insurgencies against the Administration of the first Republic of Armenia beginning on 1 July 1919 and ending on 28 July 1920. The Areas of uprising were persuaded into Insurrection by the Sedition of turkish and azerbaijani Agents who were trying to destabilize Armenia in Order to form a pan-turkic Corridor."
