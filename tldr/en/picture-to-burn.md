description: "'Picture to Burn' is a Song by the american Singer-Songwriter Taylor Swift and the fourth single from her 2006 self-titled Debut Studio Album. On 4 february 2008 big Machine Records released the Song to us Country Radio. Written by Swift and Liz rose the Lyrics were inspired by a narcissistic Classmate of Swift and depict a Protagonist's Revenge against an Ex-Lover. Nathan Chapman produced the Song which is an Incorporation of Country Rock and alternative Rock that features a Plunking Banjo and distorted electric Guitars."
