description: "Dom Pedro Afonso was the Prince imperial and heir Apparent to the Throne of the brazilian Empire. Born at the Palace of so cristvo in Rio de Janeiro he was the second Son and youngest Child of Emperor Dom Pedroii and dona teresa Cristina of the two Sicilies and therefore a Member of the brazilian Branch of House of Braganza. Pedro Afonso was seen as Vital for the future Viability of the Monarchy which had been put in Jeopardy almost three Years earlier by the Death of his older Brother Dom Afons."
