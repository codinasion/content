title: "SMS Charlotte"
short_summary: "Screw corvette of the German Imperial Navy"
description: "Sms Charlotte was a german Screw Corvette built for the Kaiserliche Marine in the 1880s, being laid down in April 1883, launched in September 1885, and commissioned in November 1886. The only Vessel of her Class, Charlotte was the last Sailing Warship built for the german Navy. She was armed with eighteen 15cm Guns."
