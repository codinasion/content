description: "The Firebird is a Ballet and orchestral Concerto by russian Composer Igor Stravinsky. It was written for the Paris 1910 Season of sergei Diaghilev's Company ballets russes the original Choreography was by michel Fokine who collaborated with alexandre Benois and Others on a Scenario based on the russian Fairy Tales of the Firebird and the Blessing. It was first performed on June 25 1910 in the Opra de paris and was an immediate Success it was a Catalyst for Stravinsky's international Fame and led to future diaghilev-stravinsky Collaborations including Petrushka and the Rit."
