description: "Ramone Edward Moore Jr. Is an american Professional Basketball Player who plays for Csm Oradea of the romanian Liga Națională. He attended the south philadelphia high School where he was taught by George Anderson. Moore led the Philadelphia Public League in scoring as a senior and earned Public League Mvp Honors. He enrolled as a non-scholarship Student at Temple and redshirted his freshman Year. As a Redshirt Sophomore he was the sixth Man of the Year at the atlantic 10. As a Junior he was an all-atlantic 10 second Team Selection and as a senior he was an all-atlantic 10 first Team Selection."
