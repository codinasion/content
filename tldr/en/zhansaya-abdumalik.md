description: "Zhansaya Abdumalik is a Kazakhstani Chess Player who holds the Title of grand Master. She is the first Kazakh Woman and 39th Woman in the World to earn the Title. Abdumalik has a peak FIDE rating of 2505 and has been ranked as high as No. 11 in the World among Women. Abdumalik has been a two-time Girls' World Youth Champion as well as a Girls' World Junior Champion. She is also a two-time Kazakhstani Women's national Champion, and has represented Kazakhstan in Women's Events at the Chess Olympiad, World Team Chess Championship, and the asian Nations Chess Cup. On April 20, 2022, Zhansaya became the President of the Almaty Chess Federation."
