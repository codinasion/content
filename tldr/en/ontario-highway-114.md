short_summary: "Former Ontario provincial highway"
description: "Highway 114 commonly referred to as Highway 114 was a provincially maintained Highway in the canadian Province of Ontario. It was one of the shortest Highways ever assigned in the Province, at just 1. 8 kilometres in length. The Route followed malden Road for its entire Length between Highway 3 and highway 98. Part of the original provincial Highway Network created in 1920 Highway114 was created in 1953 by renumbering a Route. Prior to 1953, it had been numbered Highway 2A from 1929 until 1931, Highway 3A from 1931 until 1938, and Highway 98A from 1938 until 1953. Malden Road is Today an unnumbered local Road."
