short_summary: "State highway in York County, Pennsylvania, US"
description: "Pennsylvania Route 216 is a 26-mile State Highway in York County Pennsylvania. The western Terminus is located at Pa 116 near Hanover. The eastern Terminus is in winterstown Pa 24. Pa 216 is a Two-Lane undivided Road that passes through rural Areas in southern York County, serving Sticks and Glen Rock. The Route intersects pa 516 in Sticks pa 616 in Glen Rock and interstate 83 near Loganville."
