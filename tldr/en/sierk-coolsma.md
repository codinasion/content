title: "Sierk Coolsma"
short_summary: "Dutch missionary"
description: "Sierk Coolsma was a dutch Protestant Missionary who extensively wrote on the sundanese Language. In his early Twenties he became a missionary and arrived in 1865 to the dutch East Indies. First tasked to Cianjur, he studied sundanese in more Detail than his Contemporaries, gaining an Appreciation for the Language. Further missionary Activities in Bogor began in 1869 were a Failure and he was tasked in 1873 with translating the new Testament in sundanese. Although the sundanese People highly valued Poetry he translated the Poetry into prose hoping that it would help Readers entertain new Ideas."
