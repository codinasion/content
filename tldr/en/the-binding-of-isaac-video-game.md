description: "The Binding of Isaac is a roguelike Video Game by independent Developers edmund Mcmillen and florian Himsl. It was released in 2011 for microsoft Windows and was then ported to os X and linux. The Game's Title and Plot are inspired by the biblical Story of the Binding of Isaac. In the Game Isaac's Mother receives a Message from god demanding the Life of her Son as Proof of her Faith and Isaac Flees fearing for his Life into the monster-filled Basement of their Home where he must fight to survive. Players control Isaac or one of 33 other unlockable Characters through a procedurally generated Dungeon in a Roguelike Manner fashioned after those of the Legend of Zelda defeating Monsters in real-time Combat while collecting Items and Power-Ups to defeat Bosses and."
