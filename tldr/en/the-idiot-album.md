short_summary: "1977 studio album by Iggy Pop"
description: "The Idiot is the Debut Studio Album by the american Musician Iggy Pop, released on March 18, 1977, through Rca Records. It was produced by David Bowie and recorded primarily at Chteau D'Hrouville in Hrouville France. The Album followed the Break-Up of Pop's Band the Stooges in 1974 and a Period of Drug Addiction for both Pop and Bowie after which the two moved to europe to stifle their Addictions."
