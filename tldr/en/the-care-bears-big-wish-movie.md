description: "The Care Bears' big Wish Film is a 2005 direct-to-video computer-animated musical Fantasy Film produced by Nelvana limited and released by Lions gate Home Entertainment. Directed by Larry Jacobs and Ron Pitts, and written by Jeffrey Alan Schechter, the Film is a follow-up to the Care Bears' previous Efforts in 2004's Journey to Joke-A-Lot. It was the fifth Film to feature the Bears and the second computer-animated."
