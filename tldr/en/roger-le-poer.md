title: "Roger le Poer"
short_summary: "12th-century Chancellor of England"
description: "Roger Le Poer was a medieval Lord Chancellor for king Stephen of England from 1135 until 1139. Roger Son of a powerful Bishop owed his Position to his Family Connections. He lost his Office when his Father and other Relatives lost Power. Roger was used along with his Father to secure the Surrender of a castle held by his Mother and then disappeared from History."
