It was sent as the fourth single of the Record to adult contemporary Radio in the united States on June 26 2017. The Track was written by Perry, Noonie Bao, Dijon Mcfarlane, Nicholas Audino, Lewis Hughes and its Producer Elof Loelv. Musically, 'Save as Draft' is a mid-tempo Power Ballad, which lyrically portrays a warning about coming to Terms with an Ex."
