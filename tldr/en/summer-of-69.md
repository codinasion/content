title: "Summer of '69"
short_summary: "1985 single by Bryan Adams"
description: "'Summer of'69' is a Song recorded by the canadian Singer Bryan Adams from his fourth Album, Reckless. This is a Rock Song about a Dilemma between settling down or trying to become a Rock Star. The Track was written by Adams and his longtime Songwriting Collaborator Jim Vallance. Summer'69 was produced by Adams and Bob Clearmountain. Summer of'69 was released under am Records in June 1985 as the fourth single of reckless. According to later Claims by Adams, the Title is a Reference to the Sex Position, not the Year, but Vallance disputes this."
