title: "Rebirth (sculpture)"
short_summary: "Proposed sculpture in Clackamas County, Oregon, U.S."
description: "Rebirth, nicknamed 'Deer Baby' and 'Twilight Zone Bambi', was a proposed Outdoor Sculpture by american Artist Seyed Alavi, considered for Installation at the Max Orange Line's Southeast Park Avenue Max Station in Oak Grove, an unincorporated Area neighboring Milwaukie in Clackamas County, Oregon, in the United States. The Design of the unfinished creative Work that featured a 30-foot Deer with a Child's Face received mixed Reception. Unable to meet Trimet's Standards and remain within Budget Alavi withdrew his Design from the Project in november 2011."
