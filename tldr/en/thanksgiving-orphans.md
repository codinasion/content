description: "Thanksgiving Orphans is the ninth Episode of the fifth Season of the american Television Sitcom Cheers co-written by Cheri Eichen and Bill Steinkeller and directed by James Burrows. It aired on Nbc for the first Time on november 27 1986. The Characters have no Family or Friends to spend Time with and some of their Plans go wrong. They gather for a Thanksgiving Feast which transforms into a Food Battle. Burrows filmed the Scene of the Food Fight two Times. The Episode had a generally positive Reception. TV Guide ranked it number seven on its 100 Greatest Episodes of All Time list while The Huffington Post included the food fight sequence in a list of the 10 Most Awkward Thanksgiving Scenes of All Time from movies and television."
