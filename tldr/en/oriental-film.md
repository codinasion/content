title: "Oriental Film"
short_summary: "Defunct Indonesian film company"
description: "Oriental Film was a Film Production Company in Batavia, Dutch East Indies. Established in 1940 by ethnic chinese Businessman Tjo Seng Han it completed four black-and-white Films before it closed in 1941. All the company's films were screened into the 1950s but may now be lost. They were directed by two Men, Njoo Cheong Seng and Sutan Usman Karim, and launched the Careers of Actors such as Dhalia and Soerip."
