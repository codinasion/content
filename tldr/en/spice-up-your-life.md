title: "Spice Up Your Life"
short_summary: "1997 single by Spice Girls"
description: "'Spice Up your Life' is a Song by British Girl Group the Spice Girls from their second Studio Album, Spiceworld. The Song was co-written by the Group with matt Rowe and richard Stannard as they were shooting Scenes for their 1997 Film Spice World while Production was handled by the latter two. It is a dance-pop Song with Influences of latin Rhythms such as Salsa and Samba. The Song's Theme reflects the Group's Desire to write a Song for the World while the Lyrics have been labeled as dance-orientated with a self-promoting Message."
