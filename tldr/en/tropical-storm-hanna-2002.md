title: "Tropical Storm Hanna (2002)"
short_summary: "Atlantic tropical cyclone"
description: "Tropical Storm Hanna was a moderately strong tropical Storm that affected the Gulf Coast and the southeastern Regions of the united States. The ninth tropical Cyclone and the Eighth named Storm of the atlantic Hurricane Season 2002 Hanna formed through the complex Interaction of a Surface trough a tropical Wave and an upper-level low Pressure System in the Upper Atmosphere. The Storm was designated a tropical Depression on September 12 and remained disorganized throughout its Duration although it reached the Status of a tropical Storm and a peak Intensity of 1001mbar with Winds of 60 Miles per Hour. Hanna crossed the extreme southern Louisiana and made a second Landfall along the mississippi-alabama Border."
