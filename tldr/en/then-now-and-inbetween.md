description: "Then now and Inbetween is a promotional Compilation Album by the English Rock band the Kinks. In July 1969 Reprise Records released the Album to Journalists Radio Program Directors and Disc Jockeys in Conjunction with the Promotion Campaign God save the Kinks which sought to reestablish the Kinks' commercial Status in the us after their four-year Ban."
