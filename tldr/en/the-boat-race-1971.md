title: "The Boat Race 1971"
short_summary: "Oxford versus Cambridge rowing race"
description: "On March 27 1971 the 117th Boat Race took Place. It is a side-by-side Rowing Race between Teams from the Universities of Oxford and Cambridge along the Thames held annually. It was won by Cambridge who passed the finishing Line ten lengths ahead of Oxford securing Cambridge's fourth consecutive Victory. The Winning Time was the second Fastest in the History of the Event at that Point."
