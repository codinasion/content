title: "Tropical Storm Harvey (1999)"
short_summary: "Atlantic tropical storm in 1999"
description: "Tropical Storm Harvey was a moderately strong tropical Storm that caused flooding across south Florida during the atlantic Hurricane Season of 1999. The tenth tropical Cyclone and Eighth named Storm of the Season Harvey developed on September 19 from a tropical Wave in the Gulf of Mexico. After its Formation the System slowly intensified and on 20 September became a tropical Storm. Throughout the Rest of its Duration Harvey slowly intensified as it headed East. On september 21 Harvey made Landfall in Southwest Florida with Winds of 60 Mph. Harvey emerged early on September 22 but was absorbed by an extratropical Cyclone. The Remnants of Harvey reached atlantic Canada before they dissipated."
