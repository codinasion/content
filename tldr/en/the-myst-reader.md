description: "Myst Reader is a Collection of three Novels based on the Myst Series of Adventure Games. The Collection was published in September 2004 and Combines three works previously published separately: the Book of Atrus, the Book of Ti'Ana, and the Book of D'Ni. The Novels were each written by british Science Fiction Writer David Wingrove with the Support of Myst Creators rand and robyn Miller."
