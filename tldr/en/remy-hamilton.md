short_summary: "American football player (born 1974)"
description: "Remy Martin Hamilton is a former american Football Kicker who played in the Arena Football League and the national Football League. In College he was an all-american on the michigan Wolverines Football Team. As a professional, he played for the Detroit Lions of the NFL in addition to exhibition and training camp experience with the Seattle Seahawks, St. Louis Rams and Chicago Bears. He also played for the Grand Rapids Rampage, Carolina Cobras, Milwaukee Mustangs, Los Angeles Avengers, and Dallas Desperados of the Afl. He holds Records in the Afl for all-time Career Points and single Season Kick Points."
