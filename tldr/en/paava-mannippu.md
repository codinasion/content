description: "Paava Mannippu is a 1961 Indian Tamil-language drama film directed and edited by A. Bhimsingh produced it under his Banner Buddha Pictures with avm Productions. The Film consists of an ensemble Cast of sivaji ganesan Gemini Ganesan Savitri Devika and M D. R. Radha. M. V. Rajamma, V. Nagayya, S. V. Subbaiah and T. S. Balaiah Play supporting Roles. It revolves around four Children who are separated from their Parents in Childhood and then found and raised by foster Parents of different religious Backgrounds."
