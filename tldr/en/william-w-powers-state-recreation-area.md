short_summary: "Public park in Chicago, Illinois"
description: "William W. Powers State Recreation Area is an Illinois State Park administered by the Illinois Department of Natural Resources on 580 Acres in the Hegewisch Community Area of the City of Chicago in Cook County, Illinois, United States. The Area includes 419 Acres of Water in Wolf Lake which provides approximately 6 Miles of Shoreline to Fishermen. The Park annually serves a Half a million Visitors. The Park contains numerous Species and is one of the most important biological Sites in the Chicago Region."
