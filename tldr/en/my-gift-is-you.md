It was released digitally on October 6, 2017, along with the rest of the parent album, through Interscope Records. The Track was written by Stefani, Justin Tranter, and Busbee while Production was handled by Busbee and Eric Valentine. Musically the Piece is a Christmas Song that contains Elements of pop-soul Music."
