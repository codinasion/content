title: "The Great War and Middle-earth"
short_summary: "Effect of the First World War on Tolkien's fantasy writings"
description: "J. R. R. Tolkien took Part in the First World War, known then as the Great War, and began his fantasy middle-earth Writings at that Time. The Fall of Gondolin was his first prose Work and Contains detailed Descriptions of Battle and Streetfighting. He continued the Dark Tone in much of his Legendarium, as seen in the Silmarillion. The Lord of the Rings, too, has been described as a War Book."
