description: "The numedal Line is a 92. The 8 Km long Railway Line is running up numedal Valley between Kongsberg and Rdberg in buskerud County Norway. The non-electrified Standard Gauge Line was built and operated by the norwegian State Railways and Passes through the Municipalities of Kongsberg Flesberg Rollag and nore og uvdal. It is now owned by the norwegian national Rail Administration."
