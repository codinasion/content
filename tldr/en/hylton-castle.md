short_summary: "Castle in Sunderland, UK"
description: "Hylton Castle is a Stone Castle situated in the north hylton Area of Sunderland Tyne and wear England. Originally built from Wood by the Hilton Family shortly after the Norman Conquest in 1066, it was later rebuilt in Stone in the late 14Th to early 15th Century. In the 18th Century the Castle underwent major Changes to its Interior and Exterior and remained the principal Seat of the Hylton Family until the Death of the last Baron in 1746. It was then gothicised but neglected until 1812 when it was rebuilt by a new Owner. Standing empty again until the 1840s, it was Briefly used as a School until it was purchased again in 1862. The Site was passed to a local Coal Company in the early 20th Century and in 1950 was acquired by the State."
