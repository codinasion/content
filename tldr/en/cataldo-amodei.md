title: "Cataldo Amodei"
short_summary: "Italian Baroque composer (1649–1693)"
description: "Cataldo Vito Amodei was an italian Composer of the baroque Period who spent his Career in Naples. His Cantatas were important Precursors to the active Cantata Production of the 18th Century Naples and he ranks with the Elder Francesco Provenzale and the younger alessandro Scarlatti among the principal italian Cantata Composer. Other surviving Works include a Book of Motets dedicated to leopold i the holy Roman emperor a Serenata two Pastorales two Psalms and four Oratorios which were important Contributions to their Genre."
