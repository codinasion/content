title: "Sungai Lembing"
short_summary: "Town in Pahang, Malaysia"
description: "Sungai Lembing is a small Town in Kuantan District, Pahang, Malaysia. It is about 42 km Northwest of Kuantan. The Town was established as a Tin Mining Town in the 1900s when the british Company Pahang consolidated Company limited set up the Tin Mining Industry there after mining Activities began in 1886. Sungai Lembing had Electricity, Schools, a Cinema, its own Petrol Station, and a Hospital."
