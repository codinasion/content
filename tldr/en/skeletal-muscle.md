title: "Skeletal muscle"
short_summary: "One of three major skeletal system types that connect to bones"
description: "Skeletal Muscles are Organs of the Vertebrate Muscular System and are typically attached by Tendons to Bones in a Skeleton. The Muscle Cells of skeletal Muscles are much longer than in other Types of Muscle Tissue and are often known as Muscle Fibers. The Muscle Tissue of a skeletal Muscle is striated – having a striped Appearance due to the Arrangement of the Sarcomeres."
