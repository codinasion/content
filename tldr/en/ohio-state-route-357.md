description: "State Route 357 is a 2. 353-mile-long east–west state highway in the northern portion of U. S. State of Ohio. Existing entirely on South Bass Island in Lake Erie, the highway begins at its western terminus of West Shore Boulevard, about 0. 75 Miles west of put-in-bay. Sr 357 then Travels northeast to its eastern Terminus at a dead End on Lake Erie approximately 2 Miles east of put-in-bay Village."
