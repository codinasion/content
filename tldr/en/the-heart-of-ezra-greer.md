short_summary: "1917 American silent drama film directed by Emile Chautard"
description: "The Heart of ezra Greer is an american Silent Drama Film produced by the Thanhouser Company in 1917 and directed by emile Chautard. The Film focuses on Ezra greer a successful middle-aged Man who searches for his college-aged Daughter Mary. The Wayward Mary was romanticised and abandoned by Jack Denbeigh who later bore his Child. Once Ezra is broke he finds Work as Valet for Jack Denbeigh. Mary Becomes upset after Jack's Engagement to a cabaret Girl and leaves her Child at Jack's Home. Contrary to Jack's Wishes ezra keeps the Child and Jack eventually reveals that the Child is his own. Ezra Convinces Jack to make Everything Right and ezra Convinces the cabaret Girl to leave Jack. After a Carriage Accident in which the Baby is injured ezra and Jack Rush to the Hospital and find mary a nurse Crying over the Child. The Film ends with Jack and Mary's Marriage. The Film was released on 7 October 1917 by Path. The Film was the final Release from Thanhouser and was deemed to be an average Film by most Reviewers. The Critics had relied heavily on a far-fetched Coincidence that drove the Plot. The Film is presumed lost."
