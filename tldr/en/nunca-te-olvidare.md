description: "'Nunca Te Olvidaré' is a Song by spanish Singer Enrique Iglesias for his third Studio Album Cosas del Amor. It was written by Iglesias with Rafael Pérez-Botija handling its Production. A Power Ballad, it is a confessional Song of staying in Love through Time and other Lovers. Upon its Release, it was met with generally positive Reactions from Music Critics, although one Reviewer was less impressed with it along with the other Ballads from the Album. The Song was featured as the main Theme in the mexican Telenovela of the same Name in 1999."
