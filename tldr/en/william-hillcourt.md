description: "William Hillcourt, known within the Scouting Movement as 'Green Bar Bill', was an influential Leader in the Boy Scouts of America Organization from 1927 to 1992. Hillcourt was a prolific Writer and Teacher in the Areas of Woodcraft, Troop and Patrol Structure, and Training ; his written Works include three Editions of the Bsa's official Boy Scout Handbook, with over 12. 6 million Copies printed, other scouting-related Books and numerous Magazine Articles. Hillcourt developed and promoted the american Adaptation of the Wood Badge Adult Scout Leader Training Program."
