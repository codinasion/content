title: "Peace dollar"
short_summary: "US dollar coin (1921–1928, 1934–1935, 2021–present)"
description: "The Peace Dollar is a united States Dollar minted for Circulation from 1921 to 1928 and 1934 to 1935 and starting again for Collectors in 2021. Designed by Anthony de francisci the Coin was the Result of a Competition to find emblematic Peace-Emphasis Designs. Its Obverse represents the Head and Neck of the Goddess of Liberty in Profile, and the Reverse depicts a Bald Eagle at Rest clutching an olive Branch, with the Legend 'Peace'. It was the last us Dollar coin to be struck in Silver."
