title: "Tropical Storm Emilia (2006)"
short_summary: "Pacific tropical storm in 2006"
description: "Tropical Storm Emilia was a rare tropical Cyclone that affected the Baja California Peninsula in July 2006. The sixth tropical Depression and fifth tropical Storm of the Pacific Hurricane Season 2006 developed about 400 Miles offshore mexico on July 21. It moved north toward the Coast reaching peak Winds of 65mph before turning West and encountering unfavorable Conditions. Later Emilia turned north Passing near Baja California as a strong tropical Storm. The Storm moved further away from the Coast and dissipated on July 27."
