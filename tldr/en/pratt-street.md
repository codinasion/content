description: "Pratt Street is a major Street in Baltimore, Maryland, United States. It forms a one-way pair of streets with Lombard Street that run west–east through downtown Baltimore. Pratt Street is one-way in an eastbound Direction for most of its Route and lombard Street is one-way Westbound. Both Streets begin at frederick Avenue in West Baltimore and End at Patterson Park Avenue in Butcher's Hill. Since 2005 these Streets have been open for two-way Traffic from Broadway until their End at Patterson Park. Although Lombard is also a two-way Street from Fulton Avenue to Martin Luther King Jr. Boulevard, Pratt is still one-way Eastbound in this Area."
