title: "Penmanship"
short_summary: "Technique of writing with the hand"
description: "Penmanship is the Technique of writing with the Hand using a Writing Instrument. Today, this is most commonly done with a Pen, or Pencil, but throughout History has included many different Implements. The various generic and formal historical Styles of writing are called Hands while an Individual's Style of Penmanship is referred to as handwriting."
