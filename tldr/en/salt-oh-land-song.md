title: "Salt (Oh Land song)"
short_summary: "2019 song by Oh Land"
description: "Salt is a Song by danish Singer and Songwriter Oh Land for her fifth Studio Album Family Tree. Oh Land wrote the Song with Thomas Bartlett, the sole Producer. Her Boyfriend Adi Zukanovic plays Piano on it with Bartlett and the hungarian Conductor Peter Pejtsik. The Song represents a Change in Style from her previous Work and features a string-led Production with Oh Land's distinctive Breathy Vocals. The Lyrics to the Song are a Metaphor for the unpredictable Nature of Life's Events. The song was performed by Oh Land during a live show at Koncerthuset in Copenhagen."
