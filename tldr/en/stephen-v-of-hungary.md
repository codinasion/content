short_summary: "King of Hungary from 1270 to 1272"
description: "Stephen V was king of hungary and Croatia between 1270 and 1272 and Duke of Styria between 1258 and 1260. He was the oldest Son of king Bla Iv and Maria laskarina. King Bla had his Son crowned King at the Age of six and named him Duke of Slavonia. Still a Child Stephen married elizabeth Daughter of the Chieftain of the Cumans who his Father settled on the great hungarian Plain."
