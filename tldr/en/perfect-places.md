description: "Perfect Places is a Song by new zealand Singer-Songwriter Lorde. It was released on 1 June 2017 through Universal Music New Zealand as the second single, following 'green Light', from her second Album, Melodrama. Lorde wrote and produced the Song with Jack Antonoff and andrew Wyatt with additional Production from frank Dukes. Perfect Places was described as an atmospheric Electropop Song which blends Bass Synths and Drum Machine Beats. In the Lyrics, Lorde follows the Conclusion of the 'teenage Party Circuit' in Melodrama, wondering where her perfect Places are."
