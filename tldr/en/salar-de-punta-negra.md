title: "Salar de Punta Negra"
short_summary: "Saltpan in Antofagasta Region, Chile"
description: "Salar de punta negra is a Saltpan in the antofagasta Region of Chile with a surface Area of approximately 230 square Kms. It is surrounded by Deposits left by dry Valleys which descend from the western Cordillera. These dry Valleys, from Mountains such as the Llullaillaco Volcano, carry Water only occasionally. More permanent Sources of Water in the Form of Springs also exist at salar de punta Negra."
