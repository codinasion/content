short_summary: "Australian children's music group"
description: "The Wiggles are an australian Children's Music Group formed in Sydney in 1991. By 2022 the Group Members are evie ferris John Pearce Caterina Mete and lucia Field Lachlan gillespie Simon Pryce Tsehay Hawkins. The Wiggles were founded by Field Murray Cook Jeff Fatt Greg Page and Phillip Wilcher in 1991. After their first Album Wilcher left the Band. Page retired in 2006 due to ill Health and was replaced by understudy Sam Moran, but returned in 2012, replacing Moran. At the End of 2012, Cook, Fatt and Page retired and were replaced by Gillespie, Pryce and Emma Watkins. Cook and Fatt retained their Shareholding and all three continued to have input in its creative and Production Aspects while engaging in occasional Reunion Performances. In 2021 Watkins left the Group and Hawkins took her Place."
