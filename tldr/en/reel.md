title: "Reel"
short_summary: "Device used to store elongated and flexible objects"
description: "A Reel is a Device used to store elongated and flexible Objects by wrapping the Material around a cylindrical Core known as a Spool. Many Reels also have Flanges around the Ends of the Spool to help retain wrapped Material and Prevent unwanted Slippage of the Ends. In most Cases the Spool of a Reel is hollow in Order to pass an Axle and allow it to spin like a Wheel a spinning Process known as reeling which can be done by manually turning the Reel with Handles or Cranks or by machine-powered rotating via Motors."
