title: "Hurricane Eloise"
short_summary: "Category 3 Atlantic hurricane in 1975"
description: "Hurricane Eloise was the most destructive tropical Cyclone of the 1975 atlantic Hurricane Season. On september 13 to the East of the Virgin islands eloise formed a tropical Depression which was the fifth tropical Storm 4th Hurricane and 2nd major Hurricane of the Season. The Depression tracked westward and intensified into a tropical Storm while passing to the North of Puerto Rico. Shortly thereafter Eloise attained hurricane Intensity but when it made Landfall over Hispaniola she gradually weakened back to a tropical Storm. When it struck the northern Yucatan Peninsula a weak and disorganized Cyclone Eloise rose from the open Waters of the northern caribbean Sea and turned north and began to intensify. The Cyclone rapidly matured in the Gulf of Mexico and became a Category 3 Hurricane on September 23rd. Eloise made Landfall along the Florida panhandle West of panama City before moving across Alabama Inland and dissipating on 24 September."
