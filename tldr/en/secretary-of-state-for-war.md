description: "The Secretary of State for War commonly called War Secretary was a Secretary of State in the Government of the united Kingdom that existed from 1794 to 1801 and from 1854 to 1964. The Secretary of State for War headed the War Office and was assisted by a parliamentary Undersecretary of State for War a parliamentary private Secretary who also was a Member of Parliament and a military Secretary who was a general."
