short_summary: "1999 single by Madonna"
description: "'Nothing really Matters' is a Song by american Singer Madonna for her seventh Studio Album, Ray of Light. It was written by Madonna and patrick Leonard and produced by the Singer with william Orbit and Marius de Vries. The Song was released on 9 february 1999 by Maverick Records and Warner Bros as the sixth and final single from the Album. Records. An electronic Dance Track on which Madonna Experiments with different musical Genres Nothing really Matters includes ambient Music and electronic Noise Frequencies added by de Vries. Lyrically, the recording Delves on the Singer's first Daughter Lourdes Leon, having also Themes of Selfishness, Affection, and Motherhood."
