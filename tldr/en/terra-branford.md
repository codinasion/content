description: "Terra Branford, known as Tina Branford in japanese Media, is a Character in the Final Fantasy Series and the main Protagonist of Final Fantasy Vi. Yoshitaka Amano and Tetsuya Nomura designed her for the main Series Installment, with Kazuko Shibuya designing her in-game Sprites alongside the Rest of the Characters. She also appears in the Spin-Off Fighting Game Series Dissidia Final Fantasy and the Rhythm Series Theatrhythm Final Fantasy. She has made small Appearances in several other Games in and outside the Final Fantasy Series."
