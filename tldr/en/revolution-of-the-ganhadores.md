short_summary: "1857 strike in Salvador, Bahia, Brazil"
description: "The Revolution of the Ganhadores also known as the african Porters' Strike of 1857 was a Labor Strike that involved african Porters known as Ganhadores in the brazilian City of Salvador Bahia. The Strike started after the Passage of a City Ordinance that changed the Way the Ganhadores worked in the City. The Strike ended in a partial Victory for the Strikers as the City Council changed the Ordinance with another that did away with some of the most unpopular Provisions."
