title: "SMS Cormoran (1892)"
short_summary: "Unprotected cruiser of the German Imperial Navy"
description: "Sms Cormoran was an unprotected Cruiser of the Bussard Class the fifth Member of a Class of six Ships. She was built for the imperial german Navy for overseas Duty. The Cruiser's Keel was laid in Danzig in 1890 she was launched in may 1892 and commissioned in July 1893. Cormoran was armed with a main Battery of eight. 5-centimeter guns, and could steam at a speed of 15. 5 knots."
