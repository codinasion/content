It was designed by Philip C on the southern Sidewalk of 52nd Street between second Avenue and third Avenue. Johnson and completed in 1950. The Residence was built as a Guest House for the Philanthropist Blanchette Rockefeller married to john D Parker. Rockefeller Iii. It was Johnson's only Design of a private Residence within new York City."
