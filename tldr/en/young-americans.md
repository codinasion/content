title: "Young Americans"
short_summary: "1975 studio album by David Bowie"
description: "Young Americans is the ninth Studio Album by the English Musician David Bowie, released on 7 March 1975 through Rca Records. A Departure from the Glam Rock Style of previous Albums, the Record showcased Bowie's Interest in Soul and R & B. Music Critics have described the Sound as blue-eyed Soul ; Bowie himself labelled the Album's Sound 'plastic Soul'."
