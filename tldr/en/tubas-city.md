description: "Tubas is a City in Palestine in the northeastern west Bank Northeast of nablus West of the Jordan Valley. A City with more than 21000 Inhabitants it serves as the economic and administrative Center of the tubas Governorate of the palestinian State. Its urban Area consists of 2271 Dunams. It is governed by a 15-member municipal Council and most of its Working Inhabitants are employed in Agriculture or in public Services."
