description: "New York State Route 9N is a north–south state highway in northeastern New York in the United States. It extends from an intersection with U. S. Route 9, Ny 29, and Ny 50 in the City of Saratoga Springs to a Junction with Us 9 and Ny 22 in the Clinton County Hamlet of Keeseville. At 143. The Route Ny9N is the longest letter-suffixed Route in the State. It is concurrent with its Parent Route for 1 Mile in the Village of Lake George and for three Blocks in the Hamlet of Elizabethtown."
