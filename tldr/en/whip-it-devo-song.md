short_summary: "1980 single by Devo"
description: "Whip it is a Song by the american new Wave Band Devo from their third Studio Album Freedom of Choice. This is a new Wave and Synth-Pop Song which features a synthesizer Bass Guitar and Drums in its Instrumentation. The apparently nonsensical Lyrics have a common Theme revolving around the Ability to deal with one's Problems by whipping it. Co-written by Bassist Gerald Casale and lead Singer Mark mothersbaugh Devo recorded Whip it with Producer Robert Margouleff at the Record Plant in Los Angeles."
