short_summary: "Territory of the U.S. between 1912–1959"
description: "The Territory of Alaska or alaska Territory was an organized incorporated Territory of the united States from august 24 1912 until Alaska was granted Statehood on january 3 1959. The Territory was previously russian America, 1784–1867 ; the Department of Alaska, 1867–1884 ; and the District of Alaska, 1884–1912."
