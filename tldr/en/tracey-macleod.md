description: "Tracey Macleod is an english Journalist and Broadcaster. She has presented Arts and Music Programming, including the Late Show and its musical Offshoots New West and Words and Music, Edinburgh Nights, the Booker Prize and the Mercury Music Prize. She hosted a Sunday Night Radio Show on Glr from 1990 for several Years and was one of the Launch Djs on Bbc Radio 6 Music."
