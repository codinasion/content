description: "'Prayer of the Refugee' is a Song by american Punk Rock Band Rise Against, featured on their fourth Studio album the Sufferer & the Witness. The Lyrics were written by leading vocalist tim Mcilrath and Deal with the Themes of forced Displacement and the societal Issues around Refugees. The Song uses a contrasted verse-chorus Form with slow and melancholic Verses against fast-paced and chaotic Choruses. It was released as the Sufferer & the Witness's second single on December 6, 2006."
