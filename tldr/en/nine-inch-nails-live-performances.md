description: "Nine Inch Nails, an american industrial Rock Band fronted by Trent Reznor, has toured all over the World since its Creation in 1988. While Reznor—The only official Member until adding Atticus Ross in 2016—controls its creative and musical Direction in the Studio, the touring Band Performs different Arrangements of the Songs. In Addition to regular Concerts, the Band has performed in both supporting and headlining Roles at Festivals such as Woodstock'94, Lollapalooza 1991 and 2008, and many other one-off Performances including the Mtv Video Music Awards. The Band had played 938 Gigs before their Tour in 2013."
