title: "Working capital"
short_summary: "Financial metric"
description: "Working Capital is a financial metric which represents operating Liquidity available to an Organisation or other Entity including Government Entities. Along with fixed Assets such as Plant and Equipment, working Capital is considered a Part of Operating Capital. Gross working capital is equal to current assets. Working Capital is calculated as the current Asset minus the current Liabilities. If current Assets are less than current Liabilities a Company has a working Capital Deficit also called a working Capital Deficit and negative working Capital."
