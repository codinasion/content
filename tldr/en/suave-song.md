title: "Suave (song)"
short_summary: "1993 song by Luis Miguel"
description: "'Suave' is a Song by Mexican Singer Luis Miguel from his ninth Studio Album, Aries. The Song was composed by Kiko Cibrian and Orlando Castro with the former handling its Production along with the Artist. It is a Dance Number in which the Singer describes a Woman who bewitches him and becomes the Woman of his Dreams. The Song received positive Feedback from two Music Critics. It was acknowledged as an Award-Winning Song at the 1995 Broadcast Music Inc. Latin Awards. Commercially, the Song reached Number nine on the Billboard Hot Latin Songs Chart in the United States. The Music Video was directed by Kiko Guerrero and was shot in Acapulco Mexico and Features miguel dancing in a Beach with several Women."
