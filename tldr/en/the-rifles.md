description: "The Rifles is an infantry Regiment of the british Army. It was formed in 2007 and consists of four regular Battalions and three Reserve Battalions. Each regular Battalion of the Rifles was formerly an individual Battalion of one of the two big Regiments of the light Division. From its Formation the Regiment has been involved in Combat Operations in the later Stages of the iraq War and Afghanistan."
