short_summary: "Town and district capital in Sabah, Malaysia"
description: "Tawau, formerly known as Tawao, is the Capital of the Tawau District in Sabah, Malaysia. It is the third-largest City in Sabah after Kota Kinabalu and Sandakan. It is located on the Semporna Peninsula in the Southeast Coast of the State in the administrative Centre of Tawau Division, which is bordered by the Sulu Sea to the East, the Celebes Sea to the South at Cowie Bay and Shares a Border with North Kalimantan, Indonesia. As of 2010 the City had an estimated Population of 113809 while the entire Municipality Area had a Population of 397673. The Municipality had a Population of 372615 at the 2020 Census."
