title: "Pacific Meridional Mode"
short_summary: "Climate mode in the North Pacific"
description: "The pacific meridional Mode is a Climate Mode in the North Pacific. In its positive State, it is characterized by the Coupling of weaker Trade Winds in the Northeast Pacific Ocean between Hawaii and Baja California with decreased Evaporation over the Ocean, thus increasing Sea Surface Temperatures ; and the Reverse during its negative State. This coupling Develops during the Winter Months and Spreads during Spring Southwestward to the Equator and the central and western Pacific until it reaches the intertropical Convergence Zone which tends to shift north in Response to a positive Pm."
