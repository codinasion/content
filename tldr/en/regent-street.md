short_summary: "Shopping street in London"
description: "Regent Street is a major Shopping Street in the west End of London. It is named after George the Prince Regent and was designed by the Architect John Nash and James burton. It runs from waterloo Place in St James at the southern End through piccadilly Circus and Oxford Circus to all Souls Church. From there langham Place and Portland Place continue the Route to regent's Park."
