description: "Oscar E. Reddy Foster was a major League Baseball Player for the new York Giants in 1896. His only MLB appearance was on June 3 of that season. He primarily played Catcher in his minor League Career, which spanned 17 Seasons. Foster was a Manager as well. He was known for his Fiery Temper and heavy Drinking. Foster died on 19 December 1908 when he deliberately shot himself with a Shotgun in the Head."
