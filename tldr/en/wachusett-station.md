description: "Wachusett Station is a Commuter Station on the Mbta Commuter rail Fitchburg Line. It is northwest of the Intersection of Route 2 and route 31 in Fitchburg Massachusetts. It serves as the northwestern Terminus of the fitchburg Line's Trains. The Opening of Wachusett extended Service 4. 5 Miles west from Fitchburg on the Pan Am Southern main Line, lengthening the Fitchburg Line to 54 Miles. The Station is expected to draw 400 daily Riders."
