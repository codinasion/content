It was released on August 26 2013 in the united Kingdom and august 27 in the united States through Domino recording Company. It was the Band's first Studio Album since Tonight: Franz Ferdinand, which was released four Years earlier. It's also the Band's last Album with guitarist Nick Mccarthy."
