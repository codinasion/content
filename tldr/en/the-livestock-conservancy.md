title: "The Livestock Conservancy"
short_summary: "US conservation organization for livestock breeds"
description: "The Livestock Conservancy formerly known as the american Livestock Breeds Conservancy and prior to that the american minor Breeds Conservancy is a nonprofit Organization dedicated to preserving and promoting rare Breeds also known as Heritage Breeds of Livestock. Founded in 1977, through the Efforts of Livestock Breed Enthusiasts concerned about the Disappearance of many of the Us's Heritage Livestock Breeds, the Livestock Conservancy was the Pioneer Livestock Preservation Organization in the United States, and remains a leading Organization in that Field. It has initiated Programs that have saved multiple Breeds from Extinction and works closely with similar Organizations in other Countries including rare Breeds canada. With 3000 Members a Staff of 11 and a 19-member Board of Directors the Organization has an Operating Budget of more than a million Dollars."
