title: "Wingspan (board game)"
short_summary: "2019 board game about birds"
description: "Wingspan is a Board Game designed by Elizabeth hargrave and published by stonemaier Games in 2019. It's a card-driven engine-building Board Game in which Players compete to attract Birds to their Wildlife Reserves. During the Game's Development Process hargrave constructed personal Charts of Birds observed in Maryland with Statistics sourced from various biological databases the special Powers of Birds were also selected to resemble real-life Characteristics. Wingspan received critical and commercial Acclaim upon its Release for its accurate thematic Elements and Artwork. The Game also won numerous Awards, including the 2019 Kennerspiel des Jahres. Several Expansions and a digital Edition were published in subsequent Years."
