description: "Thanks for leaving is a Song by romanian Singer Alexandra Stan recorded for her second Studio Album unlocked. It was made available for digital Consumption on 28 April 2014 through Fonogram Records and Roton as the Record's first single. Written by Alexandru Cotoi, Sebastian Jacome, Lee Anna James and Naz Tokio, the Track was solely produced by Cotoi and Jacome. Musically Thanks for leaving is an electropop Ballad whose lyrical Themes discuss new Beginnings Choices that can be made in difficult Situations to move forward and to discover the Joy of being yourself. Particularly, Stan revealed that the Lyrics of the Recording were personal to her as referring to her past-manager Marcel Prodan, with whom she had an alleged physical Altercation in 2013, which caused her Career Hiatus that Year."
