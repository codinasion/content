title: "SR Merchant Navy class"
short_summary: "Class of 30 three-cylinder 4-6-2 locomotives"
description: "The Sr Merchant Navy is a Class of air-smoothed 4-6-2 Steam Locomotives designed by Oliver Bulleid for the southern Railway. The Pacific Design was chosen in Preference to several other Designs proposed by Bulleid. The first Members of the Class were built during the second World War and the last of the 30 Locomotives was constructed in 1949."
