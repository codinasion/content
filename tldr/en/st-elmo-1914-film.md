short_summary: "1914 film"
description: "St. Elmo is a silent Drama Film produced by the Balboa Amusement producing Company and distributed by william Fox's Box Office Attractions Company in 1914. It was the first Film Adaptation of augusta Jane Evans's Novel of the same Name of 1866. The Story follows the Life of the Title Character, who kills his Cousin over the Love of Agnes, falls from Grace, and eventually finds Redemption and love with Edna. It is disputed who directed the film; many sources credit Bertram Bracken, while others list St. Elmo as J. Gordon Edwards's directorial Debut."
