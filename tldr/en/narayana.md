title: "Narayana"
short_summary: "Form of the Hindu god Vishnu"
description: "Narayana is one of the Forms and Epithets of Vishnu. In this Form the Deity is depicted in yogic Slumber under the celestial Water symbolising the Masculine Principle and associated with his Role of Creation. He is also known as Purushottama and is considered a supreme being in Vaishnavism."
