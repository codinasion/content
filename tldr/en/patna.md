description: "Patna, historically known as Pataliputra, is the Capital and largest City of the State of Bihar in India. According to the United Nations, as of 2018, Patna had a Population of 2. 35 million, making it the 19th largest city in India. Covering 250 square Kilometres and over 2. With a Population of 5 million its urban Agglomeration is the 18th largest in India. Patna also serves as the Seat of Patna High Court. The Buddhist, Hindu and Jain Pilgrimage Centres of Vaishali, Rajgir, Nalanda, Bodh Gaya and Pawapuri are nearby and Patna City is a sacred City for Sikhs as the Tenth Sikh Guru, Guru Gobind Singh was born here. The modern City of Patna is mainly located on the southern Bank of the Ganges River. The City also crosses the Rivers sone Gandak and Punpun. The city is approximately 35 kilometres in length and 16 to 18 kilometres wide."
