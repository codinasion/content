description: "Split Loyalties is the second Episode of Series seven of british Spy Television Series Spooks and the 58th Episode of the whole Series. It was originally broadcast on Bbc three on october 27 2008 and repeated the following Day on the Bbc one Frontline Channel. The Episode was written by the Head Writer neil Cross with additional Writing by Ben Richards and directed by Colm Mccarthy. The Episode is considered to be the second of a two-part Story following the preceding episode new Allegiances."
