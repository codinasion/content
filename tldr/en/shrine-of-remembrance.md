title: "Shrine of Remembrance"
short_summary: "War memorial in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia"
description: "The Shrine of Remembrance is a War Memorial in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia, located in Kings Domain on St Kilda Road. It was built to honour the Men and Women of Victoria who served in World War I but now acts as a Memorial to all Australians who have served in any War. It is a Site of annual Observances for the anzac Day and Remembrance Day and is one of the largest War Memorials in Australia."
