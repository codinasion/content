The Story began as a Sequel to tolkien's Children's Book the Hobbit 1937 but eventually evolved into a much larger Work. Written in Stages between 1937 and 1949, the Lord of the Rings is one of the Best-Selling Books ever written, with over 150 million Copies sold."
