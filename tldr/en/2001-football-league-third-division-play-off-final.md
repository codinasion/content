description: "The 2001 Football League third Division Play-Off final was an Association Football Match played between Blackpool and Leyton Orient on 26 may 2001 at the Millennium Stadium Cardiff. It was to determine the fourth and last Team to obtain Promotion from the third Division to the second Division of the Football League. The top three Teams of the 200001 Football League third Division Brighton hove Albion Cardiff City and Chesterfield achieved automatic Promotion to the second Division while the Teams from fourth to seventh in the Table took Part in Play-Offs. The Winners of the Semi-Finals of the Play-Offs competed for the final Place in the second Division for the 200102 Season. The losing Semi-Finalists were Hartlepool United and Hull City who had been defeated by Blackpool and Leyton Orient respectively. It was the first Season that the Finals of the Play-Offs were contested at the Millennium Stadium during the Redevelopment of Wembley Stadium."
