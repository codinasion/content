short_summary: "2009 single by Flo Rida featuring Kesha"
description: "'right Round' is a single performed by american Rapper Flo Rida featuring guest Vocals from american Singer Kesha. It was released as the Lead single from his second Studio Album R. O. O. T. S. It was released to radio on January 27, 2009 and was digitally released on February 10 by Poe Boy Entertainment and Atlantic Records. The Song heavily interpolates in its Hook the Chorus of the Song dead or alive from the 1980s. According to one of the Songs writers the Chorus Refers to a Stripper. Kesha contributed guest Vocals to the Song, but was uncredited in the United States and Canada during its Run atop the Charts."
