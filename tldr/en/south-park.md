The Series revolves around four Boys—Stan Marsh, Kyle Broflovski, Eric Cartman, and Kenny Mccormick—And their Exploits in and around the titular Colorado Town. South Park also features many recurring Characters. The Series became infamous for its Profanity and dark surreal Humor that satirizes a vast Range of subject Matter."
