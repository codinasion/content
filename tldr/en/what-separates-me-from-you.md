short_summary: "2010 studio album by A Day to Remember"
description: "What separates me from you is the fourth Studio Album by american Rock band a Day to Remember, and their third and final for Victory Records. Originally planned for Release in late October 2010 the Album was delayed by a few Weeks until mid November. What separates me from you was published in the Uk on 15 november 2010 and in the us on 16 november 2010. The Album which was recorded mainly from may to july 2010 at the Wade Studios in ocala Florida was the first Release featuring guitarist Kevin Skaff. The Album was mostly produced by Chad gilbert with Help from andrew Wade and Vocalist Jeremy Mckinnon of the Band. All I want was released as the first single. It reached the Number 12 in the us. S. Alternative Songs Chart and Number 25 on the Rock Songs Chart."
