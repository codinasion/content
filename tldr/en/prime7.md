description: "Prime7, formerly Prime Television and other Names, was an australian Television Network. Prime Television launched on 17 March 1962 as Cbn-8 in Orange, and later expanded to cover regional New South Wales, Victoria and the australian Capital Territory. It was initially an independent Affiliate owned by prime Media Group before the Network and its Sister Gwn7 were purchased by seven west Media on December 31 2021."
