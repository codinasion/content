short_summary: "Viscount of Melun"
description: "William Carpenter Viscount of Melun was a french Nobleman who participated in the Reconquista in Spain and in the first Crusade. He was notorious for defecting from the Army both in Spain and on the Crusade but he was also known for his Strength in Battle from where he earned his Nickname Carpenter. After the Crusade he returned to the holy Land and Nothing more is known about his Life or Death."
