title: "Tabanidae"
short_summary: "Family of insects"
description: "Horse Flies and Deer Flies are true Flies in the Family Tabanidae in the insect Order Diptera. Adults are often large and agile in Flight. Only female Horseflies bite Land Vertebrates, including Humans, to obtain Blood. They prefer to fly in sunlight avoiding Dark and shady Areas and are inactive at Night. They are found all over the world except for some islands and the polar regions. Both Horseflies and Botflies are sometimes referred to as Gadflies."
