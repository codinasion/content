title: "Vauxhall Bridge"
short_summary: "Arch bridge in central London"
description: "Vauxhall Bridge is a grade Ii listed Steel and granite Bridge with a Deck Arch in central London. It crosses the River thames in a southeast-north-west Direction between Vauxhall on the south Bank and Pimlico on the north Bank. It opened in 1906 and replaced an earlier Bridge originally known as Regent Bridge but later renamed Vauxhall Bridge built as Part of a Scheme for redeveloping the south Bank of the Thames between 1809 and. The Bridge was constructed in a Location on the River previously served by a Ferry."
