description: "Sheva Alomar is a Character in resident Evil a survival Horror Video Game Series created by japanese Company Capcom. She was introduced in the Video Game Resident evil 5 in 2009 in which she is a native african Operative in an Anti-Bioterrorism Group and the new Partner of the Series' veteran Chris redfield. When played with a single Player Sheva is controlled by the Game's artificial Intelligence and is controlled in cooperative Mode by a second Player."
