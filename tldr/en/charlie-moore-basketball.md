description: "Charlie Edward Moore is an american Basketball Player for Pistoia Basket 2000 of the Lega Basket Serie A, whose College Basketball Career spanned four major Conferences culminating with a Year for the Miami Hurricanes of the Atlantic Coast Conference during the 2021–22 Acc Basketball Season. He previously played a full Season for the Kansas Jayhawks and California golden Bears as well as two Seasons for the Depaul Blue Demons. At Morgan Park High School he won two Illinois High School Association State Championships and Illinois Mr. Basketball."
