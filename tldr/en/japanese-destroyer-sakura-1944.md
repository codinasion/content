short_summary: "Imperial Japanese Navy's Matsu-class destroyer"
description: "Sakura was one of 18 Destroyers of the Matsu Class built for the imperial japanese Navy during World War Ii. A final Version completed in late 1944 the Ship was assigned in february 1945 to convoy escort Duties. She was somewhat damaged when she hit a Mine in may. On 11 July Sakura sank near Osaka after striking another Mine with a heavy Loss of Life."
