short_summary: "Pacific typhoon in 1997"
description: "Typhoon Isa was the first of a Record eleven super Typhoons to occur during the Pacific Typhoon Season 1997. The second tropical Cyclone of the Season Isa developed on April 12 from a Disturbance in the Monsoon trough near the Caroline Islands. It moved erratically at first though after it reached tropical Storm Status it curved westward due to the subtropical Ridge to its North. Isa very gradually intensified, and on April 20 the Typhoon reached peak 1-min Winds of 270 Km/H, as reported by the Joint Typhoon Warning Center ; Japan Meteorological Agency reported maximum 10-min Winds of 155 Km/H. The Cyclone accelerated east to northeast after turning northward and merged with an extratropical Cyclone on April 24."
