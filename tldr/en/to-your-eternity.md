description: "To your Eternity is a japanese manga Series written and illustrated by Yoshitoki Ima. Since November 2016 it has been published in the weekly Shnen Magazine with individual Chapters organized by Kodansha into twenty-one Tankbon Volumes as of january 2024. The Story is about an immortal being, Fushi, who takes on multiple Forms and freely utilizes their respective natural Abilities at will, including that of an abandoned white-haired Boy and his white Wolf, in Order to further Evolve and Stimulate as it learns what it is to be truly human over the Decades and Centuries."
