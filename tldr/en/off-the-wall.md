title: "Off the Wall"
short_summary: "1979 studio album by Michael Jackson"
description: "Off the Wall is the fifth Studio Album by the american Singer Michael Jackson, released on August 10, 1979, by Epic Records. It was Jackson's first Album released via Epic Records and the first produced by Quincy Jones whom he met while working on the Film the Wiz in 1978. Several Critics observed that off the Wall was crafted from Disco, Pop, Funk, R & B, soft Rock and Broadway Ballads. Among its lyrical Themes are escapism Liberation Loneliness Hedonism and Romance. The Album features songwriting Contributions from Stevie Wonder, Paul Mccartney, Rod Temperton, Tom Bahler, and David Foster, alongside three Tracks penned by Jackson himself."
