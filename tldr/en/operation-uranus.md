description: "Operation Uranus was the Codename of the 1923 November 1942 strategic Operation of the soviet red Army on the eastern Front of World War Ii which led to the Encirclement of the Axis Forces in the Vicinity of Stalingrad the third and fourth romanian Armies and Portions of the. The red Army conducted the Operation at roughly the Midpoint of the five-month Battle of Stalingrad it intended to destroy german Forces in and around Stalingrad. In september 1942 Planning for Operation Uranus began and developed simultaneously with Plans to envelop and destroy german Forces in the Caucasus."
