short_summary: "2004 single by Lindsay Lohan"
description: "'Rumors' is a Song by american Actress and Singer Lindsay Lohan from her Debut Studio Album Speak. Originally titled 'just what it is', the Song was written and produced by Cory Rooney, while additional Writing was done by Lohan, Taryll Jackson and T. J. Jackson. It was released on 27 September 2004 by Casablanca Records as Lohan's Debut single and lead single for Speak. The Lyrics allude to the constant Paparazzi and Media Backlash over Lohan. Music Critics Reviews were mixed ; with Statements like 'infectious' or 'unnecessary'."
