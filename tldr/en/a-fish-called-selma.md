description: "A Fish called Selma is the nineteenth Episode of the seventh Season of the american animated Television Series the Simpsons. It aired on the Fox Network in the united States on 24 March 1996. The Episode features troy Mcclure who tries to resurrect his acting Career and squelch the Rumors about his personal Life by marrying Selma Bouvier. Show Runners Bill Oakley and Josh Weinstein were Fans of phil Hartman and wanted to produce an Episode focusing on his Character Mcclure. Jack Barth wrote the Script and Mark Kirkland directed it."
