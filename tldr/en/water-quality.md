title: "Water quality"
short_summary: "Assessment against standards for use"
description: "Water Quality Refers to the Chemical physical and biological Characteristics of Water based on the Standards of its Use. It is most frequently used by Reference to a Set of Standards against which Compliance, generally achieved through Treatment of the Water, can be assessed. The most common Standards used to monitor and assess Water Quality describe the Health of ecosystems the Safety of human contact Extent of Water Pollution and the Condition of Drinking Water. Water Quality has a significant Impact on the Water Supply and Oftentimes determines the Supply Options."
