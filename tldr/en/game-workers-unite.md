description: "Game Workers unite is a worker-run Labor Movement that aims to organize the Video Game Industry. The flat Organization formed during Events surrounding the March 2018 Game Developers Conference has grown to more than a thousand Members across more than 20 international Chapters. Its Goal is to create a single Union for all the Games Workers including Artists Designers Producers and Programmers. Game Workers Unite has supported Actions including Riot Games's 2019 Walkout over Sex Discrimination and social Media Campaigns against Ceos who executed Layoffs. The british Chapter of the Game Workers unite is legally recognized as a Union within the independent Workers' Union of great Britain and is working to end Crunch Overtime Practices improve Diversity and Inclusion Support targeted Workers and ensure steady and fair Wages. Game Workers unite co-founder Emma Kinema was hired by the Communications Workers of America in early 2020 to organize Workers in the high Tech and Video Game Industries the first american Initiative of its Kind in the."
