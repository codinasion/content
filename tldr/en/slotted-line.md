title: "Slotted line"
short_summary: "Device used for microwave measurements"
description: "Slotted Lines are used for Microwave Measurements and Consist of a movable Probe inserted into a Slot in a Transmission Line. They are used in Conjunction with a microwave Power Source and usually, in keeping with their low-cost Application, a low Cost Schottky Diode Detector and Vswr Meter rather than an expensive Microwave Power Meter."
