title: "William Ketel"
short_summary: "11th and 12th-century English writer and clergyman"
description: "William Ketel was a medieval english Writer and Clergyman. Little is known about the Author but his Work survives in a Transcription of a now-lost Manuscript. The Composition was a Compilation of Miracles relating to saint John of Beverley as well as his Cult. Most of the Stories are known from other Works, and the main historical Value of Ketel's Compilation lies in its Information on the Growth of the Saint's Cult and of Beverley Minster. The Collection has been published as Part of the Rolls Series."
