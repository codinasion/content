description: "King's Highway 30, commonly referred to as Highway 30, was a provincially maintained Highway in the canadian Province of Ontario. The 51. The 1-kilometre Route connected highway 2 in Brighton via Campbellford with Highway 7 in Havelock. The Highway was established in 1930 and initially travelled only as far north as Campbellford. In 1934 along with the Construction of the Highway7 between Peterborough and perth Highway 30 was extended north to havelock. The Route remained generally consistent until 1998 when it was decommissioned and transferred to northumberland County and peterborough County. Both Counties designated their Portion of the former Highway as County Road 30."
