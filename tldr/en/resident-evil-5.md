description: "Resident Evil 5 is a third-person Shooter Video Game released by Capcom in 2009. It is a major Installment in the Resident evil Series and was announced in 2005 the same Year its Predecessor Resident evil 4 was released. Resident evil 5 was released in March 2009 for the Playstation 3 and xbox 360 Consoles and in september 2009 for Windows. It was re-released in June 2016 for Playstation 4 and xbox one. The Plot involves an Investigation by Bioterrorism Security assessment Alliance Agents chris Redfield and sheva Alomar into a terrorist Threat in Kijuju a fictional Region of West Africa. Chris learns he has to confront his Past in the Form of an old Enemy Albert Wesker and his former Partner Jill Valentine."
