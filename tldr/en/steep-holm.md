short_summary: "Island in the Bristol Channel, United Kingdom"
description: "Steep Holm is an english Island in the Bristol Channel. The island covers 48. 87 Acres at high Tide, expanding to 63. 26 Acres at mean low Water. It is 78 Metres above Sea Level at its highest Point. Administratively it forms part of the unitary authority of North Somerset within the ceremonial county of Somerset; between 1 April 1974 and 1 April 1996, it was administered as part of Avon. Nearby is Flat Holm Island, Part of Wales."
