description: "Pizza Delivery is the first Segment of the fifth Episode of the first Season of the american animated Tv Series Spongebob Squarepants. It was aired in the united States for the first Time on Nickelodeon on August 14 1999. The Segment was written by Sherm Cohen, Aaron Springer, and Peter Burns, and the Animation was directed by Sean Dempsey. Cohen also functioned as storyboard Director, and Springer worked as storyboard Artist."
