title: "USS Munda"
short_summary: "Casablanca-class escort carrier of the US Navy"
description: "The uss Munda was the last of 50 Escort Ships built by the united States navy in the Casablanca Class for Service in World War Ii. She was named after the Battle of Munda point that occurred in 1943 on the Island of new georgia Part of the Solomon Islands. The Ship was launched in may 1944 and commissioned in July it served as an Aircraft Transport and a replenishment Escort Carrier in the Pacific Theatre. Postwar, she participated in Operation Magic Carpet, the repatriation of U. S. forces from bases scattered around the Pacific. She was decommissioned in April 1946 when she was mothballed in the Pacific Reserve Fleet. In June 1960 she was sold for scrapping."
