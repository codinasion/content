short_summary: "Family of laptop computers made by Asus"
description: "Zenbook is a Family of ultrabooks low-bulk Notebook Computers produced by Asus. The first Zenbooks were released in october 2011 and the original Range of Products were amended and expanded in 2012. Models range from 12-inch Laptops with Power efficient Components but lacking Connectivity and with only integrated Graphics Processors to 15-inch Laptops with discrete Graphics Processors and optical Disc Drives. Most Zenbooks use Intel Core ultra-low Voltage Processors and nvidia Gpus when no integrated Graphics are used. In late 2012 Asus introduced new Models with touch Screen Technology to take Advantage of Windows 8. Most Models drew Comparisons to the Macbook Air. The Zenbook mainly competes against Computers such as Acer's Aspire, Dell's Inspiron and Xps, Hp's Pavilion, Hp Stream and Envy, Lenovo's Ideapad, Samsung's Sens and Toshiba's Satellite."
