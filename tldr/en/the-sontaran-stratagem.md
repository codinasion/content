description: "The Sontaran Stratagem is the fourth Episode of the fourth Series of the british Science Fiction - Television - Series Doctor who that depicts the Adventures of a time-travelling humanoid Alien known as the Doctor. The Episode was broadcast on Bbc one on April 26 2008. The Episode and its Sequel, 'the Poison Sky', were written by Helen Raynor, who previously wrote the linked episodes 'Daleks in Manhattan' and 'Evolution of the Daleks' in the third Series."
