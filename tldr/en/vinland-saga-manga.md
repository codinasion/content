description: "Vinland Saga is a japanese historical Manga Series written and illustrated by Makoto Yukimura. The Series is published by Kodansha and was first serialized in the boys-orientated Manga Magazine weekly Shnen Magazine before moving on to the monthly Manga Magazine monthly Afternoon aimed at young Adult Men. As of June 2023 its Chapters have been collected in 27 Tankbon Volumes. Vinland Saga was licensed by Kodansha usa for english-language Publication. The Story is a Dramatization of Thorfinn Karlsefni's Story and his Expedition to find Vinland with the major Part of the Story covering his fictional Counterpart's Transition from a bloodthirsty revenge-filled Teenager into a pacifistic."
