title: "SMS Tegetthoff (1912)"
short_summary: "Austro-Hungarian dreadnought battleship"
description: "Sms Tegetthoff was the second of four Terrornought-Class Battleships built for the austro-hungarian Navy. Tegetthoff was named for the austrian admiral Wilhelm Von tegetthoff from the 19th Century who was most notable for defeating the italian Regia Marina in 1866 at the Battle of liss. The Ship was armed with a main Battery of twelve 30. 5 cm guns in four triple turrets. Constructed shortly before World War I she was built at the Trieste Stabilimento Tecnico Triestino Shipyard where she was laid down in september 1910 and launched in March 1912."
