short_summary: "National park in California, United States"
description: "Yosemite National Park is a national Park in California. It is bordered on the Southeast by the sierra national Forest and on the Northwest by the stanislaus national Forest. The Park is managed by the National Park Service and covers 759,620 Acres in four Counties – centered in Tuolumne and Mariposa, extending north and east to Mono and south to Madera. In 1984 Yosemite was designated a World Heritage Site and recognized internationally for its Granite Mountains waterfalls clear Streams giant sequoia Groves lakes Mountains meadows Glaciers and biological Diversity. The Park is mostly designated Wilderness. Yosemite is one of the largest and least fragmented Habitat Blocks in Sierra Nevada."
