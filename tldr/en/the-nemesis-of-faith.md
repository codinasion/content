short_summary: "Book by James Anthony Froude"
description: "The Nemesis of Faith is an epistolary philosophical Novel by James anthony froude published in 1849. Partly autobiographical the novel depicts the Causes and Consequences of a young Priest's Crisis of Faith. Like many of his Contemporaries froude came to question his christian Faith in Light of early nineteenth Century Developments in History Theology and Science. Froude was influenced particularly by the catholic Teachings of the oxford Movement and the new Approach to religious Scholarship developed by the german higher Critics."
