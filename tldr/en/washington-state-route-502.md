description: "State Route 502 is a 6. 12-mile-long State Highway in the U. S. State of Washington, serving the City of Battle Ground in Clark County. The Highway Travels east from an Interchange with interstate 5 North of Mount Vista through Dollars corner and Battle Ground until an Intersection with sr503 serving as Connector between Battle Ground and I-5. Before the Highway renumbering in 1964 Sr502 was Part of the secondary State Highway1S which was established in 1937. The Highway travelled south from its current western Terminus until the Construction of a new Interchange with I-5 in 2008 Part of a Project to widen Sr502 and install a median Barrier on the Roadway."
