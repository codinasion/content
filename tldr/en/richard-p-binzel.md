short_summary: "American astronomer"
description: "Richard 'Rick' P. Binzel is an american Astronomer and Professor of planetary Science at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. He is a Discoverer of minor Planets, Photometrist and the Inventor of the Torino Scale, a Method for categorizing the Impact Hazard associated with near-earth Objects such as Asteroids and Comets. He is also the frequent Trip Leader for the Mit alumni Association."
