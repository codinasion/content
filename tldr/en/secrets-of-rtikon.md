description: "Secrets of Rtikon is an action-adventure open World Video Game developed and published by Broken Rules. The Player controls a Bird and must explore Rtikon's Environment to find its Secrets. Game Elements include Animals with specific Behaviors and ancient Puzzles. The Game was officially announced in July 2013 and followed by Alpha and Steam early Access Releases. Secrets of Rtikon was released on 17 April 2014 for Windows os X and Linux Platforms. It received mixed Reviews from Reviewers, who praised its Artwork and Movement Physics, but criticized its Inconsistency, Lack of Cohesion, and technical Issues."
