description: "One of us must know is a Song by american Singer-Songwriter Bob Dylan which was released on february 14 1966 as a single and in June that Year as the fourth Track on his seventh Studio Album Blonde. The Song was written by Dylan and produced by Bob Johnston. The Story is the Narrative of a Burnt out Relationship. It was recorded on 25 january 1966 in Columbia Studio a in new York with Dylan and other Musicians developing the Song through more than twenty takes during the Session."
