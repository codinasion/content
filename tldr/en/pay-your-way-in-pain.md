description: "Pay your Way in Pain is a Song by the american Musician St. Vincent for her sixth studio album, Daddy's Home . The Song was written and produced by St. Vincent and Jack Antonoff. It was released on March 4, 2021, as the Album's lead single by Loma Vista Recordings and Concord Records. A synth-driven Funk Song inspired by the 1970s its Lyrics were inspired by the Singer's own Struggles."
