title: "Priscilla Jana"
short_summary: "South African human rights lawyer (1943–2020)"
description: "Devikarani Priscilla Sewpal Jana was a south african human Rights Lawyer, Politician and Diplomat. She participated in the african national Congress as a Member during the anti-apartheid Movement both in legal Activism as well as in the underground Movement to end Apartheid. She represented many significant Figures in the Movement, including south african President Nelson Mandela, Winnie Madikizela-Mandela, Steve Biko, Govan Mbeki, Walter Sisulu, and Archbishop Desmond Tutu. Jana was one of the very few South Africans who had Access to political Prisoners including Mandela at the maximum Security Robben Island Prison and served as an Emissary for coded Messages between the political Prisoners and the Anc Leadership."
