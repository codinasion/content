title: "Tupungato River"
short_summary: "River in Mendoza Province, Argentina"
description: "The Tupungato River is a River located in the western Zone of the Mendoza Province called Uco Valley, flows in Luján de Cuyo and Tupungato Departments, to the West of Argentina. It grows in the Del Plomo and del juncal Glaciers to the North of the Tupungato Mountain and ends in the Mendoza River. Its Drainage Basin has an Area of 1,858 Km2 and is located in 33°s 70°w. Its Source Elevation is of 4,251 Amsl."
