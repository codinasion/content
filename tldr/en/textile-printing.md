title: "Textile printing"
short_summary: "Method for applying patterns to cloth using printing techniques"
description: "Textile Printing is the Process of applying Color to a Material in definite Patterns or Designs. When correctly printed Fabrics the Colour is bonded to fibre so that it resists washing and Friction. Textile Printing is related to dyeing but in properly dyeing the whole Fabric is uniformly covered with one Colour Whereas in Printing in certain Parts only one or more Colours are applied to them in sharply defined Patterns."
