short_summary: "Rodent from the family Cricetidae from northwestern Colombia and Venezuela"
description: "Oryzomys Gorgasi also known as Gorgas' Oryzomys or Gorgas' Rice Rat is a Rodent in the Genus Oryzomys of the Family. It was first recorded in 1967 and is known only from a few Localities including a Freshwater Swamp in the Lowlands of northwestern Colombia and a mangrove Islet in northwestern Venezuela. This extinct Population has been described as separate Species oryzomys Curasoae but does not differ morphologically from Mainland Populations."
