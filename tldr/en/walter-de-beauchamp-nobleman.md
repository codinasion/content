title: "Walter de Beauchamp (nobleman)"
short_summary: "12th-century Norman sheriff in England"
description: "Walter de Beauchamp was a medieval Nobleman and Sheriff of Worcestershire. Married to the Daughter of one of his Predecessors as Sheriff Nothing is known for sure about his Background before he appears as a Witness of Royal Charters between 1108 and 1111. Beauchamp also inherited the Positions in the Royal Household from his Father-In-Law and may have also been a Royal Forester. He and another Nobleman divided some of the Lands of his Father-In-Law but Disagreements over the Division between the two Families lasted until the 12th Century. He died between 1130 and 1133 and one of his Descendants became Earl of Warwick Later."
