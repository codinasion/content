Wild Banteng are usually larger and heavier than their domestic Counterparts but are otherwise similar in Appearance. The Banteng shows extensive sexual Dimorphism ; Adult Bulls are generally dark Brown to black, larger and more sturdily built than Adult Cows, which are Thinner and usually pale Brown or Chestnut red.
