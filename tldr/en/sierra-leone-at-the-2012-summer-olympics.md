title: "Sierra Leone at the 2012 Summer Olympics"
short_summary: "Sporting event delegation"
description: "Sierra Leone competed at the 2012 Summer Olympics in London, from 27 July to 12 August 2012. It marked the tenth Appearance at the Summer Olympics for the Nation since its Debut in the Summer Olympics in 1968. The Sierra Leone Delegation included two Track and Field Athletes ; Ibrahim Turay, a Sprinter and Ola Sesay, a long Jumper. Sesay and Turay were selected as Flag Bearers for the Opening and Closing Ceremonies respectively. But None of them advanced beyond the first Round."
