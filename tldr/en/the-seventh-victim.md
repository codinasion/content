description: "The seventh Victim is a 1943 american Horror Film directed by Mark Robson and starring hugh Beaumont Isabel Jewell jean Brooks and tom Conway. Written by Charles o'neal and dewitt Bodeen and produced by val Lewton for Rko Radio pictures the Film focuses on a young Woman who stumbles on an underground Cult of Devil Worshippers in greenwich village new York City while searching. It marks Robson's directorial Debut, and was Hunter's first onscreen Role."
