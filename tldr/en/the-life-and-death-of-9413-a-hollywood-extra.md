short_summary: "1928 film"
description: "The Life and Death of 9413 a Hollywood Extra is a 1928 american silent experimental short Film co-written and directed by Robert Florey and Slavko Vorkapi. Considered a Landmark of american avant-garde Cinema it tells the Story of a Man who comes to hollywood with Dreams of becoming a Star he fails and becomes dehumanized with Studio Executives reducing him to the Role of an extra and writing the Number 9413 on his Forehead."
