description: "Tomba! 2: The Evil Swine Return, or simply Tomba! 2 is a platform-adventure Game developed by Whoopee Camp and published for the Playstation by Sony Computer Entertainment. The Game was released in Japan in 1999 and the following Year in other Territories. The game is a sequel to Tomba! And it centres on the Exploits of the eponymous feral Child who is trying to save his Friend Tabby from an evil Race of anthropomorphic Pigs."
