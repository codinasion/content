short_summary: "Eleven-day blockade of Reading, Berkshire 1643"
description: "The Siege of Reading was an eleven-day Blockade of reading Berkshire during the first english civil War. Reading had been garrisoned by the Royalists in november 1642 and under the Command of Sir arthur Aston had 3300 Soldiers. On April 14 1643 Robert devereux 3rd Earl of Essex brought a parliamentarian Army of 19000 Men to lay Siege to the City and two Days later began bombarding the Village."
