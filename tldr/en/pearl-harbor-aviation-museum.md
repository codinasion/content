title: "Pearl Harbor Aviation Museum"
short_summary: "Aviation museum in Hawaii, United States"
description: "Pearl Harbor Aviation Museum is a non-profit Corporation founded in 1999 to develop an Aviation Museum in Hawaii. Part of Senator Daniel Inouye's Vision of a Rebirth of Ford Island the Museum hosts a Variety of Aviation Exhibits with a Majority relating directly to pearl Harbor and World War Ii. The first Section of the Museum, Hangar 37, opened with the Museum on December 7, 2006, and Features much of the Museum's static Exhibits. The Hangars at the Museum Show Damage from the Attacks on Pearl Harbor from December 7 1941."
