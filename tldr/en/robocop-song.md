description: "Robocop is a Song by american Record Producer and Vocalist kanye West from his fourth Studio Album 808 Heartbreak. The Song contains background Vocals by tony Williams and Jeff Bhasker. It was solely produced by West, who served as a Songwriter Alongside Esthero, Malik Yusef, Consequence, Kid Cudi, Williams, Bhasker, T-Pain, and Young Jeezy. Due to the embodiment of Patrick Doyle's work, a songwriting credit for him was added. The Song was recorded in 2008 across numerous Studios, being tweaked to Perfection. Manny Marroquin explained how it was a Challenge to confirm that multiple Versions were created. An art pop number with an orchestral style, it embodies portions of 'Kissing in the Rain', performed by Doyle."
