description: "The Mavericks is an american Country Music Band from Miami Florida. The Band consists of eddie Perez Paul Deakin Raul Malo and Jerry Dale Mcfadden. In 1989 Malo and Deakin founded the Band along with robert Reynolds and Ben Peeler. After one independent Album, the Band was signed by Mca Nashville Records and David Lee Holt replaced Peeler on lead Guitar ; he would be replaced by Nick Kane shortly after their second Mca Album and third overall 1994's what a crying Shame. The Band recorded four Albums for Mca and one for Mercury Records before disbanding in 2000. In 2003 they reunited for an Album on sanctuary Records by which Point Perez became their fourth Guitarist and former touring Keyboardist Jerry dale Mcfadden became an official fifth Member. Malo Deakin Reynolds Perez and Mcfadden reunited for the second Time in 2012 for a Series of new Albums first on the Imprint big Machine Records Valory and then on Mono Mundo. In 2014 Reynolds was fired and ed Friedland served as touring Bassist until 2023."
