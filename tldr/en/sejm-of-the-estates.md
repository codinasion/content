description: "The Sejm of the Estates or Estates of Galicia were the Parliament in the first Half of the 19th Century Galicia Region in austrian Empire. The body existed from 1775 to 1845. In the History of the polish Parliament, it succeeded the general Sejm and local Sejmiks on the Territories of the austrian Partition. The Estate was disbanded after the Krakw Uprising of 1846. They were succeeded in 1861 by the Sejm of the Land."
