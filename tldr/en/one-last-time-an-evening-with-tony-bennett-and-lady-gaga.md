description: "One last Time an Evening with tony Bennett and lady Gaga is a Tv Special of american Singers tony Bennett and Lady Gaga. It originally aired on November 28, 2021, on CBS while being simulcast on Paramount+. Consisting of select Duets and Solo Performances, it was Part of the Promotion of their second collaborative Studio Album, Love for Sale, released on September 30, 2021. A Celebration of Bennett's 95th Birthday, the special was recorded on August 3 and 5, 2021, when Bennett and Gaga presented a Pair of Shows at Radio City Music Hall in New York City. They were Bennett's final public Performances as he retired shortly after from performing live due to his Health Condition."
