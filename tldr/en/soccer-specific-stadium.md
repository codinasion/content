title: "Soccer-specific stadium"
short_summary: "Type of sports stadium"
description: "Soccer-specific Stadium is a Term used mainly in the united States and Canada to refer to a Sport Stadium whose primary Function is to host Soccer Matches as opposed to a Multipurpose Stadium which Caters to a Variety of Sports. A soccer-specific Stadium may host other sporting Events and Concerts but the Design and Purpose of a soccer-specific Stadium is mainly for Soccer. Some Buildings have a permanent Stage in one End of the Stadium used for Concerts."
