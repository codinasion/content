short_summary: "Railroad line in New York City"
description: "The Park Avenue main Line which consists of the Park Avenue Tunnel and the Park Avenue Viaduct is a Railroad Line in the Borough of Manhattan in new York City running entirely along Park Avenue. The Line carries four Tracks of the metro-north Railroad as a Tunnel from grand central Terminal at 42nd Street to a Portal at 97th Street where it rises to a Viaduct North of 99th Street and continues through the Harlem River through Park Avenue Bridge. During rush Hours metro-north Uses three of the four Tracks in the Direction of Peak Traffic."
