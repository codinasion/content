title: "Titan Clydebank"
short_summary: "Cantilever crane at Clydebank, West Dunbartonshire, Scotland"
description: "Titan Clydebank, more commonly known as the Titan Crane is a 150-foot-high Cantilever Crane at Clydebank, West Dunbartonshire, Scotland. It was designed to be used in Lifting of heavy Equipment such as Engines and Boilers during the Fitting of Battleships and ocean Liners at the John brown Company Shipyard. It was the World's first electrically powered Cantilever Crane and the largest Crane of its Type at the Time of its Completion."
