title: "Battle of Romani"
short_summary: "World War I attack on Suez Canal"
description: "The Battle of Romani was the last Ground Attack by the central Powers on the suez Canal during the first World War at the Beginning of the Sinai and Palestine Campaigns. The Battle was fought between 3 and 5 August 1916 near the egyptian Town of Romani and the ancient Village of Pelusium on the Sinai Peninsula 23 Miles east of the suez Canal. This Victory by the 52nd Division and the Anzac mounted Division of the egyptian expeditionary Force over a joint Ottoman and german Force who had marched across the Sinai marked the End of the Defence of the suez Canal Campaign also known as the offensive Zur."
