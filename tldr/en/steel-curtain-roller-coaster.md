short_summary: "Roller coaster at Kennywood"
description: "Steel Curtain is a Steel Hypercoaster located at Kennywood Amusement Park in West Mifflin, Pennsylvania, United States, near Pittsburgh. The Coaster is manufactured by ss Sansei Technologies and has 9 Inversions including a 197-foot Cork Screw which is the World's tallest Inversion. Themed to the Pittsburgh Steelers, the Roller Coaster is named after the Steel Curtain, the Nickname for the Steelers' defensive Line during the 1970s."
