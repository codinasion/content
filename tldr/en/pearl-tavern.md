description: "Pearl Tavern was a Sports Bar and Restaurant in Portland Oregon's pearl District in the united States. The Restaurant was established in December 2016 by former american Football Player Joey Harrington Bartender Ryan Magarian and chefstable Restaurateur Kurt Huffman. Intended to be more casual than a Steakhouse the family-friendly Pearl Tavern served various Cuts of Steak fried chicken Fish and Risotto as well as Bar Snacks and other Options for Brunch and happy Hour. The Interior had dark leather Booths and multiple wide Screen Televisions for Sports viewing and the Servers wore Plaids. Three Percent of Harrington's Profits benefit the nonprofit Organization."
