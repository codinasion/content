title: "Virginia militia"
short_summary: "Military unit"
description: "The Virginia militia is an armed force composed of all citizens of the Commonwealth of Virginia capable of bearing arms. The Virginia Militia was established as Part of the english Militia System in 1607. Militia Service in Virginia was Compulsory for all free Males. The main purpose of the Crown's militia was to repel invasions and insurrections and to enforce the laws of the colony."
