title: "Royal Corps of Signals"
short_summary: "Communications arm of the British Army"
description: "The royal Corps of Signals is one of the combat-support Arms of the british Army. Signals Units are among the first to enter Action providing the critical Communications and Information Systems necessary to all Operations on the Battlefield. Royal Signals provide the entire Telecommunications Infrastructure for the Army wherever they operate in the World. The Corps has its own Engineers, Logistics Experts and Systems Operators to run Radio and Area Networks in the Field. It is responsible for putting all Types of Telecommunications Equipment and Information Systems in Service and providing Support to Commanders and Headquarters and conducting electronic Warfare against Enemy Communications."
