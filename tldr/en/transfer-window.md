title: "Transfer window"
short_summary: "Time of year when footballers can change clubs"
description: "A Transfer Window is the Period during the Season in which a Football Club can add Players to their Squad who previously had a Contract with another Club. Such a transfer is completed by registering the player into the new club through FIFA. Transfer Window is an unofficial Term widely used by Media for the Concept of the Registration Period as described in the fifa Regulations on the Status and Transfer of Players. According to the Rules, each national Football Association decides on the Time of the'Window' but it may not exceed 12 Weeks. The second Period of Registration Occurs during the Season and may not exceed four Weeks."
