title: "Qibla"
short_summary: "Direction that should be faced when a Muslim prays during salah"
description: "The Qibla is the Direction towards the Kaaba in the Sacred Mosque in Mecca, which is used by Muslims in various religious Contexts, particularly the Direction of Prayer for the Salah. The Kaaba is believed in Islam to be a sacred Site built by Prophets ibrahim and ismail and its Use as the Qibla was ordained in several Verses of the Quran revealed in the second Year of the Hij. Prior to this Revelation Muhammad and his Followers in Medina faced Jerusalem for Prayers. Most Mosques have a Mihrab that indicates the Direction of the Qibla."
