short_summary: "Screw corvette of the Prussian and German Imperial Navy"
description: "Sms Freya was a Steam Corvette of the german Kaiserliche Marine. She was the third Member of the Ariadne Class which included two other Ships Ariadne and Luise. Following the austro-prussian War ordered as Part of a large naval Expansion Program she was laid down after the franco-prussian War in 1872. She was launched in December 1874 and completed in October 1876. Freya was built to a different Design than her Sisters longer and carried a heavier Battery of Twelve Guns."
