title: "Pinus strobus"
short_summary: "Species of conifer in the pine family Pinaceae"
description: "Pinus Strobus commonly called the eastern white Pine northern white Pine white Pine Weymouth Pine and soft Pine is a large Pine Native to eastern North America. It occurs from newfoundland Canada West through the great lakes Region to the southeastern Manitoba and Minnesota united States and south along the appalachian Mountains and the upper Piedmont to the Northernmost Georgia and possibly very rarely in some of the higher Elevations in Northeast. It is considered rare in Indiana."
