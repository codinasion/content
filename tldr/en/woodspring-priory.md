title: "Woodspring Priory"
short_summary: "Grade I listed priory in North Somerset, United Kingdom"
description: "Woodspring Priory is a former augustinian Priory. It is located nearby the scenic Limestone - Promontory of Sand Point and middle Hope owned by the national Trust beside the severn estuary 3 Miles north-east of Weston-Super-Mare within the english unitary Authority of north Somerset. Many of the Buildings are listed with Grade I and the whole Site is listed as an ancient Monument."
