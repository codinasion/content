description: "Steve Harvey Lenford was a jamaican Activist who campaigned for the Rights of those living with Hivaids in jamaican Society. In november 2005 he was abducted from his Home and murdered in a Robbery that some Commentators thought was also a homophobic Hate Crime. Harvey, an openly Gay Man, had worked for Jamaica Aids Support for Life, since 1997 becoming the Group's Coordinator for Kingston. In this Position, he focused on distributing Information and Services surrounding Hiv/Aids to the most marginalised Sectors of Jamaican Society, among them Prisoners, Sex Workers, and lesbian, Gay, bisexual, and Transgender People. In 2005 he was selected as Project Coordinator for the Latin America and caribbean Council of Aids Service Organizations in Jamaica. Harvey was praised for his Work. According to Peter Tatchell of the British LGBT rights organisation OutRage! It is Thanks to steve and his Colleagues that many jamaican Women and Men - both Gay and straight - have not contracted Hiv. They have helped save hundreds of lives."
