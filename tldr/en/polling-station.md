title: "Polling station"
short_summary: "Place where voters cast their ballots in elections"
description: "A polling Place is where People in the Election cast their Votes. The Phrase Polling Station is also used in american English and British English, although polling Place is the Building and Polling Station is the specific Room where Voters cast their Votes. A polling Place may contain one or more polling Stations. In australian English, 'polling Place' is used."
