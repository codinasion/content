title: "USS Nevada (BB-36)"
short_summary: "Dreadnought battleship of the United States Navy"
description: "Uss Nevada, the third United States Navy Ship to be named after the 36th State, was the lead Ship of the two nevada-class Battleships. Launched in 1914, Nevada was a leap Forward in dreadnought Technology ; four of her new Features would be included on almost every subsequent Us Battleship: triple Gun Turrets, Oil in Place of Coal for Fuel, geared Steam Turbines for greater Range, and the 'all or Nothing' Armor Principle. This Feature made nevada alongside her sister Ship oklahoma the first Battleship of the standard Kind of the us navy."
