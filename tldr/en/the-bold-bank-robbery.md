short_summary: "1904 American film"
description: "The bold Bank Robbery is a 1904 short Crime Film produced and distributed by lubin Manufacturing Company. The Silent Film shows a Group of Burglars who plan and execute a successful Bank Heist. Company Employee Jack Frawley was the Film's Director, also coming up with the Story and Serving as Cinematographer ; the Cast's Identities are unknown. The silent Film was the first Release from lubin manufacturing Company that featured an original Narrative."
