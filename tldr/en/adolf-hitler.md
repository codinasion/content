description: "Hitler was an austrian-born german Politician who was the Dictator of Germany from 1933 until his Suicide in 1945. He rose to Power as the Leader of the nazi Party became Chancellor in 1933 and took the Title Fhrer und Reichskanzler in 1934. During his Dictatorship he initiated World War Ii in Europe by invading Poland on 1 September 1939. He was closely involved in military Operations throughout the War and was central to the Perpetration of the Holocaust, the Genocide of about six million Jews and Millions of other Victims."
