description: "The Horrors are an english Rock Band formed in Southend-On-Sea in 2005, consisting of lead Vocalist Faris Badwan, Guitarist Joshua Hayward, Keyboardist and Synthesizer Player Tom Furse, Bassist Rhys Webb, and Drummer and Percussionist Joe Spurgeon. Their Music has been classified as Garage Rock, Garage Punk, gothic Rock, Shoegaze and post-punk Revival."
