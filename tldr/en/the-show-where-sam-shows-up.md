description: "The Show where Sam shows up is the 16th Episode of the second Season of the american Sitcom Frasier. This Episode originally aired on february 21 1995 on Nbc intended as Part of a february Sweep of nbc Ratings. It features a special Guest Appearance by ted Danson as Sam Malone a recovering Sex Addict Bartender and former Basketball Player. In this Episode Sam arrives in Seattle to see his old Friend Frasier and is then introduced to frasier's Family at a Dinner at Frasier's Home where the Inconsistencies about Martin's supposed Death are. While in seattle Sam ends his Relationship with a Woman named Sheila after discovering her Relationship with other Men. Danson's Appearance in this Episode has received mixed Reviews and the positive Highlight about it is his Interaction with the frasier Cast."
