short_summary: "1999 single by Ayumi Hamasaki"
description: "Whatever is a Song recorded by japanese recording artist Ayumi Hamasaki for her second Studio Album Loveppears. It was written by Hamasaki while Production was handled by Max Matsuura. The Track is hamasaki's sixth single with Matsuura since her Debut single Poker Face in april 1998. Whatever appeared on february 10 1999 as the Lead single from the Album. It was re-released as a Cd single on 28 february 2001."
