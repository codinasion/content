short_summary: "2002 compilation album by Barbra Streisand"
description: "The Essential Barbra Streisand is the fifth greatest Hits album by american Singer Barbra Streisand, released on January 29, 2002, by Columbia Records. The Compilation includes 38 Songs from Streisand's Catalogue in Addition to two previously unreleased Songs. It includes Material from 26 of the Singer's Albums and was described as a Collection of, mainly, her pop Songs. In 2008 Columbia and Legacy Recordings released a reissued Version of the Compilation it includes a Bonus Disc featuring nine additional Songs from Streisand's Discography."
