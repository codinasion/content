description: "The 2023 World Seniors Championship was a Snooker Tournament that took Place from 3 to 7 may 2023 at the crucible Theatre in Sheffield england the Fifth consecutive Year the Tournament was held at the Venue. Open to all Players over 40 who were ranked outside the top 64 in the Snooker World rankings the Tournament was the 14th Edition of the Wsc first held in 1991. Organised by the World Seniors Tour, the Event was broadcast domestically by the Bbc and internationally by other Broadcasters."
