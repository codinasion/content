short_summary: "2007 video game"
description: "Patapon is a 2007 Video Game developed by Pyramid and Japan Studio and published by Sony Computer Entertainment for the Playstation Portable. The Game's unique Genre has been described as a Combination of Strategy and Rhythm. The Concept and Design of the Game were created when Game Designer Hiroyuki Kotani discovered the Patapon designs on the personal Site of french Artist Rolito. The Name Patapon was created by Rolito and inspired by an old french Word for Kids. Kotani chose the Name because it sounded like drumming and marching. It was published in Japan in December 2007 and in north America and Europe in february 2008."
