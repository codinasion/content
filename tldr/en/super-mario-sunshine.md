description: "Super Mario Sunshine is a platform Game designed and published by Nintendo for the Gamecube in 2002. It is the second Game of the 3d Super Mario Series following super Mario 64. The Game was directed by Yoshiaki Koizumi and Kenta Usui, produced by Series Creators Shigeru Miyamoto and Takashi Tezuka, written by Makoto Wada, and scored by Koji Kondo and Shinobu Tanaka."
