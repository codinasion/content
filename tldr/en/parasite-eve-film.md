description: "Parasite Eve is a 1997 japanese Science Fiction Film directed by Masayuki Ochiai and based on the 1995 Novel Parasite Eve by Hideaki Sena. Kiyomi the Wife of toshiaki Nagashima is Brain dead after a Traffic Accident on the Day of their Wedding Anniversary. Nagashima Attempts to make kiyomi Live again by negotiating with a Doctor who wants to harvest Kiyomi's Kidneys for Transplant to a young Girl in the same Hospital. Nagashima Agrees but with the Condition that he can have his Wife's Liver. While nagashima Experiments with the Organ the Doctor finds one Night the Samples have emerged in the Form of Toshiaki's dead Wife as a gelatinous Form and reveal themselves as an Organization of Sentient Mitochondria that are bent on making a new Species that will."
