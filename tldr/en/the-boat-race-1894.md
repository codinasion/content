short_summary: "Oxford versus Cambridge rowing race"
description: "The 51st Boat Race took Place on March 17 1894. The Boat Race is an annual Rowing Race along the Thames between Crews from the Universities of Oxford and Cambridge. Oxford went into the Race leading by 27–22 in the Event, and of the eighteen Participants, Half had previous Boat Race Experience. Umpired by former rower Frank Willan, Oxford won the race by 3+1⁄2 lengths in a time of 21 minutes 39 seconds, for their fifth consecutive victory in the event. It was the largest Margin of Victory since the 1883 Race."
