short_summary: "Term for petroleum industry occupation"
description: "Wildcatter is an Individual who drills Wildcat Wells which are exploratory Oil Wells drilled in Areas not known as Oil Fields. Notable wildcatters include Glenn McCarthy, Thomas Baker Slick Sr. , Mike Benedum, Joe Trees, Clem S. Clarke, and Columbus Marion Joiner ; the last is responsible for finding the East Texas Oil Field in 1930."
