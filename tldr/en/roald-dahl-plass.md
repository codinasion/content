short_summary: "Public plaza in Cardiff Bay in Cardiff, Wales"
description: "Roald Dahl Plass is a public Space in Cardiff Bay, Cardiff, Wales. It is named after the Author Roald Dahl of Cardiff and is located along the Coast South of the City Centre. The square is home to the Senedd building housing the Senedd, the Welsh parliament, and the Wales Millennium Centre, a performing arts centre. The Bowl Shape of the Space has made it a popular Amphitheatre for hosting open Air Concerts."
