short_summary: "Mexican-Italian photographer"
description: "Estevan Oriol is a los Angeles Photographer and Director of mexican-italian Descent. Best known for his distinctive gritty 35mm black and white Work documenting Chicano cholo Culture as well as Celebrities within the Film Hip Hop and Lingerie Industries his Images have been featured in Magazines on Album Covers and in Exhibitions across the World. Oriol is also known for directing several music videos for the likes of Blink 182, D12, Cypress Hill and more, as well as for his partnerships with life-long friend and collaborator, Mister Cartoon for Joker Brand Clothing
and Soul Assassins Studios, the latter of which the two opened together by way of Cypress Hill's producer, DJ Muggs."
