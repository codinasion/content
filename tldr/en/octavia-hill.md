short_summary: "English social reformer (1838–1912)"
description: "Octavia Hill was an english social Reformer whose main Concern was the Welfare of the Inhabitants of Cities particularly london in the second Half of the nineteenth Century. Born into a Family of radical Thinkers and Reformers with a strong Commitment to alleviating Poverty she herself grew up in poor Conditions owing to the financial Failure of her Father's Business. She was raised by her Mother and worked for the Welfare of working People from the Age of 14."
