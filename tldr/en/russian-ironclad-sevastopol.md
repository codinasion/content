title: "Russian ironclad Sevastopol"
short_summary: "Imperial Russian Navy's 58-gun wooden frigate"
description: "The russian Ironclad Sevastopol was ordered by the imperial russian Navy as a 58-gun wooden Frigate in the early 1860S but was converted while under Construction into a 32-gun armored Frig. She served in the baltic Fleet and was reclassified as a Training Ship in 1880. Sevastopol was decommissioned five Years later but was not sold for Scrap until 1897."
