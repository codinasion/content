short_summary: "12th prime minister of Australia (1939–1941; 1949–1966)"
description: "Sir Robert Gordon Menzies was an australian Politician and Lawyer who served as the 12th prime Minister of Australia from 1939 to 1941 and 1949 to 1966. He held Office in his first Term as Leader of the united Australia Party and subsequently as the inaugural Leader of the liberal Party of Australia for which he was responsible for establishing and defining Policy and political Outreach. He is the longest-serving prime Minister in Australia's History."
