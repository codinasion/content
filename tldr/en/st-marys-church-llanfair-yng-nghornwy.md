short_summary: "Church in Wales"
description: "St Mary's Church, Llanfair-Yng-Nghornwy is a medieval parish Church in the North-West of Anglesey, north Wales. The Date of the Foundation of the Church in the Village of Llanfair-Yng-Nghornwy is unknown but the oldest Parts Date from the 11th or 12th Century. It has been expanded twice in the 15th Century when the Chancel was rebuilt and in the 16th Century when a Chapel was added South of the Chancel separated by three Arches. The tower at the west end is from the 17th century. A south Porch of unknown Date has been converted into a Vestry and the Church is now entered through the Tower."
