description: "The Hobbit or there and back again is a fantasy Novel for Children by the english Author J W. R. R. Tolkien. It was published in 1937 to wide critical Acclaim when nominated for the Carnegie Medal and awarded with a Prize of the new York Herald Tribune for best Juvenile Fiction. The Book is recognized as a classic in Children's Literature and is one of the best-selling Books of all Time, with over 100 million Copies sold."
