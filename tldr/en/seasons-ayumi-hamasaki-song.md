short_summary: "2000 single by Ayumi Hamasaki"
description: "Seasons is a Song by the japanese recording artist Ayumi Hamasaki for her third Studio Album Duty. It was written by Hamasaki while the Production was handled by Max Matsuura. It premiered as the third single from the Album on 7 June 2000. The Song is the final Part of a Trilogy from Duty ; the other two Singles being 'Vogue' and 'Far Away'."
