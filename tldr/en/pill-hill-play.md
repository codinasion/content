short_summary: "Play written by Sam Kelley"
description: "Pill Hill is a three-act Play by the american Dramatist Sam Kelley. The Play is set in a Neighborhood on the south Side of Chicago known informally as Pill Hill and examines the Failures Successes and Relationships of six african american Steel Mill Workers as they transition from blue-collar Jobs to white-collar Careers between. The Play is regarded as an Allegory of economic Progress related to the american Dream."
