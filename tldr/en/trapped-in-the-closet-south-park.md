description: "Trapped in the Closet is the twelfth Episode of the ninth Season of the american animated Television Series South Park. The 137th Episode of the Series overall aired originally on november 16 2005 on comedy central in the united States. In the Episode, Stan joins Scientology in an Attempt to find Something 'Fun and free'. After the Discovery of his surprisingly high 'thetan Levels', he is recognized as the Reincarnation of L. Ron Hubbard, the Founder of the Church. The Episode was written and directed by Series Co-Creator trey Parker who was credited as John Smith."
