title: "Strangeways Prison riot"
short_summary: "25-day prison riot in Manchester, England"
description: "The Riot of 1990 at Strangeways Prison was a 25-day Prison Riot and rooftop Protest in Strangeways Prison in Manchester England. The Riot began on April 1 1990 when Prisoners took Control of the Prison Chapel and quickly spread throughout most of the Prison. The Incident ended on april 25 when the five remaining Prisoners were removed from the Rooftop. One Prisoner was killed during the Riot and 147 Prison Officers and 47 Prisoners were wounded. Much of the Prison's Structure was damaged or destroyed with the Cost of Repairs rising to 55million. It was the longest Prison Riot in british penal History."
