short_summary: "Pacific typhoon in 1981"
description: "Typhoon Lee known in the Philippines as Typhoon Dinang was the second Storm to affect the Philippines during December 1981. Lee originated at the End of December from a thunderstorm Area near the Truk Atoll. Following an Increase in Organisation on December 22 the System was classified as a tropical Cyclone. After becoming a tropical Storm Lee began to slowly Strengthen and reached Typhoon Status on December24. While turning west towards the Philippines lee began to intensify more rapidly. It is estimated that it reached peak Intensity the next Day with Winds of 145kmh. Later on december 25 at Peak Intensity the Storm moved to the Center of the Philippines. The next Day lee emerged as a tropical Storm in the south China Sea. Initially the Storm maintained its Intensity but soon began to weaken due to increased Wind Shear. By december 28 all Thunderstorm Activity from the Center was removed and lee dissipated on December 29. The Remnants of the Cyclone was last observed at a few hundred Kilometers south of Hong Kong."
