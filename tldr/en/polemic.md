title: "Polemic"
short_summary: "Contentious rhetoric"
description: "Polemic is contentious rhetoric intended to support a specific position by forthright claims and to undermine the opposing position. Such Argumentation is known as Polemics which is seen in Arguments on controversial Topics. Someone who writes Polemics or Speaks polemically is called a Polemicist. The Word derives from Ancient Greek Πολεμικός'Warlike, Hostile', from Πόλεμος'War'."
