title: "Pindus National Park"
short_summary: "National park of the Pindus Mountains in Greece"
description: "Pindus National Park, also known as Valia Calda, is a national Park in Mainland Greece, situated in an isolated mountainous Area at the Periphery of West Macedonia and Epirus, in the northeastern Part of the Pindus Mountain Range. It was established in 1966 and covers an Area of 6927 Hectares. The Core 3360-hectare Park covers the greater Part of the Valley of valia Calda and the Slopes of the surrounding Peaks."
