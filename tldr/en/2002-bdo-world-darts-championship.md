short_summary: "Darts tournament"
description: "The Bdo 2002 World darts Championship was a professional Darts tournament held in Frimley green Surrey England from 5 to 13 january 2002. It was the 25th Edition of the Competition since 1978 and the 17th Time it took Place at lakeside Country Club. The Tournament was the first of 12 british Darts Organisation Tournaments in 2002 and was the second Time a Women's World Championship had been held. The Host Broadcaster was the Bbc and the Competition was sponsored by the Tobacco Company Embassy."
