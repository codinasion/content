title: "Powdered sugar"
short_summary: "Fine sugar with an anti-caking agent"
description: "Powdered Sugar also known as Confectioners sugar or icing Sugar is a finely Ground Sugar produced by milling granulated Sugar into a Powder State. It usually contains between 2 % and 5 % of an anti-caking Agent – such as Corn Starch, Potato Starch or Tricalcium Phosphate – to absorb Moisture, Prevent Clumping, and improve Flow. Although most often produced in a Factory a Proxy for powdered Sugar can be made by processing ordinary granulated Sugar in a Coffee Grinder or by crushing it by Hand in a Mortar and Pestle."
