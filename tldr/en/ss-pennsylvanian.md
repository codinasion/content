title: "SS Pennsylvanian"
short_summary: "1913 cargo ship"
description: "Ss Pennsylvanian was a Cargo Ship built for the american-hawaiian Steamship Company in 1913. During World War I she was requisitioned by the united States navy and commissioned as the uss Pennsylvanian in September 1918 and was renamed to the uss Scranton two Months later. After her naval Service, her original Name of Pennsylvanian was restored."
