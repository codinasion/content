title: "Typhoon Olive (1952)"
short_summary: "Pacific typhoon in 1952"
description: "Typhoon Olive was the strongest Typhoon in the pacific Ocean in 1952. The thirteenth tropical Storm and the ninth Typhoon of the Season it developed on September 13 about 1600mi Southwest of Honolulu Hawaii. The next Day the System reached tropical Storm Intensity. Beginning to rapidly intensify, Olive attained typhoon Intensity on September 15. Olive reached category5 Intensity on the modern Saffirsimpson Hurricane wind Scale on September16."
