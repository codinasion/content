short_summary: "Energetic, invisible light energy range"
description: "Ultraviolet Light is electromagnetic Radiation with a Range of Energy higher than that of visible light but less than X-Rays. Uv Radiation is present in Sunlight, and constitutes about 10 % of the total electromagnetic Radiation Output from the Sun. It is also produced by electric Arcs ; Cherenkov Radiation ; and specialized Lights, such as mercury-vapor Lamps, tanning Lamps, and black Lights."
