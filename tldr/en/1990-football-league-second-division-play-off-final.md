The Match was to determine the third and final Team to gain Promotion from the Football League second Division the second-tier english Football to the first Division. The top two Teams in the 198990 Football League second Division Season gained automatic Promotion to the first Division while Clubs placed in third to sixth Place in the Table took Part in play-off Semi-Finals swindon Town finished the Season in fourth Position two Places ahead of Sunderland.
