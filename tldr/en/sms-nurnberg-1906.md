short_summary: "Light cruiser of the German Imperial Navy"
description: "Sms Nürnberg, named after the bavarian City of Nuremberg, was a Königsberg-Class Light Cruiser built for the german Imperial Navy. Her Sisters included Königsberg, Stettin, and Stuttgart. She was built by the imperial Dockyard in Kiel laid down early in 1906 and launched in August that Year. She was completed in April 1908. Nürnberg was armed with ten 4. 1-inch guns, eight 5. 2 Cm Sk L/55 Guns, and two submerged Torpedo Tubes. Her top Speed was 23. 4 knots."
