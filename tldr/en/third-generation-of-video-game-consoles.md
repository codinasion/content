title: "Third generation of video game consoles"
short_summary: "Third video game console generation, including the Nintendo Entertainment System"
description: "The third Generation of video Games commonly referred to as the 8-bit Era began in the History of video Games on 15 July 1983 with the japanese Release of two Systems the nintendo Family Computer and the sega Sg-1000. When the Famicom was not released outside Japan it was remodeled and marketed as the nintendo Entertainment System. This Generation marked the End of the Video Game Crash of 1983 and a Shift in the Dominance of Home Video Game Manufacturer from the united States to japan. Handheld Consoles were not a major Part of this Generation the Game Watch Series from nintendo and the Milton Bradley Microvision sold at the Time are both considered Part of the previous Generation due to the Hardware typical of the second Generation."
