short_summary: "1954 painting by Salvador Dalí"
description: "The Colossus of Rhodes is an Oil Painting painted by spanish Surrealist Salvador Dal in 1954. It is one of a Series of seven Paintings he created for the 1956 Film seven Wonders of the World each depicting one of the Wonders. The Work shows the Colossus of Rhodes, the ancient Statue of the greek Titan-God of the Sun, Helios. The Painting was not used for the Film and was donated in 1981 to the Kunstmuseum Bern where it remains."
