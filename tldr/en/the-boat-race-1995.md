description: "The 141st Boat Race took Place on 1 April 1995. Held every Year the Boat Race is a side-by-side Rowing Race between the Crews from the Universities of Oxford and Cambridge along the Thames. Marko Banovic became the first croatian Rower to participate in the Event. Cambridge won by four Lengths."
