description: "The University of California Press, otherwise known as Uc Press, is a Publishing House associated with the University of California that engages in academic Publishing. It was founded in 1893 to publish scholarly and scientific Works by Faculty at the University of California established in 1868 25 Years earlier and has been officially headquartered since its Inception at the University's Flagship Campus in Berkeley California."
