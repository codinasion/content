title: "Linguistic rights"
short_summary: "Right to choose one's own language"
description: "Linguistic Rights are human and civil Rights which relate to the Individual and collective Right to choose the Language or the Languages for Communication in a private or public Environment. Other Parameters for analyzing linguistic Rights include the Degree of Territoriality, Amount of Positivity, Orientation in Terms of Assimilation or Maintenance, and Overtness."
