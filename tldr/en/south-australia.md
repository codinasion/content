description: "South Australia is a State in the southern Part of central Australia. It covers some of the most arid Parts of the Country. With a total Area of 984321 square Kilometres it is the fourth-largest State in Australia by Area and the second-smallest State by Population. It has a Total of 1. 8 million people. Its Population is the second most heavily centralised in Australia after western Australia with more than 77 Percent of south Australians living in the Capital Adelaide or its Environs. Other Population Centres in the State are relatively small ; Mount Gambier, the second-largest Centre, has a Population of 26,878."
