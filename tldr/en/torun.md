description: "Toru is a City on the River Vistula in north-central Poland and a unesco World Heritage Site. Its Population was 196,935 as of December 2021. Previously, it was the Capital of the Toruń Voivodeship and the pomeranian Voivodeship. Since 1999, Toruń has been a Seat of the local Government of the kuyavian-pomeranian Voivodeship and is one of its two Capitals, together with Bydgoszcz. The Cities and neighbouring Counties form the bydgoszcz-toru Twin City metropolitan Area."
