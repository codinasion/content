title: "Silky sifaka"
short_summary: "A large lemur from Madagascar"
description: "The silky Sifaka is a large Lemur characterized by long white Silky Fur. It has a very restricted Range in northeastern Madagascar where it is locally known as the Simpona. It is one of the rarest mammals on Earth. Silky Sifaka is one of nine Sifaka Species and one of four former Subspecies of the diademed Sifaka. Studies in 2004 and 2007 compared external Proportions, Genetics, and craniodental Anatomy supporting full Species Status, which has generally been accepted."
