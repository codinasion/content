The Story set in a fantasy Version of medieval Germany follows the Witch Gretchen and former Executioner Heinrich as they try to stop the Schemes of the Alchemist Dr Faust who has revived Gretchen's Sisters as vengeful Monsters in. The Game involves heinrich exploring linear Levels and fighting Monsters with Gretchen as a computer-controlled Companion and Spellcaster."
