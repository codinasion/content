description: "The Quatermass Xperiment is a british Science Fiction Horror Film from Hammer Film Productions in 1955 based on the Bbc Television serial the Quatermass Experiment written by Nigel Kneale. The Film was produced by Anthony Hinds and directed by val Guest and Features brian Donlevy as the Titular Professor Bernard Quatermass and richard Wordsworth as the tormented Carroon. David King-Wood Jack Warner and margia Dean appear in co-starring Roles."
