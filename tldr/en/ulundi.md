title: "Ulundi"
short_summary: "Place in KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa"
description: "Ulundi also known as Mahlabathini is a Town in the Zululand District Municipality. At one Time the Capital of the Zulu Kingdom in south Africa and later the Capital of the Bantustan of Kwazulu Ulundi is now located in the Province of Kwazulu-Na. The Town now includes Ulundi Airport, a three-star Hotel, and some Museums amongst its Sights. The Population of the City was recorded as 18420 in the 2001 Census."
