short_summary: "1950s experimental ship"
description: "The experimental s-99 Submarine was the only Ship of the Project 617 Class the soviet Union built during the early cold War. She was the only soviet Submarine to have used a german Walter Turbine fueled by a high-test Peroxide Fuel. In 1956 the Boat was assigned to a Training Unit of the baltic Fleet. In 1959 the S-99 was severely damaged by a htp Explosion and was not repaired. The Submarine was decommissioned in 1964 and subsequently scrapped."
