short_summary: "Sporting event delegation"
description: "The national Olympic Committee of Saint Kitts and nevis sent four Athletes to beijing China at the 2008 Summer Olympics. All four participated in the Track and Field Competitions across four different Events. The kittitian Delegation consisted of three Women and one Man. Williams and Ponteen did not advance past Qualifications in their Events ; Hodge reached Quarterfinals in both the 100m and 200m Sprints ; and Collins, who participated in two Events, reached the Semifinal Round in the 100m Sprint, and ranked Sixth in the Finals in the 200m Sprint. Saint Kitts and Nevis did n't win a Medal during the Beijing Olympics. The Flag Bearer for the Team at the Opening Ceremony was first-time olympian Virgil Hodge."
