description: "Reginald Roy Inwood Vc was an australian Soldier and received the Victoria cross - the highest Award for Gallantry in Battle which could be awarded to a Member of the Australian armed Forces at the Time. Inwood enlisted in the australian imperial Force in august 1914 and landed with the Rest of the 10th Battalion on April 25 1915 in anzac Cove Gallipoli. He fought in Anzac until he was sickly evacuated to egypt in September. He remained there until he rejoined his Unit in June 1916 on the western Front. He fought in August at the Battle of Mouquet Farm."
