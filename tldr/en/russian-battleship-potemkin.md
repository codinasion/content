description: "The russian Battleship Potemkin was a Battleship built for the black Sea Fleet of the imperial russian Navy. She became famous during the 1905 Revolution when her Crew mutinied against their Officers. This Event later formed the Basis for sergei Eisenstein's silent Film Battleship Potemkin in 1925."
