description: "Richard Joseph Rypien was a canadian professional Ice Hockey Forward who spent Parts of six Seasons in the National Hockey League with the Vancouver Canucks from 2005 to 2011. After a major junior Career of four Years with the Regina Pats of the western Hockey League he was signed in 2005 to a professional Contract with the minor League Manitoba Moose of the american Hockey. The following Season he signed with the Canucks. He spent six Years with the Organization, splitting Time between the Canucks and Moose, their Ahl Affiliate. A fourth-line Player in the Nhl, he was known for his Hitting and fighting Abilities, though his Size was not typical of an Enforcer."
