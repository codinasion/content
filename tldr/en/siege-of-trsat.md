title: "Siege of Trsat"
short_summary: "Part of Frankish campaign against Avars and Slavs"
description: "The Siege of Trsat was a Battle Fought over the Possession of the Town of Trsat in Liburnia near the croatian-frankish Border. The Battle was fought in the Autumn of 799 between the dalmatian Defending Forces under the Leadership of croatian Duke Vieslav and the invading frankish Army of the Caroling. The Battle was a croatian Victory and frankish Commander Eric was killed during the Siege."
