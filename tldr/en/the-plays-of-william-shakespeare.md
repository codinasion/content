short_summary: "18th-century collection edited by Samuel Johnson and George Steevens"
description: "The Plays of William Shakespeare was an 18th-century Edition of the Shakespeare dramatic Works edited by Samuel Johnson and George Steevens. Johnson announced his Intention in his miscellaneous Observations on Macbeth to edit Shakespeare's Plays and a full Proposal for the Edition was published in 1756. The Edition was finally published in 1765."
