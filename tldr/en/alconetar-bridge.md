The Ancient Structure, which featured flattened Arches with a span-to-rise Ratio of 4–5:1, is one of the earliest of its Kind. Due to its Design it is assumed that the Bridge was erected by the Emperors trajan or hadrian in the early 2nd Century Ad possibly under the Guidance of Apollodorus of Damascus the most famous Architect of the."
