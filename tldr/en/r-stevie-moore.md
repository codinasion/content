description: "Robert steven Moore is an american Singer and Songwriter Songwriter and Multiinstrumentalist who pioneered lo-fi Music. Often called the Godfather of Home Recording he is one of the most recognized Artists of the Cassette Underground and his Influence is particularly felt in Bedroom and hypnagogic Pop Artists of the Post-Millennium. He has since 1968 self-released approximately 400 Albums while about three Dozen Official Albums on various Labels have been issued."
