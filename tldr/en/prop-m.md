description: "Prop-M were two soviet Mars Rovers launched in 1971 on the unsuccessful Mars 2 and Mars 3. Prop-M was the first Rover to be launched to Mars 26 Years before the first successful Rover Mission of Nasa's Sojourner in 1997. Since the Mars 2 and Mars 3 Missions failed the Existence of the Rovers was kept Secret for almost 20 Years."
