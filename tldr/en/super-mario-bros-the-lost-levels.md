short_summary: "1986 video game"
description: "Super Mario Bros. The Lost Levels is a platform Game developed by nintendo Rd4 and published by Nintendo in 1986. A Sequel to super Mario Bros. The Game was originally released as super Mario Bros in Japan for the Family Computer System. 2 on June 3, 1986. Nintendo of America deemed it too difficult for its north american Audience and instead released an alternative Sequel called super Mario Bros. 2, in 1988. The Game was renamed the Lost Levels and was released internationally in the Super nintendo Entertainment System Compilation super Mario All-Stars 1993. The Game has since been ported to the Game Boy Color and Game Boy Advance and will be re-released via Emulation for nintendo 3ds and switch."
