description: "Typhoon abe known as Typhoon Iliang in the Philippines was the fourteenth Storm named during the Pacific Typhoon Season 1990. The Depression which would eventually develop into Typhoon Abe was formed on august 23 from a tropical Disturbance and initially continued in a steady West-Northwest. As a Result of an intense Monsoon Surge Abe's Trajectory Briefly changed to an Eastward then northward Track before returning to his original Track.
