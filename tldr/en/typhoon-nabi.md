description: "Typhoon nabi known as Typhoon Jolina in the Philippines was a powerful Typhoon that struck southwestern Japan in September 2005. Nabi the 14th named Storm of the Pacific Typhoon Season 2005 formed to the East of the northern Mariana Islands on August 29. It moved westward and passed as an intensifying Typhoon on august 31 about 55km North of Saipan. The joint Typhoon warning Center upgraded the Storm to super Typhoon Status the following Day with Winds equivalent to those of a Category5 Hurricane on the Saffirsimpson Hurricane Scale. The Japan meteorological Agency estimated ten-minute Peak Wind Speeds on September 2 at 175 Kmh. Nabi weakened as it curled Towards the north striking the japanese Island of Kyushu on September 6. After sweeping south korea the Storm turned northeast passing over Hokkaido before becoming extratropical on september 8 before dissipating on September 12."
