description: "Thou shalt not kill is the first Episode of the british Tv Series Spooks. It was first Broadcast on Bbc one in the united Kingdom on may 13 2002. The Episode was written by Series Creator david Wolstencroft and directed by Bharat Nalluri. Thou shalt not kill Focuses on Mi5's Activities in stopping an Anti-Abortion Group who have smuggled 20 explosive Devices. The Title of the Episode is a Reference to the sixth Commandment."
