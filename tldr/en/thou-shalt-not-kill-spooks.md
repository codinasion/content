The Episode was written by Series Creator david Wolstencroft and directed by Bharat Nalluri. Thou shalt not kill Focuses on Mi5's Activities in stopping an Anti-Abortion Group who have smuggled 20 explosive Devices. The Title of the Episode is a Reference to the sixth Commandment."
