title: "Wressle Castle"
short_summary: "Late 14th-century quadrangular castle in the East Riding of Yorkshire, England"
description: "Wressle Castle is a ruined Palace-Fortress in the east Riding of Yorkshire England built in the 1390s for Thomas Percy. It is privately owned and is generally open to the Public for a few Days every Year. The Wressle Castle was originally composed of four Ranges built around a central Courtyard there was a Tower in each Corner and the Structure was entered by a Gatehouse in the East Wall facing the Village."
