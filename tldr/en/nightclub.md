short_summary: "Entertainment venue at nighttime"
description: "A Nightclub is a Club open at Night usually for drinking Dancing and other Entertainment. Nightclubs usually have a Bar and Discothque with Dance Floor Laser lighting Displays and a Stage for live Music or a disc Jockey who mixes recorded Music. Nightclubs tend to be smaller than live Music Venues like Theatres and Stadiums with few or no Seats for Customers."
