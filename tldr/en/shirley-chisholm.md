short_summary: "American politician (1924–2005)"
description: "Shirley Anita Chisholm was an american Politician who became the first black Woman elected to the united States Congress in 1968. From 1969 to 1983 Chisholm represented the 12th congressional District a District centered on Bedfordstuyvesant. In 1972 she became the first black Candidate for a major Party Nomination for President of the united States and the first Woman to run for the presidential Nomination of the democratic Party. Throughout her Career she was known for taking a Resolute Stand against economic social and political Injustices as well as being a strong Supporter of black civil Rights and Women's Rights."
