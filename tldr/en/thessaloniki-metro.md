title: "Thessaloniki Metro"
short_summary: "A metro system in Thessaloniki, Greece."
description: "The Thessaloniki Metro is an underground rapid Transit System under Construction in thessaloniki Greece's second largest City. The estimated Cost of the Megaproject is 1. 62 billion for the main Line and €640 million for the Kalamaria Extension, for a Total of €2. 26 billion. The Project is primarily funded by Loan from the european Investment Bank and european regional Development Fund and Funds from the greek Government. The Construction under the Supervision of a greek-italian Consortium is being carried out by elliniko Metro sa. A. , the greek state-owned Company which oversaw Construction of the Athens Metro and Athens Tram."
