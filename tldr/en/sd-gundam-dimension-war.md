description: "Dimension War was a tactical Role-Playing Video Game for the virtual Boy by Bandai in Japan in 1995. Being the System's only licensed Game in Japan, it is Part of the Sd Gundam Series, a Spin-Off of the larger Gundam Franchise by Sotsu and Sunrise. Players control a Fleet of Robots and Vehicles as they set out to rid the Galaxy of Enemies, with Gameplay involving moving Robots Towards Opponents and engaging in Combat Sequences."
