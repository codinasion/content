description: "Vuelve is a Song by the puerto rican Singer ricky Martin for his fourth Studio Album Vuelve. The Song was written by Franco De Vita, while the Production was handled by K. C. Porter and Draco Rosa. It was released on 26 january 1998 by Sony Discos as the Lead single from the Album. A spanish Language Power Ballad and Latin Pop Song with Elements of Rock and Gospel, it is about the Singer's true Love, who gives the Meaning of his Life. It received generally positive Reviews from Music Critics who complimented its romantic Lyrics and the Singing of Martin."
