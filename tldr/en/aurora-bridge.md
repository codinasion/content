short_summary: "Highway bridge crossing the Lake Washington Ship Canal in Seattle, Washington"
description: "The Aurora Bridge is a Cantilever and Truss Bridge in Seattle, Washington, United States. It carries State Route 99 over the west End of Seattle's Lake Union and Connects queen Anne and Fremont. The bridge is located just east of the Fremont Cut, which itself is spanned by the Fremont Bridge."
