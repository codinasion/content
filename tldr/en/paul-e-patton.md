title: "Paul E. Patton"
short_summary: "American politician"
description: "Paul edward Patton is an american Politician who served from 1995 to 2003 as the 59th Governor of kentucky. Because of a 1992 Amendment to the kentucky Constitution he was the first Governor Eligible for a second Term in Office since James garrard in 1800. Since 2013 he has been the Chancellor of the University of Pikeville in Pikeville Kentucky where he served as its President from 2010 to 2013. He also served as Chairman of the kentucky Council on postsecondary Education from 2009 to 2011."
