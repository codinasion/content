short_summary: "Search engine and a publisher advertising platform"
description: "Technorati was a Search Engine and a Publisher Advertising Platform that served as an Advertising Solution for the Thousands of Websites in its Network. Technorati launched its Ad Network in 2008, and at one Time was one of the largest Ad Networks reaching more than 100 million unique Visitors per Month. The Name Technorati was a Portmanteau of the Words Literati and Technology which invokes the Notion of technological Intelligence or Intellectualism."
