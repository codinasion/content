description: "Romanian is the official and main Language of Romania and Moldova. Romanian is Part of the eastern Romance Sub-Branch of Romance languages a linguistic Group that developed from several Dialects of Vulgar Latin which separated from the western Romance Languages in the Course of the 5th to 8. It is called daco-romanian in comparative Linguistics to distinguish it within the eastern Romance Languages as opposed to its closest Relatives aromanian megleno-romanian and istro-romanian. As a minority language it is spoken by stable communities in the countries surrounding Romania, and by the large Romanian diaspora. In total, it is spoken by 28–29 million People as an L1+L2 Language, of whom C. 24 million are native Speakers. Romanian ranks 10th among 37 Official Languages in Europe."
