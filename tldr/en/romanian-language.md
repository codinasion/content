Romanian is Part of the eastern Romance Sub-Branch of Romance languages a linguistic Group that developed from several Dialects of Vulgar Latin which separated from the western Romance Languages in the Course of the 5th to 8. It is called daco-romanian in comparative Linguistics to distinguish it within the eastern Romance Languages as opposed to its closest Relatives aromanian megleno-romanian and istro-romanian. As a minority language it is spoken by stable communities in the countries surrounding Romania, and by the large Romanian diaspora.
