description: "Tomb raider the last Revelation is an action-adventure Video Game developed by Core Design and released by eidos interactive. It was first released for Playstation and microsoft Windows in 1999 then for Dreamcast and Mac os the following Year. It is the fourth Installment of the Tomb Raider Series. The narrative follows Archaeologist-Adventurer Lara Croft as she races to imprison egyptian God set after accidentally freeing him. Gameplay features Lara Navigating Levels split into multiple Areas and Room Complexes, fighting Enemies and solving Puzzles to progress."
