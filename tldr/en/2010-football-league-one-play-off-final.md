description: "The 2010 Football League one Play-Off final was an Association Football Match played on may 29 2010 at Wembley Stadium London between Millwall and swindon Town the Game was aimed at finding the third and final Team to win Promotion from Football League one to the Football League Championship. The top two Teams of the 200910 Football League one Season norwich City and Leeds united gained automatic Promotion to the Championship while the Teams placed from the third to sixth Place in the Table took Part in the play-off Semi-Finals the Winners of these Semi-Finals competed for the final Place for. Charlton Athletic and Huddersfield Town were the losing semi-finalists, having been defeated by Swindon and Millwall respectively."
