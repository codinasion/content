For a Total of 780 Episodes, capitalizing on the Success of 'real-life' Police Series such as Cops. Production Companies were Mark Massari Productions, and Leap Off Productions, and was distributed by Genesis Entertainment, New World International, and later New World/Genesis Distribution.
