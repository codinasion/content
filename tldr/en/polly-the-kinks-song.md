short_summary: "1968 song by the Kinks"
description: "Polly is a Song of the english Rock band the Kinks. It was released as Wonderboy's B-Side in april 1968 on a non-album single. Written and sung by Bandleader Ray davies the Song was recorded in March 1968 during Sessions for the Band's 1968 album the Kinks are the Village green Preservation Society. Ray was initially inspired by the Character Polly Garter in Dylan Thomas's 1954 Radio Drama under Milk Wood, though his resulting Character does not Share Anything with Thomas's besides the same Name. The Song is one of the few Kinks Recordings from the late'60S to possibly have real Strings as arranged by David Whitaker."
