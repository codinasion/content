short_summary: "Babylonian mathematical clay tablet"
description: "Ybc 7289 is a babylonian Clay Tablet notable for containing an Accurate sexagesimal Approximation to the square Root of 2, the Length of the Diagonal of a Unit Square. This Number is given to the Equivalent of six decimal Digits, 'the greatest known computational Accuracy. . . In the ancient World'. The Tablet is believed to have been the Work of a Student in southern Mesopotamia from about 1800 to 1600 Bc."
