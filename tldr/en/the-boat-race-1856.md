title: "The Boat Race 1856"
short_summary: "Oxford versus Cambridge rowing race"
description: "On March 15 1856 the 13th Boat Race took Place on the Thames. The Event is usually held annually and is a rowing Race between Crews from the University of Cambridge and Oxford. The Race, the second to be held on the Ebb Tide, was won by Cambridge who beat Oxford by half a Length."
