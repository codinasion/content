description: "Spock Must Die! James blish's american Science Fiction Novel was published by Bantam Books in february 1970. It was the first original Novel based on the Star Trek Television Series intended for Adult Readers. It was preceded by a tie-in comic Book Line published by Gold Key and the novel Mission to Horatius by Mack Reynolds, all intended for younger Readers."
