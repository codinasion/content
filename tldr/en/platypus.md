description: "The Platypus sometimes referred to as the duck-billed Platypus is a semiaquatic Egg-Laying mammal endemic to eastern australia including Tasmania. The Platypus is the only Living Representative or Monotypic Taxon of its Family Ornithorhynchidae and Genus ornithorhynchus although a Number of related Species are found."
