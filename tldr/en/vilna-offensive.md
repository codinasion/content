description: "The Vilna Offensive was a Campaign of the polishsoviet War 1919-1921. The polish Army launched an offensive on April 16 1919 to take Vilnius from the red Army. After three Days of fighting on April 1921 the City was captured by polish Forces causing the red Army to retreat. During the offensive the Poles also succeeded in securing the nearby Cities of lida Pinsk Navahrudak and Baranovichi."
