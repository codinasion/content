short_summary: "Railway station near Bristol, England"
description: "Pilning Railway Station is a minor Station on the South Wales Main Line near Pilning, South Gloucestershire, England. It is 10 Miles from Bristol Temple Meads and is the last Station on the english Side before the severn Tunnel to Wales. It is managed by great western Railway who provide two Trains a Week from the Railway Station."
