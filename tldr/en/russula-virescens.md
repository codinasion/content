description: "Russula Virescens is a basidiomycete Mushroom of the Genus Russula commonly known as the green-cracking Russula the quilted green Russula or the green Brittlegill. It can be recognized by its distinctive Pale green Cap that measures up to 15cm in Diameter and is covered with Darker green angular Patches. It has crowded white Gills and a firm white stipe up to 8cm Tall and 4cm Thick. It is considered one of the best edible Mushrooms of the Genus Russula and is particularly popular in Spain and China. With a Taste that is described variously as Mild, nutty, Fruity, or Sweet, it is cooked by grilling, frying, saut√©eing, or eaten raw. Mushrooms are rich in Carbohydrates and Protein with low Fat Content."
