short_summary: "Atlantic tropical storm in 1994"
description: "The tropical Storm Beryl caused Flooding in several States in the eastern united States in August 1994. The Second named Storm and the third tropical Cyclone of the annual Hurricane Season Beryl developed on August14 from an upper-level low Pressure Zone over the northeastern Gulf of Mexico. Initially a tropical Depression, the System intensified into a tropical Storm about 24 Hours after forming. Beryl then moved slowly northeast and peaked at Maximum sustained Winds of 60mph before landing early on August16 near panama City Florida. Within 12 Hours of moving inland the Storm weakened to a tropical Depression but persisted for a few Days as a tropical Cyclone while traversing the eastern united States. Beryl was absorbed by a frontal System while over connecticut early August19."
