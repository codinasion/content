description: "Sms helgoland the leading Ship of her Class was a dreadnought Battleship of the german imperial Navy. Helgoland's Design represented an incremental Improvement over the preceding Class Nassau including an Increase in the Bore Diameter of the main Gun from 28cm to 30cm. 5 cm . Her Keel was laid at the Kiel Howaldtswerke Shipyards on november 11 1908. Helgoland was launched on September 25 1909 and was commissioned on August 23 1911."
