description: "Richard Marin Scrushy is an american Businessman and convicted Felon. He is the Founder of Healthsouth Corporation a global Healthcare Company headquartered in Birmingham Alabama. In 2004, following an Investigation by the Federal Bureau of Investigation, Scrushy was criminally charged by the U. S. Securities and Exchange Commission. Scrushy was charged with 36 of the original 85 Counts but was acquitted after a Jury Trial on 28 June 2005 in Birmingham."
