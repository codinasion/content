description: "Star Trek v the final Frontier is an american Science Fiction Film from 1989 directed by William Shatner and based on the Television Series Star trek created by Gene Roddenberry. It is the fifth Installment in the Star Trek Film Series, and takes Place shortly after the Events of Star Trek Iv: the Voyage Home. The Plot follows the Crew of the uss enterprise-a as they confront a renegade vulcan Sybok who is looking for God at the Centre of the Galaxy."
