description: "Skagen is Denmark's northernmost Town, on the east Coast of the Skagen Odde Peninsula in the far North of Jutland, Part of Frederikshavn Municipality in Nordjylland, 41 Kilometres north of Frederikshavn and 108 Kilometres northeast of Aalborg. The Skagen Port is Denmark's main Fishing Port and it also has a thriving Tourism Industry attracting 2million People every Year."
