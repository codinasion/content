description: "The Simpsons Wrestling is a Sports Video Game based on the Simpsons animated Television Series for the Playstation. The Game was conceived by big Ape Productions and distributed by fox interactive through a co-publishing Agreement with electronic Arts in Europe and Activision in north america the Game was released in Europe in March 2001 followed by north America a Month later. It is the only Simpsons Video Game released for the Playstation."
