description: "King's Highway 19, commonly referred to as Highway 19, is a provincially maintained Highway in the canadian Province of Ontario, connecting Highway 3 in Tillsonburg with Highway 401 Southeast of Ingersoll. The Highway began as the Plank and Gravel Road, a Toll Road formed by the Ingersoll and Port Burwell Road Company. It was first assigned in 1930. In the early 1930s a Number of Extensions were extended to tralee north to the Highway86. However a significant Amount of Highway19 was decommissioned and turned over to local and county Governments in 1997 and 1998."
