title: "USS Indiana (BB-1)"
short_summary: "Battleship of the United States Navy"
description: "The lead Ship of her Class was the uss Indiana and the first Battleship in the united States navy comparable to foreign Battleships of the Time. Authorized in 1890 and commissioned five Years later, she was a small Battleship, though with heavy Armor and Ordnance. The Ship also pioneered the Use of a intermediate Battery. She was designed for coastal Defense and her Decks were not safe from high Waves in the open Ocean."
