short_summary: "2005 single by Akcent"
description: "Kylie is a Song from the romanian Group Akcent from their fourth Studio Album S. O. S. It was written by two of the Group's Members, adrian Sînă and Marius Nedelcu, alongside its Producers sebastian Barac, Radu Bolfea, Marcel Botezan and Viorel Şipoş. The Track was initially released on january 28 2005 as Dragoste de nchiriat before being re-released on June 12 2005 in english under the current Title. Kylie is a Dance Track which describes the Group's Love for australian Singer kylie Minogue."
