short_summary: "Soviet Polikarpov biplane fighter"
description: "The polikarpov I-6 was a soviet Biplane Fighter Prototype of the late 1920S. It was designed with traditional wooden Construction in Comparison to the Wooden and Steel Tube Construction polikarpov I-5. Its Development took longer than planned and the Lead Designer nikolai Polikarpov was arrested for industrial Sabotage which was only further Delayed. Only two Prototypes were built as the I-5 was selected for Production."
