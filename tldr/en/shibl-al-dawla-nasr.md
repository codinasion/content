short_summary: "Emir of Aleppo from 1029 to 1038"
description: "Abu Kamil nasr Ibn Salih Ibn Mirdas also known by his Laqab as shibl Al-Dawla was the second Mirdasid Emir of Aleppo between may 1029 and his Death. He was the eldest Son of salih Ibn Mirdas Founder of the Mirdasid Dynasty. Nasr fought his Father in the Battle of al-uqhuwana in Tiberias in 1029 where Salih was killed by a fatimid Army led by anushtakin Al-Dizbar. Nasr later ruled the Emirate together with his Brother Thimal. Soon thereafter the young Emirs faced a large-scale Byzantine Offensive led by Emperor Romanos iii. Nasr Commanders a much smaller Force of bedouin Horsemen that routed the Byzantines at the Battle of Azaz in 1030."
