short_summary: "2004 promotional single by Björk"
description: "Oceania is a Song by icelandic Singer Bjrk for her fifth Studio Album Medlla. It was written and produced by Björk, with additional Writing by Sjón and Production by Mark Bell. The Song was written by the Singer specially for the Opening Ceremony for the 2004 Summer Olympics after a Request from the international Olympic Committee. Oceania was released as a promotional single on August 13 2004 on one little indian Records. The Song was written at the Ocean's Point of View, from which the Singer believes all Life emerged, and Details the Human's Evolution, whilst accompanied by a Choir. Oceania was generally well received by Music Critics who believed it was the best Track from Medlla although some thought it was not the best Choice for a promotional Release."
