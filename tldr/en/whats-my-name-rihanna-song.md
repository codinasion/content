short_summary: "2010 song by Rihanna"
description: "'What's My Name? ' is a Song by barbadian Singer Rihanna for her fifth Studio Album Loud. The Song accompanied by Guest Vocals from the canadian Rapper Drake was released as the second single of Loud on october 25 2010 via Def Jam Recordings and as a single in Germany on january 21 2011 through universal Music Group. The dancehall-infused Electro-R & B Song was produced by the norwegian Production Duo Stargate, and was written by the Duo along with Ester Dean, Traci Hale, and Drake. Lyrically it introduces Themes of Sex and Romance."
