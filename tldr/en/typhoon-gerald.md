title: "Typhoon Gerald"
short_summary: "Pacific typhoon in 1987"
description: "In the Philippines Typhoon gerald known as Typhoon Neneng affected the Philippines Taiwan and China during September 1987. A tropical Depression formed on September 4 and intensified into a tropical Storm within 24 Hours. After initially moving erratically within the philippine Sea Gerald moved west-northwest and then northwest while steadily deepening. On september 8 Gerald obtained Typhoon Intensity and the following Day he reached maximum Intensity. Shortly thereafter the Typhoon skirted southwestern Taiwan which resulted in a steady Weakening Trend. Gerald moved ashore on september10 North of Hong Kong near Amoy. Gerald disappeared the next Day."
