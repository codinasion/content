title: "Triangulum"
short_summary: "Constellation in the northern celestial hemisphere"
description: "Triangulum is a small Constellation in the northern Sky. Its Name is latin for Triangle derived from the three brightest Stars that form a long and narrow Triangle. Triangulum was known to the ancient Greeks and Babylonians as one of 48 Constellations listed by the 2nd Century Astronomer Ptolemy. The celestial Cartographers johann Bayer and John flamsteed catalogued the Stars in the Constellation giving six of them bayer Designations."
