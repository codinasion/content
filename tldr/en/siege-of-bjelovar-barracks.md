description: "The Siege of Bjelovar Barracks also known as the Code Name Operation Bilogora was the Blockade and Capture of the Yugoslav peoples Army Barracks and other Facilities in and around the City of Bjelovar during the croatian War of Independence. On 14 September 1991 a general Blockade of the Facilities of the Jna in Croatia continued until 29 September when the Jna Garrison was captured by croatian Forces. Its Capture took Place one Week after the Bulk of the 32nd Corps surrendered. It was Part of the Battle of the Barracks—An Effort by Croatian armed Forces to isolate Jna Units based at Barracks in Croatia, or capture the Barracks to provide Arms for Croatia's nascent Army."
