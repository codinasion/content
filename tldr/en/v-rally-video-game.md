title: "V-Rally (video game)"
short_summary: "1997 video game"
description: "V-rally is a racing Video Game developed by Infogrames Multimedia and released for the Playstation Console in 1997. The first Game in the v-rally Series is based on the World Rally championship Seasons 1997 and 1998 and includes officially licensed Cars and Tracks inspired by real Locations of Rallies. Players drive Rally Cars across a Series of Stages spread across eight different Locations from european Countries like Spain or Sweden to island Countries such as Indonesia and new Zealand. As a Simulation Game v-rally places a strong Emphasis on replicating the behavior Physics of real Cars and generally requires more Practice than arcade-style Racers."
