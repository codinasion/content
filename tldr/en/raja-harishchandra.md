short_summary: "1913 film by Dadasaheb Phalke"
description: "Raja Harishchandra is a 1913 indian Silent Film directed by and produced by Dadasaheb Phalke. It is often considered the first full-length indian Film. Raja Harishchandra features Dattatraya Damodar Dabke, Anna Salunke, Bhalchandra Phalke. And Gajanan Vasudev Sane. It is based on the harishchandra Legend with dabke playing the Title Character. The silent Film had english Marathi and Hindi Intertitles."
