description: "The Life of Pablo is the seventh Studio Album by the american Rapper and Producer Kanye West. It was released by good Music on 14 february 2016 and distributed by def Jam Recordings. Recording Sessions have taken Place in Canada Mexico Italy and the united States from 2013 to 2016. West and a Variety of Producers including co-executive Producers noah Goldstein and rick Rubin handled the Production. West enlisted guest Vocals for the Album from The-Dream, Kelly Price, Chance the Rapper, Kirk Franklin, Kid Cudi, Desiigner, Rihanna, Young Thug, Chris Brown, the Weeknd, Ty Dolla Sign, Vic Mensa, Sia, Frank Ocean, Kendrick Lamar, Post Malone, and Sampha."
