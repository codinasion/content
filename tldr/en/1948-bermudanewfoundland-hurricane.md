description: "The Bermudanewfoundland Hurricane in 1948 was an intense and long-lived Cape Verde tropical Cyclone that wrought substantial Damage in September 1948 to bermuda and Parts of Newfoundland. The Storm was the Eighth named Storm and the third Hurricane of the annual Hurricane Season. Originating on September4 as a tropical Wave off the Coast of Africa the Cyclone followed a general Westward Path for much of its initial Stages as it gradually intensified reaching tropical Storm Intensity shortly after its Development and then Hurricane Intensity a Day later. After reaching a Longitude roughly equal to that of the Lesser Antilles, the Hurricane began to curve northward on a parabolic Track, bringing it near Bermuda at peak Intensity as a Category 4 Hurricane on September 13. The Hurricane then began to accelerate to the Northeast and weaken. The waning tropical Cyclone grazed the Cape Race before transitioning into an extratropical Cyclone on september 15 these Remnants persisted for another Day."
