short_summary: "1997 video game"
description: "Tomba! This 1997 Platform-Adventure Game was developed by Whoopee Camp and published for the Playstation by Sony Computer Entertainment. It was released in Japan in 1997 and then in other Territories the following Year. The Game Centers on the eponymous feral Child who tries to recover his Grandfather's Bracelet from an evil Race of anthropomorphic Pigs."
