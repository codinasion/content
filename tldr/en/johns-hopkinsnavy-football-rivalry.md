short_summary: "Intercollegiate sports rivalry"
description: "The Johns hopkins navy Football Rivalry was an american Intercollegiate Football Rivalry between the Johns hopkins blue Jays Football Team of Johns Hopkins University and the navy Midshipmen Football Team of the united States naval Academy. The two Institutions located in the State of Maryland within a few Miles met for a Football Match in 1882. Following the initial Contest both Teams played each other each Year for eight Years before it was called off for unknown Reasons. The Teams competed again in 1911 and 1912, again disbanding the Contest until a final Match took Place in 1919."
