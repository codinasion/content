title: "Pond Eddy Bridge"
short_summary: "Petit truss bridge in Pond Eddy, US"
description: "The Pond Eddy Bridge is a petit Truss Bridge which crosses the Delaware River between the Village of Pond Eddy in lumberland new York and the Settlement informally called Pond Eddy in Shohola Township Pennsylvania. It is accessible from Ny 97 in Lumberland on the New York Side and two dead-end local Roads, Flagstone Road and Rosa Road on the Pennsylvania Side. The Bridge was built by the Oswego Bridge Company in 1903 to replace an old Suspension Bridge that washed away earlier in the Year in a Flood. It connected the Bluestone Quarries in Pennsylvania to new York."
