description: "Ours is a Song written and recorded by the american Singer Taylor Swift for the deluxe Edition of her third Studio album Speak now. It was released on December 5 2011 by big Machine Records as the last single on us Country Radio. Produced by Swift and Nathan Chapman, 'ours' is an understated Country Pop Ballad with a folk-influenced Production. The Lyrics depict a young Couple's Resilience to protect their Relationship despite Scrutiny by other People."
