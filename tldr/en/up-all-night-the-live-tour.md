description: "Up all Night: the Live Tour is a Video Album documenting the 3 January 2012 Show of english-irish Boy Band one Direction's Up All Night Tour. It was released on 28 May 2012 by Syco Music. Filmed at the international Centre in the british Town of Bournemouth the 73 Minute Recording was produced by Saunders and Tom Bairstaw. Up all Night the live Tour Offers concert Footage and Backstage Content."
