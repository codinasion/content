title: "Pesticide"
short_summary: "Substance used to destroy pests"
description: "Pesticides are Substances used to control Pests. They include Herbicides, Insecticides, Nematicides, Fungicides, and many Others. The most common of these are Herbicides which account for approximately 50 of the World's Pesticide Use. Most Pesticides are used as Plant Protection Products which in general Protect Plants from Weeds Fungi or Insects. In general a Pesticide is a Chemical or biological Agent that deters Incapacitates Kills or otherwise discourages Pests. Target Pests can include Insects Plant pathogens Weeds molluscs Birds Mammals fish Nematodes and Microbes that destroy Property Cause Nuisance or spread Disease or are disease Vectors. Together with these Benefits Pesticides have also Drawbacks such as potential Toxicity to Humans and other Species."
