title: "Typhoon Sudal"
short_summary: "Pacific typhoon in 2004"
description: "Typhoon Sudal known as Typhoon Cosme in the Philippines was the strongest Typhoon to strike the Island of Yap in the federated States of Micronesia in about 50years. Yap is one of the four Divisions of the Fsm. The entire Island, only 17 Km in Length, experienced Typhoon Force Winds, and 90 % of the Structures were damaged or destroyed. Damage was most severe in southeastern Yap, where the eyewall Struck and Winds exceeded 185 Km/H, but the Center of the Typhoon passed South of the Island."
