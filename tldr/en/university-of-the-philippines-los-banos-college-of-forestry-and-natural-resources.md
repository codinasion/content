description: "The College of Forestry and natural Resources of the University of the Philippines los Baos is one of 11 degree-granting Units of the University of the Philippines los Baos. It started as the Forest School under the Up College of Agriculture in 1910, making it the oldest Forestry School in the Philippines. It is one of the five founding Units of the Uplb founded in 1972."
