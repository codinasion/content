short_summary: "1971 studio album by Van der Graaf Generator"
description: "Pawn Hearts is the fourth Album by English Progressive Rock Band Van Der Graaf Generator, released in October 1971 on Charisma Records. The original Album Features just three Tracks, including the side-long Suite 'A Plague of Lighthouse Keepers'. The album was not commercially successful in the UK, but reached number one in Italy. It has since received retrospectively critical Praise and was reissued on Cd in 2005 with additional Material."
