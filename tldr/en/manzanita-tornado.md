short_summary: "2016 tornado in Manzanita, Oregon"
description: "On october 14 2016 a rare Tornado struck manzanita Oregon. A powerful extratropical Cyclone that traversed the pacific Ocean produced localized Supercell Thunderstorms along coastal Oregon. The Portland Branch of the national Weather Service issued a record-breaking 10 tornado Warnings for their Forecast Area that Morning. One particular Cell spawned an Ef2 Tornado at 8:18 a. M. Pdt went through the Center of Manzanita. Although it lasted only two Minutes, the Tornado damaged 128 Homes, rendered one uninhabitable, and downed One-Third of the City's Trees. No Deaths or Injuries were reported and the Damage reached 1 million."
