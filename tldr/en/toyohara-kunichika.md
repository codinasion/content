short_summary: "Japanese print artist (1835–1900)"
description: "Toyohara Kunichika was a japanese Woodblock Artist. Talented as a Child, at about thirteen he became a Student of Tokyo's then-leading Print Maker, Utagawa Kunisada. His deep Appreciation and Knowledge of the Kabuki Drama led to his Production mostly of yakusha-e Woodblock Prints of Kabuki Actors and Scenes from popular Plays of the Time."
