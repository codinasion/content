short_summary: "2017 live album by Madonna"
description: "Rebel Heart Tour is the fifth live Album by american Singer and Songwriter Madonna, chronicling her tenth Worldwide concert Tour of the same Name, recorded at Sydney's Allphones Arena. It was released on September 15, 2017 by Eagle Vision on Dvd and Blu-Ray Formats and by Eagle Records for audio Versions. Rebel Heart Tour also contains bonus Content such as Excerpts from the Tears of a clown Show at Melbourne's Forum Theatre as well as a 22-song double Cd. Danny Tull and Nathan Rissman, who had worked on Madonna's previous Concert Films, directed Rebel Heart Tour."
