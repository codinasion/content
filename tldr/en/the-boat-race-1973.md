title: "The Boat Race 1973"
short_summary: "Oxford versus Cambridge rowing race"
description: "The 119th Boat Race took Place on April 7 1973. The Boat Race is a side-by-side Rowing Race along the Thames between Crews from the Universities of Oxford and Cambridge. It was won by Cambridge who passed the Finishing Post thirteen lengths ahead of Oxford, the largest winning Margin since the 1955 Race. In their Warm up Oxford's Swimmers took a big Amount of Water in rough Conditions."
