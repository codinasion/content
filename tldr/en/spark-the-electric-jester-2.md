description: "Spark the electric Jester 2 is a platform Game created by brazilian Developer Felipe Daneluz in 2019. It is a 3d Sequel to Daneluz's previous 2d Game Spark the Electric Jester, released in 2017. Players take Control of Spark's robotic Counterpart Fark on his quest to learn his true Identity and find Doctor armstrong. Fark must be guided through various Levels and may engage Enemies in Melee Combat. While fast-paced Platforming is emphasized, Levels are approachable in multiple Ways."
