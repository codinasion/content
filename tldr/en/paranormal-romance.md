short_summary: "Subgenre of romantic fiction and speculative fiction"
description: "Paranormal Romance is a Subgenre of both speculative Fiction and romantic Fiction. Paranormal Romance Focuses on romantic Love and includes Elements beyond the Range of scientific Explanation from the speculative Fiction Genres of Fantasy Science Fiction and Horror. Paranormal Romances range from traditional Romances with a paranormal Setting to Stories with a Science-Fiction or fantasy-based Plot with a romantic Subplot included. Romantic Relationships between Humans and Vampires Shapeshifters Ghosts and other Creatures of a fantastic or otherworldly Nature are common."
