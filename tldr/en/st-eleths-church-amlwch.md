short_summary: "Church in Wales"
description: "St Eleth's Church, Amlwch is a parish Church built in the neo-classical Style in 1800 in Amlwch, a Town on the Island of Anglesey in north Wales. It stands on the Site of earlier Buildings and the first Church is said to have been built here by st Elaeth in the 6th Century. The Influx of Prosperity in the City in the 18th Century through Copper Mining led to the Construction of a new Church to serve the growing Population."
