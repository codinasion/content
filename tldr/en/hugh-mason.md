title: "Hugh Mason"
short_summary: "English miller, reformer and politician"
description: "Hugh Mason was an English Mill Owner, social Reformer and Liberal Politician. He was born in Stalybridge and brought up in Stalybridge and Ashton-Under-Lyne until he entered the Family Cotton Business in 1838 after working in a Bank for seven Years. Mason was originally against Trade Unions and became a paternalistic Mill Owner creating a Colony for his Workers with associated Facilities and ensuring that they experienced good Conditions. During the lancashire Cotton Famine of the 1860S he refused to cut Wages of Workers although it was common Practice."
