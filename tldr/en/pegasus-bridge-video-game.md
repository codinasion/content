description: "Pegasus Bridge is a turn-based strategy video game developed and published by Personal Software Services. It was released in 1987 for the Zx Spectrum Amstrad Cpc and Commodore 64 exclusively in the united Kingdom. It is the ninth Installment of the Wargamers Series. The Game is set during the Landings in normandy on D-Day in the second World War and revolves around Attempts made by the british 6th airborne Division to secure a Bridge over the caen Canal."
