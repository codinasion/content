description: "Rude Boy is a Song by barbadian Singer rihanna taken from her fourth Studio Album rated R. It was released on 5 february 2010 through Def Jam as the fourth overall and third international single of the Album. Rihanna co-wrote the Song with Ester Dean, Makeba Riddick, and Producers Rob Swire and Stargate. 'rude Boy' is a Clubby Midtempo Reggae, Dancehall and R & B Song which incorporates Elements of Raggamuffin. The Song received mixed to positive Reviews from Critics ; some have called it the Highlight of the Album, but Others criticized Rihanna's 'Monotone' and 'Icy' vocal Performance. However, Opinion of the Song has improved over Time, and several Critics have retrospectively ranked it as one of the strongest Releases in her Discography."
