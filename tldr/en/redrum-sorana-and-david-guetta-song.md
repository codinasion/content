short_summary: "2022 single by Sorana and David Guetta"
description: "'Redrum' is a Song by romanian Singer and Songwriter Sorana and french Disc Jockey David Guetta. It was released by atlantic Records on january 20 2022 for digital Download and Streaming. The Song was written by Sorana and 15 Others, including Jeffrey Bhasker, Mikkel Cox, Guetta, Malik Jones, Mr Hudson, Scott Mescudi, Giorgio Tuinfort, Kanye West and Ernest Wilson, while the Production was handled by Toby Green, Guetta and Cox. A Dance Pop Track about a Love Triangle and resulting heartbreak it interpolates the Refrain of West's song Heartless from 2008."
