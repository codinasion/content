short_summary: "Species of flowering plant endemic to the US"
description: "Symphyotrichum Kentuckiense is a rare Species of flowering Plant in the Family Asteraceae and is generally known as kentucky Aster Price's Aster miss Price's Aster Sadie's Aster or oldfield Aster Lavender. It is a perennial herbaceous Plant that is endemic to broken Limestone Cedar Glades and Roadside in Alabama Georgia Kentucky and Tennessee. It blooms from August through October it reaches Heights between 30 Centimeters and 100cm and has green to reddish-brown Stems. It is a nearly Hairless Plant with blue to blue-violet Ray Florets."
