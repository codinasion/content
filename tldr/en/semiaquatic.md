title: "Semiaquatic"
short_summary: "Spends part of their time in water, or grows partially submerged in water"
description: "In Biology semiaquatic Refers to various Macroorganisms that live regularly in both terrestrial and aquatic Environments. When referring to animals the Term describes those that spend Part of their daily Time in Water or Land Animals that have spent at least one Life Stage in aquatic Environments. When referring to Plants the Term describes Land Plants whose Roots have adapted well to tolerate regular prolonged Submersion in Water as well as Emergent and floating-leaved aquatic Plants that are only partially immersed."
