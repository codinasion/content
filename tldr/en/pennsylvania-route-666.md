short_summary: "State highway in Forest and Warren counties in Pennsylvania, United States"
description: "Route 666 is an east-west State Route in northwest Pennsylvania. The western terminus of the route is at U. S. Route 62 in the Hickory Township Municipality of East Hickory, and its eastern Terminus is at Us 6 in the Hamlet of Sheffield. It cuts through most of Allegheny National Forest, and is therefore sparsely populated, with the largest Settlement along the Road being Endeavor. Its official Name is david Zeisberger memorial Highway."
