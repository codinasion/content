short_summary: "Hong Kongese professional snooker player"
description: "Marco Fu Ka-Chun, Mh, Jp is a Hong Kong professional Snooker Player. He is a three-time ranking Event Winner, having won the 2007 Grand Prix, the 2013 australian Goldfields Open and the 2016 Scottish Open. He was a runner-up in two triple crown Events at the uk Championship 2008 and the Masters 2011. In Addition, Fu has reached the Semi-Finals of the World Championship Twice—In 2006 and in 2016."
