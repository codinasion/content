title: "USS Alabama (BB-8)"
short_summary: "Pre-dreadnought battleship of the United States Navy"
description: "The uss Alabama was an illinois-class Battleship built for the united States navy. She was the second Ship of her Class and the second Ship to carry her Name. Her Keel was laid out in December 1896 at William Cramp Sons Shipyard and she was launched in may 1898. In October 1900 she was commissioned into the Fleet. The Ship was armed with a Battery of four 13-inch Guns and had a maximum Speed of 16 Knots."
