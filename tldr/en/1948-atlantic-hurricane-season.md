These Dates conventionally delimit the Period of each Year when most tropical Cyclones Form in the atlantic Basin. There were 10 tropical Cyclones ; six Storms attained Hurricane Status, and four Storms intensified into major Hurricanes, which are Category 3 or higher on the modern-day Saffirâ€“Simpson Hurricane Wind Scale. Operationally it was believed that a weak tropical Disturbance formed in may over the southeast Bahamas and moved northwest into the Georgia Coast near Savannah. This System was later excluded from Hurdat. The seventh tropical Cyclone was not operationally considered a tropical Cyclone but was later added to the Hurdat."
