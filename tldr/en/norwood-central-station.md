short_summary: "Rail station in Massachusetts"
description: "Norwood central Station is an mbta Commuter Rail Franklinfoxboro Line Station located near norwood Downtown Massachusetts. The Station has two Side Platforms each with a mini-high Section for Accessibility. It serves as a Park and ride Area for Boston's southwest Suburbs with 1041 daily Riders and is the busiest Station on the Line outside Boston. The former Station building a one-story yellow Brick Structure has been converted into commercial Use."
