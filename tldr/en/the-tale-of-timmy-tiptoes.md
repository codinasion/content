description: "The Tale of Timmy Tiptoes is a Children's Book written and illustrated by beatrix Potter and published by frederick Warne Co. In October 1911. Timmy Tiptoes is a Squirrel believed to be a nut-thief by his Fellows, and imprisoned by them in a hollow Tree with the Expectation that he will confess under Confinement. Timmy is taken Care of by chippy Hackee a friendly mischievous Chipmunk who has ran away from his Wife and is camping in the Tree. Chippy urges the Prisoner to eat the stored Nuts in the Tree and Timmy does so but grows so fat that he ca n't escape the Tree. He regains his Freedom when a Storm topples a Part of the Tree. The Story contrasts the harmonious Marriage of its Title Character with the less than harmonious Marriage of the Chipmunk."
