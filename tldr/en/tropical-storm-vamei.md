title: "Tropical Storm Vamei"
short_summary: "Pacific and North Indian tropical cyclone in 2001"
description: "Tropical Storm Vamei was a pacific tropical Cyclone formed at about 85 nautical Miles from the Equator. The last Storm of the 2001 Pacific Typhoon Season, Vamei developed on 26 December at 1. 4° N in the South China Sea. It strengthened quickly and made Landfall along the extreme southeastern Peninsula Malaysia. The Vamei was rapidly weakened into a remnant Low over Sumatra on 28 December and the Remnants eventually reorganized in the north indian Ocean. The Storm encountered again strong Wind Shear and dissipated on 1 january 2002."
