description: "'leopard-skin Pill-Box Hat' is a Song by american Singer-Songwriter Bob Dylan, which was released on the second Side of his seventh Studio Album Blonde on Blonde. The Song was written by Dylan and produced by Bob Johnston. Dylan has denied that the Song cites any specific individual although Critics have speculated that it refers to edie Sedgwick who dylan had spent Time with in December 1965."
