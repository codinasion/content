short_summary: "1995 studio album by Radiohead"
description: "The Bends is the second Studio Album by the English Rock Band Radiohead, released on 13 March 1995 by Parlophone. It was produced by John Leckie, with extra Production by Radiohead, Nigel Godrich and Jim Warren. The Bends Combines guitar Songs and Ballads with more restrained Arrangements and cryptic Lyrics than Radiohead's Debut album pablo Honey."
