short_summary: "2017 promotional single by Camila Cabello featuring Quavo"
description: "Omg is a Song recorded by the cuban-american Singer Camila Cabello featuring american Rapper Quavo. It was released on August 3, 2017 as a promotional single alongside 'Havana', from her Debut Studio Album Camila, originally titled the Hurting. The Healing. The Loving. Omg was removed from the final Track List. The Song was written by the Artists, Charli Xcx, Noonie Bao, Sasha Sloan, and its Producers Stargate. 'Omg' has a slow, trap Production that is characterized by Use of double hi-hat Rolls, Flute Flows and a smooth Bass. In the Lyrics, Cabello praises the Appearance of her Love Interest while Quavo responds to her Affirmation in his Verses, where he also added a Line from his Migos's Song 'Bad and Boujee'. The Song was included as Part of Cabello's Set List for Bruno Mars's 24k Magic World Tour, where she served as an Opening Act."
