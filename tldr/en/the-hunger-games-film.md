description: "The Hunger Games is a 2012 american dystopian Action Film directed by Gary Ross, who co-wrote the Screenplay with Suzanne Collins and Billy Ray, based on the 2008 Novel of the same Name by Collins. It is the first Installment of the Film the Hunger Games. The Film stars donald Sutherland Stanley Tucci Josh Hutcherson liam Hemsworth Woody Harrelson elizabeth Banks lenny Kravitz and Jennifer Lawrence. In the Film, Katniss Everdeen and Peeta Mellark are forced to compete in the Hunger Games, an Elaborate televised Fight to the Death Consisting of adolescent Contestants from the 12 Districts of Panem."
