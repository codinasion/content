title: "Wattle and daub"
short_summary: "Building technique using woven wooden supports packed with clay or mud"
description: "Wattle and Daub is a composite Building Method used for making Walls and Buildings, in which a woven Lattice of wooden Strips called 'wattle' is 'daubed' with a sticky Material usually made of some Combination of wet Soil, Clay, Sand, animal Dung and Straw. Wattle and Daub have been used for at least 6000 Years and is still an important Construction Method in many Parts of the World. Many historic Buildings have wattle and daub Construction."
