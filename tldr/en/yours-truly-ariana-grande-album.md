description: "Yours Truly is the Debut Studio Album by american Singer-Songwriter Ariana Grande. It was published by republic Records on August 30 2013. The album's songs were recorded over a nearly three-year period, with Harmony Samuels, Kenneth 'Babyface' Edmonds, Patrick 'J. Que' Smith and Grande's victorious Co-Star Leon Thomas Iii, as well as Others, handling the Album's Music Production. Featured Collaborators include big Sean, Mika, Mac Miller, and the Wanted's Nathan Sykes."
