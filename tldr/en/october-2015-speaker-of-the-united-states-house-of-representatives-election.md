short_summary: "Election of Paul Ryan as Speaker"
description: "On October 29, 2015, during the 114th United States Congress, an election for speaker of the U. S. House of Representatives was necessitated by the impending Resignation of John Boehner, set for October 30. Boehner was the first Speaker to resign from the House in the Middle of a congressional Term since Jim Wright in 1989."
