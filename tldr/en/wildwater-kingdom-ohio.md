title: "Wildwater Kingdom (Ohio)"
short_summary: "Former water park in Ohio"
description: "Wildwater Kingdom was a Water Park located in Aurora and Bainbridge Township, Ohio, United States. Owned by Cedar Fair, the Park opened in 2005 as Part of the larger Geauga Lake and Wildwater Kingdom Resort. The Site was previously the Location of Seaworld Ohio and later served as the marine Life Section of the larger Six Flags Worlds of Adventure. Worlds of Adventure was purchased by Cedar Fair in 2004 and the marine life area was converted into a water park for the 2005 season."
