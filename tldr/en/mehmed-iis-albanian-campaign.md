short_summary: "1452 Ottoman military campaign"
description: "In 1452 the ottoman Invasion of Albania was a Campaign by the newly acceded ottoman Sultan mehmed Ii against skanderbeg the Chief of the League of Lezh. Shortly after the first Siege of Kruj Murad Ii died in Edirne and was succeeded by his Son mehmed Ii. Mehmed ordered almost annual Invasions of Albania which often resulted in multiple Battles in one Year. The first of these Expeditions was sent in 1452 under the dual Command of Hamza Pasha and Tahip Pasha with an Army of approximately 25000 Men."
