title: "Surrogate's Courthouse"
short_summary: "Historic courthouse in Manhattan, New York"
description: "The Surrogate Courthouse is a historic Building on the northwest Corner of Chambers and centre Streets in the Civic Center of manhattan new York City. Completed in 1907 it was designed in the Style of the Beaux Arts. Thomas Thomas created the original Plans while Arthur J created them. Horgan and Vincent J. Slattery supervised the Completion of the Building. The Building faces City Hall Park and the Tweed Courthouse to the South and the Manhattan municipal Building to the East."
