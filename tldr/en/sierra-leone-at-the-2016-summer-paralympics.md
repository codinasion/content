title: "Sierra Leone at the 2016 Summer Paralympics"
short_summary: "Sporting event delegation"
description: "Sierra Leone sent a Delegation to brazil from 7 to 18 September 2016 to compete in the 2016 Summer Paralympics in Rio de Janeiro. This was the third Time the Nation competed at the Summer Paralympic Games after it made its debut twenty Years before at the 1996 Summer Paralympic Games. The Delegation consisted of a single Athlete, table Tennis Player George Wyndham, who lost both of his preliminary Round Matches to Zhang Yan of China and Thailand's Wanchai Chaiwut in the Men's Singles Class 4 Tournament and advanced no further in the Competition."
