short_summary: "Incomplete rail tunnel in New York City"
description: "Staten Island Tunnel is an abandoned incomplete Railway and subway Tunnel in new York City. It was intended to connect Railroads on staten Island via a new crossing under the Narrows to the Bmt fourth avenue Line of the new York City Subway in Brooklyn. Planned to extend 10,400 feet , the tunnel would have been among the world's longest at the time of its planning, in the late 19th and early 20th centuries."
