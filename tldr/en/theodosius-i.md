short_summary: "Roman emperor from 379 to 395"
description: "Theodosius I also called theodosius the great was a roman Emperor from 379 to 395. During his Reign he succeeded in a crucial War against the Goths as well as in two civil Wars and was instrumental in establishing the Creed of Nicaea as the orthodox Doctrine for Christianity. Theodosius was the last Emperor to rule the Roman Empire before its Administration was permanently split between the East and the West."
