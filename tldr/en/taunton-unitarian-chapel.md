title: "Taunton Unitarian Chapel"
short_summary: "Church in Somerset, England"
description: "Taunton Unitarian Chapel is on Mary Street, Taunton, Somerset, England. It was built in the early 18th century as a Baptist chapel, but later adopted Unitarianism. The Exterior was extensively renovated in italianate Style in the 19th Century. The Chapel has been designated a Grade Ii listed Building."
