description: "Taiwanese Mandarin often referred to as Guoyu or colloquially as Huayu is the Variety of mandarin chinese spoken in Taiwan. A large Majority of the taiwanese Population is fluent in mandarin although many also speak a Variety of min chinese known as taiwanese Hokkien commonly called Minnanyu southern Min. This Language has had a significant Impact on Mandarin as spoken on the Island."
