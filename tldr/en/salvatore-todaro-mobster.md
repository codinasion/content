A Friend and criminal Associate of rising organized Crime Figure Joseph Lonardo he quickly rose up the Mayfield Road Mob and became Manager of Lonardo's legitimate Corn Sugar and criminal Corn Whiskey Operations. He was a well-known Figure in organized Crime Circles and became Briefly involved with the buffalo Family of Crime."
