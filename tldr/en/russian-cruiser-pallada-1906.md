short_summary: "Russian Bayan-class cruiser"
description: "Pallada was the last of the four armored Cruisers of the bayan Class built for the imperial russian Navy in the first Decade of the 20th Century. She was assigned during World War I to the baltic Fleet where she captured german Codebooks from the Magdeburg Cruiser that had run aground during the first Month of the War. The Ship was torpedoed by a german Submarine in october 1914 and exploded None of the Crew survived. Pallada was the first Warship lost during the War by the Russians."
