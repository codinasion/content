title: "Tropical Storm Brenda (1960)"
short_summary: "Atlantic tropical cyclone"
description: "Tropical Storm Brenda was the second named Storm of the atlantic Hurricane Season of 1960. It developed in the northeastern Gulf of Mexico on July 28 and reached tropical Storm Status after moving ashore over the florida Peninsula. It accelerated northeast along the U. S. After reaching its Peak on the east Coast as a moderate Storm with Winds of 60mph before crossing the mid-atlantic States and new England on July 31 it dissipated over southern Canada. It has caused moderate Damage in Florida the worst since Hurricane Easy of 1950 and has brought heavy Rains north of new York City. Its total Damage was estimated at Us $ 5 million, and only indirect Deaths were blamed on it."
