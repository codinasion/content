short_summary: "American video game composer"
description: "Martin O'Donnell is an american Composer, audio Director, and sound Designer known for his Work on video Game Developer Bungie's Series, such as Myth, Oni, Halo, and Destiny. O'Donnell collaborated with his musical Colleague Michael Salvatori for many of the Scores ; he has also directed Voice Talent and sound Design for the Halo Trilogy. O'Donnell was bungie's audio-lead until april 11 2014."
