title: "Violin"
short_summary: "Bowed string instrument"
description: "The Violin colloquially known as Fiddle is a Wood Chordophone and is the smallest and hence highest-pitched Instrument in regular Use in the Violin Family. Smaller violin-type Instruments exist including the Violino Piccolo and the Pochette but these are virtually unused. Most Violins have a hollow Wooden Body and commonly have four Strings usually tuned in perfect Fifths with Notes g3 D4 A4 e5 they are most commonly played by drawing a Bow across the Strings. The Violin can also be played by plucking the Strings with the Fingers and in special Cases by hitting the Strings with the wooden Side of the Bow."
