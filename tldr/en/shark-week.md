short_summary: "Television program on Discovery Channel"
description: "Shark Week is an annual, Week long Tv Programming Block at the Discovery Channel, which features shark-based Programming. Shark Week premiered on July 17 1988. It was originally devoted to conservation Efforts and Correction of Misconceptions about Sharks and was featured annually in July or early August. Over Time, it grew in Popularity and became a Hit on the Discovery Channel. Since 2010 it has been the longest running Cable Television Event in History. Broadcast in over 72 Countries, Shark Week is promoted heavily via social Networks like Facebook and Twitter. Episodes are also available for Purchase on Services like Google Play Movies & Tv/Youtube, Amazon Video, and Itunes. Some Episodes are free on subscription-based Discovery and Hulu."
