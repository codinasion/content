short_summary: "1984 studio album by R.E.M."
description: "Reckoning is the second Studio Album by the american Alternative Rock Band R. E. M. , released on April 9, 1984, by i. R. S. Records. The Album was produced by Mitch Easter and don Dixon and recorded over 16 Days in december 1983 and january 1984 at Reflection Sound Studio in charlotte north Carolina. Dixon and Easter intended to capture the Sound of R. E. M. 's live Performances, and used binaural recording on several Tracks. Lead Singer Michael Stipe Dealt with Darker subject Matter in his Lyrics with water-related Imagery a recurring Theme on the Album."
