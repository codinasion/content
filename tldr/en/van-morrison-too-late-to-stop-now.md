description: "Van Morrison too late to stop now is a Biography of the musician Van Morrison written by Steve Turner. It was first published in 1993 by Penguin Group in the united States and by Bloomsbury Publishing in great Britain. In 1985 when Turner first met Van Morrison he interviewed approximately 40 People who knew about the Subject in his Research for his Biography. Van Morrison did not consider the Biography positively and multiple Newspapers reported he attempted to purchase all 25000 Copies of the Book. He wrote a Letter to the Author asserting the 40 Individuals interviewed for the Book were not his Friends and accused Turner of fabricating inaccurate Information about me personally."
