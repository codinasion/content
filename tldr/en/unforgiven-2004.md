short_summary: "World Wrestling Entertainment pay-per-view event"
description: "The Unforgiven in 2004 was the seventh annual unforgiven professional Wrestling pay-per-view Event produced by World Wrestling Entertainment. It was exclusively held for Wrestlers from the raw Brand Division of the Promotion. The Event took Place on 12 September 2004 at rose Garden in Portland Oregon. It is one of only two wwe Pay-Per-View Events ever held in the State of Oregon the other being no Mercy in 2008."
