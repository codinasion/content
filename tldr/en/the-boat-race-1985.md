title: "The Boat Race 1985"
short_summary: "Oxford versus Cambridge rowing race"
description: "The 131st Boat Race took Place on April 6 1985. Held annually the Event is a rowing Race Side by Side along the Thames between the Crews of Universities Oxford and Cambridge. Oxford won by 434 Lengths. Bruce Philp became the first Man to row for both Universities having previously rowed for Cambridge and Henrietta Shaw was the first Female Cox for Cambridge."
