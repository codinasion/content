title: "Wallachian Revolution of 1848"
short_summary: "Liberal and Romanian nationalist uprising"
description: "The wallachian Revolution of 1848 was a romanian Liberal and nationalist Uprising in Wallachia's Principality. It was Part of the 1848 Revolutions and closely connected to the unsuccessful Revolt in the Principality of Moldavia it sought to overturn the Administration imposed by imperial russian Authorities under the Regulationul organic Regime and through many of its Leaders demanded the Abolition. The Movement led by a Group of young Intellectuals and Officers in the wallachian Militia succeeded in toppling the Ruling Prince Gheorghe Bibescu whom it replaced with a provisional Government and a Regency and in passing a Series of major progressive Reforms announced."
