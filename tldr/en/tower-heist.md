The Plot follows Employees of an exclusive Apartment Building who lose their Pensions in the ponzi Scheme of a wall Street Businessman and enlist the Assistance of a criminal Bankrupt Businessman and an Immigrant Maid to break into his Apartment and steal their Money while avoiding the."
