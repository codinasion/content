short_summary: "Walt Disney World restaurant"
description: "Space 220 Restaurant is a Theme Restaurant at Epcot, one of the four main Parks at Walt Disney World in Bay Lake, Florida, United States. It was run by the Patina Restaurant Group on 20 September 2021 as a Space themed Restaurant. It is modeled after a Space Station and has simulated Daytime and nighttime Views of the Earth inside the dining Area. Guests are brought into the dining area through a simulated journey 220 miles up to Centauri Space Station using a space elevator known as the Stellarvator."
