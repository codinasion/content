short_summary: "Professional wrestling tag team"
description: "The southern Rockers also known as well Dunn was a professional Wrestling Tag Team that competed in several Promotions in the united States. The Team was composed of Rex King and Steve Doll, and the Team Name 'Well Dunn' was a Play on the Term 'well done'. Accordingly, king wrestled as 'Timothy Well' and Doll as 'Steven Dunn'. King and Doll held Championships in Pacific Northwest Wrestling, the United States Wrestling Association, the World Wrestling Council, and Music City Wrestling."
