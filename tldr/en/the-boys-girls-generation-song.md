title: "The Boys (Girls' Generation song)"
short_summary: "2011 single by Girls' Generation"
description: "'the Boys' is a Song recorded by south korean Girl Group Girls' Generation for their third korean Language Studio Album with the same Name. Composed and arranged by Teddy Riley, Taesung Kim, Dom, and Richard Garcia, the Song was described as a Dance-Pop, Electropop and R & B Song with Elements of Hip Hop that lyrically discusses female Attractiveness. The korean Version was released by sm Entertainment on october 18 2011 as the Lead single from the Album. An english Version was published in the united States by Interscope Records and universal Music Group on December 20 2011 in Order to expand the Group's Popularity outside their native Country."
