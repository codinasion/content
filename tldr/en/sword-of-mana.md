short_summary: "2003 video game"
description: "Sword of Mana originally released in Japan as shin'yaku seiken Densetsu is an Action role-playing Game developed by square Enix and brownie brown in 2003 and published by square Enix and Nintendo for the Game Boy Advance. It is an enhanced Remake of the first Game in the Mana Series, the Game Boy Game Seiken Densetsu: Final Fantasy Gaiden, which was released as Final Fantasy Adventure in North America and as mystic Quest in Europe. Sword of Mana was the fifth Release of the Series. The Game follows an unnamed Hero and Heroine as they seek to defeat the Dark Lord and defend the Mana tree from Enemies who wish to misuse its Power."
