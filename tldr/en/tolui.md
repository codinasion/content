short_summary: "Regent of the Mongol Empire (c. 1191 – 1232)"
description: "Tolui was the youngest Son of Genghis Khan and Brte. A prominent general during the early Mongol Conquests Tolui was a leading Candidate for his Father's Successor after his Death in 1227 and served as Regent of the Mongol Empire until the Accession of his Brother Gedei two Years later. Tolui's Wife was Sorghaghtani Beki ; their Sons included Möngke and Kublai, the fourth and fifth Khagans of the Empire, and Hulagu, the Founder of the Ilkhanate."
