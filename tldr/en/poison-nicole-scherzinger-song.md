description: "Poison is a Song of the american singer-songwriter nicole Scherzinger from her Debut Solo Studio Album Killer Love. Released on October 25, 2010, it is the first single from the Album. It was produced by Redone Beatgeek and Jimmy Joker and is distinctly different from Scherzinger's previous Attempts at Solo releasing Material. Poison is supported by powerful Synthesizers and a pulsating dance-pop Beat. The single was released as a two-track digital Download and as a seven-track Remixes extended Play."
