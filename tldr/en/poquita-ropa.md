description: "Poquita Ropa is the twelfth Studio Album by Guatemalan singer-songwriter Ricardo Arjona. It was released on August 24, 2010, through Warner Music. It was recorded in Mexico City, New York City, and Los Angeles and was produced by Lee Levin and Dan Warner under their Production Name Los Gringo, with additional Works from Matt Rollings and Brian Lang."
