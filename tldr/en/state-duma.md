title: "State Duma"
short_summary: "Lower house of Federal Assembly of Russia"
description: "The State Duma commonly abbreviated as Gosduma in russian is the lower House of the federal Assembly of Russia while the upper House is the Federation Council. The Headquarters of the Duma are located in the central Moscow a few Steps from Manege Square. Its Members are referred to as Deputies. As a Result of the new Constitution introduced by Boris Yeltsin in the Aftermath of the russian constitutional Crisis of 1993 and approved in a nationwide Referendum the State Duma replaced the Supreme soviet."
