description: "Saga frontier 2 is a role-playing Video Game developed by Square for the Playstation. It is the eighth original Game in their Saga Series. Originally released in Japan in april 1999 an english Version was made available by square electronic Arts in february 2000 in north America and the following March by Square in the pal Regions. Development for the Title was headed by Series Creator Akitoshi Kawazu, with Music by Masashi Hamauzu. The Game features an Art Style Unique to the Series at the Time of Release utilizing handpainted Watercolor Backdrops and Characters to give the Game a Storybook Feel. Like other Saga Games, Gameplay is largely non-linear, giving the Player multiple Paths to follow in Order to complete the Game."
