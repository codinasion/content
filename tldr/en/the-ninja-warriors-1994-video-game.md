short_summary: "1994 SNES game"
description: "The ninja Warriors is a Beat'em up Video Game developed by Natsume for the Super nintendo Entertainment System and published in 1994 by Taito in Japan and north America and in 1995 by Titus in Europe. It is a follow-up to taito's arcade Game of the same Title 1987 and Shares similar Gameplay. The Player can choose between playing as one of three ninja Androids, each with different Attributes and a unique Set of Moves including Jumps, Dashes, Throws, and other Attacks. The Game was developed by the same natsume Team that later developed wild Guns."
