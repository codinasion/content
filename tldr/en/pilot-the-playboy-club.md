description: "The Pilot Episode of the american historical Fiction Television Series the Playboy Club premiered in the united States on Nbc on September 19 2011. It was directed by Alan Taylor and written by Chad Hodge and Becky Mode. In this Episode maureen a newly-invited Playboy bunny gets involved in the Murder of Mob Boss Bruno Bianchi. Nick Dalton, one of Chicago's top Attorneys and Club Key-Holder, comes to her Aid ; his girlfriend Carol-Lynne makes an ambitious Move and becomes the first Bunny Mother. Bunnies janie Alice and Brenda each have their own personal Issues and Secrets while Club Manager billy rosen Tries his best to keep the Club running without Interference from the Mob."
