short_summary: "2011 studio album by Jill Scott"
description: "The Light of the Sun is the fourth Studio Album by american Singer Jill Scott. It was recorded following Scott's four-year Break from her Music Career and Departure from her former Label hidden Beach Recordings. The Light of the Sun was recorded at several Studios and produced primarily by Scott and Jr Hutson a Songwriter and Producer who had previously worked on her 2007 album the real Thing Words and sounds Vol. 3. Music Journalists noted the Light of the Sun for its neo Soul Sound, Element of Improvisation, and Scott's Themes of Emotion and Womanhood."
