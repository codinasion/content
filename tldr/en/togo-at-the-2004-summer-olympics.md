description: "Togo competed at the 2004 Summer Olympics in Athens greece from august 13 to 29 2004. The Participation of the Country at Athens marked its seventh Appearance at the Summer Olympics since its 1972 Debut at the munich Summer Games in west Germany. The Delegation included two Track and Field Athletes and a single Canoeist ; Jan Sekpona in the Men's 800 Metres, Sandrine Thiébaud-Kangni in the Women's 400 Metres, and Benjamin Boukpeti in the Men's K-1. Both Sekpona and Thiébaud-Kangni were eliminated in the first Round of their Competitions, while Boukpeti progressed to the Semi-Finals."
