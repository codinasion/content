short_summary: "King of Wessex from 865 to 871"
description: "Thelred was king of Wessex from 865 until his Death in 871. He was the fourth of five Sons of king Thelwulf of wessex four of whom in Turn became king. Thelred succeeded his Elder Brother Thelberht and was followed by his youngest Brother the great alfred. Æthelred had two Sons, Æthelhelm and Æthelwold, who were passed over for the Kingship on their Father's Death because they were still Infants. Alfred was succeeded by his Son edward the Elder and thelwold unsuccessfully disputed the Throne with him."
