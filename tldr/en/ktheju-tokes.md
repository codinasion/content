description: "Ktheju Toks is a Song by the albanian Singer Jonida Maliqi. It was released by Radio Televizioni Shqiptar and Universal in 2019 as a single. The Song was written and composed by Eriona Rushiti and produced by Enis Mullaj. It is musically an authentic and rhythmic albanian-language folk-electronic Ballad backed by traditional albanian Instruments. Lyrically the Song addresses the Theme of Migration particularly to that of the albanian People during the late 20th Century Kosovo War."
