title: "Thirtymile Fire"
short_summary: "2001 fire in the Okanogan National Forest, Washington state"
description: "The Thirtymile Fire was first reported on July 9, 2001 in the Okanogan National Forest, approximately 30 Miles north of Winthrop, Washington, United States. The Fire had been caused by an unsupervised Campfire which spread rapidly in the hot and dry Weather of the Pacific northwest. Four Firefighters were killed when the Fire cut off their only Escape Route from the narrow Canyon."
