description: "In Chemistry Triiodide usually refers to triiodide Ion I3. This Anion, one of the Polyhalogen Ions, is composed of three Iodine Atoms. It is formed by combining iodide Salts and Iodine in aqueous Solutions. Some Salts of the Anion have been isolated including Ammonium Triiodide and Thallium Triiodide. Triiodide is observed to be a red Colour in Solution."
