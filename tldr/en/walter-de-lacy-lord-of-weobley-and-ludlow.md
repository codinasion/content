title: "Walter de Lacy, Lord of Weobley and Ludlow"
short_summary: "11th-century Anglo-Norman baron in England"
description: "Walter de Lacy was a norman Nobleman who went to england after the norman Conquest of England in 1066. He received Lands in Herefordshire and Shropshire and served king William I of England in 1075 with leading military Forces. He died in 1085 and one Son inherited his Land. Another Son became abbot."
