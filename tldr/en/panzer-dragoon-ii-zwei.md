short_summary: "1996 video game"
description: "Panzer Dragoon II Zwei is a 1996 rail shooter game for the Sega Saturn, published by Sega and developed by Sega's Team Andromeda studio. The second Entry in the Panzer Dragoon Series and a Prequel to the original Game, the Story follows Lundi and his Dragon Companion, Lagi, as they pursue an Airship of the Ancient Age. The Player controls an aiming Reticle representing the Dragon's Laser and Lundi's Gun, shooting Enemies while the Dragon travels through 3d Environments on a fixed Track. Levels can have multiple Pathways, and the Dragon grows stronger over the Course of the Game based on the Player's Score."
