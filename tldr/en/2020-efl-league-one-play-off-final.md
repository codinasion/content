description: "The 2020 Efl League one Play-Off final was a Football Match contested between Wycombe Wanderers and Oxford united the third and final Team to gain Promotion from efl League one to the Efl Championship. It was played on 13 July 2020 at Wembley Stadium in London. The top two Teams of the 201920 Efl League one Season gained automatic Promotion to the Championship while Teams from third to sixth Position in the Table took Part in play-off Semi-Finals and the Winners of these Semi-Finals competed for the final Place in. The Match was played behind closed Doors to comply with Restrictions during the Coronavirus Pandemic."
