short_summary: "Russian Bayan-class cruiser"
description: "Bayan was the third of the four armoured Cruisers of Bayan-Class built in the early 1900s for the russian imperial Navy. The Ship was assigned to the baltic Fleet. Shortly after World War I began she was modified to lay Mines. Bayan laid Mines herself and provided a safe Place for other Ships laying Mines. During the War the Ship fought several inconclusive Battles with german Ships including the Battle of Land Islands in Mid-1915. She also participated in the Battle of Moon Sound during the German invasion of the Estonian islands in late 1917, where she was damaged. In 1918 Bayan was decommissioned and sold for Scrap in 1922."
