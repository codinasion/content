description: "Northolt's Siege took Place on 25 and 26 December 1985 in northolt west London England. He resulted in the Shooting of the Hostage-Taker Errol Walker. It was the first Shooting by an Officer of the metropolitan Police Specialist Firearms Division. After a domestic Dispute Walker forced Entry into the Apartment of his Sister-In-Law. He took the woman, her daughter, and his own daughter hostage and shortly afterwards fatally stabbed the woman. Negotiations eventually secured the Release of Walker's Daughter but Walker still held the Child of his murdered Sister-In-Law Hostage with a large Kitchen Knife."
