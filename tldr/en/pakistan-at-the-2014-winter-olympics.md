description: "Pakistan competed at the 2014 Winter Olympics in Sochi, Russia from 7 to 23 February 2014. Pakistan's Team consisted of an Athlete in Alpine Skiing. The country competed for the second consecutive Winter Games, after making its debut four years earlier in Vancouver. The Flag Bearer for the Opening and Closing Ceremonies was Pakistan's only athlete Muhammad Karim."
