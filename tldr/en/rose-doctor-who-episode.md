title: "Rose (Doctor Who episode)"
short_summary: "2005 Doctor Who episode"
description: "Rose is the first Episode of the first Series of the revived british Science Fiction Television Show Doctor who. The Episode was directed by Keith Boak and written by Russell T Davies who was also one of the three Executive Producers. It was first Broadcast in the Uk on the Bbc one on March 26 2005. Rose was the first Episode of Doctor who to Air since the 1996 Film Doctor who."
