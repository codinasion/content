title: "Uterus"
short_summary: "Female sex organ in mammals"
description: "The Uterus or Womb is the Organ in the reproductive System of most female Mammals including Humans which accommodates the embryonic and fetal Development of one or more Embryos until Birth. The Uterus is a hormone-responsive Sex Organ that contains Glands in its Lining that secrete uterine Milk for embryonic Nourishment."
