title: "The Mermaid (1910 film)"
short_summary: "1910 American film"
description: "The Mermaid is a 1910 american silent short Comedy produced by the Thanhouser Company. The Film focuses on John Gary, a Hotel Owner, who wants to revitalize his Business. After reading about a reported Mermaid Sighting he has his Daughter ethel pose as a Mermaid and gets a Newspaper Reporter to witness and photograph the Mermaid. The Publicity Results in the Hotel becoming famous but ethel eventually discloses the Joke in her Mermaid Suit to the Guests of the Hotel. The Film was released on 29 July 1910 and received mostly positive Reviews. The Film is presumed to have been lost."
