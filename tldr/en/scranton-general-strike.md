title: "Scranton general strike"
short_summary: "1877 strikes and riots in Scranton, Pennsylvania"
description: "The Scranton general Strike was a widespread Work Stoppage of Workers in Scranton Pennsylvania in 1877 which took Place as Part of the great Railroad Strike and was the last in a Number of violent Outbreaks across Pennsylvania. On July 23 Railroad Workers started a Strike in Protest of recent Wage Cuts which included Thousands of Workers from a Variety of Industries."
