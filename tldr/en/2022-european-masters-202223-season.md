description: "The 2022 european Masters was a professional Snooker Tournament that took Place from 16 to 21 August 2022 in the stadthalle Frth in Frth Germany. Broadcast on Eurosport and other Networks worldwide the Tournament was the second ranking Event of the 202223 Season and the Second of eight Tournaments in the Season's Betvictor Series. The 24th Edition of the european Masters was the second Staging of the Event in 2022 after the previous Event in february. It contained a total Prize Fund of 427000 of which 80000 went to the Winner."
