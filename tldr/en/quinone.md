title: "Quinone"
short_summary: "Compounds having a fully conjugated cyclic dione structure"
description: "The Quinones are a Class of organic Compounds that are formally derived from aromatic Compounds by Conversion of an even Number of Ch Groups into c Groups with any necessary Rearrangement of double Bonds resulting in a fully conjugated cyclic Dione Structure. The archetypical Member of the Class is 14-benzoquinone or Cyclohexadienedione which is sometimes simply quinone. Other important Examples are the 12-benzoquinone 14-naphthoquinone and 910-anthraquinone."
