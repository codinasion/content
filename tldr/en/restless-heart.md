short_summary: "American country music band"
description: "Restless Heart was an american Country Music Band founded in 1984. The Band's longest-term Lineup consisted of greg Jennings John Dittrich Paul Gregg dave Innis and larry Stewart. Tim Dubois recorded the Band's Demos and chose Verlon Thompson as the original Lead Singer but Thompson was replaced in this Role by Stewart before the Band recorded any Material. Between 1984 and 1998 restless Heart recorded for Rca Records nashville. They released the Albums Restless Heart, Wheels, Big Dreams in a Small Town, and Fast Movin' Train with Stewart on lead Vocals."
