title: "Silicate"
short_summary: "Any polyatomic anion containing silicon and oxygen"
description: "In Chemistry a Silicate is a Member of a Family of polyatomic Anions consisting of Silicon and Oxygen with the general Formula N where 0 x 2. The Family includes orthosilicate Sio44 Metasilicate Sio23 and Pyrosilicate Si2O67. The Name is also used for any Salt of such Anions such as Sodium Metasilicate or any Ester containing the corresponding Chemical Group such as Tetramethyl Orthosilicate. The Name Silicate is sometimes extended to any Anions containing Silicon even if they do n't fit the general Formula or have other Atoms besides Oxygen such as Hexafluorosilicate 2. Most commonly Silicates are encountered as Silicate Minerals."
