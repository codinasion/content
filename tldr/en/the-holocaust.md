description: "The Holocaust was the genocide of European Jews during World War II. Between 1941 and 1945 nazi Germany and its Collaborators systematically murdered around six million Jews in german occupied Europe around Two-Thirds of european jewish Population. The Murders were carried out primarily by Mass Shootings and Poison Gas in Extermination Camps notably auschwitz-birkenau Treblinka Belzec Sobibor and Chemno in occupied Poland."
