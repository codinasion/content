description: "The 31st Boat Race took Place March 28 1874. The Boat Race is an annual Rowing Race between Crews from the Universities of Oxford and Cambridge along the Thames. Cambridge won three and a half Lengths in a Race umpired by former Oxford Rower Joseph William Chitty in their fifth consecutive Victory."
