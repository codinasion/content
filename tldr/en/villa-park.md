description: "Villa Park is a Football Stadium in Aston, Birmingham, with a seating Capacity of 42,640. Since 1897 it has been home to premier League Club Aston Villa. The Ground is less than a Mile from Witton and aston Railway Stations and has hosted sixteen England Internationals at senior Level the first in 1899 and the most recent in 2005. Villa Park has hosted more fa Cup Semi-Finals than any other Stadium and it is the 10th largest in England."
