description: "Romancing Saga is a 1992 Role playing video Game developed and published by Square for super Famicom. It is the fourth entry in the SaGa series. It was subsequently released for the Wonderswan Color in 2001 and mobile Phones in 2009. A Remake for the Playstation 2'Minstrel Song' subtitled in Japan was released by square Enix in 2005 both in Japan and North America. A Remaster of Minstrel Song was released in 2022 Worldwide for android Ios nintendo switch Playstation 4 Playstation 5 and Windows."
