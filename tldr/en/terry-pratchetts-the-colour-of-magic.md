The Fantasy Film was produced for Sky1 by the Mob, a small british Studio, starring David Jason, Sean Astin, Tim Curry, and Christopher Lee as the Voice of Death. Vadim Jean adapted the Screenplay from Pratchett's original Novels and served as Director."
