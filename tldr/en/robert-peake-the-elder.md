title: "Robert Peake the Elder"
short_summary: "English painter (c. 1551–1619)"
description: "Robert peake the Elder was an english Painter active in the later Part of the Reign of Elizabeth I and for most of the Reign of James i. In 1604, he was appointed Picture Maker to the Heir to the Throne, Prince Henry ; and in 1607, Serjeant-Painter to King James i – a Post he shared with John De Critz."
