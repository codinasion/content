title: "Underground Electric Railways Company of London"
short_summary: "Holding company for underground railways and bus operators in London"
description: "The underground electric Railways Company of London limited known operationally as the Underground for much of its Existence was established in 1902. It was the holding Company for the three deep-level Tube underground Railway Lines opened in London between 1906 and 1907 Baker Street and waterloo Railway charing cross Euston and hampstead Railway and great northern piccadilly and brompton Railway. It was also the Parent Company of the District Railway from 1902 which it electrified between 1903 and 1905. The Uerl is a Precursor to the London underground Today whose three Subway Lines form the central Sections of Today's Bakerloo northern and piccadilly Lines."
