title: "Tropical Storm Lidia (1981)"
short_summary: "Pacific tropical storm in 1981"
description: "Tropical Storm Lidia was the deadliest tropical Cyclone in the Pacific Hurricane Season of 1981. On october 6 a tropical Depression formed and formed into a tropical Storm six Hours later. Lidia struck the southern Tip of the mexican State of Baja California Sur on october 8 and made Landfall just south of Los Mochis in Sinaloa as a mid-level tropical Storm. Once onshore tropical Storm Lidia rapidly weakened and dissipated the same Day. It brought heavy Rains and flooding throughout Parts of northwestern Mexico particularly sinaloa. Overall, Lidia killed 100 people and caused at least $80 million in damage."
