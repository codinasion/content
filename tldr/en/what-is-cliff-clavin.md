description: "'What Is. . . Cliff Clavin? The fourteenth Episode of the eighth Season of the american Tv Sitcom Cheers is written by Dan O'Shannon and Tom Anderson and directed by andy Ackerman rather than James Burrows who directed the Majority of the other Episodes of the Series. It originally aired on Nbc on january 18 1990. Cliff Clavin appears on the Game Show Jeopardy in this Episode! And Game Show Host Alex Trebek guest Stars as himself. Cliff racks up an insurmountable Lead during the Game only to lose it all in the final Round. The Episode received Praise from Critics for its Concept and its guest Star."
