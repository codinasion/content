title: "SMS Prinz Eugen (1862)"
short_summary: "Ironclad warship of the Austro-Hungarian Navy"
description: "Sms prinz Eugen was the second Member of the Kaiser Max Class built for the austrian Navy in the 1860S. Her Keel was laid in October 1861 at the Stabilimento Tecnico Triestino Shipyard ; she was launched in June 1862, and was completed in March 1863. She carried her main Battery which was composed of sixteen 48-Pounders and fifteen 24-Pounders in a traditional Broadside Arrangement protected by an 110-mm armored Belt."
