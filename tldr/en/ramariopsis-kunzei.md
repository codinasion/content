title: "Ramariopsis kunzei"
short_summary: "Species of fungus"
description: "Ramariopsis Kunzei is an edible Species of coral Fungi in the Clavariaceae Family and the Type Species in the Genus Ramariopsis. It is commonly known as the white coral because of the branched Structure of the Fruit Bodies which resemble marine Coral. Fruit Bodies are up to 5cm Tall by 4cm wide with numerous Branches from a short rudimentary Stem. The branches are one to two millimeters thick, smooth, and white, sometimes with yellowish tips in age. Ramariopsis Kunzei has a widespread Distribution and is found in north america Europe Asia and Australia."
