short_summary: "1993 compilation album by 'Weird Al' Yankovic"
description: "The Food Album is a Compilation Album by american Singer-Songwriter 'weird Al' Yankovic, released on June 22, 1993, by Scotti Brothers Records. The Release features ten of Yankovic's Song Parodies, all of which Pertain to Food. A similar Album, the Tv Album, which features Songs entirely about Television, would be released two Years later."
