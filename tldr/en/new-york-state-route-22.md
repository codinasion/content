The State of new York from the Outskirts of new York City to the Town of Mooers in Clinton County near the canadian Border. It is the longest north-south Route in the State and the third longest State Route Overall after Ny5 and Ny17. Many of the state's major east–west roads intersect with, and often join, NY 22 just before crossing into the neighboring New England states, where U. S. Route 7 which originally partly followed the ny 22 Alignment Parallels the new York State Line similarly."
