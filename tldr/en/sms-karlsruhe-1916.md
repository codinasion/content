title: "SMS Karlsruhe (1916)"
short_summary: "Light cruiser of the German Imperial Navy"
description: "Sms Karlsruhe was a light Cruiser of the Knigsberg Class built during World War I for the Kaiserliche Marine. She was named after the earlier Karlsruhe which sank from an accidental Explosion in november 1914. The new Cruiser was laid down in 1914 at the Shipyard Kaiserliche Werft in Kiel launched in january 1916 and commissioned to the high Seas fleet in November 1916. Armed with eight 15cm Sk L45 guns the Ship had a top Speed of 27. 5 kn."
