title: "Parthenogenesis"
short_summary: "Asexual reproduction without fertilization"
description: "Parthenogenesis is a natural Form of asexual Reproduction in which the Growth and Development of an Embryo can occur directly from an Egg without Fertilization. In Animals Parthenogenesis is the Development of an Embryo from a unfertilized Egg Cell. Parthenogenesis is a Component of the apomixis Process in Plants. Parthenogenesis can occur in Algae as Parthenogenesis is the Development of an Embryo from either an individual Sperm or an individual Egg."
