short_summary: "American comic book editor (born 1957)"
description: "Jim Salicrup is an American comic book editor, known for his tenures at Marvel Comics and Topps Comics. At Marvel, where he worked for Twenty Years, he edited Books such as the Uncanny X-Men, Fantastic Four, Avengers and various spider-man Titles. At Topps, he edited Books such as Bram Stoker's Dracula, X-Files and Zorro."
