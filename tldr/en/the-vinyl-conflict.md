short_summary: "2010 box set by Slayer"
description: "The Vinyl Conflict is a Box set by the Thrash Metal Band Slayer, released October 12, 2010. It includes all Ten of Slayer's Studio Albums since 1986 and their 1991 live Album Decade of Aggression. The Idea of releasing a second Box Set was made Public in August 2010. It was first released with a Price of $ 199. 99. All Albums are on Vinyl and are remastered. It received generally a positive Reception from Critics."
