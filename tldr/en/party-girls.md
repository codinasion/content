short_summary: "2014 single by Ludacris featuring Wiz Khalifa, Jeremih and Cashmere Cat"
description: "'Party Girls' is a Song by american Hip Hop recording artist Ludacris, featuring Vocals from Singer Jeremih and rapper Wiz Khalifa, produced by Cashmere Cat. It contains an Interpolation of 1997 Hit single 'Barbie Girl' by danish-norwegian dance-pop Group Aqua. It was released January 30, 2014 in Promotion of his ninth Studio Album, Ludaversal ; this single along with 'Rest of My Life' and 'Representin' were not included on the Album's final Track List. The Song peaked at Number 36 on the Billboard Hot R & B/Hip-Hop Songs Chart."
