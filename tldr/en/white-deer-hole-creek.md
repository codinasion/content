title: "White Deer Hole Creek"
short_summary: "Tributary of the West Branch Susquehanna River"
description: "White Deer Hole Creek is a 20. 5-mile Tributary of the West Branch Susquehanna River in Clinton, Lycoming and Union Counties in the U. S. State of Pennsylvania. A Part of the Chesapeake Bay Drainage basin the white Deer Hole Creek watershed Drains Parts of ten Communities. The Creek flows east in a Valley of the ridge-and-valley appalachian Mountains through Sandstone Limestone and Shale from the ordovician Silurian and devonian Periods."
