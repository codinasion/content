short_summary: "Species of shrub in the family Proteaceae"
description: "Persoonia Terminalis also known as Torrington Geebung is a Shrub Belonging to the Family Proteaceae and native to northern new South Wales and southern Queensland in eastern Australia. Reported as a Subspecies of Persoonia Nutans in 1981 it was described as a Species in 1991 by Lawrie Johnson and his Colleague Peter Weston."
