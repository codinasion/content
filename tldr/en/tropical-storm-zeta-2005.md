title: "Tropical Storm Zeta (2005)"
short_summary: "Off season Atlantic tropical cyclone"
description: "Tropical Storm Zeta was a very late-developing tropical Storm that formed in the central atlantic Ocean during the atlantic Hurricane Season of 2005 a Month after the Season's official End. Zeta was declared a tropical Depression on 30 December and intensified the following Day into the 28th Storm of the Season to continue into january 2006. It was one of only two Atlantic tropical cyclones to span two calendar years."
