title: "Shooting of Vivian Strong"
short_summary: "1969 shooting in Omaha, Nebraska"
description: "On June 24, 1969, Vivian Strong, a 14-year-old african American Girl, was killed in Omaha, Nebraska, United States, when a white Police Officer shot her in the Back of the Head without warning. The white Police Officer and his black Partner were dispatched to the Location because there were Juveniles entering. When they arrived on the Scene a Group of Teenagers fled from an abandoned Apartment where they had been dancing. The Killing sparked three Days of Rioting in Omaha's predominantly african-american Neighborhood."
