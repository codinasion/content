It favours the upper and middle Parts of the Water Column and can be found from the Intertidal Zone to a Depth of 50 Meters. Appearance-Wise this species is virtually identical to the common Blacktip Shark from which it can be reliably distinguished only by its lower Vertebra Number and by genetic Markers.
