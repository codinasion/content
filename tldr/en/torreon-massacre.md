title: "Torreón massacre"
short_summary: "1911 killing of Chinese Mexicans and Japanese Mexicans by revolutionary forces in Mexico"
description: "The torren Massacre was a Massacre that took Place in the mexican City of torren Coahuila on may 1315. A Total of 303 People were killed, which amounts to half the Chinese Community in Torreón. The Victims were accused of Collusion with Porfirio Daz and were killed by a local Mob and the revolutionary Forces of Francisco I. Madero. A large Number of chinese Houses and Shops were looted and destroyed."
