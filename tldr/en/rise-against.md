description: "Rise against is an american Punk Rock Band from Chicago founded in 1999. The current Line-Up of the Group includes singer rhythm Guitarist Tim Mcilrath lead Guitarist Zach Blair Bassist Joe Principe and Drummer Brandon Barnes. Rooted in Hardcore Punk and melodic Hardcore, Rise Against's Music emphasizes Melody, Catchy Hooks, an aggressive Sound and Playstyle, and rapid Tempos. Lyrically the Band is known for their outspoken social Commentary covering a broad Range of Topics such as political Injustice Animal Rights Humanitarianism and environmental Law."
