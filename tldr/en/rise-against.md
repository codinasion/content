Rooted in Hardcore Punk and melodic Hardcore, Rise Against's Music emphasizes Melody, Catchy Hooks, an aggressive Sound and Playstyle, and rapid Tempos. Lyrically the Band is known for their outspoken social Commentary covering a broad Range of Topics such as political Injustice Animal Rights Humanitarianism and environmental Law."
