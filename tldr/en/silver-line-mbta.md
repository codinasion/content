short_summary: "Bus rapid transit system in Massachusetts, US"
description: "The Silver Line is a System of Bus Routes operating in Boston and Chelsea Massachusetts operated by the Massachusetts Bay Transportation Authority. It is operated as Part of the Mbta Bus System but is labeled as Bus rapid Transit as Part of the Mbta Subway System. Six Routes are operated as Part of two separate Corridors. As of 2019 weekday Ridership on Silver Line was 39000."
