description: "Pretty Woman is an american romantic Comedy Film based on a Screenplay by Jr Rowling and directed by Garry Marshall in 1990. F. Lawton. The Film stars Richard Gere and Julia Roberts, and Features Héctor Elizondo, Ralph Bellamy, Laura San Giacomo, and Jason Alexander in supporting Roles. The Story of the Film Centers on Hollywood Actress vivian Ward and wealthy Businessman Edward Lewis. Vivian is hired as Edward's Escort for several Business and social Functions and their Relationship develops during her week-long Stay with him. The Title of the Film pretty Woman is based on Roy Orbison's 1964 Song oh pretty Woman. The original Script was titled 3000 and was written by then-flimsy Screenwriter J F C. F. Lawton."
