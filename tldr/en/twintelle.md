description: "Twintelle is a Character in the 2017 Video Game Arms by Nintendo for the Nintendo Switch. Unlike other playable Characters in Arms, who fight using their Arms, Twintelle Fights using her Hair. She is shown as a femme Fatale and a Celebrity. The Design Process for her was more complicated than the Others in Arms as Producer Kosuke yabuki wanted to consult Nintendo's american and european Branches in an Effort to represent People of different Backgrounds and national Origins."
