description: "In J. R. R. Tolkien's high Fantasy the Lord of the Rings Harad is the vast Land South of Gondor and Mordor. Its main Port is umbar the Base of the Corsairs of Umbar whose Ships serve as the Fleet of dark Lord Sauron. Its People are the dark-skinned Haradrim or Southrons ; their Warriors wear Scarlet and Gold, and are armed with Swords and Round Shields ; some ride gigantic Elephants called Mûmakil."
