description: "Pieces of a Woman is a 2020 Drama Film directed by Kornél Mundruczó, from a Screenplay by Kata Wéber. The Film Stars Vanessa Kirby, Shia Labeouf, Molly Parker, Sarah Snook, Iliza Shlesinger, Benny Safdie, Jimmie Fails, and Ellen Burstyn as the Family and Associates of Martha involved in her traumatic Childbirth, Baby Loss, and a subsequent Court Case against the Midwife, Eva, whom Martha's Mother Elizabeth blames for the Baby's Death. Martin Scorsese and Sam Levinson served as executive producers, and the film was scored by Howard Shore."
