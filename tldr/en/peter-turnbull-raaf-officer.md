title: "Peter Turnbull (RAAF officer)"
short_summary: "Australian World War II flying ace"
description: "Peter St George bruce Turnbull Dfc was an australian Fighter Ace during World War Ii who was credited with twelve aerial Victories. He was born in armidale new south Wales but was an electrician before joining the Royal australian Air Force in january 1939. After pilot training he was posted to No. 3 Squadron, which departed for Action in the Middle East in July 1940."
