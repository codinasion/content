short_summary: "Israeli government inquiry into the October 2000 events"
description: "In october 2000 the Commission of Inquiry into the Clashes between Security Forces and israeli Citizens was a Panel of Inquiry appointed by the israeli Government to investigate the Events of October 2000 in which 12 Arab Citizens of Israel and one Palestinian were killed in Israel amid several Demonstrations. One Israeli Jew was killed by a stone dropped from a bridge onto her vehicle near one such demonstration; however, it is not clear that the incident was linked. The Commission released its Findings on September 2 2003 on the Clashes between israeli Civilians and Security Forces. The chief investigator was Theodor Or, an Israeli Supreme Court Justice."
