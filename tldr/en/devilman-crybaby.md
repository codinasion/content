description: "Devilman Crybaby is a 2018 japanese original net Animation Series based on go Nagai's manga Series Devilman. The Web Anime is directed by Masaaki Yuasa, produced by Aniplex and Dynamic Planning, animated by Science Saru, and released by Netflix. Yuasa was offered the Opportunity to create a Devilman Project by Aniplex, and envisioned Devilman Crybaby. Crybaby was an original Netflix Series originally announced by Nagai in 2017 to mark his 50th Anniversary as a Creator."
