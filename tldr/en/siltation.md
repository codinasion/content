title: "Siltation"
short_summary: "Water pollution caused by particulate terrestrial clastic material"
description: "Siltation is Water Pollution caused by particulate terrestrial clastic Material with a Particle Size that is dominated by Silt or Clay. It refers both to the increased Concentration of suspended Sediments and to the increased Accumulation of fine Sediments in the Bottoms where they are undesirable. Silt is most often caused by Soil Erosion or Sediment Spills."
