description: "Ye is the eighth Studio Album by the american Rapper Kanye West. It was released by good Music on June 1 2018 and distributed by def Jam Recordings. Following Controversy surrounding an Interview with Tmz, West re-recorded all the Work on the Album, with him completing it over the Course of just two Weeks at West Lake Ranch in Jackson Hole, Wyoming. Guest Vocals from Partynextdoor, Ty Dolla Sign, Kid Cudi, Charlie Wilson, Jeremih, and 070 Shake, among Others, are included."
