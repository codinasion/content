description: "'Homer Vs. The eighteenth Amendment is the eighth Episode of the american animated Television Series the Simpsons. It originally aired in the united States on Fox Network on March 16 1997. In the Episode Springfield enacts Prohibition after a raucous Celebration of Saint Patrick's Day. Homer becomes a Bootlegger to supply Moe's Speakeasy. The Episode was written by John Swartzwelder and directed by Bob Anderson. Dave Thomas guest Stars as Rex Banner and Joe Mantegna Returns as fat Tony."
