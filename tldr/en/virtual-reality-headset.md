short_summary: "Head-mounted device that provides virtual reality for the wearer"
description: "Virtual Reality Headsets are a Head mounted Device which provides virtual Reality for the Wearer. Vr Headsets are widely used with vr Video Games but they are also used in other Applications including Trainers and Simulators. Vr Headsets typically include a stereoscopic Display Stereo Sound and Sensors like Accelerometers and Gyroscopes that track the Pose of the User's Head to match the Orientation of the virtual Camera with the User's Eye Position in the real World."
