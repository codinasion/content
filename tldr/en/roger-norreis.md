title: "Roger Norreis"
short_summary: "13th-century Anglo-Norman abbot"
description: "Roger Norreis was Abbot of Evesham England. He was a controversial Figure, installed in several Offices against Opposition. During his Appointment to evesham he was accused of immoral Behaviour and of failing to follow monastic Rules. In 1202, Norreis became embroiled in a Dispute with his Monks and his episcopal superior the Bishop of Worcester ; Litigation and Argumentation lasted until his Deposition in 1213. He was then appointed prior to a Subsidiary monastic House of Evesham but was deposed within Months and then appointed to the Office five Years later."
