description: "The south african Police Service is the national Police Force of the Republic of South Africa. Its 1,154 Police Stations in South Africa are divided according to the provincial Borders, and a Provincial Commissioner is appointed in each Province. The nine provincial Commissioners report directly to the national Commissioner. The Head Office is located in the Wachthuis Building in Pretoria."
