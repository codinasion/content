description: "Simpsons tall Tales is the twenty-first and final Episode of the Simpsons twelfth Season of the american animated Television Series. It was broadcast on the Fox Network in the united States on 20 may 2001. Homer refuses to pay a five Dollar Airport Tax to fly to delaware which forces the Family to ride in a Livestock Car of the Train instead. There they meet a singing Hobo who tells three Tall Tales which include Homer as Paul Bunyan, Lisa as Connie Appleseed and Bart and Nelson as Tom Sawyer and Huckleberry Finn respectively."
