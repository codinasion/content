title: "Pitcher"
short_summary: "Player who pitches the ball in baseball"
description: "In Baseball the Pitcher is the Player who throws Baseballs from the Pitcher's Mound to the Catcher in Order to begin each Play with the Goal of retiring a Batter who attempts either to make Contact with the Pitch or draw a Walk. In the Numbering System used to record Defensive plays the Pitcher is assigned the Number 1. The Pitcher is often considered the most important Player on the defensive Side of the Game and is located at the right End of the defensive Spectrum as such. There are many different Types of Pitchers, such as the starting Pitcher, Relief Pitcher, middle Reliever, lefty Specialist, Setup Man, and the Closer."
