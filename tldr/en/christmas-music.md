short_summary: "Music associated with Christmas"
description: "Christmas Music generally includes a Variety of Genres of Music often performed or heard around the Christmas Season. Music associated with Christmas may be purely instrumental or in the Case of Carols may use Lyrics about Nativity of Jesus Christ Traditions such as Gift-Giving and merrymaking cultural Figures such as santa Claus or other Topics. Many Songs have a simply Winter or seasonal Theme or have been adopted into the Canon for other Reasons."
