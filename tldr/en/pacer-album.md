title: "Pacer (album)"
short_summary: "1995 studio album by The Amps"
description: "Pacer is the only Album by the Amps under the Direction of Kim Deal. It was released in October 1995. The Album was recorded as a Side Project for kim Deal's Group the Breeders. She recruited new Musicians and naming the Group the Amps, recorded Pacer at several Studios in the Us and Ireland, with different Engineers, including Steve Albini, Bryce Goggin, and John Agnello."
