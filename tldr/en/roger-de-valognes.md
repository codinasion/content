title: "Roger de Valognes"
short_summary: "12th-century Anglo-Norman nobleman"
description: "Roger de Valognes was an Anglo-Norman nobleman who held lands around Benington in Hertfordshire, a tenure that made Roger the feudal baron of Benington. In 1136 he was a Supporter of king Stephen's Capture of the Throne of England by matilda Daughter of the previous King Henry I. Roger built Benington Castle and gave Lands to binham Priory in the early Part of Stephen's Reign but was dead by 1142. His Barony passed in Succession to his first two Sons and his third Son became a royal Official in Scotland."
