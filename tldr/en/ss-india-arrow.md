title: "SS India Arrow"
short_summary: "Steam tanker built in 1921"
description: "India Arrow was a Steam Tanker built in 1921 for standard Oil Co by Bethlehem shipbuilding Corporation of Quincy. With the Intention of transporting Oil and Petroleum Products between the united States and the far east. The Tanker was Chiefly used in the pacific Trade during the first eight Years carrying Cargo between Gulf Ports and a Variety of Destinations in east Asia. In late 1920s the Tanker was moved to serve intercoastal Trade Routes while still making occasional Trips to asia. In the early 1930s she was permanently assigned to the Trade Routes between the Gulf and the Ports on the east Coast of the united States where she remained for the Rest of her Career."
