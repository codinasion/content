short_summary: "Protected cruiser of the French Navy"
description: "D'Assas was the leading Ship of her Class of protected Cruisers built in the 1890s for the french Navy. The Cruisers of the d'assas Class were ordered as Part of a Construction Program aimed at strengthening the Fleet's Cruiser Force. At the Time France was concerned about the growing naval Threat of the italian and german Fleets and the new Cruisers were designed for Use with the main Fleet and overseas in the french Colonial Empire. D'Assas was armed with a main Battery of six 164mm Guns protected by an Armor Deck that was 70 to 80mm Thick and was capable of steaming at a Speed of 20 Knots."
