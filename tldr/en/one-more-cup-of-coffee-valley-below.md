description: "One more Cup of Coffee is a Song by american Singer Bob Dylan which was released as the fourth Track on his seventeenth Studio Album Desire. The Song was written by Dylan and produced by Don Devito. The album Version of one more Cup of Coffee was recorded on July 30 1975 and released in january 1976 on Desire. Dylan said the Song was inspired by his Visit to the Romani Celebration at saintes-maries-de-la-mer in France on his 34th Birthday."
