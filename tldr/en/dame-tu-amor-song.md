title: "Dame Tu Amor (song)"
short_summary: "Song by Selena Quintanilla-Pérez"
description: "Dame Tu Amor is a Song recorded by american Singer Selena for her second Lp Record Alpha. Lyrically the Narrator addresses her Desire to give her his Love as she is longing for him to do so with a Kiss that ensures Admiration for her. The Themes explored in the Song suggest seeking Love. Dame Tu Amor is a Ranchera Ballad with Cumbia Influences. It was composed by Richard Brooks, Ricky Vela, and Selena's Father and Manager, Abraham Quintanilla, Jr. Critics praised the Song for its Instrumentation and lyrical Content and has since been released in several Compilation Albums following Selena's Death in 1995. 'Dame Tu Amor' peaked at Number 31 on the Us Billboard Hot Ringtones Chart in 2006."
