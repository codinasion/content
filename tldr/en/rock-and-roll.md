short_summary: "Genre of popular music"
description: "Rock and Roll is a Genre of popular Music that developed in the united States during the late 1940S and early 1950S. It originated from african-american Music such as Jazz, Rhythm and Blues, Boogie-Woogie, electric Blues, Gospel, Jump Blues, as well as Country Music. While Rock and Roll's formative Elements can be heard in Blues Records from the 1920s and Country Records from the 1930s the Genre did not acquire its Name until 1954."
