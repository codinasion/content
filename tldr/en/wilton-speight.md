description: "Robert Wilton Speight Iii is a former american Football Quarterback. He played College Football for the Wolverines of Michigan and Ucla. In high School, he was a 2014 under Armour All-America Game Selection and subsequently committed to play in College for Michigan. Speight redshirted in 2014 and was a backup Quarterback in 2015. As a Redshirt Sophomore he started 12 Games in 2016 and earned third-team all-big Ten Honors. Speight started four Games in 2017 before suffering a season-ending Injury. In April 2018, Speight transferred to UCLA for his final season of eligibility."
