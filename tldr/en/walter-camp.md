title: "Walter Camp"
short_summary: "American football player and coach (1859–1925)"
description: "Walter Chauncey Camp was an american College Football Player and Coach, and Sports Writer known as the 'Father of American Football'. Among a long List of Inventions he created the Line of the Sport of Scrimmage and the System of Downs. With John Heisman, Amos Alonzo Stagg, Pop Warner, Fielding H. Yost, and George Halas, Camp was one of the most accomplished Persons in the early History of american Football. He attended Yale College where he played and coached College Football. The yale Teams of Camp 1888 1891 and 1892 have been recognized as national Champions. Camp was inducted as a Coach into the College Football Hall of Fame during 1951."
