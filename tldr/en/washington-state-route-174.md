description: "State Route 174 is a 40. 66-mile State Highway that traverses douglas grant and lincoln Counties in Washington. Sr174 begins at a Junction with sr 17 in Leahy and continues east to grand Coulee near the grand Coulee Dam to intersect Sr 155. The Roadway goes from grand Coulee to the Southeast to end at sr 21 northwest of Wilbur."
