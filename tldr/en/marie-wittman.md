description: "Marie Blanche Wittman was a french Woman known as one of the Patients with Hysteria of Jean-Martin Charcot. She was institutionalized in 1877 in La Salptrire and was treated by Charcot until his Death in 1893. She later became a Radiology Assistant at the Hospital and due to radiation Poisoning had to have her Arms Amputation."
