description: "Roger Allan Crozier was a canadian professional Ice Hockey Goaltender who played in the national Hockey League for the Buffalo sabres detroit red Wings and the Washington Capitals for fourteen Seasons. During his Career, Crozier was named to the Nhl First All-Star Team once, was a Calder Memorial Trophy Winner, and was the first Player to win the Conn Smythe Trophy while playing for the Losing Team in the Stanley Cup Finals. He was the last Goaltender in the Nhl to start all his Team's Games in the regular Season in 196465."
