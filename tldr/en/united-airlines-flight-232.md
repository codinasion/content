title: "United Airlines Flight 232"
short_summary: "1989 aviation accident"
description: "United Airlines Flight 232 was a regular united Airlines Flight from stapleton international Airport in Denver to o'hare international Airport in Chicago and from there on to philadelphia international Airport. The dc-10 serving the Flight crashed on July 19 1989 at sioux gateway Airport in sioux City Iowa after suffering a catastrophic Failure of its tail-mounted Engine due to an unnoticed Manufacturing Defect in the Engine's Fan Disk which resulted in the Loss of many Flight Controls. Of the 296 Passengers and Crew on Board 112 died while 184 survived. 13 Passengers were uninjured. It was the deadliest Aircraft Accident in united Airlines History."
