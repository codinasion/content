short_summary: "Category 2 Pacific hurricane in 1986"
description: "Hurricane Paine was the 16th tropical Storm and eighth Hurricane of the relatively active Pacific Hurricane Season in 1986 Water from the System contributed to one of the most significant flooding Events in oklahoma History. Paine formed on September 28 off the southeast coast of Mexico. It moved around a Ridge later turning to the North and brushing the Baja California Peninsula. By then Paine had reached peak Winds of 100mph but it weakened slightly before hitting Sonora in Mexico. The remnant Moisture combined with a cold Front to produce heavy Rainfall in south central united States."
