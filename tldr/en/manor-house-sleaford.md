short_summary: "Set of connected buildings located on Northgate in Sleaford, Lincolnshire, England"
description: "Manor House is a Set of connected Buildings on Northgate in the english City of Sleaford Lincolnshire. Parts of the Manor House Date from the 16th Century but were extended with the Addition of the georgian Rhodes House and later gothic-revival Work. It was a private Residence until the 20th Century and is now divided into commercial and residential Properties. The House was owned by a Number of Families and Individuals including local Banker and Businessman Benjamin Handley and Sophia Peacock whose Nephews Cecil and frank Rhodes spent their Summers as."
