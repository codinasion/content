title: "See You Again (Miley Cyrus song)"
short_summary: "2007 single by Miley Cyrus"
description: "'see you again' is the Debut single by american Singer Miley Cyrus as the Lead single from Meet Miley Cyrus, the second Soundtrack Album from the Disney Channel original Series Hannah Montana and the Debut Studio Album of Cyrus. It was written by Cyrus with Producer Antonina Armato and Tim James. It was later remixed by Armato and James as the second single from Cyrus' second Studio Album Breakout. Musically, the Track is a Dance-Rock Song that contains Influences from various musical Genres, including electronic Music. Lyrically it speaks about Teenage Romance. The original Version of the Song was only released as a single in the Us, Canada, Australia and New Zealand. The Song was remixed internationally by the Rock Mafia."
