title: "Snakefly"
short_summary: "Order of insects"
description: "Snakeflies are a Group of Predatory Insects which comprise the Order of Raphidioptera with two extant Families raphidiidae and inocelliidae consisting of approximately 260 Species. In the Past, the Group had a much wider Distribution than it does now ; Snakeflies are found in temperate Regions worldwide but are absent from the Tropics and the Southern Hemisphere. The first obvious Representative of the Group was seen during the early Jurassic. They are a relict Group reaching their Apex of Diversity during the cretaceous before undergoing substantial Decline."
