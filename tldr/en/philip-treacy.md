short_summary: "Irish-born haute couture milliner"
description: "Philip Anthony Treacy is an irish Haute Couture Milliner or Hat Designer who has been mostly based in London for his Career and who was described as perhaps the greatest Living Milliner in the World. In 2000 Treacy became the first Milliner to be invited to exhibit at the haute Couture Paris Shows in 80 Years. He has won british Accessory Designer of the Year at the british Fashion Awards five Times, and has received public Honours in both Britain and Ireland. His Designs have been displayed in the metropolitan Museum of Art and the Victoria and Albert Museum."
