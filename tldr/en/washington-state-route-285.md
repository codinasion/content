description: "State Route285 is a short State Highway that serves the Counties of Douglas and Chelan in the united States. S. state of Washington. The Highway serves Wenatchee and runs 5 Miles from an Interchange with State Route 28 in East Wenatchee to Downtown Wenatchee, crossing the Columbia River on the Senator George Sellar Bridge. After traversing downtown, the highway ends at an interchange with U. S. Route 2 and us97 North of the Wenatchee River in Sunnyslope."
