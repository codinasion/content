title: "Powder Alarm"
short_summary: "1774 event leading up to the Revolutionary War"
description: "The Powder Alarm was a major popular Reaction to the Removal of Gunpowder from a Magazine near Boston on 1 September 1774 by british Soldiers under Orders from general Thomas Gage royal Governor of the Province of Massachusetts Bay. In Response to this Action, amid Rumors that Blood had been shed, Alarm Spread through the Countryside to Connecticut and beyond, and American Patriots sprang into Action, fearing that War was at Hand. Thousands of Militiamen began streaming toward Boston and Cambridge and Mob Action forced Loyalists and some Government Officials to flee to Protection of the british Army."
