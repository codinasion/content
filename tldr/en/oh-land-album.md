short_summary: "2011 studio album by Oh Land"
description: "Oh Land is the second Studio Album by danish Singer Oh Land, released on 14 March 2011 by Fake Diamond Records, Epic Records, and a: Larm Music. Prior to moving to brooklyn in 2010 she met with an Epic Records representative at a sxsw Event and was eventually signed to the Label in 2009. She began working on the Album, collaborating with Musicians like Jimmy Harry, Dan Carey, and Lester Mendez. A Digital extended Play titled Oh Land Ep was released in October 2010, featuring five Songs that would later appear on the Parent Album."
