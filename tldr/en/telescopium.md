description: "Telescopium is a minor Constellation in the southern Celestial hemisphere one of Twelve named by french astronomer nicolas-louis de lacaille in 18th Century and one of several representing scientific Instruments. Its Name is a latinized Form of the Greek Word for Telescope. Telescopium was later considerably reduced in Size by francis baily and benjamin Gould."
