short_summary: "United States historic place"
description: "The Bacon Academy, nicknamed Old Bacon Academy, was the original Bacon Academy. The Old Bacon Academy was built in 1803 and is located at 84 Main Street, Colchester, Connecticut. The main Structure is a three-story flemish Brick Structure with federal Style Details 70 Feet long by 34 Feet wide. Noted for its Plain utilitarian Floor Plan consisting of two Rooms off a central Hall and an Stairway the Interior has seen a few Renovations throughout its History. The Day hall a contributing Property purchased in 1929 by the Trustees of the Bacon Academy is a Church Hall that was used for the high School until 1962."
