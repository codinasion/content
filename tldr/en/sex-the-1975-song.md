short_summary: "2013 single by the 1975"
description: "Sex is a Song by the 1975 english Band from their Second extended Play of the same Name and their eponymous Debut Studio Album. The Song was written by Band Members ross macdonald matty Healy Adam Hann and George Daniel. The Band co-produced the Ep Version of the Song with Michael and Robert Coles, while Mike Crossey provided additional Production. The album Version produced by Crossey and the Band was released as the Lead single by Dirty Hit Polydor Records and Vagrant Records on August 23 2013. Written at the Age of 19 Healy said the Song is a love Letter to prudish Teenagers."
