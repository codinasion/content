title: "SmarTrip"
short_summary: "Contactless transit card system used in the Washington, D.C. metro area"
description: "SmarTrip is a contactless stored-value smart card payment system managed by the Washington Metropolitan Area Transit Authority . Maryland Transit Administration uses a compatible Payment System called Charmcard. A Reciprocity Agreement between the Mta and Wmata Allows either Card to be used for Travel on any of the participating Transit Systems in the baltimore-washington metropolitan Area. Unlike traditional paper farecards or bus passes, SmarTrip/CharmCard is designed to be permanent and reloadable; the term 'SmarTrip' may refer to both payment systems unless otherwise noted."
