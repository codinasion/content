short_summary: "1978 single by Joe Jackson"
description: "Is she really going out with him? This Song was written by the british musician Joe Jackson. It was released as his Debut single in october 1978 and later included on Jackson's Debut album look sharp! The Track was one of the first Jackson recorded with his new Backing Band, a Band he would perform with for his first three Albums. Written as a humorous Commentary on Women dating unattractive Men the Song contains a prominent Bass Line and a Chorus praised as memorable by Critics. Jackson has since stated that the Song's Reputation for being angry was untrue."
