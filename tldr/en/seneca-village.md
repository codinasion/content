title: "Seneca Village"
short_summary: "Former settlement in Manhattan, New York"
description: "Seneca Village was a 19th-century Settlement of mostly african american Landowners in the manhattan Borough of new York City inside what would eventually become the present-day central Park. The Settlement was located near the current Upper West Side Neighborhood, approximately bounded by Central Park West and the Axes of 82nd Street, 89th Street, and Seventh Avenue, had they been constructed through the Park."
