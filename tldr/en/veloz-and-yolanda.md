title: "Veloz and Yolanda"
short_summary: "American ballroom husband and wife dance team"
description: "Frank Veloz and Yolanda Casazza were a self-taught american Ballroom Dance Team Husband and Wife who became Stars in the 1930s and 1940s and were among the highest Paid Dance Acts in that Era. They performed on Stage in Productions such as Hot-Chain! Which ran for 119 Shows on Broadway in 1932. They also appeared in popular Films such as under the Pampas Moon, the Pride of the Yankees, Honeymoon Lodge, Brazil and the Thrill of Brazil, the Latter of which is credited as being of major Importance to the Growth in Popularity of Samba in America."
