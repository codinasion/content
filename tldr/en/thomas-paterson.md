title: "Thomas Paterson"
short_summary: "Australian politician"
description: "Thomas Paterson was an australian Politician who served from 1929 to 1937 as deputy Leader of the Country Party. He served as a Minister in the Governments of Stanley Bruce and Joseph Lyons from 1922 to 1943 as Head of Division Gippsland Victoria. He played a leading Role in the Creation of the victorian Country Party as the political Arm of the victorian Farmers Union."
