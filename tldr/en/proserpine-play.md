title: "Proserpine (play)"
short_summary: "1832 play by Mary Shelley and Percy Bysshe Shelley"
description: "Proserpine is a verse Drama written for Children by the english romantic Writer mary Shelley and her Husband Percy Bysshe Shelley. Mary wrote the blank verse Drama and Percy contributed two lyric Poems. Composed in 1820 while the Shelleys were living in Italy it is often considered a Partner of the Shelleys' Midas play. Proserpine was published in the london periodical the Winter Wreath in 1832. Whether the Drama was ever intended to be staged is a controversial Question among Scholars."
