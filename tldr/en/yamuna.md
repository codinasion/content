short_summary: "River in India"
description: "The Yamuna is the second largest River Tributary of the Ganges by Discharge and the longest Tributary in India. Originating from the Yamunotri Glacier at a Height of 4500 Metres on the southwest Slopes of bandarpunch Peaks of the lower Himalaya of Uttarakhand it travels 1376 Km and has a Drainage System of 366223 square Kilometres 40. 2 % of the entire Ganges Basin. It merges with the Ganges at triveni Sangam Prayagraj which is the Site of the Kumbh mela a Hindu Festival which is held every 12 Years."
