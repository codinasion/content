description: "Heidi Blair Pratt is an american Reality Television Personality, Model, Singer and Actress. After being cast in the Mtv Reality Series the Hills in 2006 Montag came to prominence. The Show chronicled the personal and professional Lives of lauren Conrad Montag and her Friends whitney Port and Audrina Patridge. During its Production, she briefly attended the Fashion Institute of Design & Merchandising and was “ employed ” by Event Planning Company Bolthouse Productions. As the Series progressed, Montag began dating fellow Cast Member Spencer Pratt, which ultimately ended her Friendship with Conrad. Their ensuing Feud became the central Focus of the Series, and was carried through each subsequent Season."
