short_summary: "German naval raid of World War II"
description: "Operation Sportpalast also known as Operation Nordmeer was a german naval Raid between March 6 and 13 1942 as they passed through the norwegian Sea against two allied arctic Convoys of World War Ii. It was conducted by the Battleship Tirpitz, three Destroyers and eight Submarines. The german Ships were unable to locate either of the Convoys but sank an independently sailing Merchant Vessel. However the Allies attempted to intercept german Forces without Success."
