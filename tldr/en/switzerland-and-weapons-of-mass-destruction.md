title: "Switzerland and weapons of mass destruction"
short_summary: "Planned Swiss weapons of mass destruction"
description: "During the cold War Switzerland made detailed Plans to acquire and test nuclear Weapons. Less than two Weeks after the Hiroshima and nagasaki nuclear Bombings the swiss Government started studying the Possibility of building nuclear Weapons and continued its military nuclear Program for 43 Years until 1988. It has since signed and ratified the Treaty on the Non-Proliferation of nuclear Weapons. Switzerland never owned biological Weapons but had a Program of the swiss Army high Command to develop and test Chemical Weapons."
