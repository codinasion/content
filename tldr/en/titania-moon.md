title: "Titania (moon)"
short_summary: "Largest moon of Uranus"
description: "Titania, also designated Uranus Iii, is the largest of the Moons of Uranus and the eighth largest Moon in the solar System at a Diameter of 1,578 Kilometres, with a Surface Area comparable to that of Australia. Discovered in 1787 by William Herschel it is named after the Queen of Fairies in Shakespeare's a Midsummer Night's Dream. Its orbit lies inside Uranus' magnetosphere."
