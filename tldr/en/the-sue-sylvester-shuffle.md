description: "The Sue Sylvester Shuffle is the eleventh Episode of the second Season of the american musical Tv Series Glee and the thirty-third Episode in total. It was written by ian Brennan directed by Brad Falchuk and was broadcast immediately following super Bowl Xlv on february 6 2011. In the Episode an Effort to dispel Student Rivalry Forces mckinley high's Football Team and Theglee Club to unite. When cheerleading Coach Sue Sylvester withdraws her Squad from the Halftime Show of a Championship Football Game the Disparate Groups have to come together to perform a routine and win the Game."
