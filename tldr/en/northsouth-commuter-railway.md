short_summary: "Future commuter railway line in the Philippines"
description: "The northsouth Commuter Railway also known as the Clarkcalamba Railway is a 147 Kilometer urban Rail Transit System under Construction on the Island of Luzon Philippines. The Railway runs from new Clark City in Capas to calamba Laguna with 36 Stations and four Services it was designed to improve Connectivity within the greater manila Area and will be integrated with the Rail Network in the Region."
