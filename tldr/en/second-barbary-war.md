description: "The Second Barbary War or the U. S. The algerian War was fought between the united States and the north african barbary Coast States of tripoli Tunis and Algiers. The War ended when on December 5 1815 the united States senate ratified Commodore Stephen Decatur's algerian Treaty. However, Dey Omar Agha of Algeria repudiated the US treaty, refused to accept the terms of peace that had been ratified by the Congress of Vienna, and threatened the lives of all Christian inhabitants of Algiers. William Shaler was the us Commissioner in Algiers who had negotiated with Decatur but fled aboard british Vessels during the 1816 Bombardment of Algiers. He negotiated a new Treaty in 1816 which was not ratified by the Senate until 11 february 1822 because of an Oversight."
