description: "'Together When. . . Is a Song by japanese recording artist Ayumi Hamasaki. It was released by Avex Trax on December 5, 2007, and Rhythm Republic on April 9, 2008 as a double a-side with one of Hamasaki's Tracks 'Game' as the third and final single from her ninth Studio Album, Guilty. It was released as Hamasaki's first digital single in Japan, and was Avex Trax's first single to be distributed as a digital Download only. The Track was written by Hamasaki herself while the Production was handled by longtime Collaborator Max Matsuura. Musically, 'Together When. . . 'is a Power Ballad. The lyrical Content is about the Completion and Separation of a Relationship between Lovers."
