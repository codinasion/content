short_summary: "Telephone booth in Ōtsuchi, Japan"
description: "Wind Phone is an unconnected Telephone Booth in tsuchi Iwate Prefecture Japan where Visitors can hold one-way Conversations with deceased loved Ones. Initially created in 2010 by Garden Designer itaru Sasaki to help him cope with his Cousin's Death it was opened to the public the following Year after the 2011 thoku Earthquake and Tsunami killed over 15000 People in the thoku Region. The Wind Phone has since received more than 30000 Users. A Number of Replicas have been constructed around the World and it has served as Inspiration for many Novels and Films."
