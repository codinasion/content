description: "The 2019 Tour de France was the 106th Edition of the Tour de France one of Cycling's three grand Tours. The 3,365. The 8km Race Consisting of 21 Stages was started on 6 July in brussels belgian Capital then moved throughout France and concluded on 28 July at the Champs lys in Paris. 176 Riders from 22 Teams participated in the Race. The general Classification Overall was won for the first Time by a latin american Rider egan Bernal of Team Ineos. His Teammate and 2018 Tour Winner Geraint Thomas finished second while Steven Kruijswijk came in third."
