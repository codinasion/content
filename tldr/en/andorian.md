description: "Andorians are a fictional Race of humanoid Extraterrestrials in the american Science Fiction Franchise Star Trek. They were created by writer D. C. Fontana. Within the Star Trek narrative, they are native to the blue Icy Class M Moon, Andoria. Distinctive Traits of Andorians include their blue/green Skin, a Pair of cranial Antennae, and white Hair."
