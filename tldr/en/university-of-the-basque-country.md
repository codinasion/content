short_summary: "Public university in the Basque Autonomous Community of Spain"
description: "The University of the basque Country is a public spanish University of the autonomous Basque - Country. Heir of the University of Bilbao, initially it was made up of the Faculty of Economic and Business Sciences of Sarriko, Medicine and Sciences. Following the General Law of Education, the Nautical School, the School of Business Studies of Bilbao and the Technical Schools of Engineers joined in, until it grew into the Complex of Thirty Centers that compose it presently."
