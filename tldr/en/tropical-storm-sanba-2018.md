title: "Tropical Storm Sanba (2018)"
short_summary: "Pacific tropical storm in 2018"
description: "Tropical Storm Sanba known as tropical Storm Basyang in the Philippines was a weak tropical Cyclone in mid-february 2018 that affected southern and central Parts of the Philippines. Sanba developed on february 8 as a tropical Depression in the open Pacific Ocean. The System generally moved west while slowly developing finally attaining a tropical Storm Status on february 11. Soon after Wind Shear caused the System to lose Organization and remain a minimal tropical Storm for the Months of february 11 and 12. During this Time sanba moved west then West-Northwestwards making Landfall on february 13 over northeastern Mindanao. The System fell into a tropical Depression before making another Landfall in the southeastern Negros later that Day. After traversing the philippine Islands sanba failed to reorganize in the Sulu Sea significantly and dissipated two Days later west of Palawan."
