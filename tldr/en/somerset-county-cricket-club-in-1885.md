title: "Somerset County Cricket Club in 1885"
short_summary: "Events of the 1885 cricket season"
description: "The 1885 Season was an Annus Horribilis for Somerset County Cricket Club. They played six first-class Cricket Matches under ted Sainsbury who had taken over from stephen Newton for the previous three Seasons. Somerset's only Win of the Season came in their first Match ; all the other were lost. Two of the Losses in the away Game against Gloucestershire and surrey Constitute among the County's ten Heaviest Defeats. Somerset were unable to Field a full Team for their fourth Match which coupled with their poor Results and Lack of Fixtures resulted in the Club being stripped of its first Class Status at the End of the Season."
