short_summary: "Evil character in Tolkien's fiction"
description: "In J. R. R. Tolkien's Fantasy the Lord of the Rings, Old Man Willow is a malign Tree-Spirit of great Age in Tom Bombadil's Old Forest, appearing physically as a large willow Tree beside the River Withywindle, but spreading his Influence throughout the Forest. He is the first hostile Character encountered by the Hobbits after leaving the Shire."
