description: "State Route28 is a State Highway in the united States. S. state of Washington. It passes through the central Region of the State by crossing Douglas Grant and Lincoln Counties. The highway begins at an intersection with U. S. Route 2 and us 97 near East Wenatchee and Travels east through Quincy, Ephrata, and Odessa before terminating at Us 2 in Davenport. The Route follows the Columbia River and the Columbia River Subdivision of the Bnsf Railway through largely rural Area between Wenatchee and Davenport."
