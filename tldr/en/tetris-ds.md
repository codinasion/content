description: "Tetris Ds is a puzzle Game developed by Nintendo and published by the Company. It was released for the Nintendo DS on March 20, 2006, in North America, April 13, 2006, in Australia, April 21, 2006, in Europe, and April 27, 2006, in Japan. An Installment of the Tetris franchise the Game supports up to ten Players locally and online Multiplayer of up to four Players using nintendo wi-fi Connection prior to its Discontinuation."
