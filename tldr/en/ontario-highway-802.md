description: "Tertiary Highway 802, commonly referred to as Highway 802, is a provincially maintained tertiary Road in the canadian Province of Ontario, located in Thunder Bay District. The 13. The 7-kilometre Route Branches from Highway 11 both north and south to connect with the Community of Kashabowie and the Ghost Town of Burchell Lake. The Highway was established in 1962 at the Peak of Operations of the Mine Burchell lake. Although the Mine and Town were subsequently abandoned in 1966 the Highway remains under provincial Jurisdiction. The Section which travels concurrently with Highway 11 Forms Part of the trans-canada Highway."
