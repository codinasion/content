description: "The saverne Tunnel also known as the ernolsheim-ls-saverne Tunnel is a 4-kilometer high-speed Rail Tunnel in western bas-rhin France. It carries the Lgv est Line of France's tgv High-Speed Rail Network through the narrowest Part of the Vosges mountain Range under mont saint-michel and adjacent to the saverne Pass. The Tunnel consists of two Bores each containing a Rail Track connected by Passageways every 500 Metres. The LGV Est crosses the 270 m Haspelbaechel viaduct near the western end of the tunnel. Between november 2011 and february 2013 the Tunnel was excavated by a Tunnel Borer. Civil engineering work on the tunnel ended in April 2014 and it opened with the rest of the second phase of the LGV Est on 3 July 2016. The Cost of the Tunnel totalled about 200million."
