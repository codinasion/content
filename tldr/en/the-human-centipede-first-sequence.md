description: "The human Centipede is a dutch independent Horror Film written directed and co-produced by tom six in 2009. The Film concerns a deranged german Surgeon who kidnaps three Tourists and conjoins them surgically, Mouth to anus, forming the eponymous 'human Centipede'. It stars Dieter Laser as Josef Heiter, the Creator of the Centipede ; and Ashley C. Williams, Ashlynn Yennie, and Akihiro Kitamura as Heiter's Victims."
