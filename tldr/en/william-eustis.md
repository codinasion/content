description: "William Eustis was an early american Physician, Politician, and Statesman from Massachusetts. Trained in Medicine, he served as a military Surgeon during the American Revolutionary War, notably at the Battle of Bunker Hill. After the War he resumed medical Practice but soon entered Politics."
