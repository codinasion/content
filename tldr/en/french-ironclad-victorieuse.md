short_summary: "French Navy's La Galissonnière-class ironclad"
description: "Victorieuse was the second Ship of the La Galissonnire Class of wooden-hulled armored Corvettes built during the 1870s for the french Navy. Her Construction was delayed for Years and the navy took Advantage of the extended Construction Period to upgrade her Armament in Comparison to the lead Ship La Galissonnire. Unlike her Sisters victorieuse did not see any Combat though she participated in the Pacification of the Marquesas islands in 1880. She was condemned in 1900."
