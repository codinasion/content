title: "SMS Rheinland"
short_summary: "Nassau-class battleship of the German Imperial Navy"
description: "Sms Rheinland was one of four Battleships of the Nassau Class which were the first Dreadnoughts built for the german imperial Navy. In an unusual hexagonal Arrangement Rheinland mounted twelve 28 Cm main Guns in six twin Turrets. The navy built Rheinland and her sister Ships in Response to the revolutionary british Hmsdreadnought launched in 1906. Rheinland was laid down in June 1907 launched in October the following Year and commissioned in April 1910."
