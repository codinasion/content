title: "Chuck Baldwin 2008 presidential campaign"
short_summary: "Political campaign for United States presidency"
description: "The 2008 Chuck baldwin presidential Campaign began 10 April 2008 when Pastor and Radio Host Chuck Baldwin announced his Candidacy for the presidential Nomination of the Constitution Party in Florida. He previously served as the Party's vice-presidential Nominee in 2004. Baldwin's main Opposition for the Nomination was former Ambassador Alan Keyes who had just left the republican Party. Baldwin was nominated after a Campaign touting his Views on civil Liberties foreign Affairs and Religion at the Constitution Party national Convention on April 26. Darrell Castle was appointed as his Running Mate."
