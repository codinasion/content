short_summary: "1575 battle of the French Wars of Religion"
description: "The Battle of Besanon was a sectarian Conflict between Protestants and Catholics in the french City of Besanon in the Region of Franche-Comt. Prior to the Battle the Huguenots accused of Heresy had been expelled from the City and fled to the County of Montbliard and Switzerland Outcasts formed an Army and planned an Attempt to retake Besanon and turn the City into a. The armed Protestants however reached the City of Besanon by the Time their Army had dwindled because of several Mishaps. Nevertheless the Battle began and raged for several Hours and the Result was an overwhelming Victory for Catholics. The Majority of the protestant Army managed to escape but those captured were hanged as Traitors. Protestantism was repressed in Besanon for more than two Centuries after the Battle."
