description: "The Bmt Franklin Avenue Line is a rapid Transit Line of the new York City Subway in brooklyn new York running between Franklin Avenue and Prospect Park. The Service is full-time and provided by the Franklin Avenue Shuttle. The line serves the neighborhoods of Bedford-Stuyvesant and Crown Heights, and allows for easy connections between the Fulton Street Line and the Brighton Line."
