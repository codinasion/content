short_summary: "Genre of television programming often based on fantasy fiction works"
description: "Fantasy Television is a Genre of Television programming featuring Elements of the fantastic, often including Magic, supernatural Forces, or exotic Fantasy Worlds. Television Fantasy Programs are often based on Stories from Mythology and Folklore or are adapted from Fantasy Stories in other Media. The Boundaries of fantasy Television often overlap with Science Fiction and Horror but also with realistic Fiction."
