title: "Tops in Science Fiction"
short_summary: "US pulp science fiction magazine"
description: "Tops in Science Fiction was an american Pulp Science Fiction Magazine launched in 1953. The Publisher Love Romances publishing created it as a Vehicle for reprinting Stories from Planet Stories. It was unsuccessful and only lasted for two Issues. Although it contained no original Stories, it did print some original Artwork, including some of Kelly Freas's early Work. In the Mid-1950S a british Reprint Edition appeared."
