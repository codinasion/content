description: "The Lexus Ls is a full-size Luxury Sedan that serves as the Flagship Model of lexus Luxury Division of Toyota. All ls Models featured v8 Engines for the first four Generations and were predominantly rear-wheel Drive. Lexus offered a fourth Generation all-wheel-drive hybrid and long-wheelbase Variant in the fourth Generation. The fifth Generation changed to using a V6 Engine with no V8 Option, and only one Length was offered."
