description: "Autobiography is the Debut Studio Album by american Singer-Songwriter Ashlee Simpson. Released in the united States on 20 July 2004 by Geffen Records the Album debuted at Number one on the us billboard 200 and was certified triple Platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America. Musically it combines Elements of Pop with Rock. The critical Response to the Album by Critics was mixed. Autobiography has sold more than five million Copies worldwide."
