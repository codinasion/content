short_summary: "Volcano in Argentina"
description: "Payn Matr is a shield Volcano in reserva provincial La Payunia of the Malarge Department South of the Mendoza Province in Argentina. It lies in the back-arc Region of the andean Volcanic Belt and was formed by Subduction of the nazca Plate beneath the south american Plate. Payn matr together with the Llancanelo Nevado and Salado Basin volcanic Fields form the Payenia Province. It has been proposed since 2011 as a World Heritage Site."
