description: "Warioware smooth Moves is a Party Video Game developed by intelligent Systems and nintendo Spd. The Game was released by Nintendo in December 2006 for its Wii Video Game System in Japan and in january 2007 for australia North America and Europe. It is the fifth Game in the Warioware Series and the only Game of the Series to be physically released for the Wii. Like its Predecessors warioware smooth Moves is built around a Collection of Microgames that last roughly five Seconds each and which require the Player to hold the wii Remote in specific Positions. The Game offers the Microgames to the Player in rapid Succession, by first instructing the Player to hold the Wii Remote in a specific Manner, and then showing them the Microgame. The Microgames are divided into several Stages, each of which loosely connects the Microgames with the Help of a Story. Additionally, this was the first spin-off Mario game to be released for the console."
