description: "Oppam is a 2016 indian Malayalam-Language Crime Thriller Film written and directed by Priyadarshan and based on a Story by Govind Vijayan. It was produced by Antony Perumbavoor for aashirvad Cinemas and starred Mohanlal and Samuthirakani. The Film contains Songs composed by the Group 4 Musics, and the Score was composed by Ron Ethan Yohann. N. K. Ekambaram was the Cinematographer."
