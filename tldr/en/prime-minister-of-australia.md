short_summary: "Head of government of Australia"
description: "The prime Minister of Australia is the Head of the Government of the Commonwealth of Australia. The prime Minister leads the Executive Branch of the federal Government of Australia and is accountable under the Principles of responsible Government to the federal Parliament. The prime Minister is the Chair of the federal and national Cabinet and is a Member of the federal Executive Council. The current prime Minister is anthony Albanese of the australian Labor Party who took Office on 23 may 2022."
