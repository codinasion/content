title: "USS Alaska (CB-1)"
short_summary: "Lead ship of the US Navy Alaska class of large cruisers"
description: "The uss Alaska was the lead Ship of the alaska Class of large Cruisers that served with the us navy during World War Ii. She was the first of two Ships of her Class to be completed, followed only by Guam ; four other Ships were ordered but were not completed before the End of the War. Alaska was the third Vessel of the us navy named for what was then the Territory of Alaska and was assigned the cb-1 Hull Number. She was laid down on December 17 1941 Ten Days after the us entered the War she was launched by the new York Shipbuilding Corporation in august 1943 in camden new Jersey and was commissioned in June 1944. She was armed with a main Battery of nine 12 in Guns in three triple Turrets and had a top Speed of 33 Kn."
