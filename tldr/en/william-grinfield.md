title: "William Grinfield"
short_summary: "British Army officer"
description: "General William Grinfield was a british Army Officer who served in the french Revolutionary and napoleonic Wars. Grinfield joined the 3rd Regiment of Foot Guards in 1760 and was promoted through the Ranks to become major in the Regiment in 1786. In 1793 his Regiment joined the Flanders Campaign fighting in the Siege of Valenciennes and at the Battle of Lincelles during which Time he was promoted to lieutenant Colonel. Grinfield was promoted by Seniority to major-general later in the same Year having held a higher army-wide Rank than he did regimental Rank."
