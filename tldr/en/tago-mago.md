title: "Tago Mago"
short_summary: "1971 studio album by Can"
description: "Tago Mago is the second Studio Album by the german Krautrock Band Can, originally released as a double Lp in August 1971 on the United Artists Label. It was the Band's first Studio Album to feature Damo Suzuki after the Departure of previous Vocalist Malcolm Mooney in 1970. Recorded in a rented Castle near Cologne the Album features long-form experimental Tracks combining Rock Improvisation Funk Rhythms and Musique Concrte Techniques."
