description: "Durga Shakti Nagpal is an indian Bureaucrat and indian Administrative Service Officer of Uttar Pradesh Cadre and 2010 Batch. She came into public View after launching a massive Drive against Corruption and illegal Sand Mining within her Jurisdiction of Gautam Budh Nagar as Joint Magistrate. In July 2013, she was suspended by the Akhilesh Yadav led Government of Up for allegedly demolishing an illegal Mosque Wall in Kadalpur Village near Jewar, which resulted in severe Opposition as it was perceived to be based on flimsy Grounds."
