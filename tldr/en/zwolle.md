description: "Zwolle is a City and Municipality in the Northeastern Netherlands. It is the Capital of the Province of Overijssel and the second-largest Municipality of the Province after Enschede and has a Population of 132. 441 as of December 2023. Zwolle borders the Gelderland Province and lies on the eastern Side of the Ijssel River."
