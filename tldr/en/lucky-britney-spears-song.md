short_summary: "2000 single by Britney Spears"
description: "'lucky' is a Song by american Singer Britney Spears from her second Studio Album, oops! . . . I Did It Again . It was released by jive Records as the second single from the Album on July 25 2000. After meeting with Producers Max Martin and Rami Yacoub in Sweden, the Singer recorded numerous Songs for the Album, including 'lucky'. The Song's narrative follows the Story of the eponymous famous Actress, who, despite seemingly having it all – Fame, Wealth, Beauty – is truly lonely and unhappy on the Inside. It received critical Acclaim, with Critics praising its Melody and Rhythm, and Spears' Vocals."
