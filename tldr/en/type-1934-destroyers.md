title: "Type 1934 destroyers"
short_summary: "Group of four destroyers for the German Navy"
description: "The Type 1934 Destroyers also known as the Z1 Class or after the lead Ship the leberecht Maass Class were a Group of four Destroyers built for the german Navy during the mid-1930s shortly before the Beginning of World War Ii. The Ships were engaged in training for most of the Period between their Commissioning and the Outbreak of War although they participated in early 1939 in the Occupation of Memel in Lithuania. Z3 Max Schultz collided with and sank a german Torpedo Boat shortly before the War began on 1 September 1939. All four Ships were named after german Officers killed in World War I."
