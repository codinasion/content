description: "Real Friends is a Song by cuban-american Singer Camila Cabello for her Debut Solo Studio Album Camila. Written by Cabello William Walsh Louis Bell brian Lee and its Producer Frank dukes it was released on December 7 2017 as a Promotion single with never be the same. 'Real Friends' has an acoustic guitar foundation. In the Lyrics cabello Reflections on her Life and Asks for an Honest Friendship. The Song's Minimalist Production has Elements of Reggae, Latin and tropical Music. The revised Version of the Song featuring american musician Swae Lee was released on August 16 2018."
