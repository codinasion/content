title: "Phasmatodea"
short_summary: "Order of stick and leaf insects"
description: "Phasmatodea are an Order of Insects whose Members are generally known as stick Bugs Stick-Bustles Walkingsticks stick Animals or Bug Sticks. They are sometimes referred to as the Devil's darning Needles although this Name is shared by both Crane and Dragonflies. They can be generally referred to as Phasmatodeans Phasmids or Ghost Insects Phasmids in the Family Phylliidae called leaf Insects leaf-bugs walking Leaves or Bug Leaves. The Group's Name is derived from the Ancient Greek Φάσμα Phasma, meaning an Apparition or Phantom, referring to their Resemblance to Vegetation while in Fact being Animals. Their natural Camouflage makes them difficult for Predators to detect ; still, many Species have one of several secondary Lines of Defense in the Form of startle Displays, Spines or toxic Secretions. Stick Insects from the Genera Phryganistria, Ctenomorpha, and Phobaeticus include the World's longest Insects."
