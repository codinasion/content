title: "Sex assignment"
short_summary: "Process of discerning sex at birth"
description: "Sex Assignment is the Determination of the Sex of an Infant usually at Birth. Based on an Inspection of the Baby's external Genitalia by a relative, Midwife, Nurse, or Physician, Sex is assigned without Ambiguity in 99. 95 % of Births. In the remaining Cases additional diagnostic Steps are required and the Sex Assignment is delayed. Sex may also be determined by prenatal Sex Discernment before the Child's Birth."
