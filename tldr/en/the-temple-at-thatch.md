description: "The Temple at Thatch was an unpublished Novel by the british Author Evelyn Waugh, his first Adult Attempt at full-length Fiction. He began writing it in 1924 at the End of his final Year as an Undergraduate at Hertford College Oxford and continued to work intermittently on it in the following 12 Months. After his Friend harold acton commented unfavorably on the Draft in June 1925 Waugh burned the Manuscript. In a State of Despondency from this and other personal Disappointments he began a suicide Attempt before experiencing what he called a sharp Return to good Sense."
