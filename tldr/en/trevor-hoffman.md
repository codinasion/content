short_summary: "American baseball player (born 1967)"
description: "Trevor William Hoffman is an american former professional Baseball Pitcher who played 18 Years in Major League Baseball from 1993 to 2010. A long-time Closer, he pitched for the Florida Marlins, San Diego Padres, and Milwaukee Brewers, including more than 15 Years for the Padres. Hoffman was the first Player in major Leagues to reach the Milestones of 500 and 600 Saves and was the all-time Saves Leader from 2006 to 2011. He was inducted into the Baseball Hall of Fame in 2018. Hoffman currently serves as a senior Advisor to the Padres' Baseball Operations Department."
