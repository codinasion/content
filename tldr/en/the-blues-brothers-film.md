short_summary: "1980 American musical comedy film by John Landis"
description: "The Blues Brothers is an american musical Action Comedy Film of 1980 directed by John Landis. It stars John Belushi as 'Joliet' Jake Blues and Dan Aykroyd as his Brother Elwood, Characters developed from the recurring musical Sketch 'the Blues Brothers' on Nbc's Variety Series Saturday Night Live. The Script is set in chicago Illinois and around where it was filmed the Script is written by Aykroyd and Landis. It features musical Numbers by Rhythm and Blues Soul and Blues Singers James brown cab Calloway Aretha Franklin Ray Charles chaka Khan and John Lee Hooker. It features non-musical supporting Performances by John Candy Henry Gibson kathleen Freeman and Carrie Fisher."
