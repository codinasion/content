description: "Sms Medusa was a Member of the 10-ship gazelle Class Light Cruisers which were built for the german Kaiserliche Marine in the late 1890S and early 1900s. The gazelle Class was the Culmination of earlier unprotected Cruiser and aviso Designs combining the best Aspects of both Types in what became the Progenitor of all future light Cruisers of the imperial Fleet. Built to be able to serve with the main german Fleet and as a Colonial Cruiser, she was armed with a Battery of ten 10. 5 cm guns and a top speed of 21. 5 knots. In the Span of more than forty Years medusa served in the three german naval Navies the Kaiserliche marine the Reichsmarine of Weimar Germany and the Kriegsmarine."
