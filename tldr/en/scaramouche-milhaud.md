description: "Scaramouche, Op. 165 is a 1937 Suite composed by Darius Milhaud. The Suite is based on incidental Music Milhaud wrote for two theatrical Productions le mdecin volant and Bolivar. Scaramouche Draws Inspiration from various Sources with each of the three Movements of the Suite having a distinct Character. The characteristic Use of Polytonality of Milhaud can be heard throughout the Piece."
