short_summary: "Hurricane season in the Atlantic Ocean"
description: "The 1933 Atlantic hurricane season is the most active Atlantic hurricane season on record in terms of accumulated Cyclone Energy , with a total of 259. It also set a Record for nameable tropical Storms in a single Season, 20, which stood until 2005, when there were 28 Storms. The Season ran for six Months in 1933 with tropical Cyclone Development occurring as early as may and as late as November. A System was active for all but 13 Days from June 28 to October 7."
