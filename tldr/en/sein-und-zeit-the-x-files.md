description: "Sein and Time is the tenth Episode of the seventh Season of the X-Files Science Fiction Television Series. The german Language Title directly references Martin Heidegger's best known Work, translated as 'Being and Time' in English. It premiered on the Fox Network in the united States on february 6 2000. The Episode was written by Chris Carter and Frank Spotnitz and directed by Michael Watkins. The Episode helped to explore the Series' overarching Mythology. Sein and Zeit earned a nielsen Household Rating of 8. 4, being watched by 13. 95 million People in its initial Broadcast. It received mixed to positive Reviews from Critics."
