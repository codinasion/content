short_summary: "2010 studio album by MercyMe"
description: "The Generous Mr. Lovewell is the sixth Studio Album by Christian Rock Band Mercyme. The Album was released in may 2010 and is a Concept Record revolving around the fictional allegorical Character Mr. Lovewell', and the overall Theme of Love. Produced by brown Bannister and Dan muckala the Album met with a positive critical and commercial Reception. The Album sold more than 88000 Copies in its first Week and debuted at Number three on the Billboard 200 and Number one on the christian Albums chart."
