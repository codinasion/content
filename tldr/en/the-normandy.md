title: "The Normandy"
short_summary: "Apartment building in Manhattan, New York"
description: "The Normandy is a cooperative Apartment Building located at 140 riverside Drive between 86th and 87th Streets adjacent to riverside Park in the upper west Side of Manhattan in new York City. It was designed by Emery Roth and based on the Mix of modern Art and Renaissance Revival Styles constructed from 1938 to 1939. The building was developed by a syndicate composed of Henry Kaufman, Emery Roth, Samson Rosenblatt, and Herman Wacht. The Normandy is 20 Stories tall, with small Twin Towers rising above the 18th Story. The Building is a designated Landmark in new York City."
