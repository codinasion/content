short_summary: "Pre-dreadnought battleship class of the British Royal Navy"
description: "The formidable Class of Battleships were a three-ship Class of Pre-Dreadnoughts designed by Sir William white and built in the late 1890S for the Royal Navy. The Class comprised Formidable, Irresistible, and Implacable. They were armed with a Battery of four 12-inch Guns their top Speed was 18 Knots and marked the Adoption of krupp Armour in british Battleship Designs. The class formed the basis for the nearly identical London class of five ships, and those ships are sometimes included in the Formidable class. Between 1898 and 1901 the formidable irresistible and implacable were built at the Portsmouth Chatham Dockyard and in Devonport respectively."
