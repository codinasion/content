description: "Thulium is a chemical Element it has the Symbol Tm and the atomic Number 69. It is the thirteenth and third-last Element of the lanthanide Series. Like the other Lanthanides, the most common Oxidation State is +3, seen in its Oxide, Halides and other Compounds ; however, the +2 Oxidation State can also be stable. Similar to Compounds of other late Lanthanides soluble Thulium Compounds form Coordination Complexes with nine Water Molecules in aqueous Solution."
