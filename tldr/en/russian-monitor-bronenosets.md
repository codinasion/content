title: "Russian monitor Bronenosets"
short_summary: "Russian Uragan-class monitor"
description: "Bronenosets was a uuragan-class Monitor for the russian imperial Navy built in the Mid-1860S. The Design was based on the american Passaic-Class Monitor, but was modified to suit russian Engines, Guns and Construction Techniques. The Vessel was only active when the Gulf of Finland was not frozen but very little is known about its Service. She was stricken from the navy List in 1900 converted into a Coal Barge in 1903 and was renamed barzha no. 324. The Ship was lost during World War I in a Storm."
