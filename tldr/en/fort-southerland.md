short_summary: "United States historic place"
description: "Fort Southerland, also known as Redoubt E and possibly Fort Diamond, is a Redoubt built during the American Civil War to protect Camden, Arkansas. Following a Union Victory in the 1864 little Rock Campaign Confederate Forces built it together with four other Redoubts in early 1864. Fort Southerland is about the Size of a City Block and is roughly oval. It could hold three cannons. When the Union captured Camden in april 1864 during the camden Expedition they improved the Defenses of the five Redoutts which were not enough to properly defend the City. After the Confederates retook camden later in the Month they continued to improve the City's Defenses."
