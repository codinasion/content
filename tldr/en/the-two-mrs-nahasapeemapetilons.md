description: "'the two Mrs. Nahasapeemapetilons is the seventh Episode in the ninth Season of american animated Television Series the Simpsons. It originally aired on the Fox Network in the united States on november 16 1997. It was written by Richard Appel and directed by steven Dean Moore. The Episode sees apu Nahasapeemapetilon Marry Manjula and incorporates several Aspects of Hindu Wedding Ceremonies that the Writers researched during the Production of the Episode. Appel pitched the episode several Years before Season 9 but the Idea was not used until mike scully became the Showrunner. The Subplot which sees homer Stay at the Retirement Castle in Springfield was originally conceived as separate Episode but could not be developed in sufficient Detail. The Episode received positive Reviews."
