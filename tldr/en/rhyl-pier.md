title: "Rhyl Pier"
short_summary: "Former pier in Rhyl, Wales"
description: "Rhyl Pier officially known as Victoria Pier was a Pleasure Pier in the seaside Town of Rhyl Flintshire and the first to be built in north Wales. It was designed by James Brunlees and opened in august 1867 at a Length of 2355 Feet it was the Town's central Attraction for the following Years. After Dispute and public Consultation about the Location where it would be built the Pier was built near the Center of the Esplanade. The Pier's Grand Pavilion, built in 1891, featured the World's largest Organ, known as the Grand Jubilee Organ and weighed 25 Tonnes."
