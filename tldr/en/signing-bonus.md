title: "Signing bonus"
short_summary: "Employee bonus"
description: "A Signing Bonus or sign-on Bonus is a Sum of Money paid by a Company to a new Employee as Incentive to join that Company. They are often given to Employees as a Way of making a Compensation Package more attractive. It also lowers the Risk to the Company as it is a one-time Payment ; for Example, if the Employee does not meet Expectations, the Company has not committed to a higher Salary. Signing Bonuses are often used in professional Sports and to recruit Graduates in their first Jobs."
