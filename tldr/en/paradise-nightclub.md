short_summary: "Gay nightclub in Asbury Park, New Jersey"
description: "Paradise is a gay Nightclub located in Asbury Park new Jersey. It is known for its Fundraisers and Drag Shows but the most notable is the annual Miss Paradise Pageant. The Club is integrated with the Empress Hotel ; both are owned by Record Producer Shep Pettibone. In 1999 Paradise's Opening is credited as one Factor that improved asbury Park's struggling Economy and drew the Lgbt Community back into the City after a Period of local Unrest. The Venue is a popular Destination on the Jersey Shore in the Summer and has received Praise for its Welcoming Atmosphere. After several Months of Closure due to the covid-19 pandemic Paradise opened its outdoor Pool and Bar Area in Summer 2020."
