short_summary: "Armored cruiser of the United States Navy"
description: "Uss Montana, also referred to as 'Armored Cruiser No. 13', later renamed Missoula and reclassified Ca-13, was a tennessee-class armored Cruiser of the United States Navy. She was built by newport News Drydock Shipbuilding Co. Her Keel was laid in April 1905 and it was launched in December 1906 and commissioned in July 1908. The last armored Cruiser Class built for the united States navy Montana and her Sisters was armed with a main Battery of four 10-inch Guns and they were capable of a maximum Speed of 22 Knots."
