description: "The System Has failed is the tenth Studio Album by american Thrash Metal Band Megadeth, released on September 14, 2004. It was the second and final Studio Album of the Band released by sanctuary Records. The System has failed was the first Album released after dave Mustaine recovered from an arm Injury sustained in 2002. It is also the first of four Megadeth Studio Albums not to include original Bassist and Co-Founder David Ellefson. The Album features former Megadeth Member Chris Poland who previously performed on Killing is my Business. . . and Business Is Good! and Peace Sells. . . But who is buying? , and Session Musicians Vinnie Colaiuta and Jimmie Lee Sloas."
