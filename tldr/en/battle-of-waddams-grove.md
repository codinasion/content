description: "The Battle of Waddams grove also known as Battle of yellow Creek was Part of the black Hawk War. It took Place on 18 June 1832 in present-day stephenson County Illinois. After several incidents of Sauk Indian raids on settlers along the Apple River, Captain James W. Stephenson left Galena with a Group of Volunteer Militia to pursue the native Party. The Group clashed on 18 June 18 1832 near yellow Creek and the ensuing Battle delved into a Bayonet and knife Fight in which several Sauk and three Militia Men were killed. Stephenson was seriously wounded during the Fighting by a Musketball to the Chest. The dead were later interred at a memorial Cemetery in Kellogg's Grove Illinois where a Stone Monument was erected in Memory of those killed during the War."
