short_summary: "2017 esports tournament"
description: "The International 2017 was the seventh Iteration of the International, an annual Dota 2 Esports World Championship Tournament. Hosted by Valve, the Game's Developer, the Tournament began with the Online Qualifier Phase in June 2017, and ended after the main Event at the Keyarena in Seattle in August. The grand Finals took Place between european Team Liquid and chinese Team Newbee with liquid defeating newbee 30 in a best-of-five Series that won nearly 11 million in Prize Money."
