short_summary: "State highway in Washington"
description: "State Route 433 is a 0. 94-mile long State Highway located entirely in Cowlitz County, Washington, United States. The Highway begins midway across the Columbia River at the Lewis and Clark Bridge on the national Register of historic Places and continues north to sr 432 in Longview. Prior to the State Highway renumbering in 1964 the Highway was Part of primary State Highway 12. Between 1964 and 1972 the Name of the Highway was changed from the State Route 833 to the current Sr433."
