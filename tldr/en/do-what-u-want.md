description: "Do what you want is a Song by the american Singer lady Gaga featuring R. Kelly. The Song was released on 21 october 2013 as the second single from Gaga's third Studio Album Artpop. The Singers wrote the Song with dj white Shadow Martin Bresso and William Grigahcine. Dj white Shadow presented Gaga with the Song's initial Concept two Years before its Release. Production on 'Do what U Want' was completed in 2013, with Kelly's Vocals added soon after. Its sudden Popularity upon premiering led to the Song becoming the Album's second single."
