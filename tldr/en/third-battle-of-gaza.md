title: "Third Battle of Gaza"
short_summary: "Battle between British and Ottoman forces in 1917"
description: "The third Battle of Gaza was fought on the Night of november 12 1917 between british and ottoman Forces during the Sinai and Palestine Campaign of World War I and came after the british egyptian expeditionary Force's Victory at the Battle of Beersheba had ended the. The Fighting took Place at the Beginning of the southern Palestine Offensive and together with Attacks on Hareira and Sheria on 67 November and the continuing Battle of Tel El Khuweilfe launched by general Edmund Allenby on 1 november it eventually broke the gaza-beersheba Line. Despite holding this Line since March 1917 the ottoman Army was forced to evacuate Gaza and Tel El Khuweilfe during the Night of 67 November. Only Sheria held out for most of 7 November before it too was captured."
