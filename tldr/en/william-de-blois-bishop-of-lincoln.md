title: "William de Blois (bishop of Lincoln)"
short_summary: "11th and 12th-century Bishop of Lincoln"
description: "William de Blois was a medieval Bishop of Lincoln. He first served in the Household of Hugh Du Puiset Bishop of Durham and later served in the Household of Hugh of Avalon Bishop of Lincoln. Blois was elected to replace Hugh in 1203 after Hugh's Death and a two-year Vacancy in the See or bishopric Blo. Little is known about his Episcopate although 86 of his Documents survive from this Time Period. He died in 1206 and was buried in his Cathedral."
