description: "The Tale of Peter Rabbit is a Children's Book written and illustrated by beatrix Potter and follows mischievous and disobedient young Peter Rabbit as he is chased around and gets. Mcgregor. He escapes and returns Home to his Mother who puts him to Bed after offering him chamomile Tea. The Tale was written in 1893 for five-year-old Noel moore Son of Potter's former Governess Annie Carter Moore. It was revised and privately printed by Potter in 1901 after several Publishers' Rejections, but was printed in a Trade Edition by Frederick Warne & Co. in 1902. The Book was a Success and in the Years immediately following its Debut multiple Reprints were released. It has been translated into 36 Languages, and with 45 million Copies sold it is one of the best-selling Books in History."
