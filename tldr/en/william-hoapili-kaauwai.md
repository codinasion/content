title: "William Hoapili Kaʻauwai"
short_summary: "Hawaiian high chief and politician"
description: "William Hoapili KaʻAuwai was a hawaiian high Chief and Politician, and religious Deacon of the Kingdom of Hawaii. In 1862 and 1870 he served two Terms as the Member of the House of Representatives of the Legislative of the Kingdom. He became the only native Hawaiian ordained priest of the anglican Church of Hawaii and traveled between 1865 and 1866 with queen Emma circumnavigating the Globe on his Return east via new Zealand to europe."
