short_summary: "Constituent college of the University of Durham"
description: "University College, informally known as Castle, is a College of Durham University in Durham, England. Centred on Durham Castle on Palace Green, it was founded in 1832 and is the oldest of Durham's Colleges. It is listed as a constituent College of durham University under Section 216 of the 1988 Education Reform Act as a higher Education Institution. Academic Activities such as Research and tutoring occur almost all at the University Level."
