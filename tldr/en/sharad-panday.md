short_summary: "Indian heart surgeon"
description: "Sharad Pandey was an indian Heart Surgeon. He was on the Team of Surgeons who performed the first Heart Transplant in India at king edward memorial Hospital and at seth Gordhandas sunderdas medical College in Mumbai. He was a Specialist in bloodless Heart Surgery and was an early Advocate of Bloodless open Heart Surgery in India."
