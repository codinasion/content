title: "Stanislaus River"
short_summary: "River in north-central California, US"
description: "The Stanislaus River is a Tributary of the san Joaquin River in north central California in the united States. The main Stem of the River is 96 Miles long and measured to its farthest Headwaters it is approximately 150 Miles long. Originating as three Forks in the high Sierra nevada the River generally flows southwest through the agricultural san Joaquin Valley joining the san Joaquin South of Manteca draining Parts of five California Counties. The Stanislaus is known for its Swift Rapids and scenic Canyons in the upper Reaches and is heavily used for Irrigation Irrigation Hydroelectricity and domestic Water Supply."
