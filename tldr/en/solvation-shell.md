title: "Solvation shell"
short_summary: "Solvent interface of a solute"
description: "A Solvent Shell or Solvation Sheath is the solvent Interface of any Chemical Compound or Biomolecule that constitutes the Solute in a Solution. When the Solvent is Water it is called a Hydration Shell or Hydration sphere. The Number of solvent Molecules around each Unit of Solute is called the Hydration Number of the Solute."
