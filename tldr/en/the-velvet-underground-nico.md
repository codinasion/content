description: "The Velvet Underground & Nico is the Debut Studio Album by the American Rock band the Velvet Underground and the german Singer Nico, released in March 1967 through Verve Records. It was recorded in 1966 while the Band was featured on andy Warhol's exploding Plastic inevitable Tour. The Album features experimental Performance Sensibilities and explicit lyrical Topics including Drug Abuse Prostitution Sadomasochism and sexual Deviance."
