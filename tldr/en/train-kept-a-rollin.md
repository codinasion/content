description: "Train kept a Rollin' is a Song first recorded in 1951 by the american Jazz and Rhythm and Blues musician tiny Bradshaw. Originally performed in the Style of Jump Blues bradshaw borrowed Lyrics from an earlier Song and set them to an upbeat Shuffle Arrangement that inspired other Musicians to perform and record. In 1956 Johnny Burnette and the Rock and Roll Trio made an important Contribution they reworked it as a Guitar-Driven Song which features an early Use of intentionally distorted Guitar in Rock Music."
