The Movies primarily focus on Michael Myers who was sent to a Sanitarium as a Child for the Murder of his Sister Judith Myers. Fifteen Years later he escapes to stalk and kill the People of the fictional Town of Haddonfield Illinois. Michael's Killings occur on the halloween Holiday on which all the Films are primarily shot. Throughout the Series various Protagonists try to stop Myers most notably babysitter laurie Strode and Psychiatrist Dr C Irene. Samuel Loomis.
