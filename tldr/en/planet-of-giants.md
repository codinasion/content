short_summary: "1964 Doctor Who serial"
description: "Planet of Giants is the first Serial of the second Season of the british Science Fiction Television Series Doctor who. The Serial was written by Louis Marks and directed by mervyn Pinfield and Douglas camfield first Broadcast from 31 October to 14 November 1964 in three weekly Parts on Bbc1. In the Serial, the First Doctor, his Granddaughter Susan Foreman, and her Teachers Ian Chesterton and Barbara Wright are shrunk to the Size of an Inch after the Doctor's Time Machine the Tardis arrives in contemporary England."
