short_summary: "Kentucky politician and former college basketball standout"
description: "Richard Dwight Farmer is an american former Collegiate Basketball Player and Republican Party Politician from the U. S. State of Kentucky. He served as Agriculture Commissioner for Kentucky from 2004 to 2012 and was the running Mate of david L Smith. Williams in the 2011 gubernatorial Election. After leaving Office Farmer was investigated for violating State Campaign Finance Laws and misappropriating State Resources and was sentenced to 27 Months in federal Prison along with a concomitant 12 Months in State Prison."
