short_summary: "2014 video game"
description: "Wolfenstein the new Order is an action-adventure first-person Shooter Video Game developed by Machinegames and published by Bethesda Softworks in 2014. It was released on 20 May 2014 for Playstation 3, Playstation 4, Windows, Xbox 360, and Xbox One. The Game is the seventh main Entry in the Wolfenstein Series, set in an alternate History 1960s Europe where the Nazis won the second World War. The Story follows the War Veteran William B. J. Blazkowicz and his Efforts to stop nazi Rulers from ruling the World."
