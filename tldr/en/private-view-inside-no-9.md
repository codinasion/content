description: "Private View is the sixth and final Episode of the third Series of the british black Comedy Anthology Television Programme inside no. 9. Written by steve Pemberton and Reece shearsmith the Episode was directed by Guillem Morales and first shown on Bbc two on March 21 2017. It stars Pemberton, Shearsmith, Fiona Shaw, Montserrat Lombard, Morgana Robinson, Felicity Kendal, Johnny Flynn, and Muriel Gray. The comedian Peter Kay makes a cameo Appearance, with his Character being killed in the Episode's Opening Seconds."
