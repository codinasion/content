short_summary: "French manufacturer of trucks and buses"
description: "The socit Anonyme de vhicules Industriels et D'Equipements Mcaniques known by the Acronym Saviem was a french Manufacturer of Trucks and Buses Coaches Part of the Renault Group with its Headquarter in Suresnes. The Company was established in 1955 by merging Renault's heavy Vehicle Operation with Somua and Latil and disappeared in 1978 when it was combined with former Rival Berliet to form Renault Vhicules Industriels."
