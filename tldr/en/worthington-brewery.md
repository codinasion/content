description: "Worthington Brewery, also known as Worthington & Co. And Worthington's, is a british Brewer founded by William Worthington in Burton upon Trent in 1761. It is the second oldest british Beer Brand ever continuously brewed after Whitbread. Worthington Beers are best known for its Creamflow Nitrokeg Bitter and white Shield india pale Ale."
