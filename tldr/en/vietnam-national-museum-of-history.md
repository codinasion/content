title: "Vietnam National Museum of History"
short_summary: "Archeological museum in Hanoi, Vietnam"
description: "The Vietnam National Museum of History is in the Hoan Kiem District of Hanoi, Vietnam. The Museum Building was an archaeological Research Institution of the french School of the Far East under French Colonial Rule of 1910, was extensively refurbished in 1920. It was designed by Architect ernest Hbrard between 1926 and 1932. The Museum was acquired by the Government of North Vietnam in 1958 and then the artifact Collections were expanded to cover eastern Arts and national History."
