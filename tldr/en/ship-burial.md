title: "Ship burial"
short_summary: "Burial in which a ship or boat is used"
description: "A ship Burial or a Boat Grave is a Burial in which a Ship or Boat is used either as a burial Site for the dead and grave Goods or as Part of the Graves itself. If the Ship is very small it is called a Boat Grave. This Style of Burial was practiced by various seafaring Cultures in Europe and Asia. Notable ship burial Practices include those by the Germanic Peoples, particularly by viking Age Norsemen, as well as the pre-colonial Ship Burials described in the Boxer Codex in the Philippines."
