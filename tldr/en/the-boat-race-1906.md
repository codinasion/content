title: "The Boat Race 1906"
short_summary: "Oxford versus Cambridge rowing race"
description: "The 63rd Boat Race took Place on April 7 1906. The Boat Race is held annually a side-by-side Rowing Race between Crews from the Universities of Oxford and Cambridge along the Thames. Oxford went into the Race as reigning Champions, having won the previous Year's Race, and their Crew was slightly heavier than their Opponents. In a Race where Frederick I was an Umpire. Pitman, Cambridge won by 3+1⁄2 Lengths in a Time of 19 Minutes 25 Seconds, the fastest Winning Time for four Years. The win took the overall record to 34–28 in Oxford's favour."
