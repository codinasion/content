The Episode was written by Vince Gilligan and was directed by Michael W. Watkins. The Episode is a Story about the Monster of the Week that is unrelated to the Series' Wider Mythology. Tithonus earned a nielsen Household Rating of 9. 2, being watched by 15. 90 million people in its initial broadcast. The Episode received positive Reviews."
