description: "Wilkins peak is a small Mountain in sweetwater County wyoming between green River and Rock Springs. It is primarily used for Radio and Television Station Transmitters but it also serves the Radio Needs of the faa Ems and local Businesses such as Questar Gas. A Tower on the Peak was knocked down in 2003 by a Vehicle that was delivering Propane to Customers. The Tower was owned by wyoming public Radio and provided local Programming from national public Radio. There are numerous Access Roads to the Peak and it is not gated and open Year-Round Weather permitting. Along with the Radio uses, the Mountain is also a popular Location for Mountain Biking and Hiking. The Mountain contains geology Characteristic of its Location in southwestern Wyoming."
