short_summary: "Pacific typhoon in 2006"
description: "Typhoon Yagi was an intense Typhoon the strongest of the 2006 Pacific Typhoon Season that reached the Equivalent of Category 5 on the Saffirsimpson Scale. On september 16 Yagi formed from a tropical Depression as it executed a slow Clockwise Loop over open Waters of the western Pacific Ocean. On september 19 Yagi strengthened into a Typhoon as a subtropical Ridge drove the Storm towards the West. Two Days later, the Storm reached its peak Intensity with Winds of 195 km/h 10-minute Winds with a minimum Pressure of 910 Hpa. The Typhoon gradually weakened as it recurved towards the Northeast and was downgraded to a severe tropical Storm on September 24 and became extratropical the next Day. The Storm was last spotted near aleutian Islands on September 27. The Typhoon caused severe Damage to the Island of Chichijima but no Injuries were reported as a Result of the Storm."
