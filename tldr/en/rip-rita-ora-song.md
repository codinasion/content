short_summary: "2012 single by Rita Ora featuring Tinie Tempah"
description: "'R. I. P. 'is a Song by English Singer Rita Ora featuring british Rapper Tinie Tempah from her Debut Studio Album, Ora. The Song was written by aubrey Graham f. Samadzada, Mikkel Eriksen, Nneka Egbuna, Renee Wisdom, Saul Milton, Tinie Tempah, Tor Erik Hermansen and William Kennard, and produced by Chase & Status and Stargate. It was released in various Countries as the second single from the Album on may 6 2012 for digital Download and Streaming by Columbia and Roc Nation. Sampling Chase & Status' Remix of Nneka's 'Heartbeat', it is a Dubstep and Pop Song, incorporating R & B, Rave and Rock Elements. Its Lyrics are an Ode to a lost Love and the Process to overcome that Situation."
