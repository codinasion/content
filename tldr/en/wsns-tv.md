description: "Wsns-Tv is a Television Station in Chicago, Illinois, United States, serving as the local Outlet for the Spanish-Language Network Telemundo. It is owned and operated by Nbcuniversal's Telemundo Station Group alongside Nbc Outlet Wmaq-Tv ; it is also sister to regional Sports Network Nbc Sports Chicago. Wsns-tv and wmaq-tv Share Studios at the Nbc Tower on north columbus Drive in the City's streeterville Neighborhood both Stations are broadcast from the same Transmitter in Willis Tower in the Chicago Loop."
