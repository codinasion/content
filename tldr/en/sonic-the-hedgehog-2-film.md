description: "Sonic the Hedgehog 2 is an Action Comedy Film released in 2022 based on the sega Video Game Series and the Sequel to sonic the Hedgehog. It was directed by Jeff Fowler and written by Pat Casey Josh Miller and John Whittington. Ben Schwartz james marsden tika Sumpter Natasha Rothwell adam pally lee Majdoub colleen O'Shaughnessey and Jim Carrey reprised their Roles with idris Elba and Shemar Moore joining the Cast. In the film, Sonic and Tails must find the Master Emerald before the villain Dr. Robotnik and a new rival knuckles the Echidna."
