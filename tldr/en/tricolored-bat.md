Formerly known as the eastern Pipistrelle based on the incorrect Belief that it was closely related to european Pipistrellus species the closest known Relative of the tricolored Bat is now recognized as the Canyon Bat. Its common Name tricolored Bat Originates from the Coloration of the Hairs on its Back which have three distinct Color Bands.
