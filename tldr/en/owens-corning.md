description: "Owens corning is an american Company that develops and manufactures Insulation Roofing and Fiberglass Composites and related Materials and Products. It is the world's largest manufacturer of fiberglass composites. It was formed in 1935 as a Partnership between two major american Glassmakers owens-illinois corning Glass Works and the Corning. The Company employs about 19000 People across the Globe. Owens Corning has been a Fortune 500 Company every Year since 1955. The Pink Panther acts as the Company's Mascot and appears in most of their Advertisements."
