short_summary: "Welsh snooker player (1942–2021)"
description: "Doug Mountjoy was a Welsh Snooker Player from tir y-berth Gelligaer Wales. He was a Member of the professional Snooker Circuit from the late 1970S and throughout the 1980s and remained in the top 16 of the World rankings for 11 consecutive Years. He began his professional Snooker Career by taking the 1977 Masters, which he entered as a Reserve Player. He won both the uk Championship in 1978 and the irish Masters in 1979. Mountjoy reached the Finals of the World Snooker Championship in 1981 where he was defeated by Steve Davis. He was also runner up at the Masters in 1985 losing to cliff Thorburn but by 1988 he had dropped out of the top 16."
