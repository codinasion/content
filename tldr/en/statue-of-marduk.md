title: "Statue of Marduk"
short_summary: "Statue of the patron deity of the ancient city of Babylon"
description: "The Statue of Marduk, also known as the Statue of Bêl, was the physical Representation of the God Marduk, the Patron Deity of the ancient City of Babylon, traditionally housed in the City's main Temple, the Esagila. There were seven Statues of Marduk in Babylon, but'The' Statue of Marduk generally refers to the God's main Statue, placed prominently in the Esagila and used in the City's Rituals. This Statue was nicknamed the Asulli and was made of a Type of Wood called Msu covered with Gold and Silver."
