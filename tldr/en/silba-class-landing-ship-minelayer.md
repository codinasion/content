description: "The silba Class is a Class of three landing Ships used as Minelayers as Built for the Yugoslav and croatian Navy during the 1980s and 1990s. The Ships were built at the Brodogradilite Specijalnih Objekata Shipyard in Split with slight Differences between the last two Ships in Armament Configuration. By the Time of the croatian War of Independence one Ship was in Service with the Jrm while another was being completed."
