The Song served as the Album's third single on November 18, 2012, through Arista Nashville. Two black Cadillacs by underwood hillary Lindsey and Josh Kear is a Country Pop Song with Lyrics addressing the Story of two Women who when they realize they are both involved with the same Man decide to kill him. It was produced by Mark Bright, and the Instrumentation was compared to Songs by Dixie Chicks and Miranda Lambert."
