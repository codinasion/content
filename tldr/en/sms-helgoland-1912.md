short_summary: "Scout cruiser of the Austro-Hungarian Navy"
description: "Sms Helgoland was a novara Cruiser of Scout Class built for the austro-hungarian navy Right before World War I. Helgoland participated in several Raids on the Ships defending the Strait of Otranto including the Battle of Strait of Otranto in may 1917. She was transferred to italy in 1920 in Accordance with the Peace Treaties ending World War I and renamed Brindisi. After Modifications the Ship was assigned until 1924 to the Squadron responsible for the eastern Mediterranean. After two Years of Operation she spent the next five Years based in Libya and Italy before Brindisi was disarmed and renamed into a Depot Ship in 1929. The Ship was removed from the 1937 navy List and later broke up."
