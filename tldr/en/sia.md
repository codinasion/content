short_summary: "Australian singer and songwriter (born 1975)"
description: "Sia Kate Isobelle Furler is an australian Singer and Songwriter. In the mid-1990s she started her Career as a Singer in the acid Jazz Band Crisp. When Crisp disbanded in 1997, she released her Debut Studio Album, Onlysee, in Australia. She moved to london and provided Vocals for british Duo zero 7. Sia released her second Studio Album, Healing is Difficult, in 2001, and her third, Colour the Small One, in 2004."
