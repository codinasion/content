short_summary: "2016 song by Swedish indie pop band Miike Snow"
description: "The Heart of me is a Song by swedish Indie pop Band Miike snow from their third Studio Album Iii. Written and produced by the Band the Song was originally released as a Buzz single on february 19 2016 in Anticipation of the Release of the Album. On november 15 2016 atlantic Records sent it as the fourth and last single from the Album to alternative Radio in the united States. Musically the Heart of me is a synth-laden Chillwave Song with Indie Rock Elements and emotional Lyrics. It features background Vocals by English Singer Charli Xcx."
