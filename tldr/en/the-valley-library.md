description: "The Valley Library is the primary Library of Oregon State University and is located on the School's main Campus in Corvallis in the us. S. State of Oregon. The Library was established in 1887 and for the first Time in 1918 in its own Building what is now kidder Hall. The current Building opened in 1963 as the William Jasper Kerr Library and was expanded and renamed in 1999 as the Valley Library. The Library is named after the Philanthropist F. Wayne Valley, who played Football for Oregon State."
