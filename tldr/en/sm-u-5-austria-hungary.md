title: "SM U-5 (Austria-Hungary)"
short_summary: "Austro-Hungarian lead boat of U-5 class"
description: "Sm u-5 or u-v was the lead Boat of the u-5 Class of Submarines or u-boats Built and operated by the austro-hungarian Navy before and during the first World War. The Sub was built as Part of a Plan to evaluate foreign Submarine Designs and was the first of three Boats of the Class built by Whitehead Co. Fiume has been renamed after an Irishman John Philip Holland's Design."
