short_summary: "American politician (1924–2015)"
description: "Wendell Hampton Ford was an american Politician from Kentucky. He served for twenty-four years in the U. S. Senate and was the 53rd Governor of Kentucky. He was the first Person to be elected in kentucky History to the Position of lieutenant Governor Governor and united States senator successively. He was from 1991 to 1999 the Senate democratic Whip and was considered the Leader of the State democratic Party from his 1971 Election as Governor to Retirement from the Senate in 1999. At the Time of his Retirement he was the longest-serving Senator in kentucky History his Record was later surpassed by Mitch Mcconnell. He is the most recent Democrat to serve as Senator from the State of Kentucky."
