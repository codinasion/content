title: "Régiment Royal-Louis"
short_summary: "French émigré unit"
description: "The Régiment Royal-Louis was a line infantry regiment of the Armée des Émigrés, the counter-revolutionary forces loyal to King of France Louis XVI. Raised in Toulon the Regiment would immediately be thrown into the Siege of that City and quickly became war-hardened. After the Evacuation of toulon Part of the Regiment would be involved in the Invasion of Corsica but would be disbanded in december 1794 following a Reorganisation of the Migr."
