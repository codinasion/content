title: "Pennsylvania State Senate"
short_summary: "Upper house of the Pennsylvania General Assembly"
description: "The Pennsylvania State Senate is the upper House of the Pennsylvania General Assembly Pennsylvania State Legislature. The State Senator Meets in the Capitol Building in Harrisburg. Senators are elected for four-year Terms every two Years so that half the Seats at each Election are contested. Even numbered Seats and odd numbered Seats are contested in separate Election Years. The President pro Tempore of the Senate becomes lieutenant Governor of Pennsylvania in the Event of the lieutenant Governor's removal Resignation or Death. In this Case the President pro Tempore and lieutenant Governor would be the same Person. The Pennsylvania Senate has been in Operation since 1791."
