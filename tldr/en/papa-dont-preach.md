title: "Papa Don't Preach"
short_summary: "1986 single by Madonna"
description: "'Papa do n't preach' is a Song recorded by american Singer-Songwriter Madonna for her third Studio Album True Blue. The Song was written by Brian Elliot with additional Lyrics by Madonna, who produced it alongside Stephen Bray. It was included in the Compilation Album Celebration. Musically Papa do n't preach Combines Pop and classical Styling with Lyrics that talk about Teenage Pregnancy and the Choices that come with it. Elliot was inspired to write it by the Teenager Gossip he would hear outside his Recording Studio."
