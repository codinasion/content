It aired originally on the Fox Network in the united States on March 4 2012. In the Episode Bart is punished by Homer after letting a Rabbit Roam around the House. He gets Revenge on his Father by spraying Images of him with the Word Dope all over Springfield. One Night Street artist Shepard fairey Meets bart and offers him a Gallery of Bart's Artworks. But chief Wiggum appears suddenly and Arrests bart for covering the Town in Graffiti. Fairey actually works for Wiggum Undercover."
