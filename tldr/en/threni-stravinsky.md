short_summary: "Musical composition by Stravinsky"
description: "Threni - Id est Lamentationes Jeremiae prophetae often referred to simply as Threni is a musical Setting by igor Stravinsky of Verses from the Book of Lamentations in Latin of the Vulgate for Solo. It is important among Stravinsky's Compositions as his first and longest completely dodecaphonic Work but it is not often performed. It has been described as austere but also as the culminating Point in his Career as an Artist both spiritually and stylistically the most ambitious and structurally the most complex of all his religious Compositions and even among Stravinsky's greatest Works."
