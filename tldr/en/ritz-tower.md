title: "Ritz Tower"
short_summary: "Residential building in Manhattan, New York"
description: "The Ritz Tower is a Luxury residential Building located at 465 Park Avenue at the Corner of east 57th Street in the midtown manhattan Neighborhood of new York City. It was built from 1925 to 1926 as an Apartment Hotel and was designed by Emery Roth and thomas Hastings for the Journalist Arthur Brisbane who was the Developer. The Ritz Tower is about 541 feet with 41 stories, making it the tallest residential building in New York City upon its completion. Because it was initially classified as an Apartment Hotel the Building was constructed in greater Height than was generally allowed."
