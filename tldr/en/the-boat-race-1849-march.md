title: "The Boat Race 1849 (March)"
short_summary: "Oxford versus Cambridge rowing race"
description: "The 9th Boat Race took Place on March 29 1849 on the Thames. The Event is typically held annually and is a side-by-side Rowing Race between Crews from the Universities of Oxford and Cambridge. The Race was won by Cambridge who racked up easy Victory over Oxford. As a Result of the Nature of the Defeat Oxford challenged Cambridge to a second Race that Year which was to be held in December the only Time for which the Boat Race was competed twice in a Calendar Year. All Members of the Cambridge Crew came from Trinity College."
