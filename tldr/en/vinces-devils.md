short_summary: "Professional wrestling stable"
description: "Vince's Devils was an Alliance of female professional Wrestlers in World Wrestling Entertainment on its raw Brand. It consisted of Divas Candice Michelle, Torrie Wilson, and Victoria. They formed their Alliance in August 2005 after Wilson and Michelle were traded to raw by smackdown and drafted! . Vince's Devils, named after Wwe Chairman Vince Mcmahon, helped each other in their Matches and Rivalries until Tension began growing between Wilson and Candice Michelle over the Latter's April 2006 Playboy Pictorial. The Group had officially split up by March 2006."
