title: "Operation Windsor"
short_summary: "1944 Canadian operation in Normandy during WWII"
description: "Operation Windsor was a canadian Attack at the Battle of normandy during the second World War. The Attack was initiated by the 3rd canadian Infantry Division to take Carpiquet and the adjacent Airfield from Troops of the 12th ss-panzer Division Hitler Jugend of Panzergruppe West. The Attack was originally planned to take Place during the later Stages of Operation Epsom to protect the eastern Flank of the main Assault but was delayed for a Week."
