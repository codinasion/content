title: "USS Congress (1799)"
short_summary: "United States Navy frigate"
description: "The uss Congress was a us navy nominally rated 38-gun wood-hulled three-masted heavy Frigate. James Hackett built her in Portsmouth New Hampshire and she was launched on 15 August 1799. She was one of the original six Frigates whose Construction was authorized by the 1794 naval Act. The Name Congress was among ten Names submitted to President george Washington in March 1795 by Secretary of War Timothy pickering for the Frigates that were to be built. Joshua Humphreys designed these Frigates to be the Capital Ships of the young Navy and so congress and her Sisters were larger and more heavily armed than standard Frigates of the Period."
