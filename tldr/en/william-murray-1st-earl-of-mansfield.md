short_summary: "British barrister, politician and judge (1705–1793)"
description: "William Murray, 1st Earl of Mansfield, Pc was a british Barrister, Politician and Judge noted for his Reform of English Law. Born to Scottish Nobility, he was a Member of the scottish Clan Murray and was educated in Perth, Scotland before moving to London at the Age of 13 to take up a Place at Westminster School. He was admitted to christ Church Oxford in may 1723 and graduated four Years later. On 23 november 1730 he returned from Oxford to london and was called to the Bar by Lincoln's Inn he quickly gained a Reputation as an excellent Barrister."
