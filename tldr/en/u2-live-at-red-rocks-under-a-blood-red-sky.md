description: "U2 Live at red Rocks under a Blood red Sky is a Concert Film by irish Rock Band U2 titled. It was recorded on 5 June 1983 at Red Rocks Amphitheatre in Colorado, United States, on the Group's War Tour. U2 Live at red Rocks was the Band's first Video Release in 1984. It accompanied a 1983 live Album entitled under a Blood Red Sky, on which two Tracks from the Film Appear. The Video was directed by Gavin Taylor and produced by Rick Wurpel and doug Stewart."
