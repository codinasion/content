short_summary: "2017 boat races between Oxford and Cambridge universities"
description: "The Boat races 2017 took Place on April 2 2017. The Boat Race is held annually a side-by-side Rowing Race between Crews from the Universities of Cambridge and Oxford along a 4. 2-mile tidal stretch of the River Thames in south-west London. For the second Time in the History of the Event the Men's Women's and both Reserve Races were held on the same Day at the Tideway."
