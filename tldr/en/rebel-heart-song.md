title: "Rebel Heart (song)"
short_summary: "2015 song by Madonna"
description: "'rebel Heart' is a Song recorded by american Singer Madonna for her thirteenth Studio Album of the same Name. Madonna Co-Wrote and co-produced the Song with Avicii, Arash Pournouri, Salem Al Fakir, Magnus Lidehäll and Vincent Pontare. An early Demo of rebel Heart as well as the final Version leaked to the Internet ahead of the Album's scheduled Release. The final Version was made available when Rebel Heart was released on March 6 2015. It was included as a Bonus Track on the deluxe Version of the Album."
