short_summary: "1983 video game"
description: "Pepsi Invaders is a 1983 fixed Shooter Video Game developed by Atari inc not available for commercial Sale. For the Atari 2600. It was commissioned by coca-cola Company to be handed out to salespeople at its Sales Convention in 1983. A Reskin of the System's Version of Space Invaders, the Player shoots down a flying Formation of Letters spelling 'Pepsi', Coca-Cola's long-time Rival. Only 125 Copies were distributed."
