title: "Operation Winter '94"
short_summary: "1994 battle of the Croatian War of Independence and the Bosnian War"
description: "Operation Winter'94 was a joint military Offensive by the croatian Army and the croatian Defence Council it fought between november 29 and december 24 1994 in southwestern Bosnia and Herzegovin. The Operation formed Part of the Croatian War of Independence and the Bosnian War fought between Bosnia and Herzegovina, Croatia and two unrecognized Para-States proclaimed by croatian Serbs and Bosnian Serbs. Both of the Para-States were supported by Serbia and the yugoslav People's Army. The Jna pulled out in 1992 but as it withdrew many of its Equipment were transferred to the bosnian Serb and croatian Serb Forces."
