short_summary: "2010 Artists for Haiti song"
description: "'we are the World 25 for Haiti' is a Charity single recorded by the Supergroup artists for Haiti in 2010. It is a Remake of the Song we are the World written by american Musicians michael Jackson and Lionel Richie and recorded by Usa in 1985 for Africa to benefit famine Relief in Africa. In late 2009 it had been suggested to richie and quincy Jones Producer of the original we are the World that a re-release Version of the Song be re-released under the Title live 25. Following the Magnitude 7. 0 Mw Earthquake in early 2010 in Haiti which devastated most of the Country and killed Thousands of People was agreed that the Song would be re-recorded by new Artists in the Hope that it would reach a new Generation and help benefit the People of Haiti."
