title: "Faroe Islands at the 2016 Summer Paralympics"
short_summary: "Sporting event delegation"
description: "From 718 to 718 September 2016 the Faroe islands sent a Delegation to brazil to participate in the Summer Paralympics 2016 in Rio de Janeiro. They sent one Participant Krista Mrkre who participated in three Swim Competitions. Her top Finish was 10th in Women's 400 m freestyle S10, and she did not qualify for the Finals of any of her three Events."
