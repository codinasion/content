title: "Tertiary education"
short_summary: "Advanced level of education, usually for adults"
description: "Tertiary Education also referred to as third-level Third-Stage or post-secondary Education is the educational Level following the Completion of secondary Education. The World Bank, for Example, defines tertiary Education as including Universities as well as Trade Schools and Colleges. Higher Education is taken to include undergraduate and postgraduate Education while vocational Education beyond secondary Education is known as further Education in the united Kingdom or under the Category of continuing Education in the united States."
