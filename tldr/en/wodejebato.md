description: "Wdejebato is a cretaceous Guyot or Tablemount in the northern Marshall Islands pacific Ocean. Wdejebato is probably a shield Volcano and is connected to the smaller Pikinni Atoll through a Submarine Ridge 74 Kilometers Southeast of Guyot unlike Wdejebato Pikinni rises above Sea Level. The Seamount rises to 4420 Metres to 1335 Metres depth and is formed by basaltic Rocks. The Name Wdejebato Refers to the Pikinni Sea God."
