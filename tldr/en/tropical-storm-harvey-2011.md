description: "Tropical Storm Harvey was the last tropical Cyclone in a record-breaking String of eight consecutive Storms which failed to reach Hurricane Intensity. On august 19 the eighth tropical Cyclone and Eighth named Storm of the 2011 atlantic Hurricane Season Harvey developed in the western caribbean Sea from a tropical Wave. It moved over warm Waters around central America. On august 19 the System had strengthened into tropical Storm Harvey while offshore Honduras. Additional Organization occurred and Harvey attained its peak Intensity of 65mph before coming ashore in Belize on August 20th. Harvey weakened to a tropical depression on AugustÂ 21, but re-intensified to a tropical storm after emerging into the Bay of Campeche. Early on August 22 it made Landfall in Veracruz then weakened and dissipated several Hours later."
