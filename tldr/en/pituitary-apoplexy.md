title: "Pituitary apoplexy"
short_summary: "Medical condition"
description: "Pituitary Apoplexy is a bleeding into or impaired Blood Supply of the pituitary Gland. This usually occurs in the Presence of a Tumor of the pituitary although in 80 of Cases it has not been previously diagnosed. The most common initial Symptom is a sudden Headache often associated with a rapidly worsening visual Field Defect or double Vision caused by Compression of Nerves around the Gland. This is often followed by Acute Symptoms caused by Lack of Secretion of essential Hormones especially adrenal Insufficiency."
