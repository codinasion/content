The Couple's Relationship is soon threatened by an incurable Prion Disease that is spreading across the Nation. Infected Dogs become unnaturally violent and bloodthirsty Culminating in the Deaths of several People. As public Fear Increases and the Government Intervenes to control the Outbreak logan struggles to reform his Life and remain with Jack."
