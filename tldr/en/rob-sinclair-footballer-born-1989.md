Sinclair started his Career in the Youth System at luton Town signing a professional Contract in July 2007. He was loaned out to Conference Premier club Salisbury City in January 2008, until the end of the 2007–08 season. On returning to his Parent Club in may 2008 Sinclair was released and later that Month he joined Salisbury permanently. Injury affected his Time at the Club playing 54 Games over a two-and-a-half-year Spell."
