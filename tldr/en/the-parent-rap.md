description: "The Parent Rap is the second Episode and Official Premiere of the thirteenth Season of american animated Tv Series the Simpsons. It was aired first on november 11 2001 on the Fox Network in the united States. In the Episode Bart and his Father Homer are sentenced by the Cruel Judge Constance Harm to be tied to each other as a Result of Bart stealing Police Chief Wiggum's Car Thanks to homer's Negligence. Eventually, Homer's Wife, Marge, is fed up with the Punishment and cuts the Rope, which instead leads to Judge Harm sentencing them to have their Heads and Hands locked up in wooden Stocks."
