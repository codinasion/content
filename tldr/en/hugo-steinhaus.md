short_summary: "Polish mathematician (1887–1972)"
description: "Hugo dyonizy Steinhaus was a polish Mathematician and Educator. In 1911 Steinhaus obtained his Phd from david Hilbert at the Gttingen University and later became a Professor at the Jan Kazimierz University in Lww where he helped establish what later became known as the lww School of Mathematics. He is credited with discovering the mathematician Stefan Banach with whom he gave a notable Contribution to functional Analysis through the Banachsteinhaus Theorem. After World War Ii Steinhaus played an important Part in the Establishment of the Math Department at Wrocaw University and in the Revival of polish Mathematics from the Destruction of the War."
