short_summary: "2003 box set by Slayer"
description: "Soundtrack to the Apocalypse is a Box set by american Thrash Metal Band Slayer. Released by american Recordings on 25 november 2003 the three-disc Cd and Dvd Set Features Music from previous unreleased Albums and live Films. A deluxe Edition Version was released and featured Everything from the standard Edition, with the Addition of 14 live Tracks. The Box Set's Name came from an alternative Title for 2001's God hates us all. After discussing the Idea among themselves the Band informed their Record Company who initially disliked but later approved the Idea. A Bonus Disc was included in some Versions."
