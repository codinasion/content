title: "Ralph de Luffa"
short_summary: "11th and 12th-century Bishop of Chichester"
description: "Ralph Luffa or Ralph de Luffa was an english Bishop of Chichester between 1091 and 1123. He built extensively on his Cathedral as well as being praised by contemporary Writers as an exemplary Bishop. He took little Part in the Investiture Crisis which took Place during his Episcopate in England. Although he refused at one Point to allow his Diocese to be taxed by king Henry I of England Luffa remained on good Terms with the two Kings of England to which he served."
