description: "Ouvrage La Ferté, also known as Ouvrage Villy-La Ferté, is a petit ouvrage of the Maginot Line, located in the Fortified Sector of Montmédy, facing Belgium. The Ouvrage lies between villy and la Fert-Sur-Chiers. It has two Combat Blocks linked by an underground Gallery. The westernmost Position in its Sector, it was a comparatively weakly armed Fortification in an exposed Position that left it vulnerable to Isolation and Attack. After a sustained Attack during the Battle of France the Position was overwhelmed by german Forces and destroyed with its entire Garrison destroyed. The Fighting at la Fert was the heaviest Position in the Maginot Line. It is preserved as a Memorial of War."
