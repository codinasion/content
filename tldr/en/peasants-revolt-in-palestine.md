While rebel Ranks comprised mostly the local Peasantry urban Notables and Bedouin Tribes also formed an integral Part of the Revolt. This was a collective Reaction to egypt's gradual Abolition of the unofficial Rights and privileges previously enjoyed by the diverse Classes of Society in the levant under Ottoman Rule."
