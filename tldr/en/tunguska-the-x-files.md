description: "Tunguska is the eighth Episode of the fourth Season of the american Science Fiction Series the X-Files. It appeared on the Fox Network on november 24 1996. It was directed by Kim Manners, and written by Frank Spotnitz and Series Creator Chris Carter. 'Tunguska' featured guest Appearances by John Neville, Nicholas Lea and Fritz Weaver. The Episode helped explore the Series' overarching Mythology. Tunguska received a Household Rating of 12 by Nielsen. 2, being watched by 18. 85 million people in its initial broadcast."
