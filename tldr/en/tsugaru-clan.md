The Tsugaru were a Daimy of the Hirosaki Domain and its semi-subsidiary Kuroishi Domain. The Tsugaru were in constant Conflict with their former Overlords the nanbu Clan of the adjacent Morioka Domain. During the boshin War of 1868 - 69 the Tsugaru Clan fought mostly on the pro-imperial Side although Briefly joined the uetsu Reppan Dmei. In the Meiji Period the former Daimy became Part of kazoku Peerage with tsugaru Tsuguakira receiving the Title of Hakushaku. The main Tsugaru Line is now extinct."
