short_summary: "Japanese samurai clan"
description: "The Tsugaru clan was a Japanese samurai clan who ruled the northwestern half of what is now Aomori Prefecture in the Tōhoku region of Japan under the Edo period Tokugawa shogunate. The Tsugaru were a Daimy of the Hirosaki Domain and its semi-subsidiary Kuroishi Domain. The Tsugaru were in constant Conflict with their former Overlords the nanbu Clan of the adjacent Morioka Domain. During the boshin War of 1868 - 69 the Tsugaru Clan fought mostly on the pro-imperial Side although Briefly joined the uetsu Reppan Dmei. In the Meiji Period the former Daimy became Part of kazoku Peerage with tsugaru Tsuguakira receiving the Title of Hakushaku. The main Tsugaru Line is now extinct."
