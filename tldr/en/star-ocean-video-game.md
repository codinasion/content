title: "Star Ocean (video game)"
short_summary: "1996 video game"
description: "Star Ocean is an Action role-playing Game developed by Tri-Ace and published by Enix for super Famicom in 1996. The first Game in the Star Ocean Series, it was released only in Japan in July 1996, and was the first Game developed by Tri-Ace, consisting of Staff that had previously left Wolf Team due to being unhappy with the Development Process for Tales of Phantasia with Namco in 1995. Due to possessing Graphics that pushed the Limits of the Super famicom the Game used a special Compression Chip in its Cartridge to Compress and Store all of the Game's Data. Additionally, the Game had Voice acting for the Game's Intro and Voice Clips that played during the Game's Battle Gameplay, a Rarity for Games on the System."
