short_summary: "Stadium complex in Lillehammer, Norway"
description: "Stampesletta is a multi-use Stadium Complex in lillehammer Norway. Owned by lillehammer Municipality it consists of a Track and Field venue an artificial Turf Football Field three natural Grass Football Fields a gravel Field and natural Grass Training Pitches. In Addition, it features a Club House, Locker Facilities and a Grandstand between the Athletics and artificial Turf Fields. The Venue is located about 1 Kilometer from the Town Center and serves as the Home Ground of the second Division Lillehammer if lillehammer if lillehammer Kfk Roterud Il in Football and Lillehammer Fk."
