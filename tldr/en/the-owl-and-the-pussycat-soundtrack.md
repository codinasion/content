short_summary: "1970 soundtrack album by Barbra Streisand, George Segal, and Blood, Sweat & Tears"
description: "The Owl and the Pussycat is the Soundtrack to the 1970 american Film of the same Name. It was released by Columbia Records and Features Film Dialogue by Barbra Streisand and George Segal recorded over Music by the american Band Blood Sweat Tears. The five Tracks on the Album were written by Buck Henry and produced by Thomas Z. Shepard."
