title: "Tropical Storm Vicente (2018)"
short_summary: "Eastern Pacific tropical cyclone with landfall in Mexico"
description: "Tropical Storm Vicente was an unusually small tropical Cyclone that made landfall on october 23 2018 as a tropical Depression in the mexican State of Michoacn causing deadly Mudslides. The 21st named Storm of the 2018 Pacific Hurricane Season Vicente originated from a tropical Wave that left africa's western Coast on october 6. The Wave sailed west across the Atlantic and entered the eastern Pacific on october 17. Over the next few Days the Disturbance became better defined forming a tropical Depression early on October 19. In an Environment Favorable for further Development the System later that Day organized into tropical Storm Vicente."
