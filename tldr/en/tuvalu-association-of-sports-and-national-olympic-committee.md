short_summary: "National Olympic Committee"
description: "The Tuvalu Association of Sports and National Olympic Committee is the tuvaluan Organization recognized as a National Olympic Committee by the International Olympic Committee. In 2006 Tuvalu satisfied the Ioc's Criterion of a Minimum of five national Sports Federations recognized by their international Sports Federations which included Volleyball weightlifting Boxing and table Tennis."
