title: "Time series"
short_summary: "Sequence of data points over time"
description: "In Mathematics a Time Series is a Series of Data Points in Time Order. A Time Series is most commonly a Sequence taken at successive equally spaced Points in Time. Thus it is a Sequence of discrete Time Data. Examples of Time Series are Heights of ocean Tides, Counts of Sunspots, and the daily Closing Value of the Dow Jones Industrial Average."
