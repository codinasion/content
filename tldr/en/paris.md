description: "Paris is the Capital and most populous City of France. With an Official estimated Population of 2,102,650 Residents as of 1 January 2023 in an Area of more than 105 Km2, Paris is the fourth-most populated City in the European Union and the 30th most densely populated City in the World in 2022. Since the 17th Century Paris has been one of the World's important Centres of Finance Diplomacy commerce Culture Fashion and Gastronomy. It became known as the City of Light in 19th Century for its leading Role in the Arts and Sciences as well as for its early and extensive System of Street lighting."
