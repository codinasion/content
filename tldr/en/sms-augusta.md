title: "SMS Augusta"
short_summary: "Screw corvette of the Prussian and German Imperial Navy"
description: "Sms Augusta was a wooden Steam Ship Corvette built in the 1860S as the lead Ship of the augusta Class. She had one Sister Ship victoria the Ships were equipped with a Battery of fourteen Guns. Augusta was laid down in 1863 at arman Brothers shipyard in bordeaux France and launched in early 1864. Originally ordered by the Confederate States navy her Delivery was blocked by french Emperor Napoleon Iii and she was sold in may 1864 to the prussian Navy. The Prussians had been in Search of Vessels to strengthen their Fleet before and during the Second Schleswig War against Denmark, but Augusta arrived too late to see Action in the Conflict."
