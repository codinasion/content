short_summary: "Light cruiser of the German Imperial Navy"
description: "Sms Gazelle was the lead Ship of the gazelle Class of Light Cruisers which were built in the late 1890S for the german Kaiserliche Marine. The gazelle Class was the Culmination of earlier unprotected Cruiser and aviso Designs combining the best Aspects of both Types into what became the Progenitor of all future light Cruisers of the imperial Fleet. Built to be able to serve with the main german Fleet and as a Colonial Cruiser, she was armed with a Battery of ten 10. 5 cm guns and a top speed of 19. 5 knots. Her niclausse Boilers proved troublesome in Service and were later replaced in the Mid-1900S."
