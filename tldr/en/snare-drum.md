title: "Snare drum"
short_summary: "Type of percussion instrument"
description: "The Snare Drum is a Percussion Instrument that produces a sharp Staccato Sound when the Head is struck with a Drum Stick due to the Use of a Series of stiff Wires held under Tension against the lower Skin. Snare Drums are often used in orchestras Concert Bands marching Bands Parades Drumlines drum Corps and many more. It is one of the central Pieces in a Drum set a Collection of Percussion Instruments designed to be played by a sitting Drummer and used in many Genres of Music. Because basic Rhythms are very easy to learn to play on a Snare Drum even for Children, the Instrument is also suitable for the Music Education for young Children and a Rhythm Band."
