The Episode written by Hideaki Anno and akio Satsukawa and directed by Seiji Mizushima was first Broadcast on november 29 1995 on Tv Tokyo. The Series is set fifteen Years after a worldwide Cataclysm and is mostly set in the futuristic fortified City of tokyo 3. The Protagonist of the Series is shinji Ikari a Teenage Boy who is recruited by his Father Gendo to nerv to pilot a gigantic bio-mechanical Mecha named Evangelion into Combat with Beings called Angels. The Episode follows two Nerv mecha Pilots, Asuka Langley Soryu and Shinji, who must defeat an Angel Capable of splitting into two Individuals, Israfel."
