title: "Velepromet camp"
short_summary: "Detention facility during the Croatian War of Independence."
description: "The Camp of Velepromet was a Detention Facility established during the final Days of the Battle of Vukovar during the croatian War of Independence. The Camp was established by the Yugoslav People's Army which shared Control of the Facility with croatian Serb Rebels. The Facility originally an industrial Storage Site was located on the southern Suburbs of the City of Vukovar in close Proximity to the Jna Barracks. It consisted of eight Warehouses surrounded by a Wire Fence and was established on 16 november 1991 when the first Detainees were brought there."
