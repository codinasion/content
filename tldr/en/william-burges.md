title: "William Burges"
short_summary: "English Gothic revival architect and designer (1827–1881)"
description: "William Burges was an english Architect and Designer. Among the greatest of the victorian Art-Architects, he sought in his Work to escape from both nineteenth-century Industrialisation and the neoclassical architectural Style and re-establish the architectural and social Values of a utopian Medieval England. Burges Stands within the Tradition of the gothic Revival his Works echo those of the Pre-Raphaelites and symbolise the Arts and Crafts Movement."
