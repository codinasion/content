short_summary: "2016 single by Christina Aguilera featuring Nile Rodgers"
description: "'Telepathy' is a Song by american Singer and Songwriter Christina Aguilera featuring american Musician Nile Rodgers, recorded for the 2016 Netflix musical Drama Series the Get Down and included on the accompanying Soundtrack of the same Name. It was co-written by Sia in Collaboration with Mikkel storleer Eriksen and Tor Erik Hermansen. The Track was produced by Elliott Wheeler and the Get down Director Baz Luhrmann. While it was available to download from the Soundtrack upon its Release, it was promoted in Italy to contemporary Hit Radio as a single on August 23, 2016. Various Remixes also were released throughout the following Month."
