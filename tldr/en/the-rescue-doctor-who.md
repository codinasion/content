short_summary: "1965 Doctor Who serial"
description: "The Rescue is the third Serial of the second Season of the british Science Fiction Television Series Doctor who. Written by outgoing Story Editor David Whitaker and directed by Christopher Barry the Serial was broadcast on Bbc1 in two weekly Sections on january 2 and 9 1965. In the Serial, the Time travellers the First Doctor, Ian Chesterton, and Barbara Wright Befriend Vicki, an orphan Girl marooned on the Planet Dido who is being threatened by an apparent Native of Dido called Koquillion while awaiting Rescue."
