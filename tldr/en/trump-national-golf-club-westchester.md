short_summary: "Private golf club in Briarcliff Manor, New York"
description: "Trump National Golf Club Westchester is a private Golf Club in Briarcliff Manor, New York. The 140-acre Course has 18 Holes with a 75000 square Foot Clubhouse. Founded in 1922 as Briarcliff Country Club, it later operated as Briar Hills Country Club and Briar Hall Golf and Country Club. In 1996 the former President of the united States donald Trump purchased the Property and renamed the Club Westchester after its County in a similar Way to his other Golf Properties. He rebuilt the Clubhouse and the Course for its 2002 reopening the Course was designed by Jim Fazio. Donald Trump served as President over Trump National Golf Club Llc from August 2000 until January 19, 2017, the Day before his Inauguration."
