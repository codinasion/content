It was originally announced in 2012 with the Intention of meeting the Campus Population of nearby University of Minnesota. The complex comprises 327 residential Units that are distributed in three Apartment Towers. It also contains designated retail Space, currently leased by Tenants such as Bank of America, Blaze Pizza, and local Restaurants."
