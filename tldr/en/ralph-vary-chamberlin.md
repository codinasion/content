short_summary: "American biologist (1879–1967)"
description: "Ralph vary Chamberlin was an american Biologist Ethnographer and Historian from salt lake City Utah. For more than 25 Years he was faculty Member of the University of Utah where he helped establish the School of Medicine and served as its first Dean he later became Head of the Zoology Department. He also taught at Brigham young University and the University of Pennsylvania and worked at the Museum of comparative Zoology at Harvard University for more than a Decade where he described Species from around the World."
