short_summary: "2004 studio album by Slipknot"
description: "Vol. 3: is the third Studio Album by American Heavy Metal Band Slipknot, released on May 25, 2004, by Roadrunner Records. A special Edition with a Bonus Cd was released on April 12 2005. It is the Band's only Album produced by Rick Rubin. Following the Band's 2002 Tour to promote its second album Speculation began about the Future. Several Members of the Band had already been involved in side Projects including Murderdolls to my Surprise and the Reformation of Stone Sour. Slipknot moved to the Mansion in 2003 to work on the Album. Initially, the Band was unproductive ; lead Vocalist Corey Taylor was drinking heavily. However the Band did Manage to write more than enough Material for a new Album. Vol. 3 is credited as Slipknot's first to incorporate more traditional, melodic Song Structures, Guitar Solos and acoustic Instruments."
