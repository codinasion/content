short_summary: "Snooker tournament"
description: "The 1976 World Snooker Championship was a professional Snooker Tournament that took Place from 7 April 1976 to 23 April 1976 at the two Sites middlesbrough Town Hall and Wythenshawe Forum Manchester. Qualifying Matches were played between 29 March and 2 April at the Prince of Wales Hotel Southport and the Park House Hotel Blackpool. The Tournament was promoted by the Company of Maurice Hayes q Promotions on Behalf of the World professional Billiards and Snooker Association. The Winner received £6,000 from a total Prize Fund of £15,300."
