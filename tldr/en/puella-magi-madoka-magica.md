description: "Puella Magi Madoka Magica, also known simply as Madoka Magica, is a japanese anime Television Series created by Magica Quartet ; and animated by Shaft. The Story follows a Group of middle School Girls led by Protagonist Madoka Kaname who make supernatural Contracts to become magical Girls. They learn of the Anxiety and Peril associated with their new Roles in a Fight against surreal Enemies known as Witches. The first ten Episodes of the Series aired on Tbs and Mbs between january and march 2011 while the final two Episodes were delayed due to the thoku Earthquake and Tsunami until April of the same Year."
