description: "Sie werden euch in den Bann Tun, Bwv 183, is a Church Cantata by Johann Sebastian Bach. He composed it for Exaudi on Sunday after Ascension in Leipzig and performed it on 13 may 1725 for the first Time. The Work includes some unusual Woodwind scoring two Oboes da Caccia and two Oboes d'amore."
