short_summary: "Military campaign during the Vietnam War"
description: "The Tet Offensive was a major Escalation and one of the largest military Campaigns in the Vietnam War. The Viet Cong and North Vietnamese People's Army of Vietnam launched a sneak Attack on January 30, 1968, against the Forces of the South vietnamese Army of the Republic of Vietnam, the United States Armed Forces and their Allies. It was a Surprise Campaign of Attacks against military and civilian Command and Control Centers throughout south Vietnam. The Name is the truncated Version of the Lunar New Year Festival Name in vietnamese, Tết Nguyên Đán, with the offense Chosen during a holiday Period as most Arvn Personnel were on Leave. The Purpose of the broad-scale Offensive by the Hanoi Politburo was to trigger political Instability in the Belief that Mass armed Assault on urban Centers would trigger Defections and Rebellions."
