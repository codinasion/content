short_summary: "Type of pale lager beer"
description: "Three Horses Beer is a Pale Lager which is brewed by Star Breweries of Madagascar since 1958. It is the highest-selling Beer in Madagascar and has been described as emblematic of the Country. Thb is sold nationwide and, since 2005, has been exported to such Markets as France, Reunion Island, Comoros, and Mayotte. Malagasy Beer is produced at two Breweries in Madagascar the first centrally in Antsirabe and the other in the northern City of Antsiranana. Thb pilsener the most common Variant of Thb has a light Taste and is produced mostly from local Barley Corn and Hops. Star Breweries also produces Thb Fresh, Thb Special, and Thb Lite. Recent Investments in Star Brewery Infrastructure have allowed a 20 % Increase in Production since 2011."
