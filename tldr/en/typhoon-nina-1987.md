description: "Typhoon nina known as Typhoon Sisang in the Philippines was the strongest Typhoon to strike the Philippines since Typhoon Irma in 1981. In november 1987 Typhoon Nina swept through a Convection Area near the Marshall Islands. It gradually became more organized and was classified as a tropical Cyclone on november 19th. Moving west-northwest Nina achieved tropical Storm Intensity in the Evening. Late on november 20 Nina travelled through the Chuuk Lagoon in the federated States of Micronesia. On november 22 Nina intensified after a brief Pause in Intensification into a Typhoon. Two Days later the Typhoon suddenly intensified before attaining its peak Intensity of 10 Minutes at 165kmh. During the Afternoon of november 25 Nina moved at the same Speed into southern Luzon. It slowly weakened over Land before entering the south China Sea and turning to the North.
