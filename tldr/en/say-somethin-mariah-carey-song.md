short_summary: "2006 single by Mariah Carey"
description: "Say Somethin' is a Song by american Singer and Songwriter Mariah Carey. It was written by Carey, Snoop Dogg, Chad Hugo, and Pharrell Williams, and produced by the Neptunes as the sixth and final single from Carey's tenth Studio Album, the Emancipation of Mimi. The Song is one of few Songs from Carey's Catalog in which she does not Share Production Credits. 'Say Somethin'' Features Dogg as a Guest Artist, and is influenced by R & B and hip-hop Music Genres. Lyrically, the Song is a Dialogue in between and Male and Female, that discuss sexual Themes and Acts of which they plan to engage in a Restroom."
