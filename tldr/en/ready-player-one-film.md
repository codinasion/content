description: "Ready Player one is a 2018 american Action Film based on ernest Cline's Novel of the same Name. The Film was co-produced and directed by Steven Spielberg, written by Cline and Zak Penn, and Stars Tye Sheridan, Olivia Cooke, Ben Mendelsohn, Lena Waithe, T. J. Miller, Simon Pegg, and Mark Rylance. The Film is set in 2045 where much of Humanity uses the virtual Reality Simulation Oasis to escape the real World. A Teenage orphan finds Clues to a Contest that promises the Owner of Oasis to the Winner and he and his Allies try to complete it before an evil Corporation can."
