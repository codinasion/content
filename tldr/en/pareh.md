description: "Pareh, released internationally as Pareh, Song of the Rice, is a 1936 Film from the Dutch East Indies. Directed by the Dutchman Albert Balink and Mannus franken it starred an amateur native Cast and starred raden Mochtar and Soekarsih. The Story follows the forbidden Love between a Fisherman and the Daughter of a Farmer."
