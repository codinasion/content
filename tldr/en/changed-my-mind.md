short_summary: "2018 single by Tove Styrke"
description: "'changed My Mind' is a Song by swedish Singer Tove Styrke from her third Studio Album Sway. The Song was written by Singer Elof Loelv Michael Trewartha kyle Trewartha Kathryn Ostenberg and Linnea Sdahl with Production by Loelv. It was released on 2 february 2018 through Sony Music as the third single from the Album. The Synth-Pop and tropical House Song includes dancehall Influences and Vocoder Effects. Its Lyrics detail an On-Off-Off Relationship where the Singer changes her Mind about her Partner."
