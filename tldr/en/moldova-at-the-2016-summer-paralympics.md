description: "Moldova sent a Delegation to participate in the 2016 Summer Paralympics in Rio de janeiro Brazil from 7 to 18 September 2016. It was the eastern european Country's sixth Appearance in the Summer Paralympic Games since their Debut twenty Years prior at the Summer Paralympics 1996. Moldova sent three Athletes to these Games shot-put Thrower Oxana Spataur Powerlifter Larisa Marinenkova and Short-Distance Swimmer Alexandr Covaliov. Spataur qualified on Merit and Covaliov and Marienkova were invited by the Bipartite Commission. No Spataur nor Covaliov advanced from the Heats of their Events and Marienkova finished seventh in the Women's 73 Kg powerlifting Category."
