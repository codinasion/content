title: "South Wales Brigade"
short_summary: "Military unit"
description: "The south Wales brigade was a territorial Force Infantry Brigade of the british Army. Formed in 1908, it was not Part of any Division, instead serving as Army Troops, though attached to the Welsh Division in Peacetime. It was dissolved in early 1915 as its Constituent Battalions were sent to other Formations."
