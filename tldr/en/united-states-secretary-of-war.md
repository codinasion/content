title: "United States Secretary of War"
short_summary: "Position in the United States Cabinet from 1789 to 1947"
description: "The secretary of war was a member of the U. S. President's Cabinet, beginning with George Washington's Administration. A similar Position called Secretary at War or Secretary of War had been appointed between 1781 and 1789 to serve the Confederation Confederation Congress under the Articles of Confederation. Benjamin Lincoln and later Henry Knox held the Position. When Washington was inaugurated as the first President under the Constitution he appointed Knox as Secretary of War."
