description: "Ice Hockey Hair is an Ep by the Welsh alternative Rock Band Super Furry Animals, released in 1998. The Record contains four Songs which the Band felt did not fit in with either their previous Album, 1997's Radiator, or its follow-up Guerrilla. The Title Song Refers to an alternative Name for Mullet Hairstyle. The Opening Song Smokin' on the Ep was commissioned by british Television Station Channel 4 for a Programme about Sloth presented by Howard Marks. Ice Hockey Hair was later included on the'greatest Hits' Compilation songbook the Singles vol. 1 was released in 2004 while smokin' appeared on the 1998 B-Side and Rarities compilation out spaced."
