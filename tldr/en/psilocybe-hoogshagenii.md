description: "Psilocybe Hoogshagenii is a psilocybin Species of the Hymenogastraceae Family. The Mushroom has a brownish Conical or bell-shaped Cap up to 3cm wide which has an extended Papilla up to 4mm Long. The Stem is slender and 5 to 9 Cm long. The Variety P. hoogshagenii var. The Convexa lacks a long Papilla."
