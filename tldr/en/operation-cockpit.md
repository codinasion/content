short_summary: "April 1944 British and American aircraft carrier raid on the Japanese-held island of Sabang"
description: "Operation Cockpit was an allied Attack on the Japanese-Held Sabang Island on April 19 1944. It was carried out by Aircraft from british and american Aircraft Carriers and targeted japanese Shipping and Airfields. A small Number of japanese Ships and Aircraft were destroyed and an american Aircraft was lost. While the Attack was tactically successful it did not divert japanese Forces from other Areas as had been hoped."
