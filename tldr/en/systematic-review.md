title: "Systematic review"
short_summary: "Comprehensive review of research literature using systematic methods"
description: "A systematic Review is a scholarly Synthesis of the Evidence on a clearly presented Topic using critical Methods to identify, define and assess Research on the Topic. A systematic Review Extracts and Interprets data from published Studies on the Topic, then analyzes, Describes, critically Appraises and summarizes Interpretations into a refined evidence-based Conclusion. In general a systematic Review of randomized controlled Trials is a Way of summarizing evidence-based Medicine."
