It aired on the Fox Network in the united States on December 16 1993. Springfield legalizes gambling in the Episode to revitalize its Economy. Mr. Burns opens a Casino where Homer is hired as a Blackjack Dealer. Marge develops a Gambling Addiction, Bart opens a Casino in his Treehouse, and Burns' Appearance and mental State Deteriorate in a Parody of Howard Hughes."
