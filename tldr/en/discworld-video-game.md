It is based on Terry Pratchett's Novels of the same Name. Players take the Role of Rincewind the Wizzard voiced by eric idle as he becomes involved in exploring the Discworld for the Means to prevent a Dragon terrorising Ankh-Morpork. The game's story borrows elements from several Discworld novels, with its central plot loosely based on the events in Guards! Guards!"
