description: "Tracy Enid Flick is a fictional Character who is the Subject of the 1998 novel Election by Tom Perrotta and portrayed by reese Witherspoon in the 1999 Film Adaptation of the same Title. She is a smart ambitious high School Student whose Quest to win a School Election is nearly derailed by her Teacher. She is the main Antagonist in both the Book and Film."
