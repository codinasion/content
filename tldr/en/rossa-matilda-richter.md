title: "Rossa Matilda Richter"
short_summary: "English aerialist and actor who became the first human cannonball"
description: "Rossa Matilda Richter who used the Stage Name Zazel was an english Aerialist and Actress who became known as the first human Cannonball at the Age of 17. At a very young Age she began to perform performing aerial Stunts like Tightrope Walking in an old London Church. She began to learn Ballet Gymnastics and Trapeze by the Age of 6 and went on Tour with a traveling Acrobat Troupe at 12. In 1877 she was the first to be shot from a Cannon before a large Crowd at the Royal Aquarium."
