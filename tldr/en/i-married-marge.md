In the Episode Marge Worries about being pregnant again and Visits dr. Hibbert's Office. While anxiously waiting for Home Homer Tells bart Lisa and maggie the Story of their Marriage and the Birth of Bart. The episode was written by Jeff Martin and directed by Jeffrey Lynch."
