description: "The Legend of Zelda Phantom Hourglass is an action-adventure Game developed and published by Nintendo for the nintendo Ds handheld Game Console in 2007. It is the fourteenth Installment in the Series the Legend of Zelda and direct Sequel to the 2002 Gamecube title the Wind Waker. Phantom Hourglass was released Worldwide in 2007 with the Exception of south Korea in April 2008. The Game was re-released in november 2015 in north America through the virtual Console Service in the pal Region in may 2016 and in Japan in August."
