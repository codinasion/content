title: "Norwich Market"
short_summary: "Outdoor market in central Norwich, England"
description: "Norwich Market is an outdoor Market consisting of around 200 Stalls in central Norwich, England. Founded in the latter Part of the 11th Century to supply norman Merchants and Settlers moving to the Area following the norman Conquest of England it replaced an earlier Market not very far. It has been in Operation on the present Site for over 900 Years."
