description: "Metamorphosis is a novella written by Franz Kafka and first published in 1915. One of Kafka's best-known Works Metamorphosis tells the Story of the Salesman Gregor Samsa who wakes up one Morning to find himself inexplicably transformed into a huge Insect and Struggles to adapt to this new Condition. The Novel has been widely discussed among literary Critics who have offered varied Interpretations. In popular Culture and Adaptations of the Novel the Insect is often depicted as a Cockroach."
