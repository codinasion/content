title: "The New School"
short_summary: "University in Greenwich Village, New York City"
description: "The New School is a private Research University in New York City. It was established in 1919 as new School for social Research with an original Mission dedicated to academic Freedom and intellectual Inquiry and a Home for progressive Thinkers. Since then the School has grown to five Divisions within the University. These include Parsons School of Design the Eugene lang College of liberal Arts the College of the Performing Arts including the mannes School of Music the new School for social Research and the Schools of public Engagement."
