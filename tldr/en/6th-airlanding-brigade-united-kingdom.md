description: "The 6th airlanding Brigade was an airborne Infantry Brigade of the british Army during the second World War. The Brigade was created in may 1943 and consisted of three Glider Infantry Battalions and supporting Units and was assigned to the 6th airborne Division alongside the 3rd and 5th Parachute Brigade."
