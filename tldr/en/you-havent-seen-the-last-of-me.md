description: "You have n't see the last of me is a Song performed by the american Singer-Actress Cher from the Soundtrack Burlesque original Motion Picture Soundtrack of the Accompanying Film Burlesque. After a long Hiatus in her acting Career Cher returned to the Screen in Burlesque in which christina Aguilera Stars. It was released as the first single from the Soundtrack of Rca Records on january 15 2011 to an Adult contemporary Radio Station in the united States. Prior to the Release, a Remix Ep which includes uptempo Dance Versions of the Song was made available to purchase Itunes Stores on November 24, 2010. Written by Diane Warren and produced by Matt Serletic and Mark Taylor, 'you have n't Seen the last of Me' is a Power Ballad."
