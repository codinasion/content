description: "The Sun Bear is a Species of ursidae Family that is found in the tropical Forests of Southeast Asia. It is the smallest bear Species, standing nearly 70 Cm at the Shoulder and weighing 25–65 Kg. It is stockily built, with large paws, strongly curved claws, small, rounded ears and a short snout. The Fur is typically short and jet black but can range from Grey to red. The Sun Bear got its Name from its characteristic Orange to cream chest Patch. Its unique morphology—inward-turned front feet, flattened chest, powerful forelimbs with large claws—suggests adaptations for climbing."
