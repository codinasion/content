short_summary: "1914–1918 global conflict"
description: "World War i or the first World War was a global Conflict Fought between two Coalitions: the Allies and the Central Powers. Battles took Place throughout europe the Middle East africa the Pacific and Parts of Asia. One of the deadliest Wars in History, it resulted in an estimated 9 million Soldiers dead and 23 million wounded, plus another 5 million civilian Deaths from various Causes. Millions more People died as a Result of Genocide and the War was a major Factor in the 1918 spanish Flu Pandemic."
