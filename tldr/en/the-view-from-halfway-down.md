It was written by Alison Tafel and directed by amy Winfrey. The Episode was originally released as one of eight Episodes made available on january 31 2020 to streaming via Netflix. Guest stars in this episode include Stanley Tucci, Kristen Schaal, Wendie Malick, Lin-Manuel Miranda, Brandon T. Jackson, and Zach Braff."
