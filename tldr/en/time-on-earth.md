short_summary: "2007 studio album by Crowded House"
description: "Time on Earth is the fifth Studio Album by the pop-rock Band Crowded House. Tracks were produced by both ethan Johns and steve Lillywhite and the Album was released in Australia on June 30 2007 and in the united Kingdom on July 2 and in north America on July 10. Time on Earth is the Band's first Studio Album since 1993 and marks the Reunion of the Band 11 Years after they disbanded it features new Drummer Matt Sherrod in Place of the original Drummer Paul Hester who died in 2005."
