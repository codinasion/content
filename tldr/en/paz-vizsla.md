description: "Paz Vizsla is a fictional Character in the Star Wars franchise. He first appeared as a Member of the same Tribe of mandalorian Warriors in the Service of the Armorer in the first Season of the Disney Television Series the mandalorian as the titular Character with whom vizsla initially Clashes but later Assists in repelling. The two had another Altercation in the spin-off Series the Book of Boba Fett over Ownership of the Darksaber a legendary Lightsaber forked by Paz's Ancestor Tar. He then returned in the third Season of the Mandalorian, in which he dies protecting Bo-Katan Kryze."
