description: "Abu' l-walid Ismail Ibn Faraj was from 1314 to 1325 the fifth nasrid Ruler of the Emirat Granada on the iberian Peninsula. A Grandson of muhammad Ii on the Side of his Mother Fatima he was the first of the Line of Sultans now known as al-dawla al-isma'iliyya Al-Nas. Historians characterise him as an effective Ruler who improved the Emirate's Position during his Reign with military Victories."
