description: "Turn up the Music is a Song by the american Singer chris brown. It was written by Alexander Fuego Palmer brown damon Thomas Harvey Mason Jr. Michael mike j Jimenez Terence Coles and Agent X were involved in the Production while Production was handled by Fuego and the Underdogs. Turn up the Music was sent to the contemporary Hit Radio Playlists in the united States on february 7 2012 as the Lead single from Brown's fifth Studio Album Fortune. It was released on february 10 2012 as a digital Download. Turn up the Music is an Uptempo Song which draws from electronic Dance and House Genres. Instrumentation consists of pulsating Beats, Synthesizers, a 'throbbing Bass' and Percussion. According to Mason Jr. The Inspiration came from visualizing how he dances and Performs as an Artist and giving him Something to match that Energy."
