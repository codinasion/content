short_summary: "2012 dungeon crawler video game"
description: "Silent Hill: Book of Memories is a 2012 Dungeon Crawler Video Game developed by Wayforward Technologies and published by Konami. Released for the Playstation Vita, it is a Spin-Off of the Silent Hill Video Game Series. It features a Player created Protagonist who receives the titular Book on their Birthday which contains their entire Life Story and makes Changes to it with unexpected and often unfavorable Consequences. Book of Memories Features gameplay Elements from role-playing Games: as the Player Character traverses the Dungeons and defeats Monsters there, they gain Experience Points and thus improve their respective Abilities. The Character progresses from each Theme Level by collecting Pieces of a Puzzle and then solving it. Five Endings are available depending on the Character's Alignment the sixth is a Joke ending in the Tradition of Silent Hill Games."
