description: "King's Highway 28, commonly referred to as Highway 28, is a provincially maintained Highway in the canadian Province of Ontario. The Route from the Southwest to the Northeast extends from highway7 East of Peterborough to highway41 in Denbigh. The Route passes through andulating Hills before entering the canadian Shield near burleigh Falls and gradually turns Eastwards."
