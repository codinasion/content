short_summary: "2008 3D concert film with U2"
description: "U2 3d is a 2008 american-produced 3d Concert Film featuring the Rock Band u2 Performing during the 2006 Vertigo Tour. Directed by Catherine Owens and Mark Pellington, the Film contains Performances of 14 Songs, including Tracks from How to dismantle an Atomic Bomb, the Album that the Tour supported. The Concert Footage includes political and social Statements made during the Shows. It is the Band's second Feature Film, following their 1988 rockumentary Rattle and Hum. Among several cinematic Firsts, U2 3d was the first live-action digital 3d Film."
