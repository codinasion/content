title: "Shale oil extraction"
short_summary: "Process for extracting oil from oil shale"
description: "Shale Oil Extraction is an industrial Process for unconventional Oil Production. This Process converts Kerogen in Oil Shale to shale Oil by Pyrolysis Hydrogenation or thermal Dissolution. The resultant shale Oil is used as fuel Oil or is upgraded by adding Hydrogen and removing Sulfur and Nitrogen Impurities to meet refinery Feedstock Specifications."
