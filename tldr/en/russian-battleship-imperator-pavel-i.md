short_summary: "Russian Andrei Pervozvanny-class battleship"
description: "Imperator Pavel I was one of two predreadnought Battleships of the andrei Pervozvanny Class built for the imperial russian Navy in the first Decade of 20th Century. The Ship's Construction was greatly delayed by Design Changes as a Result of the russo-japanese War and Labor unrest after the 1905 Revolution and took nearly six Years to build. Imperator Pavel i was not very active during World War i and her bored Sailors were the first to mutiny in early 1917. The Ship was laid up in 1918 and she was scrapped in 1923."
