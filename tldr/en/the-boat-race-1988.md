title: "The Boat Race 1988"
short_summary: "Oxford versus Cambridge rowing race"
description: "The 134th Boat Race took Place on April 2 1988. Held annually the Event is a side-by-side Rowing Race on the River thames between Crews from the Universities of Oxford and Cambridge. Oxford won by 512 Lengths in a Time of 18 Minutes and 27 Seconds this was the equal fourth fastest Time in the History of the Event. The Race was umpired by former Cambridge rowing blue Mike Sweeney."
