description: "The Wood Thrush is a north american Passerine Bird in the turdidae Family and is the only Species within the Genus Hylocichla. It is closely related to other Thrushes such as the american Robin and is widespread across north America Wintering in central America and southern Mexico. The wood thrush is the official bird of the District of Columbia."
