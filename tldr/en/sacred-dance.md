title: "Sacred dance"
short_summary: "Use of dance in religious ceremonies and rituals"
description: "Sacred Dance is the Use of Dance in religious Ceremonies and Rituals present in most Religions throughout the History and Prehistory. Several Religions including Islam and Christianity Prohibit dancing due to its Relationship with the human Body and Fertility. Dance has formed a major Element of Worship in Hindu Temples with strict formalized Styles such as Bharatanatyam which require skilled Dancers and Temple Musicians. In the 20th Century sacred Dance was revived as a Method of promoting Community Spirit by Choreographers such as Bernhard Wosien."
