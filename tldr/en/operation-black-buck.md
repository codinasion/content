title: "Operation Black Buck"
short_summary: "Series of British long-range bombing raids during the Falklands War"
description: "Operation black Buck 1 to black Buck 7 were seven extremely long-range Ground Attack Missions conducted by royal Air Force vulcan Bombers of the Raf Waddington wing during the Falklands War 1982. 44, 50 and 101 Squadrons, against Argentine Positions in the Falkland Islands. Five of the Missions completed Attacks. The Mission's Objective was to attack the Port Stanley Airport and associated Defences. The Raids, at almost 6,600 nautical Miles and 16 Hours for the Round Trip, were the longest-ranged Bombing Raids in History at that Time."
