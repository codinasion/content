Winstead and Paul play a married Couple, Kate and Charlie Hannah, both Alcoholics. Kate decided to get sober after a Series of embarrassing Incidents caused by her Drinking Habit with the Help of her Coworker and a Sponsor from Alcoholics anonymous."
