description: "Vivah is a 2006 indian Hindi-Language romantic Drama Film, written and directed by Sooraj R. Barjatya. Produced and distributed by Rajshri Productions, the Film Stars Shahid Kapoor and Amrita Rao as Lead, alongside Anupam Kher, Alok Nath, Seema Biswas, Samir Soni and Lata Sabharwal. Vivah tells the Story of two People and describes their Journey from Engagement to marriage and the Consequences."
