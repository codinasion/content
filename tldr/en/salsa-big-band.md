description: "Salsa Big Band is the fourth Studio Album by Rubén Blades and Roberto Delgado & Orquesta, released on 29 April 2017 by Rubén Blades Productions. After the Release of Son de Panamá in 2015, also recorded with Delgado & Orquesta, Blades decided to record another Album with them, on which included eleven Songs. He had previously recorded nine Songs and two of them were Covers of Songs written by Luis Demetrio and Rene Touzet. Delgado was the Producer of the Lbum. Blades' principal Inspiration for the Album was his Admiration for puerto rican Performers such as tito Puente Tito Rodrguez and Willie Rosario and the big Band Sounds of the 1950S."
