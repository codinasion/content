It is a silvery-metallic Transition Metal chemically similar to Lanthanides and has often been classified as a rare Earth Element. Yttrium is almost always found in Combination with lanthanide Elements in rare Earth Minerals and is never found as a free Element in Nature. 89y is the only stable Isotope and the only Isotope found on Earth's Crust."
