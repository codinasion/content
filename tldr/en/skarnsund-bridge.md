short_summary: "Bridge in Inderøy, Norway"
description: "The Skarnsund Bridge is a 1010-metre long concrete Cable-Stayed Bridge that crosses the skarnsundet Strait in the Municipality of Indery in the trndelag County Norway. When completed in 1991 it replaced the Vangshyllakjerringvik Ferry giving Communities in the Municipalities of Mosvik and Leksvik easier Access to the central Areas of innherred. The Bridge is the only Road Crossing of Trondheimsfjord and is located along the 755 norwegian County Road."
