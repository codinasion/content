short_summary: "Oxford versus Cambridge rowing race"
description: "The 159th Boat Race between Crews from University of Oxford and University of Cambridge took Place on March 31 2013. Held annually the Event is a side-by-side Rowing Race along the River thames between Crews from Universities oxford and cambridge. The Cambridge Crew included the first Rower from czech Republic to compete in the Event. Oxford was won by a Margin of 112 Lengths in a Time of 17 Minutes and 27 Seconds contested by ex-olympic Medallist and former Oxford Rower Matthew Pinsent."
