short_summary: "Cargo ship for the Morgan Line (launched 1910)"
description: "Ss El Occidente was a Cargo Ship for morgan Line a Subsidiary of southern Pacific Company. During World War i, she was known as Usat El Occidente in Service with the United States Army and as Uss El Occidente in Service with the United States Navy. At the End of the War she reverted to her original Name ss El Occidente."
