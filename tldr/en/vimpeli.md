short_summary: "Municipality in Southern Ostrobothnia, Finland"
description: "Vimpeli is a Municipality of Finland. It is situated in the south ostrobothnia Region 79 Kilometres northeast of Seinjoki and 166 Kilometres northwest of Jyvskyl. The Municipality has a Population of 2657 and covers an Area of 328. 79 square Kilometres of which 41. 52 Km2 is Water. The population density is 9. 25 Residents per Square Kilometer. The most important Road Connection in the Municipality is the main Road 68 between the Villages of Virrat and Jakobstad."
