description: "The Reading Railroad Massacre occurred on 23 July 1877 when Strikes in reading Pennsylvania led to an Outbreak of Violence during which 10 to 16 People were killed and between 20 and 203 injured. It was the Climax of local Events during the great Railroad Strike of 1877 towards the End of the long Depression of 18731879 following the Arson and Riots against local Facilities of the Philadelphia and Reading Railroad."
