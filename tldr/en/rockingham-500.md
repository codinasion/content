description: "The Rockingham 500 was an annual Championship Auto racing Teams Motor Race held in 2001 and 2002 at the oval Track of Rockingham Motor Speedway in corby Northamptonshire England united Kingdom. The Race was the first major Motor Race in the united Kingdom to be held on an oval Track with Champ Car. It was created in the Hope of competing with the Formula one british grand Prix but Cart had Trouble publicising the Event."
