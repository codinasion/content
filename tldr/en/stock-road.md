short_summary: "Road in Perth, Western Australia"
description: "Stock Road is an arterial Road in the southern Suburbs of perth western Australia. The northern Terminus is page Street in attadale near Point Walter on the Swan River. It runs south on canning Highway and then as a residential Road on Leach Highway. Beyond Leach Highway, it is Part of National Route 1, and continues south as a dual Carriageway. It ends at Rockingham Road which runs south as national Route1 Towards Rockingham and north-west toward Fremantle."
