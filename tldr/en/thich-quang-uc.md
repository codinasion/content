short_summary: "Vietnamese Buddhist monk and self-immolator (1897–1963)"
description: "Thch Qung C was a vietnamese Mahayana Buddhist Monk who died on June 11 1963 by Self-Immolation at a busy Intersection on Saigon Road. Quảng Đức was protesting against the Persecution of Buddhists by the South vietnamese Government of Ngô Đình Diệm, a staunch Roman Catholic. Photographs of his Self-Immolation circulated around the World, drawing Attention to the Policies of the Diệm Government. John F. Kennedy said of one photograph, 'No news picture in history has generated so much emotion around the world as that one. Malcolm Browne won the World Press Photo of the Year for his Photograph of the Death of the Monk."
