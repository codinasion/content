title: "Remember December"
short_summary: "2010 single by Demi Lovato"
description: "'remember December' is a Song by american Singer Demi Lovato for her second Studio Album, here we go again. It was written by John Fields lovato and anne Preven and produced by Fields. The Song was released in Europe on 18 january 2010 by Hollywood Records as the second and final single on the Album. Remember December Adapts Powerpop and synth Pop Genres and Diverges from Lovato's usual Pop Rock Sound. The Lyrics describe the Speaker's Attempt to remind her Boyfriend of the good Times in their Relationship. Lovato described the Sound of the Song as an Example of what she wanted her future Music to sound like."
