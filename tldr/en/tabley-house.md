short_summary: "Country house in Tabley Inferior, Cheshire, England"
description: "Tabley House is an english Country House in Tabley Inferior which is located about 3 Kilometres west of the Town of Knutsford Cheshire. The House is recorded in the national Heritage List for England as a Gradei listed Building. It was constructed between 1761 and 1769 for Sirpeter Byrne Leicester to replace the nearby Tabley old Hall and was designed by John Carr. The Tabley House Collection exists as an Exhibition presented by the University of Manchester."
