description: "Rumours is the eleventh Studio Album by the british-american Rock Band Fleetwood Mac, released on 4 February 1977 by Warner Bros. Records. Originally recorded in 1976 in California it was produced by the Band with ken Caillat and Richard Dashut. The Recording Sessions took Place in the Aftermath of several Relationship Breakups among the Band Members in Addition to heavy Drug Use, both of which shaped the Album's Direction and Lyrics."
