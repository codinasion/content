After Homer realizes that he has a bizarre medical Condition which renders him unable to be knocked out moe Convinces him to start a Career as a Boxer and allow the Bartender to manage. The Episode was written by Jonathan Collier and directed by Mark Kirkland. It guest Stars Michael Buffer as himself and Paul Winfield as Lucius Sweet."
