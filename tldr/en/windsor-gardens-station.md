short_summary: "Railway station in Norwood, Massachusetts, US"
description: "Windsor Gardens Station is a Massachusetts Bay Transportation Authority Franklin/Foxboro Line Station in southern Norwood, Massachusetts. The Station has a single Platform on its Side and serves a single Track it is not accessible. The only Entrance to the Station is from an adjacent Apartment complex Use of the Station is not restricted to Residents of the complex though there is no public Parking."
