short_summary: "Riverside area of Liverpool, England"
description: "The Pier Head is a riverside Location in the City Centre of Liverpool, England. It was Part of the former Liverpool Maritime mercantile City unesco World Heritage Site which was inscribed in 2004 but was revoked in 2021. As well as a Collection of Landmark Buildings, recreational open Space, and a Number of Memorials, the Pier Head was the Landing Site for Passenger Ships travelling to and from the City."
