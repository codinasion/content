description: "Star Trek: Secret of Vulcan Fury is a cancelled action-adventure Game, in Development by Interplay Entertainment from 1997 until its Cancellation in 1999. Based on the Star Trek: the Original Series License, the Game would have followed Captain James T. Kirk and the Crew of uss Enterprise have encountered a Series of Adventures in the vulcan and romulan Races. Secret of vulcan Fury was to have been the latest in a Series of Games developed by Interplay based on the Licence. It would have featured a Control Method similar to those seen in Lucasarts' Games of that Time and nearly seven Hours of full Motion Video Capture using Clay Models."
