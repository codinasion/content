description: "Usrc Surveyor was a Ship of the United States Revenue Marine captured by the United Kingdom during the War of 1812. The Gallant and desperate Defense of her Crew against a superior Force of the Royal Navy and the Corps of royal Marines is commemorated by the united States coast guard despite the Vessel's Loss. Together with the Royal Navy Frigate which topped her in Battle Hms Narcissus Surveyor is among six legendary Ships memorialized in the Lyrics of the Coast Guard March Semper Paratus."
