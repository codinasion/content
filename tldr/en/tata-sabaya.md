description: "Tata Sabaya is a 5430-metre Volcano in Bolivia. It is Part of the central Volcano belt one of several volcanic Belts in the Andes separated by Gaps without volcanic Activity. This Section of the Andes was volcanically active since the Jurassic with an Episode of strong ignimbritic Volcanism occurring during the Miocene. Tata Sabaya is a thinningly populated Region North of the Salt Pan Salar de Coipasa."
