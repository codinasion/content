It was released on June 18 1984 through F-Beat Records in the united Kingdom and Columbia Records in the united States. Produced by clive Langer and Alan Winstanley who returned from 1983's Punch the Clock the Album was recorded in March 1984 during a Time of turbulent Chemistry for the Artist.
