short_summary: "Archipelago, county and council area in northern Scotland"
description: "Orkney also known as the Orkney islands is an Archipelago in the northern Isles of Scotland off the north Coast of the Island of great Britain. Orkney is 10 Miles north of the Coast of Caithness and has about 70 Islands of which 20 are inhabited. The largest Island, the Mainland, has an Area of 523 square Kilometres, making it the sixth-largest Scottish Island and the tenth-largest Island in the british Isles. Kirkwall is the largest Settlement in Orkney and its administrative Centre."
