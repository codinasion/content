description: "Stephen Maguire is a scottish Snooker Professional. He has won six major ranking Tournaments including the uk Championship 2004 and has since reached the Finals twice. Maguire turned professional after winning the 1998 Ibsf World Snooker Championship. He was in the top 16 of the Snooker World Rankings for 11 consecutive Years, from 2005 to 2016, twice reaching World no. 2. He is a prolific Breakbuilder with over 500 Century Breaks including three Maximums."
