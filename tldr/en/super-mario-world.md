description: "Super Mario World, known in japanese Marketing as Super Mario World: Super Mario Bros. 4 is a Platform Video Game developed by nintendo Ead and published by Nintendo for the Super nintendo Entertainment System. It was released in Japan in 1990, North America in 1991 and Europe and Australia in 1992. The Player controls Mario on his quest to save princess Peach and dinosaur Land from the Antagonist of the Series Bowser and the Koopalings. The Gameplay is similar to that of earlier super Mario Games where Players control Mario through a Series of Levels in which the Goal is to reach the Goalpost at the End."
