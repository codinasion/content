description: "The Rega Planar 3, together with its Successors, the P3 and Rp3, is a well-known Budget Audiophile turntable by british hi-fi Manufacturer, Rega Research available since 1977. It was a belt-drive Deck which broke from Convention by employing a solid Plinth in Lieu of compliantly suspended Chassis or Sub-Chassis used in many Quality Turntables since the early 1960S."
