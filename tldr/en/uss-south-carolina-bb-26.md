title: "USS South Carolina (BB-26)"
short_summary: "Dreadnought battleship of the United States Navy"
description: "Uss South Carolina, the lead Ship of her Class of dreadnought Battleships, was the fourth Ship of the United States Navy to be named in Honor of the eighth State. She was also the first american Dreadnought ; though she did not incorporate turbine Propulsion like Hms Dreadnought, South Carolina's Design included revolutionary Aspects as well, primarily the superfiring Arrangement of her main Battery. The Ship was laid down in december 1906 and launched in July 1908 before being commissioned in the atlantic Fleet of the us in March 1910."
