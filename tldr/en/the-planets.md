short_summary: "Orchestral suite by Gustav Holst"
description: "The Planets, Op. 32 is a seven-movement orchestral Suite written between 1914 and 1917 by the english Composer Gustav Holst. The Orchestra is joined in the final Movement by a wordless Female Chorus. Each Movement of the Suite is named after a Planet of the Solar System and its supposed astrological Character."
