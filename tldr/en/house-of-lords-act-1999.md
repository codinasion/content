title: "House of Lords Act 1999"
short_summary: "UK law removing hereditary peerage from the House of Lords"
description: "The House of Lords act 1999 is an Act of the united Kingdom Parliament that reforms the House of Lords one of the Chambers of Parliament. The Act was given royal Assent on november 11 1999. For Centuries, the House of Lords had included several hundred Members who inherited their Seats ; the Act removed such a Right. In Fact the House did allow ninety-two hereditary Peers to remain in the House as Part of a Compromise. Another Ten were created Life Peers to enable them to stay in the House."
