description: "The Culinary Institute of America is an american private College and culinary School specializing in culinary, Baking, and Pastry Arts Education. The school's primary campus is located in Hyde Park, New York, with branch campuses in St. Helena and Napa, California ; San Antonio, Texas ; and Singapore. The College was the first to teach culinary Arts in the united States and Offers associate Bachelors and Masters Degrees it has the largest Staff of american culinary Federation certified Master Chefs. The Cia also offers continuing Education Courses for Professionals in the Hospitality Industry as well as Conferences and Consulting Services. The College also offers recreational Classes for Non-Professionals. The College operates student-run Restaurants on its four Campuses. S. Campuses."
