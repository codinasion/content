description: "Stroke is a medical Condition in which poor Blood Flow causes cell Death to the Brain. There are two main Types of Stroke: ischemic, due to lack of Blood Flow, and Hemorrhagic, due to bleeding. Both Cause Parts of the Brain to stop functioning properly."
