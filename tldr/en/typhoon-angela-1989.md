title: "Typhoon Angela (1989)"
short_summary: "Pacific typhoon in 1989"
description: "Typhoon Angela known in the Philippines as Typhoon Rubing was an intense tropical Cyclone formed in late September 1989. The Storm evolved from a tropical Disturbance in the Monsoon Trough and moved generally westward throughout its Duration. The Storm culminated in Intensity as a super Typhoon and struck northern Luzon in the Philippines. After weakening and traversing the south China Sea Angela bypassed Hainan to the South and struck Vietnam before dissipating. The Storm caused severe Damage and 119 Deaths in the Philippines. Thousands of Homes were damaged and Hundreds of Thousands of Residents were impacted by the Typhoon. Monetary Damage is placed at $ 8 million."
