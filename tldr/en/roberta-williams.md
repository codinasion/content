description: "Roberta Lynn Williams is an american Video Game Designer and Writer, who co-founded Sierra On-Line with her Husband, Game Developer Ken Williams. In 1980, her first Game, Mystery House, became a modest commercial Success ; it is credited as the first graphic Adventure Game. She is also known for creating and maintaining the King's Quest Series, as well as designing the full Motion Video Game Phantasmagoria in 1995."
