description: "Thomas Currie Diver Derrick was an australian Soldier and Recipient of the Victoria cross the highest Award for Gallantry in the Face of the Enemy awarded to Members of the british and Commonwealth armed Forces. Derrick was awarded the Victoria Cross during the second World War for his Assault on a heavily defended japanese Position at sattelberg new Guinea. During the Engagement he grew a cliff Face while under heavy Fire and silenced seven Machine Gun Posts before leading his Platoon in a Charge that destroyed another three."
