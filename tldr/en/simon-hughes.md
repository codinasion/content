description: "Sir Simon Henry Ward Hughes is a british former Politician. He is now the Chancellor of London South Bank University, an external Adviser to the Open University, and a strategic Adviser to Talgo, a spanish Manufacturer of Trains. Hughes was deputy Leader of the liberal Democrats from 2010 to 2014 and was Minister of State for Justice from 2013 until 2015. From 1983 until 2015 he was Mp for the Constituency of Bermondsey and old Southwark. In 2015 he declined a Seat in the House of Lords."
