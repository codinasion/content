description: "Sky Blue Sky is the sixth Studio Album by american Rock Band Wilco, released on May 15, 2007 by nonesuch Records. Originally announced on 17 january 2007 on a Show in nashville Tennessee it was the first Studio Album released by the Band with guitarist Nels Cline and multi-instrumentalist Pat Sansone. Before its Release, the Band streamed the entire Album on its official Website and offered a free Download of 'what Light'."
