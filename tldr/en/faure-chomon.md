short_summary: "Cuban historian and politician (1929–2019)"
description: "Faure Chomn Mediavilla was a cuban Historian and Politician. He was one of the Founders and Leaders of the 13th Marzo Directorio Revolucionario. After the Triumph of the Revolution he joined fidel Castro's Government. In early Years of his Career he served as Secretary of Communication and Transportation and Ambassador to the soviet Union. Later he served as Ambassador to Vietnam and Ecuador as well as Historian of the Revolution. He was also Member of the National Assembly of People's Power from 1976 to his Death."
