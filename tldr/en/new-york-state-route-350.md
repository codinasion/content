short_summary: "State highway in Wayne County, New York, US"
description: "New York State Route 350 is a north–south state highway in western Wayne County, New York, in the United States. It extends for 11. 40 Miles from an Intersection with ny31 and ny31f in the Village of Macedon to a Junction with Ny104 in the City of Ontario. Ny350 Serves as the eastern Terminus for three Routes the above-mentioned Ny31F Ny286 and ny441 all of which Originate in the Rochester Area. Aside from the Village of Macedon at its south End and the Town of Ontario Center at its north Terminus Ny350 serves mostly rural Areas dominated by Farmland. Ny 350 was assigned C. 1932 to the Portion of its routing South of Cator Corners, the north Junction with Ny 31f. It was extended northward to its current Terminus in the 1940S."
