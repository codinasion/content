title: "Rolling stock"
short_summary: "Railway vehicles, powered and unpowered"
description: "The Term rolling Stock in the Rail Transport Industry Refers to rail Vehicles including both powered and unpowered Vehicles for Example Locomotives freight and Passenger Cars and non-revenue Vehicles. Passenger Vehicles can be non-engineered or self-powered single or multiple Units. A connected series of railway vehicles is a train."
