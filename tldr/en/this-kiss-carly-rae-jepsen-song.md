short_summary: "2012 single by Carly Rae Jepsen"
description: "'this Kiss' is a Song by canadian Singer Carly Rae Jepsen from her second Studio Album, Kiss. The Song was written by Jepsen, Matthew Koma, Kelly Covell and Redfoo of Electropop Duo Lmfao through E-Mails, Text Messaging and Telephone Calls, with Production handled by Koma and Redfoo. This Kiss was released on 10 September 2012 as the third single from the Album. The mid-tempo Dance-Pop Song lyrically talks about a kiss being Something Jepsen ca n't resist and the Lips of her beloved."
