description: "View of the World from 9th Avenue is a 1976 Illustration by Saul Steinberg that served as the Cover of the new Yorker Magazine on 29 March 1976. The Work presents the View from Manhattan of the Rest of the World showing manhattan as the Center of the World. The Work of Art is an artistic Representation of distorted Self-Importance Relative to one's true Place in the World a Form of perception-based Cartography Humor."
