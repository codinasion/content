title: "Youth International Party"
short_summary: "1960s American youth-oriented counter-cultural political party"
description: "The Youth international Party whose Members were commonly called Yippies was an american youth-oriented radical and countercultural revolutionary Offshoot of the free Speech and anti-war Movements of the late 1960S. It was founded on December 31, 1967. They used theatrical Gestures to mock the social Status Quo such as advancing a Pig as a Candidate for President of the united States in 1968. They have been described as a highly theatrical anti-authoritarian and anarchist Youth Movement of symbolic Politics."
