title: "Siege of Badajoz (1658)"
short_summary: "1658 battle during the Portuguese Restoration War"
description: "The fourth Siege of Badajoz took Place during the portuguese Restoration War from July to october 1658. It was an Attempt by a huge portuguese Army under the Command of Joanne Mendes de vasconcelos Governor of Alentejo to capture the spanish City of Badajoz which was the Headquarters of the spanish Army of Extremadura. The Fortifications of Badajoz were essentially medieval and considered vulnerable by the portuguese and had already been attacked three Times during this War."
