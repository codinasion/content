description: "Lord of the Flies is the fifth Episode of the ninth Season of the Science Fiction Television Series the X-Files and the 187th Episode of the Show overall. It first premiered on the Fox Network in the united States on December 16 2001 and was later broadcast on Bbc two in the Uk. The Episode was written by Thomas Schnauz and directed by Kim Manners. The Episode is a 'monster-of-the-week' Episode, a Stand-Alone Plot which is unconnected to the Mythology, or overarching fictional History, of the X-Files. Lord of the Flies earned a nielsen Household Rating of 6. 2, and was watched by 9. 9 million viewers. The Episode received mixed Reviews from Television Critics with many criticizing the Episode's Reliance on Humor."
