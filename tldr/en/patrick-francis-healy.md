title: "Patrick Francis Healy"
short_summary: "American Jesuit educator (1834–1910)"
description: "Patrick Francis Healy was an american Catholic Priest and Jesuit who was an influential President of Georgetown University, becoming known as its 'second Founder'. His Name is found in the University's Flagship Building Healy Hall. Though he considered himself and was largely accepted as White, Healy was posthumously recognized as the first Black American to earn a Phd, to enter the Jesuit Order, and to become the President of a predominantly White University."
