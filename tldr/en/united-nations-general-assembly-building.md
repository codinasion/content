title: "United Nations General Assembly Building"
short_summary: "Building in Manhattan, New York"
description: "The United Nations General Assembly Building is Part of the Headquarters of the United Nations in the Turtle Bay Neighborhood of Manhattan in New York City, United States. It contains the main Assembly Hall of the United Nations General Assembly, the main Deliberative, Policymaking, and representative Organ of the United Nations. The Building was designed by a Group of Architects under the Direction of Wallace Harrison. It is connected to the other Buildings in the un Headquarters including the Dag Hammarskjld Library and the Secretariat Building. Although the Building is physically located within the us it is exempt from some local Regulations because the Site is under un Jurisdiction."
