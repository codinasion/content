description: "Rika's Landing Roadhouse, also known as Rika's Landing Site or the Mccarty Roadhouse, is a Roadhouse located at a historically important Crossing of the Tanana River, in the Southeast Fairbanks Area, Alaska, United States. It is located off mile 274. 5 of the Richardson Highway in Big Delta."
