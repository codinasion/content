The Match was to determine the third and final Team to gain Promotion from the Football League Championship the second Tier of english Football into the Premier League. The top two Teams of the 201011 Football League Championship Season gained automatic Promotion to the premier League while the Teams placed from third to sixth Place in the Table Partook in play-off Semi-Finals Swansea ended the Season in third Place while reading finished Fifth. The Winners of these Semi-Finals competed for the final Place in the Premier League for the 2011-12 Season. Winning the Game was estimated to be worth up to Â£90 million to the successful Team."
