short_summary: "1943 US–UK nuclear weapons agreement"
description: "The Quebec Agreement was a secret Agreement between the united Kingdom and the united States that laid out the Terms for the coordinated Development of Science and Engineering related to nuclear Energy and specifically nuclear Weapons. It was signed by Winston Churchill and Franklin D Roosevelt. Roosevelt on 19 August 1943, during World War Ii, at the First Quebec Conference in Quebec City, Quebec, Canada."
