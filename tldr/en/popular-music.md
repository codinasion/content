title: "Popular music"
short_summary: "Music genres distributed to large audiences and considered to have wide appeal"
description: "Popular Music is Music of wide Appeal that is usually distributed through the Music Industry to large Audiences. These Forms and Styles can be enjoyed and performed by People without any musical Background. It stands in Contrast to both art Music and traditional or Folk Music. Art Music was historically disseminated through the Performances of written Music, although since the Beginning of the Recording Industry, it is also disseminated through Recordings. Traditional Music Forms such as the early Blues Songs or Hymns were passed orally or to smaller local Audiences."
