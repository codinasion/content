description: "The Cabinet of Dr. Caligari is a german silent Horror Film of 1920 directed by robert Wiene and written by Hans Janowitz and Carl Mayer. Considered to be the quintessential Work of german Expressionist Cinema it tells the Story of an insane Hypnotist who uses a brainwashed Somnambulist to commit Murder. The Film features a Dark and twisted visual Style, with sharp-pointed Forms, Oblique and Curving Lines, Structures and Landscapes that lean and Twist in unusual Angles, and Shadows and Streaks of Light painted directly onto the Sets."
