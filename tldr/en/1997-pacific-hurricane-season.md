description: "The Pacific Hurricane Season of 1997 was a very active Hurricane Season. With Hundreds of Deaths and Hundreds of Millions of Dollars of Damage this was one of the deadliest and costliest Pacific Hurricane Seasons on Record. This was due to an extremely strong El nio Event in 199798. The Season officially began in the eastern Pacific on may 15 and in the central Pacific on June 1 and lasted until november 30. These Dates conventionally delimit the Period of each Year when nearly all tropical Cyclones Form in the Northeast Pacific Ocean."
