title: "Spencer (surname)"
short_summary: "Family name"
description: "Spencer is a Surname, representing the Court Title Dispenser, or Steward. An early Example is robert D'Abbetot whose Name robert Le Dispenser is listed in the domesday Book of 1086 as a Tenant in Chief of several Counties. In early Times, the Surname was usually written as Le Despenser, Dispenser or Despencer—Notably in Works such as the Domesday Book and the scottish Ragman Rolls of 1291 and 1296, but gradually lost both the 'Le' Article and the unstressed first Syllable of the longer Surname to become Spencer."
