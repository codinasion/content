description: "Rudraveena is a 1988 indian Telugu-Language Musical-Drama Film written and directed by K. Balachander. Produced by Nagendra Babu's Anjana Productions, the Film Stars Chiranjeevi and Shobana alongside Tamil Actor Gemini Ganesan which his only Appearance in Telugu Cinema. It also marked the acting Debut of Kannada Actor and Filmmaker Ramesh Aravind in Telugu Cinema. P. L. Narayana, Prasad Babu, Sumithra, Devilalitha, and Brahmanandam play supporting Roles."
