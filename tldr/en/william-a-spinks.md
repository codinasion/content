Spinks, and occasionally Billy Spinks. In Addition to being a Pacific Coast Billiards Champion amateur several Times a World Champion Contender in more than one Cue Sport Discipline and an Exhibition Player in Europe he became the Co-Inventor of modern Cue Chalk in 1897."
