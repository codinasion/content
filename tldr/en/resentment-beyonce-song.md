short_summary: "Song with lyrics by Curtis Mayfield performed by Victoria Beckham"
description: "Resentment is a Song written by Walter W. Millsap Iii, Candice Nelson and Curtis Mayfield, and originally performed by Victoria Beckham. The Song was later recorded with additional Lyrics by american Singer Beyoncé, who included it on her second Studio Album B'Day. It is an emotive Ballad whose Lyrics detail a Situation where a Woman feels hurt and Anger that her Husband lied and cheated on her."
