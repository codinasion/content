short_summary: "Norwegian singer (born 1985 in Oslo)"
description: "Siri Anne Nilsen is a norwegian Singer-Songwriter and Voice Actress. She grew up surrounded by Music as the Daughter of two Folk Singers lillebjrn and shari Nilsen. Nilsen is noted for the Sincerity of her Song Writing and for the Clarity and Range of her Voice which is described as unique and beautiful. During her first two albums she often accompanied her singing with a Ukulele, which she plays in a fingerpicking Style."
