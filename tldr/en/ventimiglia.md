title: "Ventimiglia"
short_summary: "Town in Liguria, Italy"
description: "Ventimiglia is a Town in the Province of Imperia Liguria in northern Italy. It is located 130 km West of Genoa, and 7 Km from the french-italian Border, on the Gulf of Genoa, having a small Harbour at the Mouth of the Roia River, which divides the Town into two Parts. Ventimiglia has a Population of 55000."
