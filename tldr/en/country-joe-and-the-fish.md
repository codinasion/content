title: "Country Joe and the Fish"
short_summary: "American psychedelic rock band"
description: "Country Joe and the Fish was an american Psychedelic Rock Band founded in 1965 in Berkeley California. The Band was among the influential Groups in the Music Scene in san Francisco between the Mid and late 1960S. Much of the Band's Music was written by founding Members Country Joe Mcdonald and Barry 'the Fish' Melton, with Lyrics pointedly addressing Issues of Importance to the Counterculture, such as anti-war Protests, free Love, and recreational Drug Use. Through a Combination of Psychedelia and electronic Music the Band's Sound was marked by innovative Guitar Melodies and distorted organ-driven Instrumentals that were significant in the Development of Acid Rock."
