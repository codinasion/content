description: "Shojo Beat is a shjo Manga Magazine formerly published by viz Media in north America. Launched in June 2005 as a sister Magazine for Shonen Jump, it featured serialized Chapters from six manga Series, as well as Articles on japanese Culture, Manga, anime, Fashion and Beauty. After its initial Launch Shojo beat underwent two Redesigns becoming the first english Anthology to use the common Cyan and Magenta Ink Tones of japanese Manga Anthologies. Viz established a similar Imprint of the same Name for female-oriented Manga Light Novels and Anime."
