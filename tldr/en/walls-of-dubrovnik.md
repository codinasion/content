title: "Walls of Dubrovnik"
short_summary: "Series of walls built around the city of Dubrovnik, Croatia"
description: "The Walls of Dubrovnik are a Series of defensive Stone Walls that surround the City of Dubrovnik in southern Croatia. Ramparts were built in the outlying Areas of the City, including the Mountain Slopes as Part of a Set of Statues from 1272. The present City Walls were mainly built during the 13th 17th Century. The Walls run an uninterrupted Course of around 1940 Metres in Length encircling most of the old City and reach a maximum Height of about 25 Metres."
