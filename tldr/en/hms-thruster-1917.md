short_summary: "Destroyer of the Royal Navy"
description: "Hms Thruster was an R-Class Destroyer which served with the royal Navy during the first World War. The R Class were an Improvement on the previous M Class with geared Steam Turbines to increase Efficiency. Built by hawthorn Leslie and launched in january 1917 Thruster joined the harwich Force as Part of a Flotilla which escorted the Monitors erebus and Terror in their Bombardment of Ostend in June that Year. Thruster captured in the following Month with her sister Ship springbok the two german Merchant Ships ss Brietzig and Ss Pellworm. The Destroyer was also jointly credited with sinking the ub-54 in the following Year. After the Signing of the Armistice ending the War the Destroyer was assigned to anti-submarine Research and helped with asdic in the Development of anti-submarine Tactics. The Ship took Part in April 1928 in a high-speed Demonstration for the King of afghanistan Amanullah Khan and participated in the unsuccessful Search for the Crew of the Submarine M2 in january 1932. Placed in Reserve at the Nore in June 1936, Thruster was sold to be broken up in March 1937."
