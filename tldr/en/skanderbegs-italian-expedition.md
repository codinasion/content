description: "Skanderbeg's italian Expedition was undertaken to aid his Ally ferdinand I of Naples whose Rulership was threatened by the Angevin Dynasty. Skanderbeg Gjergj Kastrioti was the Ruler of Albania he had been leading a Rebellion against the Ottoman Empire since 1443 and allied himself with several european Monarchs in Order to consolidate his Domain. In 1458, Alfonso V of Aragon, Ruler of Sicily and Naples and Skanderbeg's most important Ally, died, leaving his Illegitimate Son, Ferdinand, on the Neapolitan Throne ; René D'Anjou, the french Duke of Anjou, laid Claim to the Throne. The Conflict between Ren and Ferdinand's Supporters erupted into a civil War soon. Pope Calixtus Iii of spanish Origin could only do little to secure Ferdinand so he turned to skanderbeg for Assistance."
