description: "Steins ; Gate Drama Cd Alpha, Beta, and Gamma, stylized as Α, Β, and Γ, are a Trilogy of audio Dramas based on the 2009 Video Game Steins ; Gate, and are Part of the Science Adventure Series. It follows Kurisu Makise, Mayuri Shiina, and Rintaro Okabe, three Characters from the Steins ; Gate Game, in three different alternative Histories. Alpha shows Kurisu's Actions during the tenth Chapter of the Video Game, Beta shows what happened after Okabe failed to save Kurisu, and Gamma shows a Reality where the Year 2000 Problem caused a Disaster and Okabe became an Agent working for the Organization Sern."
