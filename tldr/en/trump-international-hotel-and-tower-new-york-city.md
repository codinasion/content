short_summary: "Residential skyscraper in Manhattan, New York"
description: "The trump international Hotel and tower originally named the Gulf and western Building is a high-rise Building at 15 columbus Circle and 1 central Park West on the upper west Side of manhattan new York City. It was originally designed by Thomas E. Stanley as an Office Building and completed in 1970 as the Headquarters of Gulf and Western Industries. In the Mid-1990S a joint Venture consisting of general electric Pension Fund Galbreath Company and Developer Donald Trump renovated the Building into a Hotel and residential Tower. The Renovation was designed by Philip Johnson and Costas Kondylis."
