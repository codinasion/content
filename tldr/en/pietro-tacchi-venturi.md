title: "Pietro Tacchi Venturi"
short_summary: "Jesuit priest and historian"
description: "Pietro Tacchi Venturi was a Jesuit Priest and Historian who served as unofficial Liaison between Benito mussolini the fascist Leader of Italy from 1922 to 1943 and the Popes pius X. He was also one of the Architects of the Lateran Treaty 1929 that ended the roman Question and recognized the Sovereignty of vatican City which made it an Actor of international Relations. A claimed attempt to assassinate Venturi with a paper knife, one year before the treaty's completion, made headlines around the world. Venturi had started the Process of Reconciliation by convincing Mussolini to donate the valuable Library of Palazzo Chigi to the vatican."
