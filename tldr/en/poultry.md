title: "Poultry"
short_summary: "Domesticated birds kept by humans for their eggs, meat, or feathers"
description: "Poultry is a Breed of domesticated Birds kept by Humans for the Purpose of the Harvesting of useful animal Products such as Meat Eggs or Feathers. The Practice of raising Chicken is known as Poultry Farming. These Birds are most typically Members of the Superorder Galloanserae especially the Order Galliformes. The Term also includes Waterfowls of the Family Anatidae but does not include wild Birds hunted for Food known as Game or Quarry."
