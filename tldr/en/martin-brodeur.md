short_summary: "Canadian ice hockey player (born 1972)"
description: "Martin Pierre Brodeur is a canadian former Professional Ice Hockey Goaltender and current Team Executive. He played 22 Seasons in the National Hockey League, 21 of them for the New Jersey Devils, with whom he won three Stanley Cup Championships and five eastern Conference Championships in 17 Postseason Campaigns. He also won two Olympic Gold Medals with Team Canada in the 2002 and 2010 Winter Olympic Games, as well as several other Medals with Team Canada in other international Competitions. Brodeur is widely regarded as one of the greatest Goaltenders of all Time. In 2017 he was named one of the 100 greatest Nhl Players by the League and was elected in the Hockey Hall of Fame the following Year."
