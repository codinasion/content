description: "11 south lasalle Street Building or eleven south lasalle Street Building is a chicago Landmark Building that is listed on the national Register of historic Places and is located at 11 south lasalle Street in the Loop Community in Chicago Illinois Usa. This Address is located on the southeast Corner of Lasalle and madison Street in cook County Illinois and is across madison Street from one North Lasalle Building. The Building Sits on the Site of a former Roanoke Building that once served as a national Weather Service Official Climate Site and replaced major Block 1 after the great Chicago Fire. The current Building has incorporated the Frontage of other Buildings east of the original Site of major Block 1."
