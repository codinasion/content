short_summary: "English businessman and the founder of Briarcliff Manor, New York"
description: "Walter William Law was a Businessman and the Founder of the 8000-person Village of Briarcliff manor new York. He was Vice President of Furniture and Carpet Retailer W. & J. Sloane, and later founded the Briarcliff Lodge, the Briarcliff Table Water Company, Briarcliff Farms, and the Briarcliff Greenhouses. He founded or assisted in establishing several Schools Churches and Parks in the Village and rebuilt its Train Station in 1906. Walter Law was the largest individual Landholder in westchester County in the early 1900s."
