short_summary: "Church cantata by Johann Sebastian Bach"
description: "Sehet, Wir Gehn Hinauf Gen Jerusalem, Bwv 159, is a Church Cantata by Johann Sebastian Bach. He composed the Music in Leipzig for sunday estomihi the last Sunday before Lent and probably performed the first Time on 27 february 1729. The Gospel Reading for the Sunday from the Gospel of Luke has Jesus announcing his Suffering and Death in Jerusalem. The Cantata's Theme and Bach's Music foreshadow his Passion."
