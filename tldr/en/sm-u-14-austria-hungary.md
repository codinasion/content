title: "SM U-14 (Austria-Hungary)"
short_summary: "Austro-Hungarian Navy's submarine (former French Brumaire class submarine)"
description: "Sm u-14 or u-xiv was a u-boat or Sub in the austro-hungarian Navy during the first World War. She was launched as the french Brumaire-Class Curie in 1912 but captured and rebuilt for Service in the austro-hungarian Navy. At the End of the War the Submarine was returned to France and returned to her former Name."
