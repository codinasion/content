short_summary: "Painting by Gustav Klimt"
description: "Portrait of adele Bloch-Bauer I is an Oil Painting on Canvas with gold Leaf by Gustav Klimt completed between 1903 and 1907. The Portrait was commissioned by the Sitter's Husband Ferdinand Bloch-Bauer a german and jewish Banker and Sugar Producer. The Painting was taken in 1941 by the Nazis and displayed at the Sterreichische Galerie belvedere. The Portrait is the final and most fully representative Work of Klimt's golden Phase. It was the first of two Depictions of Adele by klimt the Second was completed in 1912 and these were two of several Works by the Artist that the Family owned."
