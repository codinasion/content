description: "Spoken for is the second Studio Album by Christian Rock Band Mercyme. The Album was produced by pete Kipley and released on Ino Records on october 1 2002. The Album's Production following the Success of I can only imagine on christian Radio was described as nerve-racking by lead Singer bart Millard musically the Album represents a Change in the Band's musical Style with Critics variously identifying it as Roots rock Pop Rock or Adult contemporary. Lyrically the Album concentrates on Worship and the Concept of christian as Spoken for by God."
