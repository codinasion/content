title: "The Boat Race 1993"
short_summary: "1993 boat race between Oxford and Cambridge universities"
description: "The 139th Boat Race took Place on March 27 1993. The Boat Race is held annually as a side-by-side Rowing Race between Teams from the Universities of Oxford and Cambridge along the Thames. Cambridge for the first Time in the History of the Race using Cleaver Blades won by 312 Lengths in a Victory described as 'crushingly conclusive. The winning Time of 17 Minutes was the fourth-fastest Time in the Event. In winning the Event, Cambridge prevented Oxford making it seventeen Wins from the last eighteen Races and levelling the overall Score for the first Time since the 1929 Race."
