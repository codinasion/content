short_summary: "Medical surgical specialty"
description: "Plastic Surgery is a surgical Specialty involving the Restoration Reconstruction or Alteration of the human Body. It can be divided into two main Categories reconstructive Surgery and cosmetic Surgery. Reconstructive Surgery includes craniofacial Surgery Hand Surgery Microsurgery and Treatment of Burns. While reconstructive Surgery Aims at reconstructing a Part of the Body or improving its Functioning cosmetic Surgery Aims at improving the Appearance of it. A comprehensive Definition of Plastic Surgery has never been established because it has no distinct anatomical Object and thus Overlaps with practically all other surgical Specialty. During Plastic Surgery a critical Feature is that it involves the Treatment of Conditions that require or may require Tissue Relocation Skills."
