description: "Shrek is an american animated fantasy Comedy Film based loosely on the 1990 Children's Picture Book of the same Name by William Steig. Directed by Andrew Adamson and Vicky Jenson and written by Ted Elliott, Terry Rossio, Joe Stillman, and Roger S. H. Schulman, it is the first installment in the Shrek film series. The Film stars john Lithgow Eddie Murphy Mike Myers and Cameron Diaz. In the Film an embittered Ogre named shrek finds his Home in the Swamp overrun by fairy Tale Creatures banished by the obsessive Ruler Lord Farquaad. Shrek with the Help of Donkey makes a Pact with Farquaad to rescue Princess Fiona in Exchange for regaining Control of his Swamp."
