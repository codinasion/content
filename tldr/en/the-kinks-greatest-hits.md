short_summary: "1966 greatest hits album by the Kinks"
description: "The Kinks Greatest hits! Is a Compilation Album of the english Rock band the Kinks. Released by Reprise Records in the united States in august 1966 the Album mostly consists of Singles issued by the Group between 1964 and 1966. The Band's first greatest Hits album, it remained on the Billboard Top Lps Chart for over a Year, peaking at Number 9, making it the Kinks' highest charting Album in the Us. The Album was in Print for Decades and was the only Gold Album by the Kinks in America until 1980."
