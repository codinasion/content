description: "Jerusalem is an ancient City in west Asia on a Plateau in the Judaean mountains between the Mediterranean and the dead Sea. It is one of the oldest Cities in the World and is considered Holy to the three major abrahamic Religions Christianity and Islam. Israel and palestine both Claim Jerusalem as their Capital Jerusalem maintains its primary governmental Institutions there and the State of Palestine ultimately foresees it as its Seat of Power. No Claim has been widely acknowledged internationally."
