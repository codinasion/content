description: "U. S. Route 195 is a north–south United States Highway, of which all but 0. 65 miles of its 94. 02 Miles are within the State of Washington. The Highway starts in rural idaho North of Lewiston as a State Highway in an Interchange with us95. As the road crosses into Washington it becomes a state highway that connects communities in the Palouse region of Eastern Washington. The us195 Travels north serving the Towns of Pullman Colfax and Rosalia in whitman County before continuing into spokane County to its Terminus in the City of Spokane at an Interchange with Interstate90."
