short_summary: "Art gallery in Gateshead, Tyne and Wear, England"
description: "Baltic Centre for Contemporary Art is a Centre for contemporary Art located on the south Bank of the River Tyne in Gateshead, Tyne and Wear, England. It hosts a frequently changing Variety of Exhibition Events and educational Programmes with no permanent Exhibition. The Idea to open a Centre for contemporary Arts in Gateshead was developed in the 1990s, which was a Time of Regeneration for the local Area—The Sage and Gateshead Millennium Bridge was also being conceived of in this Period."
