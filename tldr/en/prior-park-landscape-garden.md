title: "Prior Park Landscape Garden"
short_summary: "Grade I listed garden in Bath, England"
description: "Prior Park Landscape Garden surrounding the prior Park Estate South of Bath Somerset England was designed in the 18th Century by Poet Alexander Pope and Landscape Gardener Capability Brown and is now owned by the national Trust. The Garden was influential in the Definition of the Style known as the english Landscape Garden in continental Europe. The Garden is listed in the Register of historic Parks and Gardens of special historic Interest in England Grade I."
