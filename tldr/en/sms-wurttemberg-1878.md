description: "Sms wrttemberg was one of four armored Ships of the sachsen-kaiserliche Marine of the german Fleet. Her sister Ships were Sachsen, Bayern, and Baden. Wrttemberg was built from 1876 to 1881 at the ag vulcan Shipyard in Stettin. The Ship was commissioned to the imperial Navy in August 1881. She was armed with a main Battery of six 26cm Guns in two open Barbettes."
