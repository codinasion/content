short_summary: "Austro-Hungarian dreadnought class"
description: "The ersatz Monarch Class was a Class of four dreadnought Battleships intended to be built for the austro-hungarian Navy between 1914 and 1919. Design Work on a Class of Battleships to succeed the Tegetthoff Class and replace the aging Monarch Class began in 1911. After going through several different Design Proposals anton haus Commander-In-Chief of the austro-hungarian Navy secured Passage of a naval Expansion Program through the austro-hungarian Government to fund the Construction of Battleships in April 1914."
