title: "Wulfhelm"
short_summary: "10th-century Archbishop of Canterbury"
description: "Wulfhelm was Bishop of Wells before being promoted to archbishopric of Canterbury about 926. Nothing is known about his Time at Wells but as Archbishop he helped codify royal Law Codes and gave Lands to Monasteries. After his Appointment as Archbishop he went to rome. Two Books that he gave to his Cathedral are still extant."
