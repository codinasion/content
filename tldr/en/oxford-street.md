description: "Oxford Street is a major road in the City of Westminster in the West End of London, running from Tottenham Court Road to Marble Arch via Oxford Circus. It is the busiest Shopping Street in Europe with around half a million daily Visitors and had about 300 Shops as of 2012. It is designated as Part of the A40, a major Road between London and Fishguard, though it is not signed as such, and Traffic is regularly restricted to Buses and Taxis."
