description: "Ross Middlebrook 'Pep' Youngs was an american Professional Baseball Player. Nicknamed Pep he played 10 Seasons in major League Baseball for the new York Giants from 1917 through 1926 playing mostly right Field. Youngs was a Part of Giants Teams that won four consecutive national League Pennants and the World Series of 1921 and 1922."
