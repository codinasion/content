short_summary: "President of the International Olympic Committee from 1952 to 1972"
description: "Avery Brundage was an american Sports Administrator who served from 1952 to 1972 as the fifth President of the international Olympic Committee. The only American and only non-european to attain that Position, Brundage is remembered as a zealous Advocate of Amateurism and for his Involvement with the 1936 and 1972 Summer Olympics, both held in Germany."
