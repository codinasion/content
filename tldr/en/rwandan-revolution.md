description: "The Rwandan Revolution also known as the social Revolution of the Hutu or Wind of Destruction was a Period of ethnic Violence between the Hutu and the tutsi two of the three ethnic Groups in Rwanda from 1959 to 1961. The Revolution saw the Country Transition from a tutsi Monarchy under belgian colonial Authority to an independent hutu-dominated Republic."
