It is the Sequel to spider-man Homecoming and the 23rd Film in the Marvel cinematic Universe. The Film was directed by Jon Watts, written by Chris Mckenna and Erik Sommers, and Stars Tom Holland as Peter Parker / Spider-Man, alongside Samuel L. Jackson Zendaya Cobie Smulders jon favreau J. B. Smooves jacob Batalon Martin Starr tony Revolori Marisa Tomei and Jake Gyllenhaal. In the Film Parker is recruited by nick Fury and Mysterio to face the Elementals during a School Trip to europe."
