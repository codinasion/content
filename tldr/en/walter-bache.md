title: "Walter Bache"
short_summary: "British pianist and composer"
description: "Walter Bache was an english Pianist and Conductor he was known for his Emphasis on franz Liszt's Music and other Music of the new german School in England. From 1863 to 1865 he studied privately with Liszt in italy one of the few to do so he continued to attend Liszt's Master classes regularly in Weimar Germany until 1885 even after embarking on a Solo Career. This Period of Study was unlike any other Student of Liszt and led to a particularly close Bond between Bache and Liszt. After initial Hesitation from english Music Critics because he was a liszt Pupil Bache was publicly embraced for his Keyboard Prowess even as Parts of his Repertoire were questioned."
