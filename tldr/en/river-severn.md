title: "River Severn"
short_summary: "River in the United Kingdom"
description: "The severn River at 220 Miles long is the longest River in great Britain. It is also the River with the most voluminous Flow of Water by far in all of England and Wales, with an average flow Rate of 107 M3/S at Apperley, Gloucestershire. It rises in the cambrian Mountains in mid Wales at an Altitude of 2001 Feet on the Plynlimon Massif near the Ceredigionpowys Border near Llanidloes. The River flows then through Shropshire Worcestershire and Gloucestershire. The County Towns of Shrewsbury, Gloucester and Worcester lie on its Course."
