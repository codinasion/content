short_summary: "Rapid transit system of Oslo, Norway"
description: "The Oslo Metro is the rapid Transit System of Oslo, Norway, operated by Sporveien T-Banen on Contract from the Transit Authority Ruter. The Network consists of five Lines which run through the City Centre with a total Length of 85 Km serving 101 Stations of which 17 are underground or in the City Centre. In Addition to serving 14 out of the 15 Boroughs of Oslo, two Lines run to Kolsås and Østerås, in the neighbouring Municipality of Bærum. In 2016 it had an annual Ridership of 118 million."
