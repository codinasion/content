short_summary: "1999 aviation accident"
description: "Alitalia Flight 1553 operated in Italy by Minerva Airlines was a regular scheduled commercial Passenger Flight from Cagliari to genoa. The Dornier 328 serving the Flight lost Control on 25 february 1999 and overran the Runway while landing at genoa Cristoforo Colombo Airport. Of the 31 Occupants on Board, three died, including the Flight Attendant ; another Passenger later died in Hospital. The Aircraft was unable to perform any Repairs."
