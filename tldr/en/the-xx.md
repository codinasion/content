short_summary: "British indie pop band"
description: "The Xx are an english Indie Rock Band from Wandsworth, London, formed in 2005. The Band consists of Romy Madley Croft, Oliver Sim, Jamie Smith, also known as Jamie Xx, and formerly Baria Qureshi. They are known for their Distinct and Minimalist Sound that blends indie Rock, indie electronic, indie Pop, Dream Pop and Electro-Rock and the dual Vocalist Setup of both Madley Croft and Sim. Their Music employs soft, echoed Guitar, prominent Bass, light electronic Beats and ambient Soundscape Backgrounds."
