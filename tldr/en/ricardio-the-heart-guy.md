The Episode was written and storyboarded by Bert Youn and Sean Jimenez, from a Story by Merriwether Williams, Tim Mckeon and Adam Muto. It originally aired on Cartoon Network on April 26 2010. The Episode guest Stars george takei as the Title Character Ricardio."
