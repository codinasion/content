short_summary: "1965 Doctor Who serial"
description: "The web Planet is the fifth Serial of the second Season of the british Science Fiction Television Series Doctor who. The Serial was written by Bill Strutton and directed by Richard Martin and was broadcast in six weekly Parts on Bbc1 from 13 february to 20 March 1965. In the Serial, the First Doctor and his Travelling Companions Ian Chesterton, Barbara Wright, and Vicki ally themselves with the Menoptra, the former Inhabitants of the Planet Vortis, as they struggle to win back the Planet from the malignant Animus and its Zarbi Slaves."
