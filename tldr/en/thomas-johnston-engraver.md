description: "Thomas Johnston was an american Engraver, Japanner, and heraldic Painter in Colonial Boston. Johnston engraved the first Print of an historical Event in Colonial America and was also the first Church Organ Maker in the Colonies. The Pipe Organ he built in 1758 1759 for the old north Church in Boston was in Use for more than a hundred Years until another Organ replaced it in 1886."
