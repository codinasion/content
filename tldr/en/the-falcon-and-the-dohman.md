description: "'the Falcon and the D'Ohman' is the twenty-third Season Premiere of the American animated Television Series the Simpsons. It aired for the first Time on the Fox Network in the united States on September 25 2011. In the Episode the Springfield nuclear Power Plant hires a new Security Guard named Wayne and Homer soon becomes Friends with him. 'the Falcon and the D'Ohman' also reveals the Fate of the Relationship between the Characters Ned Flanders and Edna Krabappel that was initiated in the previous Episode of the Series, 'the Ned-Liest Catch', that aired in May 2011."
