description: "The senghenydd Colliery Disaster also known as the Senghenydd Explosion took Place on 14 october 1913 at the universal Colliery in Senghenydd near caerphilly glamorgan Wales. The Explosion that killed 439 Miners and a Rescuer is the worst Mining Accident in the Uk. Universal Colliery on the south Wales coalfield extracted Steam Coal that was greatly in Demand. Some of the Region's Coal Seams contained high Quantities of Firedamp, a highly explosive Gas Consisting of Methane and Hydrogen."
