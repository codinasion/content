short_summary: "Cycling race"
description: "The Giro D'Italia 2010 was the 93rd Edition of the Giro d'italia one of the World's leading Cycling Stages. The Race started in Amsterdam on 8 may and remained in the Netherlands for three Stages before leaving. The Route included Climbs such as Monte Zoncolan, Plan De Corones, the Passo del Mortirolo and the Passo Di Gavia before ending in Verona with an individual Time Trial."
