short_summary: "Anti-Jewish rioting and violence in Slovakia"
description: "Postwar anti-jewish Violence in Slovakia resulted in at least 36 Deaths of Jews and more than 100 Injuries between 1945 and 1948 according to Research by polish historian Anna Cichopek. Overall, it was significantly less severe than in Poland. The Causes of the Violence included Antisemitism and Conflict over Restitution of Property stolen from Jews during the Holocaust in Slovakia."
