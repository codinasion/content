short_summary: "Assembly of photovoltaic cells used to generate electricity"
description: "A solar Panel is a Device that Converts sunlight by using photovoltaic Cells into Electricity. Pv Cells are made of Materials that create excited Electrons when exposed to Light. Electrons flow through a Circuit and produce Electricity directly which can be used to Power various Devices or stored in Batteries. Solar Panels are also known as solar Cell Panels solar electric Panels or Pv Modules."
