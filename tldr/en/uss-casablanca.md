short_summary: "Casablanca-class escort carrier of the US Navy"
description: "The uss Casablanca was the first of fifty Escort Carrier of the Casablanca Class built for the united States navy during World War Ii. She was named after the naval Battle of Casablanca conducted as Part of Operation Torch which pitted the united States navy against the Remnants of the french Navy controlled by Vichy France. The american Victory was able to clear the Way for the Capture of Casablanca Port as well as the Occupation of french Morocco by the allied Government. The Ship was launched in April 1943, commissioned in July, and served as a Training and Transport Carrier throughout the War. Postwar, she participated in Operation Magic Carpet, repatriating U. S. Servicemen from throughout the Pacific. She was decommissioned in June 1946 when she was mothballed by the atlantic Reserve Fleet. She was sold in april 1947 for Scrap."
