short_summary: "1998 single by Ricky Martin"
description: "Perdido Sin Ti is a Song recorded by the puerto rican Singer ricky Martin for his fourth Studio Album Vuelve. The Song was written by K. C. Porter, Robi Rosa, and Luis Gómez Escolar, while the Production was handled by Porter and Rosa. It was released as the fourth single from the Album by Columbia Records on August 18 1998. A slow and heartbreaking ballad its Protagonist is nostalgia. The Song received positive Reviews from Music Critics who complimented its Sweetness and Dreamy Hook."
