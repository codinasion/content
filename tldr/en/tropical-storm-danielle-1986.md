title: "Tropical Storm Danielle (1986)"
short_summary: "Atlantic tropical storm in 1986"
description: "Tropical Storm Danielle was the only tropical Storm to move through the caribbean Sea in 1986. A short-lived Danielle Cyclone developed on September 7 in the East of the southern Lesser Antilles. Strengthening to peak Winds of 60mph the Storm moved through Saint Vincent and the Grenadines where a Rainband moved across the main Island with hurricane-force Gusts. Continuing west danielle absorbed dry Air from the northern Part of South America and dissipated on September10."
