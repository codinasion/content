short_summary: "Pacific typhoon in 2005"
description: "Typhoon Matsa also known as Typhoon Gorio in the Philippines was the second of eight tropical tropical Cyclones to landfall on China during the 2005 Pacific Typhoon Season. On 30 July the ninth tropical Storm and fifth Typhoon of the Season Matsa emerged to the East of the Philippines. Matsa intensified as it tracked northwest and attained peak 10-minute sustained Winds of 150kmh near Taiwan before weakening and striking the chinese Province of Zhejiang on August 5. The System continued to the North into the yellow Sea and on 7 august Matsa became extratropical after moving again ashore along the liaodong Peninsula. Matsa is a laotian Name for a lady Fish."
