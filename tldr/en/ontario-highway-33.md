description: "King's Highway 33, commonly referred to as Highway 33 or Loyalist Parkway, is a provincially maintained Highway in the canadian Province of Ontario. The Route begins at Highway 62 in Bloomfield and Travels east to the Collins bay Road Junction at Collins bay in Kingston a Distance of 60 Miles. 9 kilometres . The Highway continues farther East into Kingston as Bath Road, ending at the former Highway 2, now Princess Street. Highway 33 is divided by the Bay of Quinte into two Sections. Glenora Ferry Service Crosses between the two Sections east of Picton transporting Vehicles and Pedestrians for free throughout the Year."
