title: "United States Marine Corps Women's Reserve"
short_summary: "World War II U.S. military organization"
description: "The united States marine corps Women's Reserve was the World War ii Women's Branch of the united States marine Corps reserve. It was authorized by the us Government. S. Congress and signed into law by President Franklin D. Roosevelt on 30 July 1942. Its purpose was to release officers and men for combat, and to replace them with women in U. S. Shore Stations for the Duration of the War plus six Months. Ruth Cheney Streeter was appointed first Director. The Reserve did not have an official Nickname as did the other Women's military Services of World War Ii."
