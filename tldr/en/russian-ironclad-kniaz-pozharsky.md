description: "The ironclad russian Kniaz Pozharsky was an iron-hulled armored Frigate built during the 1860S for the imperial russian Navy. She was the first russian armored Ship to leave european Waters when she cruised the pacific Ocean in 187375. The Ship did not participate in the russo-turkish War of 187778 and remained in the baltic Sea until 187980 when she made another Cruise in the Pacific. Kniaz Pozharsky was assigned to the baltic Fleet for the Rest of her Career. After the Refit in 1885 she mainly served as a Training Ship until she was hulked in 1909 and probably scrapped in 1911."
