title: "Pope John XXIII"
short_summary: "Head of the Catholic Church from 1958 to 1963"
description: "From october 28 1958 until june 1963 Pope John Xxiii was Head of the catholic Church and Sovereign of the State of vatican City. He was among 13 Children of Marianna Mazzola and Giovanni Battista Roncalli in a Family of Sharecroppers who lived in Sotto Il monte a Village in the Province of Bergamo Lombardy. He was ordained a Priest on August 10 1904 and served in a Number of Positions as a Nuncio in France and Delegate to bulgaria Greece and Turkey. In a Consistory on 12 January 1953 Pope Pius Xii made Roncalli a cardinal as the Cardinal-Priest of Santa Prisca in Addition to naming him as the Patriarch of Venice. On 28 october 1958 Roncalli was unexpectedly elected Pope aged 76 after eleven Votes. Pope John Xxiii surprised those who expected him to be a Caretaker Pope by calling the historic second vatican Council its first Session opening on 11 October 1962 which is now his Feast Day."
