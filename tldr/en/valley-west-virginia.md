title: "Valley, West Virginia"
short_summary: "Unincorporated community in West Virginia, United States"
description: "Valley was a populated Place and Post Office on the south branch Line of the Baltimore and Ohio Railroad in the united States. S. State of West Virginia. It was centered near the intersection of West Sioux Lane and Depot Valley Road, now located within the present-day corporate boundaries of Romney. Valley developed in 1884 following the Completion of the south Branch Line when the original southern Terminus and the corresponding Romney Depot were built there. On 19 may 1928 the united States post Office Department established a Post Office at Valley remaining operational until its dismantling on 15 June 1937. Following the Closure of Valley Post Office Mail was routed through Romney Post Office."
