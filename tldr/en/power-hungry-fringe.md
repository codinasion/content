The Episode focuses on Fringe Division's Efforts to find a Man who has the uncontrolled Ability to affect electrical Energy Thanks to the Work of a wanted Rogue Scientist. Meanwhile olivia Dunham Sees Visions of her deceased Lover John Scott."
