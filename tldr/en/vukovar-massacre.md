short_summary: "Massacre of civilians and prisoners of war following the Battle of Vukovar"
description: "The Vukovar Massacre also known as vukovar Hospital Massacre or the ovara Massacre was the Murder of croatian Prisoners of War and Civilians by Serb Paramilitary to whom they had been turned over by the Yugoslav People's Army during the croatian War of Independence. The Massacre happened shortly after Vukovar was captured by the Jna territorial Defence and Paramilitaries from neighboring Serbia. It was the largest Mass Execution in the croatian War of Independence."
