title: "Pont-y-Cafnau"
short_summary: "Bridge in Wales"
description: "The Pont-y-Cafnau, is a 14. 2-metre long Iron Truss Bridge over the River Taff in Merthyr Tydfil, Wales. The Bridge was designed by Watkin George and built for his Employer the Cyfarthfa Ironworks in 1793 to support both a Tramway and an Aqueduct for bringing Limestone and Water into the Works. A Grade Ii listed Building and scheduled ancient Monument Pont-Y-Cafnau is the earliest Surviving Iron Railway Bridge on the World."
