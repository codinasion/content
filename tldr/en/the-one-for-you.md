title: "The One for You"
short_summary: "1965 single by Tages"
description: "The One for you is a Song written by swedish Guitarist Danne Larsson and recorded in 1965 by his Group Tages. Following an intensive Tour of the Sweden, while also previously having composed Songs for the Band, Larsson wrote the Song inspired by their Trip to London, allegedly about a Girl he had met there. It was the first Recording by Tages produced by Anders Henriksson, who would come to produce the Majority of their coming Output, along with being their first single recorded at Europafilm Studios in Bromma, Stockholm."
