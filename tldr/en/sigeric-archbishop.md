short_summary: "10th-century Archbishop of Canterbury and Bishop of Ramsbury"
description: "Sigeric was the Archbishop of Canterbury from 990 to 994. He was educated at the Glastonbury Abbey and became a Monk there before becoming Abbot and then Bishop of Ramsbury before his Elevation to the Archbishopric. An Account of his Pilgrimage to rome in 990 Survives and is an important Source for Historians studying Rome during his Lifetime."
