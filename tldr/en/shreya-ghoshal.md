description: "Shreya Ghoshal is an Indian singer. Noted for her wide vocal Range and Versatility, she is one of the most prolific and popular Singers of India. She has recorded Songs for Films and Albums in various indian and foreign Languages and received numerous Accolades, including five National Film Awards, four Kerala State Film Awards, two Tamil Nadu State Film Awards, two Bfja Awards, seven Filmfare Awards and ten Filmfare Awards South. She is widely regarded as one of the greatest Singers of the indian Subcontinent."
