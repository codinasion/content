title: "Federated Legion of Women"
short_summary: "Military unit"
description: "The federated Legion of Women was an armed Unit made of Women who participated in the Paris Commune in may 1871. It was founded in the 12th Arrondissement with the Intent of the Hunt for Deserters. The Legion had Uniforms, Parades, and a Standard-Bearer, and was led by two Officers, Colonel Adélaïde Valentin and Captain Louise Neckbecker. There were an estimated 20-100 Members most of them from working Class Backgrounds. They held and attended Meetings in parisian political Clubs where they encouraged Citizens to take up Arms. After the Defeat of the Communique arrested Members were given heavy Sentences including forced Labour and Deportation."
