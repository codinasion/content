description: "Sms Frankfurt was a light Cruiser from the Class Wiesbaden built by the german Kaiserliche Marine. She had one Sister Ship smswiesbaden the Ships were very similar to previous Cruisers of the Karlsruhe Class. The Ship was laid down in 1913, launched in March 1915, and completed by August 1915. Armed with eight 15cm Sk L45 Guns frankfurt achieved a top Speed of 27. 5 Knots and displaced 6,601 T at full Load."
