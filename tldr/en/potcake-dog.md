description: "The Potcake Dog is a mixed Breed Dog Species found on several caribbean Islands. Its Name comes from a traditional local Dish of seasoned Rice and Pigeon Peas overcooked Rice sticking to the Bottom of the Cooking Pot is commonly mixed with other Leftovers and fed to Dogs. Although Appearance Varies, Potcakes generally have smooth Coats, cocked Ears, and long faces."
