short_summary: "WWII-era Uragan-class ship"
description: "The soviet Guard Ship Groza was a guard Ship of uragan Class that was constructed during the 1920s and 1930s for the soviet Navy. The Ship was one of the Ships of Series I officially known as Project 2. Shortly after her Enlistment in 1932 she was transferred to the northern Flotilla and played a minor Role in the 1939-1940 Winter War against Finland."
