short_summary: "Light cruiser of the German Imperial Navy"
description: "Sms Emden was the second and last Member of the light Cruisers from dresden Class built for the german Kaiserliche Marine. She was named for the Town of Emden and was laid down in 1906 on Kaiserliche Werft in Danzig. The Hull was launched in may 1908 and was completed in July 1909. She had a sister Ship Dresden. Like the previous Knigsberg-Class Cruisers emden was equipped with ten Ten ft. 5 cm guns and two torpedo tubes."
