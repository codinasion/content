title: "Phoenician sanctuary of Kharayeb"
short_summary: "Historic temple remains in Lebanon"
description: "The phoenician Sanctuary of Kharayeb is a historic Temple in the Hinterland of Tyre in southern Lebanon. It was excavated in three Stages. Maurice Chehab then Head of the Lebanon Directorate General of Antiquities led the first Mission which revealed a hellenistic Period Temple and Thousands of Clay Figurines dating from the sixth to the first Century Bc. Subsequent Excavations conducted by lebanese Archaeologist Brahim Kaoukabani in 1969 and in 2009 by the italian Government yielded Evidence of cultic Practices and produced a detailed Reconstruction of the Orthodox."
