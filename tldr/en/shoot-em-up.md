short_summary: "Subgenre of action game"
description: "Shoot-Em-Ups are a Sub-Genre of Action Games. There is no Consensus as to which Design Elements compose a Shoot'em up ; some restrict the Definition to Games featuring Spacecraft and certain Types of Character Movement, while Others allow a broader Definition including Characters on Foot and a Variety of Perspectives."
