description: "Shrub's Wood long Barrow is an unchambered long Barrow in the south-eastern english County of Kent near the Village of elmsted. It probably was constructed in the fourth Millennium Bce during the early neolithic Period in Britain. The long Barrow is constructed of solid Earth and consists of a subtrapezoidal Tumulus flanked by Side Ditches."
