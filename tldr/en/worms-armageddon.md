description: "Worms armageddon is a turn-based Strategy Video Game released by Team17 in 1999. It was originally released for the Windows operating System and was later ported to the Playstation dreamcast nintendo 64 and Game Boy Color. Worms Armageddon is the third Installment in the Worms Series. In the Game the Player controls a Team of up to eight Earthworms tasked with defeating the opposing Team using a wide Range of Weapons. The Game takes Place on a destructible and customizable two-dimensional Board and is characterized by cartoonish Graphics and a unique Brand of Humour."
