description: "Yo-Yo is a Song by the british Recording Artist Nicola Roberts published on 6 january 2012 as the third and last single from Roberts' Debut Solo Album Cinderella's Eyes. Originally, 'yo-yo' was set to be Roberts' Debut single, but 'Beat of My Drum' was selected in its Place. Beat of my Drum and lucky Day roberts previous Singles garnered positive Comments from Critics but failed to reach commercial Success. 'yo-yo' was written by Roberts, Maya Von Doll and Dimitri Tikovoi while it was produced by Latter, and was the first Song to be composed by the Group. Roberts said the Song had defined her musical Style."
