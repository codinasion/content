title: "Root nodule"
short_summary: "Plant part"
description: "Nodules are found on the Roots of Plants primarily legumes which form a Symbiosis with nitrogen-fixing Bacteria. Under nitrogen-limiting Conditions capable Plants form a symbiotic Relationship with a host-specific Strain of Bacteria known as Rhizobia. This Process has evolved multiple Times within the Legumes as well as in other Species found within the Rosid Family. Legume Crops include Beans Peas and Soybeans."
