description: "The Siege of Trichinopoly was Part of an extended Series of Conflicts between the Nizam of Hyderabad and the Maratha Empire in the quest to control the carnatic Region. On 29 August 1743, after a six-month Siege, Murari Rao surrendered, giving Nizam ul Mulk the Suzerainty of Trichinopoly. At the End of 1743 the Nizam had regained the whole Control over deccan. This stopped the Maratha Interference in the Region and ended their Hegemony over the carnatic.
