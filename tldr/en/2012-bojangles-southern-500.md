description: "The southern 500 in 2012 was the 56th Running of the Event that dates back to the 1957 Rebel 300 and the eleventh Stock Car Race of the 2012 Nascar Sprint Cup Series. It was held on may 12 2012 at Darlington Raceway in Darlington South Carolina. The 368-lap Race was won by Jimmie Johnson for the Hendrick motorsports Team. It was Johnson's first Win of the Season and hendrick Motorsport's 200th in Nascar denny Hamlin of Joe Gibbs racing finished second and stewart-haas Racing Driver tony Stewart took third."
