short_summary: "Research carried out for the children's TV shows"
description: "Sesame Street was premiered on the national educational Television Network in the united States in 1969. Unlike earlier Children's programming the Show's Producers used Research and over 1000 Studies and Experiments to create and test the Show's Impact on its young Viewers' Learning. At the End of the first Season of the Programme Children's Television Workshop which was founded to oversee sesame Street Production developed what became called the Ctw Model a System of planning Production and Evaluation that combined the Expertise of Researchers and early Childhood Educators."
