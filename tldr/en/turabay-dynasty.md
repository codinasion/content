title: "Turabay dynasty"
short_summary: "Dynastic house in 16th–17th century Palestine"
description: "The Turabay Dynasty was the preeminent Household of the Bedouin Banu Haritha Tribe in northern Palestine whose Chiefs traditionally served as Multazims and sanjak Beys of lajjun Sanjak during ottoman Rule. The Sanjak stretched over the Towns of lajjun Jenin Haifa and Atlit and the Surrounding Countryside. In the late 15th Century the Progenitors of the Family had served as the Chiefs of Marj Bani Amir under the Mamluks."
