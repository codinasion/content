short_summary: "Species of carpet sharks"
description: "The Zebra Shark is a Species of Carpet Shark and is the only Member of the Family Stegostomatidae. It is found throughout the tropical indo-pacific frequenting coral Reefs and sandy Flats to a Depth of 62 Metres. Adult Zebra Sharks are distinctive in Appearance with five longitudinal Ridges on a cylindrical Body a low Caudal Fin that covers nearly Half of their total Length and generally a Pattern of dark Spots on a Pale Background. Young Zebra Sharks under 5090cm have a completely different Pattern Consisting of light vertical Stripes on a brown Background and lack the Ridges. This species attains a Length of 2. 5 M."
