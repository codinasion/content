short_summary: "British opera singer and actor, born 1853"
description: "Rutland Barrington was an english Singer, Actor, comedian and edwardian musical Comedy Star. Best remembered for originating the lyric baritone roles in the Gilbert and Sullivan operas from 1877 to 1896, his performing career spanned more than four decades. He also wrote at least a Dozen Works for the Stage."
