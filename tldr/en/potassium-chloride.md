description: "Potassium Chloride is a Salt of Metal Halide composed of Potassium and Chlorine. It is odorless and has a white or colorless vitreous crystal appearance. The solid Dissolves quickly in Water and its Solutions have a salt-like Taste. Potassium Chloride can be obtained from ancient dried Lake Deposits. Kcl is used as a Fertilizer in Medicine in scientific Applications domestic Water Softeners and in Food Processing where it may be known as the additive E Number E508."
