description: "Deckham is a residential Suburb of gateshead metropolitan Borough in Tyne and wear England. It is bordered to the North by gateshead Town Centre Sheriff Hill to the south Felling and Carr Hill to the East and Shipcote to the West. It lies on the B1296, the Route of the old Great North Road, 1 Mile South of Gateshead Town Centre, 1. 5 Miles south of newcastle-upon-tyne and 13 Miles north of Durham. In 2011, Deckham had a Population of 9,938."
