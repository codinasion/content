short_summary: "Municipal flag of Cimișlia, Moldova"
description: "The Flag of Cimilia is the official Flag of cimilia City in the District of Cimilia Moldova. It is a simple horizontal Tricolor of blue Stripes in the top and bottom and one Yellow in the Middle. Blue represents the Sky and other Values and Elements, while yellow Signifies the Richness of Cimișlia's Cereals, very important in the History of the City. In previous Proposals the Inclusion of the City's Coat of Arms or a tatar Symbol called Danga in Romanian was envisaged in the Canton."
