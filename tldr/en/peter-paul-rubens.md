short_summary: "Flemish artist and diplomat (1577–1640)"
description: "Sir Peter Paul Rubens was a Flemish artist and diplomat. He is considered the most influential artist of the Flemish Baroque tradition. Rubens's highly charged Compositions reference erudite Aspects of classical and christian History. His Unique and immensely popular Baroque Style emphasized Movement Colour and Sensuality which followed the immediate dramatic artistic Style promoted in the Counter-Reformation. Rubens was a Painter who produced Altarpieces Portraits Landscapes and History Paintings of mythological and allegorical Subjects. He was also a prolific Designer of Cartoons for flemish Tapestry Workshops and of Frontispieces for the Publishers in Antwerp."
