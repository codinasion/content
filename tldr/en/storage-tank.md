title: "Storage tank"
short_summary: "Container for liquids or compressed gas"
description: "Storage Containers are Containers that hold Liquids compressed Gases or Mediums used for Storage of Heat or Cold for short or long Periods. The Term can be used for Reservoirs and for manufactured Containers. The Usage of the Word Tank for Reservoirs is unusual in american English but is moderately common in british English. The Term is often used to refer to only artificial Containers in other Countries."
