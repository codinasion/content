title: "West Crater"
short_summary: "Lava dome in Washington, United States"
description: "West Crater is a small lava Dome with associated Lava flows in southern Washington united States. It sits in skamania County at an Elevation of 4131 Feet and Forms Part of the Cascade Volcanic Arc. It is also Part of the marble Mountain-Trout Creek Hill volcanic Field a little-known quaternary Volcano in the southern Cascades of Washington State. The Area can be hiked and can be accessed by Roads in Gifford pinchot national Forest."
