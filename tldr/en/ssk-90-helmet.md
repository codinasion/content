short_summary: "World War II Luftwaffe helmet"
description: "The Ssk 90 Helmet was a short-lived Luftwaffe Helmet during World War Ii. Manufactured by Siemens, the Helmet consisted of an inner Core of interlocking Steel Plates, a Goatskin Exterior, and an underside with foam Rubber Padding and a cloth Lining ; a prominent Protrusion at the Front helped to put on and take off the Helmet quickly, and served as extra Padding in Case of a Crash. The Helmet had Cutouts for Ears and was designed to be worn over a cloth Flight Helmet fitted with Radio Gear."
