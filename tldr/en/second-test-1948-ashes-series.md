short_summary: "Australia-England cricket test match"
description: "The second Test of the 1948 Ashes Series was one of five Tests in the Ashes Cricket Series between Australia and England. The Match was played in London's Lord's from 24 to 29 June with a Rest Day on 27 June 1948. Australia won the Match by 409 Runs to take a 2–0 Lead, meaning that England would need to win the remaining three Matches to regain the Ashes."
