title: "Rita Miljo"
short_summary: "South African conservationist"
description: "Rita Miljo was a south african Conservationist and Animal Rights Pioneer noted for founding and managing the Centre for animal Rehabilitation and Education near Phalaborwa in south Africa. Born in east Prussia shortly before World War Ii began she had Dreams from an early Age of becoming a veterinarian. When the War broke out she became involved with the Girls' Wing of the Hitler Youth but Quit when her Father no longer supported Nazis. After a short Time studying Psychology at the University she worked in a Factory and then in the Hagenbeck Zoo."
