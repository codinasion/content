short_summary: "House in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada"
description: "Rideau Cottage is a historic residential Building located on the Grounds of Rideau Hall in Ottawa, Ontario. The two-level 22-room georgian Revival Home is owned by the canadian Crown and has traditionally been inhabited by People associated with the Governor General of Canada including the private Secretary of the Viceroy. It has been occupied by Prime Minister Justin Trudeau and his Family in Lieu of 24 Sussex Drive since 2015."
