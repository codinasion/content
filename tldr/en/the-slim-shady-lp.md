It was released on February 23, 1999, by Aftermath Entertainment and Interscope Records. Recorded in ferndale Michigan following Eminem's Recruitment by Dr. The Album features Production by Dr Dre and Jimmy Iovine. Dre, the Bass Brothers, and Eminem himself."
