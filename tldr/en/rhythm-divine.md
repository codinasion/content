title: "Rhythm Divine"
short_summary: "1999 single by Enrique Iglesias"
description: "'rhythm Divine' is a Song by spanish Singer Enrique Iglesias from his fourth Studio Album, Enrique. The Song was written by Paul Barry and Mark Taylor with Taylor and brian rawling directing its Production. It is a dance-pop rhythmic romantic Ballad that Mixes with Latin and mediterranean Music. Lyrically, Iglesias asks the Listener if they can hear the Rhythm, and one Music Journalist compared it to Usa for Africa's 'we are the World'. A spanish-language Translation of ritmo Total was also recorded and features additional lyrical Contributions from rafael Prez-Botija."
