short_summary: "2010 single by Lady Gaga featuring Beyoncé"
description: "'Telephone' is a Song by american Singer Lady Gaga from her Third extended Play, the Fame Monster —The Reissue of her Debut Studio Album, the Fame. Featuring american Singer Beyoncé, it was released as the Ep's second single on January 26, 2010. Gaga and Rodney Jerkins wrote and produced 'Telephone', with additional Songwriting by Lashawn Daniels, Lazonate Franklin and Beyoncé. The Song was written originally for Britney Spears who also recorded a Demo. 'Telephone' Conveys Gaga's Fear of not finding Time for Fun given the increasing Pressure for her to work Harder as an Artist. Musically the Song consists of an expanded Bridge Verse-Rap and a sampled Voice of a Phone Operator announcing that the Line is unreachable. In the Middle of the Song Beyonc sings the Verses in a rapid Fire Way accompanied by double Beats."
