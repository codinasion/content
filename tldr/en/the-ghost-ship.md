description: "The Ghost Ship is a 1943 american black-and-white psychological Thriller Film directed by Mark Robson and starring Richard Dix. It was produced by val Lewton for Rko Radio Pictures as Part of a Series of low-budget Horror Films. The Film can be seen as a 'low-key psychological Thriller', a 'suspense Drama', and a 'waterlogged Melodrama'. Russell Wade Edith Barrett Ben Bard and Edmund Glover were among the Supporters."
