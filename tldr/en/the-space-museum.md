short_summary: "1965 Doctor Who serial"
description: "The Space Museum is the seventh Serial of the second Season of british Science Fiction Television Series Doctor who. Written by Glyn Jones and directed by mervyn Pinfield he was broadcast in four weekly Parts on Bbc1 from 24 April to 15 may 1965. In the Serial, the First Doctor and his Travelling Companions Ian Chesterton, Barbara Wright, and Vicki arrive in a Space Museum on the Planet Xeros, where they seek to change their Fate after seeing themselves turned into Museum Exhibits in the Future. In Addition they become embroiled in a Conflict between the militaristic Moroks who run the Museum and the Servile indigenous Xerons who work for them."
