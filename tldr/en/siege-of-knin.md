description: "The Siege of Knin was a 1522 Siege by the Ottoman Empire on the City of knin Capital of the Kingdom of Croatia. In the Spring of 1522 Ottoman Forces led by Ghazi Husrev bey Sanjak-Bey of the Sanjak of Bosnia began a major Offensive on south Croatia after two failed Attempts. In May, his Forces, reinforced with Troops from the Sanjak of Herzegovina and Constantinople, besieged the Knin Fortress."
