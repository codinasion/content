title: "Richard J. Daronco"
short_summary: "American judge"
description: "Richard Joseph Daronco was an american Lawyer and Judge. He was born in new York City studied at Providence College and the albany Law School before serving in the united States army for several Years. Daronco was elected as a Judge of westchester County Family Court in 1971. He was elected to the westchester County Court three Years later. In 1979 Daronco was appointed a Justice of the new York State supreme Court by Governor Hugh Carey. He was then appointed in 1987 by President Ronald reagan to the united States District Court for the southern District of new York."
