description: "Tintin and Alph-Art is the unfinished 24th and final Volume of the Adventures of Tintin the Comics Series by the belgian Cartoonist Herg. After Herg's Death the Manuscript was posthumously published in 1986. The Story revolves around Brussels modern art Scene where the young Reporter Tintin Discovers that a local Art Dealer has been murdered. Investigating further he encounters a Conspiracy of Art Forgery led by a religious Teacher named endaddine Akass."
