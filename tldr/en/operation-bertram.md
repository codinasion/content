title: "Operation Bertram"
short_summary: "World War II deception operation"
description: "Operation Bertram was a allied Deception Operation in egypt held by bernard Montgomery in the Months leading up to the second Battle of el Alamein in 1942. Bertram was created by Dudley Clarke to deceive erwin Rommel about the Timing and the Location of the allied Attack. The Operation consisted of physical Deceptions using Dummies and Camouflage designed and made by the british middle East Command Camouflage Directorate under the Leadership of Geoffrey Barkas. These were accompanied by electromagnetic Deception codenamed Operation Canwell using false Radio Traffic. All of these were planned to make the Axis believe that the Attack would take Place towards the South far from the Coast Road and Railway about two Days later than the real Attack."
