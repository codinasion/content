short_summary: "2006 single by Nelly Furtado"
description: "'say it Right' is a Song by canadian Singer Nelly Furtado from her third Studio Album, Loose. It was written by Furtado, Tim 'Timbaland' Mosley, and Nate 'Danja' Hills, with Furtado crediting the Eurythmics' Song 'here comes the Rain Again' as her Inspiration. The Song was released as the third single from Loose on 31 October 2006 by Geffen Records and Mosley Music Group ; in Europe, it was released as the fourth."
