description: "Kronstadt was a Kresta ii - Class Cruiser from the 1134a Project of the soviet Navy named after the naval Base Kronstadt. The first Ship of her Class, the Ship served during the Cold War, from 1969 to 1991. She served with the Northern Fleet, with her shakedown Cruise being through the Mediterranean Sea. She spent five Years being repaired and modernized after colliding with a Destroyer in 1975. She was decommissioned in 1991 but sold two Years later for Scrap due to reduced naval Funding and Deteriorating Conditions."
