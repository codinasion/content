title: "Public sphere"
short_summary: "Area in social life with political ramifications"
description: "The Public sphere is an Area in social Life where Individuals can come together to discuss and identify societal Problems and influence political Action through this Discussion. A 'public' is 'of or concerning the People as a Whole. Such a Discussion is called public Debate and is defined as the Expression of Views on Matters of Concern to the Public which sometimes but not always with opposing or divergent Views expressed by Participants in the Discussion. Public Debate is usually conducted through the Mass Media but also at Meetings or through social Media academic Publications and Government Policy Documents."
