title: "Romance (Luis Miguel album)"
short_summary: "1991 studio album by Luis Miguel"
description: "Romance is the eighth Studio Album by Mexican Singer Luis Miguel. It was published by wea Latina on november 19 1991. Although the Production was originally intended as another Collaboration with juan Carlos Caldern this Plan was scrapped when Caldern was unable to compose Songs for the Album. Miguel faced a new-material Deadline in his Recording Contract at his Manager's Suggestion and chose Bolero Music for his next Project. Armando Manzanero mexican Singer-Songwriter was hired by wea Latina to co-produce the Album with Miguel. Recording began in august 1991 at ocean Way recording in Hollywood California with Bebu silvetti the Arranger."
