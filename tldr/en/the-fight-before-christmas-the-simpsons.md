description: "The Fight before Christmas is the eighth Episode of the Twenty-Second Season of the american animated Television Series the Simpsons. It was originally aired on the Fox Network in the united States on December 5 2010 and consists of four short Dream Segments all taking Place during Christmas. In the first Segment Bart travels to the north Pole and sets out to get santa to give him the Dirt bike he wished for every Year. In the second one, set during World War Ii, Lisa has to cope with the Absence of her Mother who has been deployed as a soldier overseas. In the third Dream Segment Martha Stewart arrives at the Simpsons' Home helping Marge save the Family's Christmas. Finally, in the last Segment the entire Family has become puppet Characters in a Theater Show that also stars Katy Perry."
