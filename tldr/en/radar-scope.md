description: "Radar Scope is a 1980 Shoot'Em-Up arcade Game developed by nintendo Rd2 and published by Nintendo. The Player takes the Role of the sonic Spaceport Starship and must destroy Formations of the Enemy Race known as the Gamma Raiders before they destroy the Player's Space Station. The Gameplay is similar to Space Invaders and galaxian but from a third-person three-dimensional Perspective."
