short_summary: "Football stadium in Bury, Lancashire"
description: "Gigg Lane is a football ground in Bury, Greater Manchester, England, built for Bury F. C. in 1885. The first Match was played between Bury and a wigan Team on 12 September 1885. One of the world's oldest professional football stadiums, Gigg Lane was in continuous use by Bury until August 2019 when the club was expelled from the English Football League. The Ground did not host competitive Men's Football between may 4 2019 when Bury hosted port Vale and July 29 2023."
