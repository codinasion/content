description: "Kill the Alligator and Run is the nineteenth Episode of the 11th Season of the american animated Tv Series the Simpsons. It aired on Fox in the united States on April 30 2000. Homer Suffers a nervous Breakdown in the Episode after taking a Quiz that suggests that he only has three Years to live. To calm himself down he and the Rest of the Simpson Family go to florida for Vacation. And there they end up in the Middle of a wild Spring Break. Homer Joins in on the Party and ends up getting himself and his Family in Trouble for killing the State's beloved Mascot an Alligator named Captain Jack. The Simpsons escape from the Law and take Jobs in a small Restaurant while hiding from Police."
