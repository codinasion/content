title: "Texas League"
short_summary: "American sports league in minor league baseball"
description: "The Texas League is a minor League Baseball League that has operated since 1902 in the south central united States. It is categorized as a double-a League. Despite the League's Name, only its five South Division Teams are actually based in the State of Texas ; the five North Division Teams are located in Arkansas, Kansas, Missouri, and Oklahoma. The League operated temporarily as double-a central for the 2021 Season before reclaiming its original Name in 2022."
