description: "The Sims 4 is a social Simulation Game developed by Maxis and published by electronic Arts. It is the fourth major Title in the Sims Series, following the Sims 3. The Game was released for Windows on 2 September 2014 an Os x Version was released in february 2015 and Versions of Playstation 4 and xbox one were released in november 2017. The Game was moved to a free-to-play Model on 18 october 2022 and monetized by the Purchase of various paid downloadable Content Packs that have been developed since its Release."
