short_summary: "2006 single by Rihanna"
description: "'Sos' is a Song by barbadian Singer Rihanna, and included in her second Studio Album, A Girl like Me. It was released as the Lead single on def Jam Recordings on february 14 2006. Sos was written by J. R. Rotem and E. Kidd Bogart, with additional Credit assigned to Ed Cobb for Inspiration built around a Sample of Soft Cell's 1981 Recording of 'tainted Love'. This Song was written by Cobb in 1965 and later influenced the Creation of Sos. Production of the Dance, Dance-Pop and R & B Song was handled by Rotem. Sos received generally positive Reviews with most Music Critics praising the Inclusion of the Sample tainted Love. Sos was compared with Rihanna's Debut single Pon de Replay by some Critics."
