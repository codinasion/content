short_summary: "IUCN conservation category"
description: "Threatened Species are Species which are vulnerable to Extinction in the near Future. Species that are threatened are sometimes characterised by the Measure of Population Dynamics of critical Depensation a mathematical Measure of Biomass related to population Growth Rate. This quantitative metric is one Method of evaluating the Degree of Endangerment without Reference to human Activity."
