short_summary: "American professional wrestler"
description: "Victoria 'Wendi' Richter is an american former professional Wrestler. She began her professional wrestling Career in Companies such as the national Wrestling Alliance where she teamed with Joyce grable with whom she twice held the nwa Women's World Tag Team Championship. She joined the World wrestling Federation in the 1980S. She held the wwf Women's Championship twice and feuded with the fabulous Moolah over the Title. She was also involved in a Story Line with Singer Cyndi Lauper called the Rock and Wrestling Connection. Richter, however, left the Wwf after losing the Championship in controversial Fashion. She then worked for the american Wrestling Association and the World Wrestling Council where she held both Women's Titles."
