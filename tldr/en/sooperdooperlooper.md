short_summary: "Looping roller coaster at Hersheypark"
description: "Sooperdooperlooper is a Steel Roller Coaster that is situated in Hersheypark Hershey Pennsylvania. Design and manufactured by Anton schwarzkopf the Roller Coaster was opened to the Public on 8 may 1977. Sooperdooperlooper is located in the hollow Section of the Park and Cost more than 3 million to build and construct. The Roller Coaster reaches a maximum Height of 70 Feet with a maximum Speed of 45 Miles per Hour and a total Length of 2614 Feet."
