title: "Triton (moon)"
short_summary: "Largest moon of Neptune"
description: "Triton is the largest natural Satellite of the Planet Neptune and was the first neptunian Moon discovered by the english Astronomer William Lassell on 10 October 1846. It is the only large Moon in the Solar System with a Retrograde Orbit, an Orbit in the Direction opposite to its Planet's Rotation. Triton is thought to have been a dwarf Planet captured from the Kuiper Belt because of its Retrograde Orbit and similar Composition to pluto."
