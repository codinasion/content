title: "Performance appraisal"
short_summary: "Method to document and evaluate an employee's job performance"
description: "A Performance Appraisal also referred to as a Performance Review Performance Evaluation Development Discussion or an Employee Appraisal sometimes shortened to pa is a periodic and systematic Process whereby the Job Performance of an Employee is documented and evaluated. This is done after Employees have been trained about Work and settled into their Jobs. Performance Appraisals are Part of Career Development and Consist of regular Reviews of Employee Performance within Organizations."
