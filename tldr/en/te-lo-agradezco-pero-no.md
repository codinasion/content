short_summary: "2006 single by Alejandro Sanz featuring Shakira"
description: "Te Lo Agradezco Pero no is a Song by spanish Singer Alejandro Sanz and colombian Singer Shakira for Sanz's eighth Studio Album el Tren de los Momentos. It was released by Warner Music Latina in December 2006 as the second single from the Record. The Track was written by Sanz, while Production was handled by him along with Lulo Pérez. 'Te Lo Agradezco, Pero No' is the second Duet recorded by the two Singers, following 'La Tortura' for Shakira's Album Fijación Oral Vol. 1. The Song came about after she approached Sanz telling him that she wanted to work with him on Something different from her own Material."
