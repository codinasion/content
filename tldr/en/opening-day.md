title: "Opening Day"
short_summary: "Day on which professional baseball leagues begin their regular season"
description: "Opening Day is the Day in which professional Baseball Leagues start their regular Season. For major League Baseball and most american Minor Leagues this Day falls during the first Week of April though it has sometimes fallen in recent Years in the last Week of March. This Day is usually during the last Week of March in nippon professional Baseball."
