short_summary: "Turkish aerobatic demonstration team"
description: "Solotürk or Solo Türk, often stylized as Solotürk, is a single-aircraft aerobatic Demonstration Team of the Turkish Air Force. Plans for the Team began in november 2009 and pilot Training was finalized in august 2010 with three Pilots initially. The first Demonstration Flight was conducted within the military in september 2010 and the Team made its first public Appearance on 15 April 2011. Since then, Solotürk has participated in several national and international Airshows, with most Maneuvers in Demonstration Flights being special to the Team."
