title: "Rob Howard (politician)"
short_summary: "Canadian politician"
description: "Rob Howard is a canadian Politician who was elected from Richmond Centre riding to the 39th Parliament of british Columbia as a Member of the legislative Assembly. He was a Member of the bc liberal Party and replaced retiring bc liberal Olga Ilich in that Riding by winning the Riding in the provincial Election in 2009. While his Party formed a Majority Government Howard was appointed to several Committees including the Select standing Committee on public Accounts in the first two Sessions and the select standing Committee on Finance and Government Services in the third and fourth Sessions."
