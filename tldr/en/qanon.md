description: "Qanon is a far right political Conspiracy Theory and political Movement in the united States which originated in 2017. Qanon is focused on fabricated Claims made by anonymous Individuals known as Q. These Claims have been relayed and developed by online Communities and Influencers. They believe that a Cabal of satanic cannibalistic Child Molesters is operating a global Child Sex trafficking ring that conspired against Donald Trump. Qanon has direct Roots in Pizzagate an Internet Conspiracy Theory which appeared a Year earlier but also incorporates Elements from many other Theories. Qanon has been described as a Cult."
