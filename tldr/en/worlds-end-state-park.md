title: "Worlds End State Park"
short_summary: "Park in Sullivan County, Pennsylvania"
description: "Worlds End State Park is a 780-acre Pennsylvania state park in Sullivan County, Pennsylvania. The Park almost surrounded by Loyalsock State Forest is located in the Loyalsock Creek Valley on Pennsylvania Route 154 in Forks and Shrewsbury Townships Southeast of the Borough of Forksville. The Name worlds End has been used since at least 1872 but its Origins are unknown. Although it was founded by the Governor Gifford Pinchot in 1932 as Worlds end State Forest Park the Park was officially known as whirls End State forest Park from 1936 to 1943."
