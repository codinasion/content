description: "Zheng Manuo was the first chinese international Student in Europe and the first chinese Jesuit Priest. In 1645 Zheng left Macau with french Priest Alexandre de Rhodes and came to rome in 1650. Zheng first studied at the Novitiate of Sant'Andrea in Rome. He studied and taught at the roman College from 1653 to 1660. Afterwards he studied Theology at the University of Bologna and finished his Studies in 1665 at the University of Coimbra Portugal."
