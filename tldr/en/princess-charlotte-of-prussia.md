short_summary: "Duchess of Saxe-Meiningen from 1914 to 1918"
description: "Princess Charlotte of Prussia was Duchess of Saxe-Meiningen from 1914 to 1918 as the Wife of Bernhard Iii, the Duchy's last Ruler. She was born at the Neues palais in Potsdam the second Child and eldest Daughter of Prince Frederick of Prussia a Member of the House of Hohenzollern who became crown Prince of Prussia in 1861 and german Emperor in 1888. Princess Royal Charlotte was the eldest Granddaughter of queen Victoria of the united Kingdom and Prince Albert of Saxe-Coburg and Gotha through her Mother Victoria."
