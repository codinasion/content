The Episode was written by Chris Carter and directed by Kim Manners. It helps explore the Series' overarching Mythology. Beginning earned a nielsen Household Rating of 11. 9, being watched by 20. 34 million People in its initial Broadcast. The Episode received mixed Reviews from Television Critics."
