description: "Stop crying your Heart out is a Song from the english Rock Band Oasis. The Song was written by Noel Gallagher and produced by Oasis. It was released as the second single from the Band's fifth Studio Album Heathen Chemistry on June 17 2002 in the united Kingdom. It was serviced to radio in the united States several Weeks before its uk Release in may 2002. Liam Gallagher is the lead Vocalist of the Song with Noel on the Backing Vocals."
