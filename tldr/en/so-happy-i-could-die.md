title: "So Happy I Could Die"
short_summary: "2009 Song by Lady Gaga"
description: "'so happy i could die' is a Song from american Singer Lady Gaga, taken from her second major Release and her third Ep, the Fame Monster. It was written by herself, Nadir 'Redone' Khayat, and Nicolas 'Space Cowboy' Dresti, with Production helmed by Redone. Inspired by Gaga's Fear of what she described as the 'Alcohol Monster', the Song is about the Influences of Alcoholism, and also explores several sexual Themes in its Lyrics as well as Liberalism."
