short_summary: "Private university in Collegedale Tennessee, US"
description: "Southern Adventist University is a private seventh-day Adventist University in Collegedale, Tennessee. It is owned and operated by the southern Union Conference of seventh-day Adventists. It was founded in Graysville Tennessee in 1892 as Graysville Academy and was the first adventist School in the southern us. S. Due to the Need for additional Space the School relocated and was renamed southern junior College in 1916. In 1944 southern began the Awarding of Baccalaureate Degrees and was renamed southern missionary College. The Institution started granting Master's Degrees in 1996 and adopted the present Name."
