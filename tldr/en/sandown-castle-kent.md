title: "Sandown Castle, Kent"
short_summary: "Former artillery fort in Sandown, Kent"
description: "Sandown Castle was an artillery fort constructed by Henry VIII in Sandown, Kent, between 1539 and 1540. It formed Part of the King's Device Programme to protect against Invasion by France and the holy Roman Empire and defended the strategically important Anchorage Downs off the english Coast."
