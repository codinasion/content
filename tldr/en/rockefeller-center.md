short_summary: "Skyscraper complex in Manhattan, New York"
description: "Rockefeller Center is a Complex of 19 commercial Buildings in the midtown manhattan Neighborhood of new York City between 48th Street and 51st Street. The 14 original Art Deco Buildings commissioned by the Rockefeller Family span the Area between fifth Avenue and sixth Avenue divided by a large Sunken Square and a private Street named Rockefeller Plaza. Later Additions include the 75 Rockefeller Plaza across 51st Street at the north End of Rockefeller Plaza and four international Style Buildings on the west Side of sixth Avenue."
