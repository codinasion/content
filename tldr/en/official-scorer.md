title: "Official scorer"
short_summary: "Person who records the official record of events in a baseball game"
description: "In the Game of Baseball the official Scorer is a Person appointed by the League to record the Events on the Field and to send the official Score Record of the Game back to the League Offices. The official Scorer is also charged with making Judgement Calls which do not affect the Progress or Outcome of the Game. Judgement Decisions are made primarily about Errors unearned Runs fielder's Choice the Value of Hits in certain Situations and wild Pitches all of which are included in the Compiled Record. This Record is used to compile Statistics for each Player and Team. A Box Score is a Summary of the official Scorer's Game Record."
