title: "The Boat Race 2008"
short_summary: "Oxford versus Cambridge rowing race"
description: "The 154th Boat Race took Place on March 29 2008. The Event annually is a side-by-side Rowing Race between Crews from the Universities of Oxford and Cambridge along the Thames. Oxford won the Race. The Crew at Oxford featured the oldest Competitor in Boat Race History. The Race took Place in very difficult Weather Conditions strong Winds and heavy Rain resulting in the slowest Winning Time in more than sixty Years. Oxford won by six Lengths the biggest Margin of Victory since the 2004 Race."
