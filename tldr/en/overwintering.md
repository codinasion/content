short_summary: "Process by which some organisms pass through or wait out the winter season"
description: "Overwintering is the Process by which some Organisms pass through or wait out the Winter Season or Pass through the Period of the Year when Winter Conditions make normal Activity or even survival difficult or near impossible. In some Cases Winter is characterized not necessarily by cold but by dry Conditions passing through such Periods could likewise be called overwintering."
