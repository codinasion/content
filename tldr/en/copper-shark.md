It is distributed in a Number of separate Populations in the northeastern and southwestern atlantic off southern Africa in the northwestern and eastern Pacific and around Australia and new Zealand with scattered Reports from the equatorial Regions. The Species can be found from brackish Rivers and Estuaries to shallow Bays and Harbours to offshore Waters 100m or more deep. Females are found apart from Males for most of the Year and Conduct seasonal Migrations.
