short_summary: "1967 song by David Bowie"
description: "Silly boy Blue is a Song written and recorded by the english Singer David Bowie. Originally demoed as a mod-influenced Track about a teenage Runaway in 1965 it was revised in 1966 with new Lyrics and released in June 1967 on Bowie's self-titled Debut Album. This Version reflects Bowie's Fascination with tibetan Buddhism by featuring various spiritual and geographical References in the Lyrics and varied Instrumentation such as a Trumpet Cello and chinese Gong. Bowie remade the Song twice during his Stint with the Riot Squad in April 1967: as an instrumental for live Performances and acoustically, which has appeared on Compilations."
