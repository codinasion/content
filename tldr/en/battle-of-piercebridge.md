short_summary: "Battle of the First English Civil War"
description: "The Piercebridge Battle was fought on 1 December 1642 in County Durham England during the first english civil War. The Earl of Newcastle was advancing to york from newcastle upon Tyne with an Army of 6000 to strengthen the local Royalists. Aware of his Approach the Parliamentarians at Piercebridge defended the main Crossing of the Tees River. About 580 Troops had barricaded the Bridge under Command of Captain John Hotham."
