description: "Storo is a rapid Transit Station on the Ring Line of the oslo Metro and a tram Station on the grnerlkkatorshov Line of the Oslo Tramway. It is located at Storo in the nordre Aker Borough of oslo Norway. The Tram Station opened on 28 November 1902, and the rapid Transit Station on 20 August 2003. Metro Lines 4 and 5 Run to the Station. The Tram Station serves Lines 11, 12 and 18. The Station is within walking Distance of the gjvik Line Station Grefsen. The Station also serves as a Bus Hub along ring 3. Storo is a mixed residential and commercial Area and a Shopping Center is located North of the Subway Station."
