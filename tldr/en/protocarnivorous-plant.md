title: "Protocarnivorous plant"
short_summary: "Carnivorous plant that can not digest prey"
description: "According to some Definitions a protocarnivorous Plant Traps and Kills Insects or other Animals but lacks the Ability to either directly digest or absorb Nutrients from its Prey like a carnivorous Plant. The morphological Adaptations such as sticky Trichomes or pitfall Traps of protocarnivorous Plants parallel the trap Structures of confirmed carnivorous Plants."
