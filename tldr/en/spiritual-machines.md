short_summary: "2000 studio album by Our Lady Peace"
description: "Spiritual Machines is the fourth Studio Album by the Canadian alternative Rock band our Lady Peace, released by Columbia Records in December 2000. Although not initially intended the Project evolved into a conceptual Interpretation of the Futurist and Inventor Raymond Kurzweil's Book the Age of spiritual Machines. Short Tracks of spoken Dialogue from Kurzweil himself are interspersed among the actual Songs on the Album. The Keyboard k250 one of his Inventions was used throughout the Recording of the Album."
