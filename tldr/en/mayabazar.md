description: "Mayabazar is a 1957 indian Epic Hindu mythological Film directed by K. V. Reddy. It was produced under their Banner vijaya Productions by nagi Reddi and Chakrapani. The Film was shot simultaneously in Telugu and Tamil, with a few Differences in the Cast. The Story is an Adaptation of the folktale Sasirekha Parinayam based on Characters from the Epic Mahabharata. It revolves around the Roles of Krishna and Ghatotkacha as they try to reunite Arjuna's Son Abhimanyu with his Love Balarama's Daughter Sasirekha. The Telugu Version Features Gummadi, Mukkamala, Ramana Reddy, and Relangi in supporting Roles, with D. Balasubramaniam, R. Balasubramaniam, V. M. Ezhumalai, and K. A. Thangavelu plays these Parts in the Tamil Version."
