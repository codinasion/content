description: "Highway 132 commonly referred to as Highway 132 is a provincially maintained Highway in the canadian Province of Ontario. Its western Terminus is located on Highway 41 near Dacre while its eastern Terminus is located on Highway 60 in Renfrew. The Length of Highway 132 is 30. 3 Km, situated entirely in Renfrew County. It travels through the Communities of Shamrock, Dacre and Renfrew."
