title: "Tajikistan at the 2016 Summer Paralympics"
short_summary: "Sporting event delegation"
description: "Tajikistan has sent a Delegation to rio de Janeiro Brazil in September 2016 for the 2016 Summer Paralympics. This was the fourth consecutive Appearance of the Country at the Paralympic Games after it made its Debut at the 2004 Summer Paralympics. Tajikistan was represented in Rio de Janeiro by a single Athlete the Sprinter Romikhudo Dodikhudoev. He finished 14th overall in both of the Men's 400 Metres T47 and the Men's 100 Metres T47 and these Performances meant he did not qualify for the final of both Competitions."
