description: "Pot O' Gold is the fourth Episode of the third Season of american musical Television Series Glee and the forty-eighth overall. It was written by ali Adler and acted by adam Shankman and was first Broadcast on Fox in the united States on november 1 2011. The Episode featured the Arrival of Irish foreign Exchange Student Rory Flanagan at Mckinley High, a new Challenger to Sue Sylvester in her congressional Race, and the ongoing Fragmentation of the Show's central Glee Club, New Directions."
