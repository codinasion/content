title: "Tropical Storm Etau (2009)"
short_summary: "Pacific tropical storm in 2009"
description: "Tropical Storm Etau was the deadliest tropical Cyclone to strike Japan since Typhoon Tokage in 2004. On 8 august 2009 Forming from a low Pressure Area the System slowly intensified into a tropical Storm. As it approached japan the Tracking of the Tracks along a curved Path around the Edge of a subtropical Ridge continued to intensify. By august 10 the Cyclone reached its peak Intensity as a weak tropical Storm with Winds of 75kmh and a barometric Pressure of 992hpa. Shortly after, Etau began to weaken. Increasing Wind Shear led to the Center becoming Devoid of Convection and the System weakened on August 13 to a tropical Depression. The Remnants of Etau persisted nearly three Days before dissipating early on August 16."
