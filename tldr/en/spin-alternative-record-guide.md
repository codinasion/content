short_summary: "Reference book edited by Eric Weisbard and Craig Marks"
description: "The Spin Alternative Record Guide is a Music Reference Book compiled by the american Music Magazine Spin and published by Vintage Books in 1995. It was edited by Rock Critic eric Weisbard and Craig Marks who was at the Time the Magazine's editor-in-chief. The Book has essays and reviews from a Number of prominent Critics on Albums, Artists and Genres considered relevant to the alternative Music Movement. Contributors who were consulted for the Guide include Ann Powers, Rob Sheffield, Simon Reynolds and Michael Azerrad."
