short_summary: "Armored corvette of the German Imperial Navy"
description: "Sms Baden was one of four armored Ships of the Sachsen-Class of the german Kaiserliche Marine. Her sister Ships were Sachsen, Bayern, and Württemberg. Baden was built from 1876 to 1883 in the Kaiserliche Marine in Kiel. The Ship was officially put in the imperial Navy in September 1883. She was armed with six 26cm Guns in two open Barbettes."
