description: "U. S. Route 89 in the U. S. The State of Utah is a north-south united States highway that Stretches over more than 502 Miles in the central Part of the State Making it the longest Road in utah. Between Provo and brigham City us-89 Serves as a local Road paralleling interstate 15 but Portions from Arizona to provo and brigham City Northeast to wyoming serve separate Corridors. The former provides Access to several national Parks and Arizona, and the latter Connects I-15 with Logan, the State's only Metropolitan Statistical Area not on the Interstate."
