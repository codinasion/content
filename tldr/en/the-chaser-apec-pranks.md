description: "The Chaser apec Pranks were a Series of comic Stunts coordinated and performed by the australian Satire Group the Chaser for the Tv Series the Chaser's War on Everything. Pranks were done during the Asia pacific economic Cooperation Leaders summit in Sydney Australia. The most notable Prank was the Breach of an Apec restricted Zone in the Heart of Sydney's central Business District on September 6. Julian Morrow directed a fake canadian Motorcade that was permitted by Police through the restricted Zone and not detected until chas Licciardello was dressed as osama Bin Laden."
