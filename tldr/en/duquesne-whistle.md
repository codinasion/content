short_summary: "2012 single by Bob Dylan"
description: "Duquesne Whistle is a Song written by Bob Dylan and the grateful dead Lyricist Robert Hunter that appears as the Opening Track on Dylan's Studio Album Tempest. It was first released as a digital single on August 27, 2012 through Columbia Records then as a Music Video two Days later. A limited Edition Record Store Day 7 Stereo single was released on november 23 2012. It was also anthologized on the 2014 Reissue of the essential Bob Dylan. Like much of Dylan's 21st-century Output he himself produced the Song using the Pseudonym Jack Frost."
