short_summary: "1892 comic novel by George and Weeden Grossmith"
description: "The Diary of a Nobody is an english comic Novel written by the Brothers George and weedon Grossmith with Illustrations by the Latter. It was originally published in punch Magazine in 188889 as an intermittent Serial and first appeared in Book Form in 1892 with extended Text and added Illustrations. The diary Records daily Events in the Lives of a London Clerk Charles pooter his Wife carrie his Son William Lupin and numerous Friends and Acquaintances over a Period of 15 Months."
