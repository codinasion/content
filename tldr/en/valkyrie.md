title: "Valkyrie"
short_summary: "Figures in Norse mythology"
description: "In norse Mythology a Valkyrie is one of a Host of female Figures who guide Souls of the dead to the Hall of Odin Valhalla. There, the deceased Warriors become Einherjar. When the Einherjars do n't prepare for the Events of Ragnark the Valkyries bear Mead. Valkyries also appear as Lovers of Heroes and other Mortals where they are sometimes described as Daughters of Royalty sometimes accompanied by Ravens and sometimes connected with Swans or Horses."
