short_summary: "Church in Anglesey, Wales"
description: "St Michael's Church, Llanfihangel Ysgeifiog, is a former parish Church in Anglesey, Wales, which is now closed and in Ruins. The Structure dates from the 15th Century and a Chapel was added on the north Side in the 17th Century. A Replacement Church was constructed elsewhere in the Parish in 1847 and the old Church was partly demolished and abandoned. Some Restoration Work has taken Place in the 21st Century and occasional Services have taken Place."
