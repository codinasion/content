The Character was created by Series Producer shonda Rhimes and portrayed from 2005 to 2010 by the Actress Katherine Heigl. Introduced as a surgical Intern at the fictional Seattle Grace Hospital, Izzie worked her Way up to resident Level, while her Relationships with her Colleagues Meredith Grey, Cristina Yang, Alex Karev and George O'Malley formed a focal Point of the Series."
