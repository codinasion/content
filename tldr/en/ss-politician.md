title: "SS Politician"
short_summary: "Cargo ship that operated between 1923 and 1941"
description: "Ss Politician was a Cargo Ship that ran aground in 1941 off the Coast of the hebridean Island of Eriskay. Her Cargo consisted of 22000 Cases of Scotch Whisky and 3 million of jamaican Bank Notes. Much of the Whisky was recovered by Islanders across the Hebrides contrary to marine Salvage Laws. Because no Duty had been paid on the whisky Members of the Hm Customs and excise pursued and prosecuted those who removed the Cargo."
