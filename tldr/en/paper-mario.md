description: "Paper Mario is a video Game Series Part of the Mario Franchise developed by intelligent Systems and produced by Nintendo. It combines elements from the role-playing, action-adventure, and puzzle genres. Players control a paper cutout version of Mario, usually with allies, on a quest to defeat the antagonist, primarily Bowser. The Series consists of six Games and one Spin-Off ; the first, Paper Mario, was released for the Nintendo 64, and the most recent, Paper Mario: the Origami King, for the Nintendo Switch."
