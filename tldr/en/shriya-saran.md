short_summary: "Indian actress (born 1982)"
description: "Shriya Saran Bhatnagar is an indian Actress who primarily works in Telugu, Tamil, and Hindi Films. Although Saran aspired to be a well-known Dancer she became an Actress and made her Film Debut with the Telugu Film Ishtam. Saran had her first commercial Success with Santosham. She is a Recipient of one South indian International Movie Award along with four Filmfare Awards South Nominations."
