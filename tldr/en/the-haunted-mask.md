The Book follows Carly Beth, a Girl who buys a Halloween Mask from a Store. After putting on the Mask, she starts acting differently and Discovers that the Mask has become her Face ; she is unable to pull the Mask off. R. L. Stine says he got the Idea for the Book from his Son who had put on a Frankenstein Mask and had Trouble getting off."
