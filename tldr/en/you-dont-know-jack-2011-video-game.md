The Game was released in north America on 8 february 2011 for microsoft Windows nintendo ds Playstation 3 Wii and xbox 360 Platforms. The Game took Advantage of online Connectivity and other Features of modern Gaming Consoles. A single Player Ios Port was released about two Months later but was later pulled in Anticipation of a more robust Client based on Facebook's Version of the Game."
