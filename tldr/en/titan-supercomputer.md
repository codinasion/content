short_summary: "American supercomputer"
description: "Titan or Olcf-3 was a Supercomputer from Cray built for Use at Oak ridge national Laboratory for a Variety of Science Projects. Titan was an Upgrade of Jaguar, a previous Supercomputer at Oak Ridge, that uses Graphics processing Units in Addition to conventional central processing Units. Titan was the first Hybrid in Terms of Performance over 10petaflops. The upgrade began in October 2011, commenced stability testing in October 2012 and it became available to researchers in early 2013. The initial Cost of the Upgrade was 60 million and financed primarily by the united States Department of Energy."
