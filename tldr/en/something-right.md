short_summary: "2008 Westlife song"
description: "'something right' is a Song by Irish Boy Band Westlife from their eighth Studio Album Back Home. The Song was released as the Band's second single in Asia and Europe following their first single, 'Home', as well as the Album's overall third single. The Song was composed by Arnthor Birgisson Savan Kotecha and Rami Yacoub. A Music Video was filmed in London in December 2007 and premiered on 7 March 2008. This single was not released as a single in Uk and Ireland."
