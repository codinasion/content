title: "USS Oregon (BB-3)"
short_summary: "Indiana-class pre-dreadnought battleship of the United States Navy"
description: "Uss Oregon was the third and last Member of the indiana Class of pre-dreadnought Battleships built for the united States navy in the 1890s. The three Ships were built as Part of a Modernization Program aimed at strengthening the american Fleet to prepare for a potential Conflict with a european Navy. The three indiana-class Ships were designed for short-range Operations in Defense of the united States and carried a main Battery of four 13-inch Guns in a Pair of Gun Turrets. Oregon and her sister Ships were the first modern Battleships built for the united States though they suffered significant Stability and seakeeping Problems owing to their small Size and insufficient Freeboard."
