short_summary: "2002 video game"
description: "Star Fox adventures is an action-adventure Game that was developed by rare and published by Nintendo in 2002. The Game had a long Development Cycle beginning in 1997. Originally developed as Dinosaur Planet with rare-created Characters as the Protagonists, Nintendo Producer Shigeru Miyamoto convinced Rare to redesign the Game as Part of the Star Fox Franchise. Its planned Release on the Nintendo 64 was cancelled, with Development ultimately shifting forward one Hardware Generation to the Gamecube."
