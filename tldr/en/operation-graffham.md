title: "Operation Graffham"
short_summary: "1944 Allied military deception operation"
description: "Operation Graffham was a military Deception used by the Allies during the second World War. It formed Part of Operation bodyguard a broad strategic Deception intended to disguise the imminent allied Invasion of Normandy. Graffham provided political Support for the visual and wireless Deception of Operation fortitude north. The Operations together created a fictional Threat to norway in the Summer of 1944."
