short_summary: "American football player (born 1962)"
description: "William Henry Fuller Jr. Is an american former Professional Football Player who was a defensive End for 13 Seasons in the National Football League. Fuller played College Football for the North Carolina Tar Heels and professionally for the Philadelphia/Baltimore Stars of the United States Football League, and the Houston Oilers, Philadelphia Eagles, and San Diego Chargers of the Nfl. Fuller was one of the better Pass Rushers in the Nfl during his Time in the League and finished his Career with 100. 5 sacks. He was named to the pro Bowl four Times and was twice named to the all-american List while in College. Fuller was inducted into the virginia Sports Hall of Fame in 2004. During his Career and after his Retirement Fuller also did considerable Work in raising Funds for Diabetes Research."
