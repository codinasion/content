title: "SMS Kaiser Max (1862)"
short_summary: "Ironclad warship of the Austro-Hungarian Navy"
description: "Sms kaiser Max was the lead Ship of the armored Frigates of Kaiser Max built in the 1860S for the austrian Navy. Her Keel was laid in October 1861 at the Stabilimento Tecnico Triestino Shipyard ; she was launched in May 1862, and was completed in 1863. She carried her main Battery composed of sixteen 48-pounder Guns and fifteen 24-Pounders in a traditional Broadside Arrangement protected by an 110-mm Thick armored Belt."
