description: "The Kingston Trio is an american Folk and pop Music Group that helped launch the Folk Revival of late 1950S to late 1960S. The Group began as a nightclub Act in san francisco Bay Area with an original Lineup of dave Guard Bob Shane and nick Reynolds. It rose to international Popularity driven by unprecedented Sales of Lp Records and helped alter the Direction of popular Music in the us. S."
