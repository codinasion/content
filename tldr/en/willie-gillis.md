short_summary: "Fictional character by Norman Rockwell"
description: "Willie Gillis, Jr. A fictional Character created by Norman Rockwell for a Series of Paintings from World War Ii that appeared in 11 the Saturday evening Post Issues between 1941 and 1946. Gillis was an Everyman with the Rank of private whose Career was tracked on the Cover of the Post from Induction through Discharge without being depicted in Battle. He and his Girlfriend were modeled by two of Rockwell's Acquaintances."
