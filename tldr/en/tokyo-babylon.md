description: "Tokyo Babylon also known as Tokyo Babylon a save Tokyo City Story is a japanese manga Series written and illustrated by Clamp. It follows subaru sumeragi the Head of the Sumeragi Clan and his sister Hokuto as they work to protect Tokyo from a Myriad of supernatural Threats while living with a Man named seishiro Sakurazu. From 1990 to 1993 Shinshokan marketed it in the South and Wings Magazines and collected it in 7 Tankbon Volumes."
