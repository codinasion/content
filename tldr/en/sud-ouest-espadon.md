title: "Sud-Ouest Espadon"
short_summary: "French post-war prototype interceptor"
description: "The Sud-Ouest So. Espadon 6020 was a french post-war Prototype Interceptor designed and built by Sncaso in the late 1940S. The french Air Force considered the Design a Failure despite some Records being set and cancelled Plans to put it into Service in 1951. Only four Aircraft were built and were later modified to serve as Testbeds for the mixed Rocket and turbojet-powered Sncaso so. 9000 Trident program. Only one badly damaged Aircraft Survives."
