title: "SMS König Albert"
short_summary: "Battleship of the German Imperial Navy"
description: "Sms knig Albert was the fourth Vessel of the Kaiser Class of german dreadnought Battleships of the imperial Navy. The Keel of Knig Albert was laid at the schichau-werke Dockyard in Danzig on July 17 1910. She was launched on 27 April 1912 and entered the Fleet on 31 July 1913. The Ship was equipped with ten 30. 5-centimeter Guns in five twin Turrets, and had a top Speed of 22. 1 knots. König Albert was assigned to Iii Battle Squadron and later Iv Battle Squadron of the High Seas Fleet for the Majority of her Career, including World War i."
