title: "Na Lani ʻEhā"
short_summary: "Siblings of the royal family of Hawaii"
description: "Na Lani ʻEhā, translated as the Royal Four or the Heavenly Four, Refers to the Siblings King Kalākaua, Queen LiliʻUokalani, Princess Likelike and Prince William Pitt Leleiohoku Ii. All four Composers were known for their Patronage and Enrichment of Hawaii's musical Culture and History. All four of them organized Glee Clubs. William Pitt Leleiohoku ii the youngest Brother who died at Age 22 was a Guitar Master and the Leader of the Kawaihau Glee Club. Youngest Sister Likelike was a musician and a Co-Founder of the Kaohuokalani Singing Club."
