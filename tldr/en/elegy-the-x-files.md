description: "Elegy is the twenty-second Episode of the fourth Season of the american Science Fiction Tv Series the X-Files. It was written by john Shiban and directed by James charleston. The Episode aired on the Fox Network in the united States on may 4 1997. It is a 'monster-of-the-week' Story, a Stand-Alone Plot which is unconnected to the Series' Wider Mythology, although Scully's Battle with Cancer is an important Plot Point. Elegy earned a nielsen Rating of 10. 6 and was seen by 17. 1 million Viewers upon its initial Broadcast. The Episode received mostly positive Reviews from Televisions Critics ; the Performance of lead Actress Gillian Anderson was especially praised."
