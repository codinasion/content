description: "The Rescuers is an american Animation Adventure Comedy-Drama Film produced by Walt Disney Productions in 1977 and released by Buena Vista Distribution. Bob Newhart and eva Gabor respectively play Bernard and Bianca two Mice who are Members of the Rescue Aid Society an international Mouse Organization dedicated to helping Abduction Victims around the World. Both must rescue the young orphan Penny from two Treasure Hunters who intend to use her to help them obtain a huge Diamond. The Film is based on a Series of Books by Margery Sharp, including the Rescuers and Miss Bianca."
