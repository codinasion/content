short_summary: "Supertall skyscraper in Brooklyn, New York"
description: "The Brooklyn Tower is a supertall mixed-use, primarily residential skyscraper in the Downtown Brooklyn neighborhood of New York City. It was developed by Jds Development Group on the north Side of Dekalb Avenue near Flatbush Avenue. The main Portion of the Skyscraper is a 74-story 1066-foot residential Building designed by Shop Architects built from 2018 to 2022. The Dime Savings Bank Building designed by Mowbray and Uffinger Dates back to the 1900s and is positioned on the Base of the Skyscraper."
