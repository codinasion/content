short_summary: "North–south parkway in Westchester County, New York"
description: "The Sprain Brook Parkway is a 12. 65-mile controlled-access Parkway in Westchester County, New York, United States. It begins at an Interchange with the Bronx River Parkway in the City of Yonkers and Ends at the former Site of the Hawthorne Circle where it merges with the taconic State Parkway. The Parkway serves an Alternate to the Bronx River parkway boasting an Interchange Connection through western Westchester with Interstate287. New York State Route987F is internally named by the Department of Transportation as an unsigned Reference Route."
