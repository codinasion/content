description: "The Uk Border Agency was the Border Control Agency of the Government of the United Kingdom and Part of the Home Office that was superseded by Uk Visas and Immigration, Border Force and Immigration Enforcement in April 2013. It was formed on 1 april 2008 as an Executive Agency as a Result of a Merger of the Border and Immigration Agency Ukvisas and the Detection Functions of Hm Revenue and Customs. The Decision to set up the Country's single Border Control Organisation was taken following a cabinet Office Report."
