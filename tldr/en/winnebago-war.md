title: "Winnebago War"
short_summary: "1827 conflict in United States"
description: "The winnebago War also known as Winnebago Uprising was a brief Conflict that took Place in the upper Mississippi River Region of the united States in 1827 primarily in what is now wisconsin State. Not quite a War, the Hostilities were limited to a few Attacks on american Civilians by a Portion of the Winnebago Native American Tribe. The Ho-Chunks were responding to a Wave of lead Miners trespassing on their Lands and to false Rumors that the united States had sent two ho-chunk Prisoners to a rival Tribe for Execution."
