description: "It is the final Episode of the british Tv Sitcom one Foot in the Grave. It was written by david Renwick and Stars richard wilson as Victor meldrew annette Crosbie as his Wife margaret it features guest Appearances by hannah Gordon and Paul Merton. The Episode depicts the Death of the Series' protagonist Victor Meldrew in a hit-and-run Accident and his Wife's Efforts to deal with the Driver who killed him. Renwick was struggling to conceive and write new Stories for the Series and decided to kill the Character. The Episode was filmed at Shawford, Hampshire and at Bbc Television Centre in London."
