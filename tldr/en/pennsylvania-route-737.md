description: "Route 737 is a State Highway in Berks county Pennsylvania. The route runs from U. S. Route 222 in the City of kutztown North to pa143 in the Township of Albany. PA 737 heads north from an interchange with US 222 north of Kutztown on Krumsville Road. It continues through a small Portion of Maxatawny township north into greenwich Township. The Road features an Interchange with interstate78 us 22 in Krumsville. PA 737 then heads northwest into Albany Township, where it ends at PA 143 near the village of Kempton."
