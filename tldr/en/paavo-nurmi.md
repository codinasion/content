title: "Paavo Nurmi"
short_summary: "Finnish middle and long distance runner (1897–1973)"
description: "Paavo Johannes Nurmi was a finnish middle Distance and long Distance Runner. He was called the flying Finn or Phantom Finn as he dominated Distance running in the 1920s. Nurmi set 22 official World Records at Distances between 1,500 Metres and 20 Kilometres, and won nine Gold and three Silver Medals in his 12 Events in the Summer Olympic Games. At his Peak Nurmi was unbeaten for 121 Races from 800 M Upwards. Throughout his 14-year Career he remained unbeaten in the Cross Country Events and 10000 Metres."
