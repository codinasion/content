short_summary: "Sporting event delegation"
description: "The united States sent a Delegation to beijing China for the Paralympics in 2008. A total of 213 U. S. Only two Sports in which Americans did not compete were soccer 5-a-side and 7-a-side. The american Delegation included 16 former Members of the U. S. Military, including 3 Veterans of the Iraq War. Among them were shot-putter Scott Winkler who was paralyzed in an Accident in Iraq and Swimmer Melissa stockwell a former united States army Officer who lost her left Leg in the War."
