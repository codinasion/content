description: "Rob-B-Hood is a 2006 Hong kong Action Comedy Drama written produced and directed by Benny Chan an ensemble Cast includes matthew Medvedev Louis Koo Yuen Biao Michael Hui Gao Yuanyu. It tells the Story of a Kidnapping gone wrong in hong Kong a Trio of Burglars consisting of the Thongs Octopus and the Landlord kidnap a Baby from a wealthy Family on Behalf of. With the Landlord arrested, Thongs and Octopus take Care of the Baby for a short Time, developing strong Bonds with him. Reluctant to Hand the Baby over the two are forced to protect him from the Triads who hired them in the first Place."
