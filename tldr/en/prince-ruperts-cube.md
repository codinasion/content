short_summary: "Cube that fits through hole in smaller cube"
description: "In Geometry Prince Rupert's Cube is the largest Cube that can pass through a Hole Cut through a Unit Cube without splitting it into two Parts. Its Side Length is approximately 1. 06, 6 % larger than the Side length 1 of the Unit Cube through which it passes. The Problem of finding the largest Square that lies entirely within a Cube Unit is closely related and has the same Solution."
