title: "Love Power (Disenchanted song)"
short_summary: "2022 single by Idina Menzel"
description: "Love Power is a Song written by Composer Alan menken and lyricist Stephen Schwartz for Disney's musical Fantasy Film disenchanted the Sequel to the 2007 Film enchanted. The Song is performed by american Actress and Singer Idina Menzel, who plays Nancy Tremaine in both Films. It is a broadway-inspired Power Ballad with Lyrics advocating for the Strength that Love and Memories have to overcome Challenges. Menzel also recorded a pop Version of the Song, entitled 'Love Power ', for the Film's Closing Credits, which Walt Disney Records released as its Soundtrack's lead single in November 2022."
