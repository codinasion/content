It premiered in the united States on the Fox Network on 17 may 1996. The Teleplay was written by Series Creator chris Carter based on a Story developed with lead Actor david Duchovny and directed by R. W. Goodwin. The Episode is one of several that explored the Series' overarching Mythology. Talitha Cumi achieved a nielsen Household Rating of 11. 2, being watched by 17. 86 million People watch it first. The Episode received mostly positive Reviews from Critics."
