description: "The second Season of Cobra Kai, stylized as Cobra Kaii, was released on Youtube Premium on April 24, 2019, and consisted of 10 Episodes. The Series is a direct Sequel to the original four Films in the Karate Kid Franchise, focusing on the Characters of Daniel Larusso and Johnny Lawrence over 30 Years after the original Film. This is the final Season to be originally released on Youtube's streaming Service as Netflix would acquire the Streaming Rights to Cobra Kai following Youtube's Decision not to renew the Series past a third Season."
