short_summary: "1971 studio album by Jethro Tull"
description: "Aqualung is the fourth Studio Album by the british Progressive Rock Band Jethro Tull, released on 19 March 1971, by Chrysalis Records. It is widely regarded as a Concept Album which carries a central Theme of the Distinction between Religion and God though the Band have said there was no Intention to make a Concept Album and that only a few Songs have a unifying Theme. Aqualung's Success signalled a turning Point in the Career of the Band, which went on to become a major Radio and touring Act."
