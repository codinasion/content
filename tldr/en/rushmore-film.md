short_summary: "1998 American film"
description: "Rushmore is a 1998 american Comedy Film directed by Wes Anderson about a Teenager named Max Fischer, his Friendship with rich Industrialist Herman Blume, and their shared Affection for elementary School teacher Rosemary Cross. The Film was written by Anderson and Owen Wilson. The Soundtrack features multiple Songs from Bands associated with the british Invasion of the 1960S. The Filming began around Houston Texas in november 1997 and lasted 50 Days until late january 1998."
