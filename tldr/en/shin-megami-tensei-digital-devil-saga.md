description: "Shin Megami Tensei digital Devil Saga is a Duology of role-playing Video Games developed by Atlus for Playstation 2. These are a Spin-Off of Megami Tensei. The first Digital Devil Saga was released in Japan in 2004, North America in 2005, and Europe in 2006. Its direct Sequel Shin Megami tensei digital Devil saga 2 was released in Japan and north America in 2005 and in Europe and Australasia in 2007. The Games were published by Ghostlight in Europe and Australasia and by atlus and its north american Subsidiary Atlus Usa in other Regions."
