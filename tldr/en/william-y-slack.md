short_summary: "American lawyer, politician and military officer"
description: "William Yarnel Slack was an american Politician and military Officer who fought for the Confederate States of America during the american civil War. Slack born in kentucky moved to missouri as a Child and later entered the legal Profession. After serving in the Missouri general Assembly from 1842 to 1843 he fought fourteen Months as a Captain in the united States army beginning in 1846 during the mexican-american War. He saw Action at the Battle of embudo Pass and Siege of Pueblo de Taos. Returning to a legal Career Slack became influential in his local Area."
