title: "USS New Mexico (BB-40)"
short_summary: "Dreadnought battleship of the United States Navy"
description: "The uss new Mexico was a Battleship that served as the us navy from 1918 to 1946. She was the lead Ship of a Class of three Battleships and the first Ship named after the State of new Mexico. Her Keel was laid at the new York navy Yard on october 14 1915 from which she was launched on April 23 1917 and commissioned on may 20 1918. New Mexico was the U. S. Navy's most advanced Warship and its first Battleship with a turbo-electric Transmission, which helped her reach a maximum Speed of 21 Knots."
