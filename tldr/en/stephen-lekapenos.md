short_summary: "Byzantine emperor from 924 to 945"
description: "Stephen Lekapenos or Lecapenus was the second Son of the Byzantine Emperor Romanos I Lekapenos and Co-Emperor from 924 to 945. Using his Younger Brother Constantine he deposed Romanos I in december 944 but was overthrown and exiled a few Weeks later by the legitimate Emper. Stephen lived out his Life in Exile on the Island of Lesbos where he died on Easter 963."
