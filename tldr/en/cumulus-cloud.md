title: "Cumulus cloud"
short_summary: "Genus of clouds, low-level cloud"
description: "Cumulus Clouds are clouds with flat Bases that are often described as puffy Cotton-Like or Fluffy in Appearance. Their Name stems from the latin Cumulus meaning a Heap. Cumulus Clouds are low-level Clouds, generally less than 2,000 M in Altitude unless they are the more vertical Cumulus Congestus Form. Cumulus Clouds can appear by themselves in Lines or in Clusters."
