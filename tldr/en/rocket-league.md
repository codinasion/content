short_summary: "2015 video game"
description: "Rocket League is a vehicular Soccer Video Game developed and released by Psyonix. The Game was released in July 2015 for Playstation 4 and Windows with later Versions for Xbox one and nintendo Switch. Rocket League was available on Steam for Windows and in the Playstation Store for Playstation 4 Users. In June 2016 505 Games began distributing a physical retail Version for the Playstation 4 and xbox one with Warner Bros. Interactive Entertainment has taken over these Duties by the End of 2017. Versions for Linux and Macos were also released in 2016 but Support for their Online Services was dropped in 2020. The Game went to Free-To-Play in September 2020 when Epic Games took Control."
