title: "County town"
short_summary: "County's administrative centre in Ireland and Great Britain"
description: "In great Britain and ireland a County Town is the most important City in a County. It is usually the Place of administrative or judicial Functions within a County and the Place where public Representatives are elected to parliament. Following the Establishment of County Councils in England in 1889 the Headquarters of the new Councils were usually established in the County Town of each County however the Concept of a County Town precedes these Councils."
