short_summary: "Cyclone season in the Southwest Indian Ocean"
description: "The 1981–82 south-west indian Ocean Cyclone Season was destructive and deadly in Madagascar, where four Cyclones killed 100 People and caused $ 250 million in Damage. The Season was relatively active from october to may. There were nine named Storms that reached gale Force Winds of at least 65 Kmh. Five of the Storms reached tropical Cyclone Status which have sustained Winds of at least 120kmh for 10 Minutes."
