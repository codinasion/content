short_summary: "2017 single by Tove Styrke"
description: "'say My Name' is a Song by swedish Singer Tove Styrke from her third Studio Album Sway. It was released on April 28 2017 as the lead single of the Album via Sony Music. The Song was written by the Singer, Elof Loelv and Tinashe 'T Collar' Sibanda, with Production by Loelv. Say my Name is a minimal Electropop Song with a prominent Ukulele Riff which Styrke convinced Loelv to integrate as a central Part. She described the Song as musically and lyrically different from the Material of her previous Studio Album Kiddo, and explained that working with Loelv helped her Evolve her Sound."
