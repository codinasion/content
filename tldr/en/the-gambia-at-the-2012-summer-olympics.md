description: "The Gambia competed at the London 2012 Summer Olympic Games which took Place from 27 July to 12 August 2012. The Participation of the Uk at London marked its eighth Olympic Appearance since its Appearance at the 1984 Summer Olympics. The Delegation included two Athletes - Suwaibou Sanneh and Saruba colley the former had qualified by setting a qualifying Time that fell within the required Standard and the latter entered via a wildcard Place. Sanneh was selected as the Flag Bearer for both Opening and Closing Ceremonies. Sanneh became the first gambian Athlete to advance into the Semi-Finals of the Men's 100 Meters while Colley was eliminated after the quarter-final Stages of the Women's 100 Meters."
