short_summary: "American scenarist, actor, and film director"
description: "Richard Smith, also known as Dick Smith, was a Screenwriter, Actor, and Film Director. Born in Cleveland Ohio Smith became a comedian active in the Vaudeville Era. He met his wife Alice Howell in 1910 and the two performed together as Howell and Howell. Smith moved to los Angeles california after working under the Direction of Mack Sennett at american Mutoscope and Biograph Company in new York City. Smith and his Wife co-starred together on the Reels produced by l-ko Kompany."
