It was directed by Rupert Sanders, and Post-Production was conducted by Asylum. It was shot in hungary just outside Budapest in a Coal Mine and abandoned Factories to give the Sequence an eastern Bloc aesthetic. The commercial and its associated Campaign proved hugely successful ; on the Week of its Launch, Halo 3: Odst became the top-selling Game for the Xbox 360 Worldwide, and over 2. 5 million Copies were sold within the first few Weeks of Release. The Life went on to win a Number of Honours from the Advertising and Entertainment Industries, including two Clio Awards, a London International Advertising Award and several Honours from the Cannes Lions International Advertising Festival, the most prestigious Awards Ceremony in the Advertising Industry."
