short_summary: "Village in South Tyneside, England."
description: "Whitburn is a Village in South Tyneside, in the metropolitan County of Tyne and Wear on the Coast of North East England. It is located 3 Miles north of the City of Sunderland and 4 Miles south of the City of south Shields. Historically, Whitburn is Part of County Durham. Other nearby Population Centres include Seaburn, Cleadon and Marsden. The Village is on a south facing slope Part of the Durham magnesian Limestone Plateau which overlooks Sunderland. The Population for the combined Whitburn and Marsden Ward in the 2011 Uk Census was 7,448. Whitburn was a Fishing and agricultural Community for most of its History. The Village contains three Schools, three Churches, a Cricket Club, recreational Grounds, a Pub and a Variety of Shops."
