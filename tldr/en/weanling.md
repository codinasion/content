title: "Weanling"
short_summary: "Young animal just weaned from its mother"
description: "A Weanling is an Animal that has been weaned. The Term is usually used to refer to a Type of young Horse, a Foal that has been weaned, usually between six Months and a Year. Once the Horse is a Year old the Horse is called Yearling."
