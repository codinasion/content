title: "State-of-the-Art Car"
short_summary: "Prototype rapid transit railway vehicle"
description: "The state-of-the-art Car was a heavy Rail Mass Transit Demonstrator Vehicle produced in the 1970s for the united States Department of Transportation's urban Mass Transportation Administration. It was intended to show the latest Technologies to Operators and the riding public and serve to promote existing and proposed Transit Lines. A single married pair was produced by the St. Louis Car Company in 1972. It operated from may 1974 to january 1977 in intermittent Revenue Service on six rapid Transit Systems in five us Cities. Two Cars have been on Display at Seashore Trolley Museum in Kennebunkport Maine since 1989."
