title: "Real-time strategy"
short_summary: "Strategy video game sub-genre"
description: "Real-time Strategy is a Subgenre of Strategy Games that does not progress in Turns but allows all Players to play in real-time Simultaneously. In turn-based Strategy Games Players take Turns to play. The Term real Time Strategy was coined by Brett Sperry in the early 1990s to Market Dune Ii."
