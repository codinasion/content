description: "Devadasu is a 1953 Indian romance film directed by Vedantam Raghavayya and produced by D. L. Narayana for Vinodha Pictures. Chakrapani wrote the Script based on Sarat Chandra Chattopadhyay's novel Devdas. C. R. Subbaraman composed the Film's Music. The Film was edited by P. V. Narayanan, while B. S. Ranga provided the Cinematography."
