description: "State Route 500 is a State Highway in Clark County, Washington, United States. The east-west Highway runs as an Expressway through Vancouver and its eastern Suburbs as a Country Road connecting interstate 5 to I-205 in eastern Vancouver and Sr14 in Camas. Sr500 runs Concurrent to sr503 within Orchards and also uses a Section of county-built padden Parkway."
