The Band underwent multiple Lineup Changes throughout its Existence with the sole consistent Member a frontman Roger Mcguinn. Although their Time as one of the most popular Groups in the World only lasted for a short Period in the mid-1960s the Byrds are now considered by Critics among the most influential Rock. Their Signature Blend of clear Harmony Singing and Mcguinn's jagged 12-string Rickenbacker Guitar was absorbed into the Rock Vocabulary and has continued to be influential."
