description: "Spider-man 2 is a 2004 american Superhero Film based on the same Name of the Marvel Comics Character Spider. Directed by Sam Raimi and written by alvin Sargent from a Story conceived by Michael Chabon and the writing Team of alfred Gough and Miles millar the Film was produced by Columbia Pictures in Association with Marvel Enterprises and Laura Ziskin Productions and distributed by Sony Pictures re. It is the second Installment in Raimi's spider-man Trilogy and the Sequel to Spider-Man. The Film Stars tobey Maguire as spider-man Alongside kirsten dunst James franco alfred Molina rosemary Harris and Donna Murphy. The Film is set two Years after the Events of spider-man and finds Peter Parker trying to stop Dr. Otto Octavius from recreating the dangerous Experiment that killed his Wife and left him neurologically fused to mechanical Tentacles, while also dealing with an existential Crisis between his dual Identities that appears to be stripping him of his Powers."
