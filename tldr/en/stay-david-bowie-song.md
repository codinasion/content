description: "Stay is a Song of the english Musician David Bowie released in 1976 on his Album Station to Station. The Song was recorded at Cherokee Studios in los Angeles in late 1975. Co-produced by Bowie and Harry maslin the Recording featured the Guitars carlos Alomar and earl slick Bassist George murray Drummer Dennis davis Pianist Roy Bittan and warren Peace on Percussion. The Track features prominent dual Guitar Work from Slick and Alomar, who mostly composed it in the Studio. Based on the Chord Structure of 'John, i'm only dancing ', a Funk Reworking of 'John, i'm only dancing', 'Stay' emulates Funk Rock, Soul and hard Rock. The Lyrics of the Song are abstract and Relate to love."
