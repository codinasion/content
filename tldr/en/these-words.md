description: "These Words is a Song by the british Singer-Songwriter Natasha bedingfield. It was written by Steve Kipner, Andrew Frampton, Wayne Wilkins and Bedingfield for her 2004 Debut Album, Unwritten. The Song is the Album's Opening Track, and was released as its second single. These Words Details bedingfield's Lack of Inspiration and her Reaction to Pressure from her Record Label to produce a Hit Song."
