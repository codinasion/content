description: "Tekken Tag Tournament 2 is the eighth Installment in the Tekken fighting Game Series and the Sequel to tekken tag Tournament. It was released in September 2011 for the Arcades. In March 2012 it received a Update subtitled unlimited. An xbox 360 Version based on the Update was released for Playstation 3 and xbox 360 in September 2012. It was ported to the Wii U in november 2012 as one of the System's launch Titles under the subtitle Wii u Edition."
