description: "Mega Man known in Japan as Rockman is a platform Game developed and published by Capcom in 1987 for the nintendo Entertainment System. It was directed by Akira Kitamura, with Nobuyuki Matsushima as lead Programmer, and is the first Game of the Mega Man Franchise and the original Video Game Series. Mega Man was produced by a small Team specifically for the Home Console Market, a first for Capcom, which previously focused on arcade Video Games."
