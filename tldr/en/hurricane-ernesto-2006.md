short_summary: "Category 1 Atlantic hurricane in 2006"
description: "Hurricane Ernesto was the costliest tropical cyclone of the 2006 Atlantic hurricane season. The sixth tropical Storm and the first Hurricane of the Season Ernesto developed from a tropical Wave on august 24 in the eastern Caribbean Sea. Ernesto first affected the northern caribbean reaching minimal Hurricane Status near Haiti before weakening and moving as a tropical Storm across eastern Cuba. Despite initial Predictions for Ernesto to track through the eastern Gulf of Mexico as a major Hurricane Ernesto moved across eastern Florida as a weak tropical Storm. After turning towards the Northeast it re-intensified and made Landfall on august 31 in the north Carolina Coast just below the Status of a Hurricane. Late the next Day ernesto became extratropical after entering southern Virginia. The Remnants spread Moisture across the northeastern united States before dissipating over eastern Canada on 4."
