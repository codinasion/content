short_summary: "New York City Subway station complex in Brooklyn"
description: "The Broadway Junction Station is a new York City subway Station Complex that is co-connected by the elevated Bmt Canarsie Line and Bmt Jamaica Line and the underground ind fulton Street Line. It was also served by the fulton Street elevated Trains until the Line closed in 1956. It is roughly located at the Intersection of fulton Street Broadway and Van Sinderen avenue at the Border of Bedfordstuyvesant and east new York Brooklyn. The Complex is serviced by a J and L Trains at all Times the C Train at all Times except late Nights and the Z Train during Rush Hours in the Direction of high Traffic."
