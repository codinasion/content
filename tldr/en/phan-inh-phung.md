title: "Phan Đình Phùng"
short_summary: "Vietnamese revolutionary (1847–1896)"
description: "Phan nh phng was a vietnamese Revolutionary who led Rebel Armies in Vietnam against french Colonial Forces. He was the most prominent of the confucian Court Scholars involved in anti-french military Campaigns in the 19th Century and was cited after his Death by 20th-century Nationalists as a national Hero. He was renowned for his Uncompromising will and Principles even when the french had desecrated his ancestral Tombs and arrested and threatened to kill his Family on one Occasion."
