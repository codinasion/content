short_summary: "1823 historical fiction novel by John Neal"
description: "Seventy-Six is a historical Fiction Novel by american Writer John Neal. It was published in Baltimore in 1823 as the fourth Novel about the american revolutionary War. Historically distinguished for its Pioneering Use of colloquial Language Yankee dialect Battle Scenes realism high Characterization Stream of Consciousness narrative Profanity and Depictions of Sex and romance the Novel influenced later american Writers. The narrative Prose Resembles spoken american English more than any other Literature of its Period. It was the first american Fiction to use the Phrase Son-Of-A-Bitch."
