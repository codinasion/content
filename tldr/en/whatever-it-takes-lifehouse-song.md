short_summary: "2007 single by Lifehouse"
description: "Whatever it takes is a Song by american alternative Band Lifehouse. It is the second single released from their fourth Studio Album, who we are. The Song was written by Lifehouse's lead Singer Jason Wade and american Record Producer Jude Cole. When discussing the Song wade felt that the Song's Message Dealt with how difficult it can be to be in a Relationship. It was first released with the Band's fourth Studio Album who we are on June 18, 2007 and was then solicited to mainstream Radio on November 13, 2007. It is a moderately slow Pop-Rock Ballad that has Influences of Adult Alternative."
