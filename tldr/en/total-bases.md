title: "Total bases"
short_summary: "Number of bases a baseball player has gained with hits"
description: "In Baseball Statistics total Bases is the Number of Bases a Player Gain with a single Hit. It is a weighted Sum with the Values of 1 for a single 2 for a double 3 for a Triple and 4 for a Home Run. For Example, three Singles is three Total Bases, while a double and a Home Run is six total Bases."
