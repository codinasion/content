short_summary: "State highway in Adams County, Pennsylvania, US"
description: "Pennsylvania Route 97 is one of two Pennsylvania State Highways that carries the Pa 97 Designation ; the other Pa 97 is in Erie County. This southern Pa 97 known as the Baltimore Pike for most of its Length runs 9 Miles. 363 miles from the Maryland state line near Littlestown, where the highway continues as Maryland Route 97, northwest to U. S. Route 15 near Gettysburg. Pa 97 Connects gettysburg and littlestown in southeastern Adams County. The Highway also connects these Communities with Westminster and Baltimore. From Pa 97's northern End, Baltimore Pike continues toward Gettysburg as State Route 2035 through the Gettysburg Battlefield Historic District, where it provides Access to the Gettysburg Museum and Visitor Center."
