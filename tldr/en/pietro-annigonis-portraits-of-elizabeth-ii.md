title: "Pietro Annigoni's portraits of Elizabeth II"
short_summary: "Three portraits of Queen Elizabeth II, painted between 1954 and 1972"
description: "Between 1954 and 1972 Pietro Annigoni completed a Number of Portraits of queen Elizabeth ii. In 1955 he painted her for the worshipful Company of Fishmongers and in 1969 for the national Portrait Gallery. The two Portraits were united for the National Portrait Gallery's Exhibition ; the Queen: Art and Image, held to mark the Diamond Jubilee of Queen Elizabeth Ii in 2012. In 1972 Annigoni completed a circular Drawing of Queen and Prince Philip Duke of Edinburgh to mark their Silver Wedding Anniversary."
