short_summary: "1983 studio album by Duran Duran"
description: "Seven and the Ragged Tiger is the third Studio Album by English Rock Band Duran Duran, released on 21 November 1983 through Emi and Capitol Records. Between April and october 1983 the Recording Sessions took Place in France the caribbean and australia following Duran Duran's Decision to record outside the uk as Tax Exiles. Unlike their previous two Studio albums the Sessions were marred by a Lack of Productivity and Tensions rose between the Band Members over the Direction of the Session."
