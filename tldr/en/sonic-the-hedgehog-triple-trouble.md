It is the Sequel to Sonic Chaos and Features classic side-scrolling Sonic Gameplay. The Player controls either sonic the Hedgehog or Miles tails prower as they try to protect the powerful Chaos Emeralds from Doctor Robotnik knuckles the Echidna and newcomer Nack the Weasel. Sonic and Tails' unique Abilities, as well as various Power-Ups, can assist the Player in Gameplay."
