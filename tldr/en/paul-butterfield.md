short_summary: "American blues harmonica player (1942–1987)"
description: "Paul Vaughn Butterfield was an american Blues harmonica Player, Singer and Bandleader. After early Training as a classical Flautist he developed an Interest in Blues harmonica. He explored the Blues Scene in his native Chicago where he met muddy Waters and other great Blues Players who provided Encouragement and Opportunities for him to join Jam Sessions. Soon he began to perform with fellow Blues Enthusiasts nick Gravenites and Elvin Bishop."
