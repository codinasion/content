title: "Greek battleship Salamis"
short_summary: "Cancelled dreadnought battleship of the Greek Navy"
description: "Salamis was a partially built Capital Ship referred to either as a dreadnought Battleship or Battlecruiser which was ordered by the ag vulcan Shipyard in Hamburg Germany in 1912 for the Greek. She was ordered as Part of a greek naval Rearmament Program Meant to modernize the Fleet following the ottoman naval Expansion following the greco-turkish War of 1897. Salamis and several other Battleships None of which were delivered to either navy represented the Culmination of a naval Arms Race between the two Countries that had significant Effects on the first balkan War and World War I."
