description: "William C. Clayton was an american Educator, Lawyer, Politician, and Businessperson in the U. S. State of West Virginia. Clayton served from 1875 until 1879 in the West Virginia Senate representing the eleventh senate District of west Virginia. He was twice principal of the Romney Classical Institute in Romney in 1853 and 1866."
