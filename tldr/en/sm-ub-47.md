title: "SM UB-47"
short_summary: "German Imperial Navy's Type UB II submarine"
description: "Sm Ub-47 was a Type ub Ii Sub or Submarine for the german imperial Navy during World War I. The Ub-47 was sold during the War to the austro-hungarian Navy. In the austro-hungarian Service the B was dropped from her Name and she was known as sm u-47 or u-xlvii as a Member of the austro-hungarian u-43 Class."
