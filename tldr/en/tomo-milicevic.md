description: "Tomislav 'Tomo' Miličević is a Bosnian-American musician and record producer. From 2003 to 2018 he was the lead Guitarist of the Rock Band Thirty Seconds to Mars. Born in Sarajevo but raised in the United States, Miličević moved to Troy, Michigan, in the early 1980s, where he became active in the local heavy Metal Scene and played in a Number of Bands, co-founding Morphic. In 2003, he joined Thirty Seconds to Mars, with whom he achieved worldwide recognition in the mid-2000s after recording the band's second album A Beautiful Lie . Its full-length Sequels this is War and love Lust Faith and Dreams received further critical and commercial Success."
