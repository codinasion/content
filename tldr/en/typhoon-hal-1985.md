title: "Typhoon Hal (1985)"
short_summary: "Pacific typhoon in 1985"
description: "Typhoon Hal known as Typhoon Kuring in the Philippines was the strongest Cyclone to affect southern China since 1983. Hal was formed by a Monsoon Trough formed in early June 1985. The System gradually became better organized and the Storm reached tropical Storm Status on June20. The Intensity continued and the Storm reached Typhoon Intensity later that Day. Hal reached peak Intensity on the Evening of June 21 before passing South of Taiwan. The Storm weakened slightly on June 22 and lost Intensity on Days two Days later. Later on June 24, Hal moved onshore Northeast of Hong Kong. Hal vanished three Days later."
