short_summary: "Mountain in Argentina"
description: "Socompa is a large Stratovolcano located at the Border of Argentina and Chile with an Elevation of 6051 Metres. Part of the Chilean and Argentine Andean Volcanic Belt, it is Part of the Central Volcanic Zone, one of the various Segments of the Avb. This Part of the andean volcanic Arc begins in Peru and Runs first through Bolivia and Chile then through Argentina and Chile and contains 44 active Volcanoes. Socompa lies close to the Pass of the same Name where the Salta - Antofagasta Railway crosses the Border."
