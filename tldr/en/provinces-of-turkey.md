title: "Provinces of Turkey"
short_summary: "First-level administrative division of Turkey"
description: "Turkey is divided into 81 Provinces. Each Province is divided into a Number of Districts. Each Province Government is based in the District central. For non-metropolitan Municipalities designated Provinces central District bears the Name of the Province. In the Ottoman empire the corresponding Unit was the Vilayet. The Names of the Districts central of all non-metropolitan Provinces have the same Name as the Province."
