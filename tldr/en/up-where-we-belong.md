title: "Up Where We Belong"
short_summary: "1982 song by Joe Cocker and Jennifer Warnes"
description: "Up where we belong is a Song written by Jack Nitzsche buffy Sainte-Marie and will Jennings recorded for the Film an Officer and a Gentleman in 1982 by Joe Cocker and Jennifer Warnes. Warnes was recommended to sing a song from the film because of her previous soundtrack successes, and she had the idea for the song to be a duet that she would perform with Cocker. Jennings selected various Sections of the Score by Nitzsche and Sainte-Marie to create the Structure of the Song and added Lyrics about the Struggles of Life and Love and the Obstacles that People attempt to dodge. It was released in July of that Year to coincide with the Release of the Film."
