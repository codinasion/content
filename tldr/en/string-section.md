title: "String section"
short_summary: "Section of a symphony orchestra composed of string instruments"
description: "The string section is composed of bowed instruments belonging to the violin family. It typically consists of first and second Violins violas Cellos and double Basses. It is the most numerous group in the standard orchestra. In Discussions of the Instrumentation of a musical Work the Phrase the Strings or and Strings is used to indicate a String Section as defined above. An Orchestra composed of one principal Section is called String Orchestra. Smaller String Sections are sometimes used in Jazz Pop and Rock Music and in musical Theater Pit Orchestras."
