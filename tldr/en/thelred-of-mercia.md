title: "Æthelred of Mercia"
short_summary: "7th and 8th-century King of Mercia"
description: "Thelred was king of Mercia from 675 to 704. He was the Son of Penda of Mercia and came to the Throne in 675 when his Brother Wulfhere of Mercia died of an Illness. Within a Year of his Accession he invaded Kent where his Forces destroyed the City of Rochester. He defeated his Brother in Law Ecgfrith of Northumbria at the Battle of Trent in 679 the Battle was a major Setback for the Northumbrians and effectively ended their military Involvement in english Affairs South of the Humber. It also permanently returned the kingdom of Lindsey to Mercia's possession. Thelred however was unable to re-establish the Domination of his Predecessors in southern Britain."
