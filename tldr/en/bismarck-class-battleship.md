short_summary: "Pair of fast battleships"
description: "The Bismarck Class was a Pair of fast Battleships built for the Kriegsmarine before World War Ii. The Ships were the largest and most powerful Warships built for the Kriegsmarine and typically delivered more than 41000 metric Tons they were armed with a Battery of eight 38cm Guns and could reach a top Speed of 30 Knots. Bismarck was laid down in July 1936 and completed in September 1940, while her Sister Tirpitz's Keel was laid in October 1936 and work finished in February 1941. The Ships were ordered as a Response to the french Battleships of the Class Richelieu. They were designed with the traditional Role of engaging enemy Battleships in Home Waters in Mind though the german naval Command envisioned the Ships as long-range Commerce Raiders against british Ships in the atlantic Ocean. Their Design represented strategic Confusion which dominated german naval Construction in the 1930s."
