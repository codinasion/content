description: "One Day at Horror Land was originally published in february 1994 and is the sixteenth Children's Horror Novel in R a. L. Stine's Goosebumps Series. It was adapted into a two-part Episode for the Television Series, which was later released on Vhs and Dvd. A comic Adaptation of the Book was included in the graphic novel Compilation Terror Trips Part of the Goosebumps Graphix Series. There were two Video Games, an Audiobook, and an adult-aimed Interactive Show based on the Book. A Sequel in the 2000 Goosebumps spin-off Series titled Return to horrorland was released in 1999. The Horrorland Theme Park was expanded upon in the Book Series Goosebumps Horrorland. The two-part Episode was released on Vhs and Dvd. The Book and Episodes received positive Reception."
