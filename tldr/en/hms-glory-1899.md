short_summary: "Pre-dreadnought battleship of the British Royal Navy"
description: "Hms Glory was a british Royal navy pre-dreadnought Battleship and a Member of the Canopus Class. Intended for Service in asia Glory and her sister Ships were smaller and faster than the previous majestic-class Battleships but retained the same Battery of four 12-inch Guns. She also carried thinner Armor but included a new Krupp Steel which was more effective than the harvey Armor used in the Majestics. Glory was laid down in December 1896 launched in March 1899 and commissioned into the Fleet in november 1900."
