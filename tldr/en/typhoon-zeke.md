short_summary: "Pacific typhoon in 1991"
description: "In the Philippines Typhoon zeke known as Typhoon Etang was the first of two Typhoons to landfall in China in a Week during the Middle of July 1991. A disturbance-hit Area developed East of the Philippines towards the End of the first Week of July. Tracking West-Northwestward the Disturbance became a tropical Depression on 9 July. After tracking across the Philippines, where it left two People missing and injured three Others, the Depression intensified into a tropical Storm on July 10. The Storm steadily deepened as it passed across the south China Sea and became a Typhoon on July 12. During its peak Intensity of 120 Kmh zeke moved onshore at Hainan where it began to weaken. The System tracked across Vietnam from July 13 and dissipated within two Days of moving into the Inland Waters."
