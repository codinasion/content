title: "Testaroli"
short_summary: "Type of pasta or bread in Italian cuisine"
description: "Testaroli also referred to as Testarolo is a Kind of thin Spongy Pasta or Bread in italian Cuisine prepared using Water Flour and Salt which is then sliced into Diamond or rectangular Shapes. A common Dish in the Lunigiana Region and historical Territory of Italy, it is an ancient Pasta originating from the Etruscan Civilization of Italy. Testaroli has been described as the earliest recorded Pasta. It is also a native Dish of the southern Regions of the Liguria and the northern Tuscany of Italy."
