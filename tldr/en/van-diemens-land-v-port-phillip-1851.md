title: "Van Diemen's Land v Port Phillip, 1851"
short_summary: "1851 cricket match in Australia"
description: "On 11 and 12 february 1851 Teams from van Diemen's Land and port Phillip District played the first Cricket Match between two australian Colonies recognised in later Years as the inaugural First-Class Match in Australia. It took Place at launceston Racecourse now known as the Ntca Ground in Tasmania. The Match was incorporated into Celebrations marking the Separation of the Port Phillip District from new south Wales as a Colony of Victoria in 1851."
