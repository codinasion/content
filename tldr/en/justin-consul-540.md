short_summary: "Byzantine aristocrat and general"
description: "Flavius Mar. Theodorus Valentinus Rusticius Boraides germanus Iustinus simply and commonly known as Justin was an eastern Roman Aristocrat and general. He was a Member of the justinian Dynasty and Nephew of Emperor Justinian I he was appointed one of the last Roman consuls in 540 before going on to assume senior military Commands in the Balkans and in. He fought against slavs the Sassanid Persians and supervised the first Contacts of the Byzantine Empire with the Avars. At the Time of Justinians Death he was seen as a probable Successor but was beaten to the Throne by his Cousin Justin Ii who exiled him to egypt where he was murdered."
