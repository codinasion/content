title: "Ted Alford"
short_summary: "American gridiron football player (born 1971)"
description: "Ted Alford is a former Professional Football Receiver and Gridiron Receiver. After playing College Football at Langston University, he played for seven Teams in the Canadian Football League, including the Edmonton Eskimos, Bc Lions, Winnipeg Blue Bombers, Hamilton Tiger-Cats, Montreal Alouettes, Toronto Argonauts, and Calgary Stampeders. Alford became a consistent Starter in his Rookie Season with the Bc Lions and was nominated for the most outstanding Rookie Award. He spent the next several Years moving from Team to team Playing in only 8 Cfl Matches between 1997 and 2000."
