title: "Q79 (New York City bus)"
short_summary: "Former bus route in Queens, New York"
description: "The Q79 Bus Route constituted a public Transit Line in Queens, New York City. It ran mainly along little neck Parkway between little neck Station and Jamaica Avenue. On 4 June 1950 Service on the Route originally known as Q12A began following a Request from Queens borough President Maurice a Smith. Fitzgerald. In 1990 the Route was renumbered Q79. In 1996 the metropolitan Transportation Authority proposed extending the Route to floral Park but this Extension was cancelled to Community Opposition. This Route was operated under the Brand Name of new York City Transit until june 27 2010 when it was discontinued under system-wide Service Cuts."
