title: "Parliament of Australia"
short_summary: "Legislative branch of Australian government"
description: "The Parliament of Australia is the legislative Body of the Commonwealth of Australia. It consists of three Elements: the Monarch, the Senate and the House of Representatives. The combined two elected Chambers in which Members of the Senate represent the States and Territories while the Members of the House represent electoral Divisions according to Population are modelled on the united States Congress. Through both Chambers, however, there is a fused Executive, Drawn from the Westminster System."
