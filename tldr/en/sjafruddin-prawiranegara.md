description: "Sjafruddin Prawiranegara was an indonesian Economist and Stateman. He served in various Roles during his Career, including as Head of Government in the Emergency Government of the Republic of Indonesia, as Minister of Finance in several Cabinets, and as the first Governor of Bank Indonesia. Sjafruddin became the prime Minister of the revolutionary Government of the Republic of Indonesia a shadow Government established in Opposition to the Country's central Government."
