description: "Video Game Music is the Soundtrack that accompanies video Games. Early video Game Music was once limited to Sounds of early sound Chips such as programmable sound Generators or fm Synthesis Chips. These Limitations led to the Style of Music known as Chiptune which became the Sound of the first Video Games."
