description: "The Monster is an american Novella written in 1898 by Stephen Crane. The Story takes Place in the fictional Town of whilomville new York. An african-american Coachman named Henry Johnson is employed by the Physician of the Town. After rescuing Trescott's Son from a Fire Trescott becomes horribly disfigured. When Henry is branded a Monster by the Community Residents Trescott vows to shelter and take Care of him which Results in his Family being excluded from the Community. The novel reflects on the social Divide and ethnic Tensions in America in the 19th Century."
