title: "The Boat Race 1982"
short_summary: "128th boat race between Oxford and Cambridge universities in England"
description: "The 128th Boat Race took Place on March 27 1982. The annual Boat Race is a side-by-side Rowing Race on the Thames between Crews from the Universities of Oxford and Cambridge. Oxford won 314 Lengths in a seventh successive Victory. Their Number five boris Rankov won a Record Fifth Boat Race as a Rower and the Clay Brothers from Oxford were the first twins to win the Event."
