title: "United Nations Security Council Resolution 82"
short_summary: "1950 resolution on the Korean War"
description: "Resolution 82 was adopted on June 25 1950 by the united Nations Security Council. It condemned the armed Attack by north korean Forces on the Republic of Korea while calling for the immediate Cessation of Hostilities and for the Authorities in north Korea to withdraw their armed Forces to the 38th Parallel. The Measure was adopted with 9 Voting for, None opposed, and one Abstention by the Soviet Union, who was boycotting the Un at the Time for its Recognition of the Republic of China as China's Representative to the Organization."
