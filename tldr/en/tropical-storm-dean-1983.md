title: "Tropical Storm Dean (1983)"
short_summary: "Atlantic tropical storm in 1983"
description: "Tropical Storm Dean caused minor Flooding along Parts of the east Coast of the united States in September1983. The seventh and final tropical Cyclone of the very inactive atlantic Hurricane Season 1983 Dean developed from a frontal Low on September 26 to Northeast of the Bahamas. Initially subtropical it gained Characteristics of a tropical Cyclone while slowly turning north-northeast. The System was upgraded to tropical Storm Dean by september 27. Dean peaked on September 28 while tracking north with Winds of 65mph shortly before curving West-Northwestward and slowly leveling off in Intensity. On september 29 Dean eventually made Landfall on Virginia on the Delmarva Peninsula as a Weakening tropical Storm. Dean weakened rapidly over Land and by early October1 was no longer classifiable as a tropical Cyclone."
