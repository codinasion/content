description: "Lisa the Iconoclast is the sixteenth Episode of the seventh Season of the Simpsons american Animation Tv Series. It was originally broadcast on february 18 1996 on the Fox Network in the united States. In this Episode Lisa writes an Essay for the Town's bicentennial about springfield Founder Jebediah Springfield. While doing Research she learns that he was a murderous Pirate who viewed the Citizens of the Town with Contempt. Lisa and Homer Try to reveal the Truth about Jebediah but they only anger springfield Residents. It was originally advertised in Commercials as a Presidents' Day special Episode ; the Episode aired the Day before Presidents' Day."
