short_summary: "Origami toy used for various children's games"
description: "A fortune Teller is a Form of Origami used in Children's Games. Parts of the Fortune Teller are labelled with Colors or Numbers that serve as Options for a Player to choose from, and on the Inside are eight Flaps, each concealing a Message. The Person operating the Fortune Teller manipulates the Device based on the Choices made by the Player and finally one of the hidden Messages is revealed. These Messages may be meant to answer Questions or they may be Activities the Player must perform."
