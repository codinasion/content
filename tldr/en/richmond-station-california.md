short_summary: "Railway station in Richmond, California, US"
description: "Richmond Station is an Amtrak Intercity Rail and Bay Area Rapid Transit Station located in downtown Richmond, California. Richmond is the north Terminus of bart Service on the Orange Line and red Line and a Stop for Amtrak's Capitol Corridor San Joaquins and California Zephyr Routes. The accessible Station has one Island Platform for the two Bart Tracks with a second Island Platform serving two of three Tracks of the martinez Subdivision of the Union Pacific Railroad for Amtrak Trains. It is one of two Transfer Points between Bart and Amtrak, along with Oakland Coliseum Station."
