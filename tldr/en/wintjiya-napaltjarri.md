description: "Wintjiya Napaltjarri, and also known as Wintjia Napaltjarri No. 1 is a pintupi-speaking aboriginal Artist from the western Desert of Australia. She is the Sister of Artist Tjunkiya Napaltjarri ; both were Wives of Toba Tjakamarra, with whom Wintjiya had five Children."
