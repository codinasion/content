short_summary: "British B-class destroyer"
description: "Hms Brilliant was a Destroyer of B-Class built for the Royal Navy around 1930. She was initially assigned to the mediterranean Fleet and transferred in 1936 to the Home Fleet. The Ship then patrolled spanish Waters enforcing the Arms Blockade during the first Year of the spanish civil War 193639. She spent the Majority of World War Ii on Convoy Escort Duties in the english Channel and the north atlantic from Dover Gibraltar and freetown Sierra Leone. During the War Brilliant never destroyed a Submarine but did sink during Operation torch a vichy french Minesweeper. She also intercepted two german Supply Ships and rescued Survivors of two Troopships torpedoed by Submarines. At the End of the War the Ship became a Target Ship and was scrapped in 1948."
