title: "Tropical Storm Gabrielle (1995)"
short_summary: "Atlantic tropical storm in 1995"
description: "Tropical Storm Gabrielle caused moderate Flooding in august 1995 in northeastern Mexico and southern Texas. The eighth tropical Cyclone and Seventh named Storm of the atlantic Hurricane Season 1995 Gabrielle developed on august 9 from a tropical Wave in the west-central Gulf of Mexico. Initially a tropical Depression the System gradually intensified and by the following Day it became a tropical Storm. Favorable Conditions caused Gabrielle to continue to strengthen with the Storm nearly reaching Hurricane Status late on August 11. However it soon made Landfall near La Pesca Tamaulipas thus stopping further Intensification. Once inland Gabrielle quickly weakened and dissipated by early August 12."
