description: "New Zealand is an Island State in the southwestern Pacific Ocean. It consists of two main landmasses—the North Island and the South Island —And over 700 smaller Islands. It is the sixth largest Island Country by Area and lies East of Australia across the Tasman Sea and South of the Islands of new Caledonia Fiji and Tonga. The Country's varied Topography and sharp Mountain Peaks including southern alps owe much to tectonic Uplift and volcanic Eruptions. The Capital City of new Zealand is wellington and the most populous City is auckland."
