description: "Tropical Storm Odette was a rare tropical Cyclone in Off-Season that hit the Island of Hispaniola in early December 2003. As the Fifteenth named Storm of the atlantic Hurricane Season 2003 Odette formed near the Coast of Panama a few Days after the official End of the atlantic Hurricane Season ended on november 30 and ultimately made Landfall on the dominican Republic as a moderate tropical Storm before becoming extratropical on December 7 Diss."
