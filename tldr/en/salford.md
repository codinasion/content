description: "Salford is a City in Greater Manchester, England. The City is situated on a Meander on the western Bank of the Irwell River which forms its boundary with Manchester and its Centre. Landmarks in the City include the 100 Greengate Skyscraper, the old Town Hall, cathedral and St Philips Church. It is the main Settlement of the Wider metropolitan City of Salford."
