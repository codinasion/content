title: "Trento-class cruiser"
short_summary: "Heavy cruiser class of the Italian Royal Navy"
description: "The Trento Class was a Group of two heavy Cruisers built in the late 1920S for the italian Regia marina the first such Vessel built for the italian Fleet. The two Ships of the Class Trento and Trieste were named after the redeemed Cities of Trento and Trieste annexed by the austro-hungarian Empire after the Victory in World War I. The Ships were very lightly armored with only a 70mm Thick armored belt though they had a high Speed and heavy main Battery of eight 203mm Guns. Both were nominally built under the Restrictions of the Washington Naval Treaty however the two Cruisers exceeded the Displacement Limits imposed by the Treaty."
