title: "Hurricane Faith"
short_summary: "Category 3 Atlantic hurricane in 1966"
description: "Faith was a long-lived Cape Verde Hurricane and was the sixth named Storm and fifth Hurricane of the atlantic Hurricane Season 1966. Faith developed on august 21 from an Area of disturbed Weather between Cape Verde and the west Coast of Africa. Tracking westward the Depression slowly intensified and became tropical Storm faith the following Day. Moving west across the atlantic Ocean it continued to slowly Strengthen reaching Hurricane Status early on August 23rd. Over the next few Days the Storm trembled North of the lesser Antilles weakening into a tropical Storm on August 26. Located near the Lesser Antilles, the Outer Bands of Faith produced gale-force Winds in the Region, especially Puerto Rico, the Virgin Islands, and Antigua. Minor coastal Damage took Place in south Trinidad and Tobago."
