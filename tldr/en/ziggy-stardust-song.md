description: "Ziggy Stardust is a Song by english Singer-Songwriter David Bowie for his 1972 album the Rise and Fall of Ziggy Stardust and the Spiders from Mars. He recorded it in november 1971 with his backing band the Spiders marscomptor mick Ronson Trevor Bolder and Mick Woodmansey at trident Studios in London. Lyrically the Song is about Ziggy stardust a bisexual Alien Rock Star who acts as a Messenger for extraterrestrial Beings. The Character was influenced by English Singer Vince Taylor, as well as the Legendary Stardust Cowboy and Kansai Yamamoto. Although Ziggy is introduced earlier on the Album, this Song is its Centrepiece, presenting the Rise and Fall of the Star in a very human-like Manner. Musically it is a Glam Rock Song like its Parent Album and is based around a Ronson Guitar Riff."
