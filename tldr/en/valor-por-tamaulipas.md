description: "Valor Por Tamaulipas is a Facebook Page that covers Security Updates in the State of Tamaulipas in Mexico. It was founded on 1 january 2012 by an anonymous User and its Goal is to Share with other social Media Users Information on drug-related Violence and Risk Situations all across the State. With over 515,000 Likes on Facebook, Valor Por Tamaulipas routinely posts Messages and Photos of Crime Scenes on its Page. The Page survives under Anonymity but has not been immune to Threats from a Country where many Journalists have been assassinated for writing about Drug Trafficking and organized Crime."
