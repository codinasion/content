short_summary: "1711 failed British attempt to attack Quebec during Queen Anne's War"
description: "The Quebec expedition, or the Walker expedition to Quebec, was a British attempt to attack Quebec in 1711 in Queen Anne's War, the North American theatre of the War of Spanish Succession. It failed when seven Transports and one Storeship were wrecked and 850 Soldiers drowned in one of the worst naval Disasters in british History."
