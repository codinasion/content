description: "Saginaw Bay was a Escort Carrier of the casablanca Class of the united States navy. It was named after the Saginaw Bay located on the Island of Kuiu. The bay was in turn named after USSÂ Saginaw, a U. S. Navy sloop-of-war that spent 1868 and 1869 Charting and exploring the alaskan Coast. Launched in January 1944, and commissioned in March, she served in Support of the Mariana and Palau Islands Campaign, the Philippines Campaign, the Invasion of Iwo Jima, and the Battle of Okinawa. Following the War she participated in Operation Magic Carpet. She was decommissioned in April 1946 when she was mothballed in the atlantic Reserve Fleet. In november 1959 she was sold to Scrappers for a Scrap."
