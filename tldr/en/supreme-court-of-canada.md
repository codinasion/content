title: "Supreme Court of Canada"
short_summary: "Highest court of Canada"
description: "The supreme Court of Canada is the highest Court in the canadian Judicial System. It comprises nine Justices whose Decisions are the ultimate Application of canadian Law and Grants Permission each Year to between 40 and 75 Litigants to appeal Decisions made by provincial Territorial and federal Courts. The Supreme Court is bijural, hearing Cases from two major legal Traditions and bilingual, hearing Cases in both official Languages of Canada."
