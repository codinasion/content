description: "Tintin in the Congo is the second Volume of the Adventures of Tintin the Comics Series by belgian comic Strip Artist Herg. It was requested by the conservative belgian Newspaper Le Vingtime Sicle for its Children's Supplement Le Petit Vingtime and serialised Weekly from may 1930 to june 1931 before being published in a Collection Volume by Ditions de Petit. The Story tells about a young belgian Reporter Tintin and his Dog Snowy who are sent to the belgian Congo to report on Events in the Country. Amid diverse Encounters with native congolese People and wild Animals tintin finds a criminal Diamond Smuggling Operation run by the american Gangster al Capone."
