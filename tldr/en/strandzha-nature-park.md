description: "Strandzha Nature Park is the largest protected Area in Bulgaria with a total Area of 1161 square Kilometers situated in the Strandzha Mountain in the extreme south-eastern Corner of the Country close to the Border. It was established on 25 january 1995 to protect Ecosystems and Biodiversity of european Importance as well as the traditional cultural historical and folklore Heritage of the Region.
