title: "Spider Clark"
short_summary: "American baseball player (1867–1892)"
description: "Owen F. 'Spider' Clark was a professional baseball player. He played two Seasons in major League Baseball 1889 for the Washington Nationals of the national League and 1890 for the buffalo Bisons of the Players' League. While he was primarily an Outfielder he played every Position at least once including a Game as a Pitcher for the Bisons. He was the first MLB player nicknamed 'Spider."
