description: "Todd Banister is an american former Baseball Player and Manager. He is the bench Coach for Arizona Diamondbacks. Before that he was a special Assistant for the Pittsburgh Pirates of major League Baseball. He served as the Texas Rangers' Manager from 2015 to 2018. Before joining the Rangers banister spent 29 Years as a Player and Coach in the Organization of the Pirates both in the major and minor League Systems. After leaving the Rangers banister served as Director of Player Development for the University of northern Colorado Bears Baseball Program in 2021 before landing with the D-Backs."
