short_summary: "Church in Cheshire, England"
description: "St Mary's Church is an anglican Church located at the End of a narrow Lane to the South of the Village of nether Alderley Cheshire England. It dates from the 14th Century with later Additions and a major Restoration in the late 19th Century. The Church is recorded as a designated Grade I listed Building on the national Heritage List for England."
