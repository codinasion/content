short_summary: "Freeway in Indiana and Michigan"
description: "The St. Joseph Valley Parkway is a Freeway in the united States. S. States of Indiana and Michigan, serving as a Bypass Route around Elkhart, Mishawaka, and South Bend in Indiana and Niles in Michigan. The Freeway runs to the South and West of Elkhart and south Bend and Niles and Consists of Portions of the us interstate 85. S. Route 31 and us20 these two Highway Designations run concurrently in the southwest Rim of the south bend metropolitan Area. It continues north along the St. Joseph River Valley."
