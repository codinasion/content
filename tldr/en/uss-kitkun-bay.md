title: "USS Kitkun Bay"
short_summary: "Casablanca-class escort carrier of the US Navy"
description: "Uss Kitkun Bay was the 17th of fifty Escort Carriers of the casablanca Class designed for the us navy during World War Ii. She was launched in November 1943 and transferred to the Navy and was commissioned in December. She served in the Mariana and Palau Islands Campaign, the Battle off Samar, in which she was the first Ship to undergo kamikaze Attack, and the Invasion of Lingayen Gulf, during which she was damaged by another Kamikaze and forced to withdraw. Post-war, she participated in Operation Magic Carpet, repatriating U. S. Servicemen from around the Pacific. She was decommissioned in April 1946 and was sold for scrapping in November. In early 1947 she was eventually separated."
