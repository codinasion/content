short_summary: "Russian Delta-IV-class submarine"
description: "The k-114 Tula is a nuclear-powered nuclear-powered ballistic Missile Submarine of the delfin Class from Project 667bdrm. As such, she carries a Complement of R-29Rm Shtil and R-29Rmu Sineva nuclear submarine-launched ballistic Missiles as her primary deterrent Mission, along with anti-ship Missiles and Torpedoes, the Latter for Self-Defense. She was built in the late 1980S in Severodvinsk and served with the soviet Navy before being transferred to the russian Navy following the Dissolution of the soviet Union. Tula underwent an extensive Overhaul during 2000–2004 and was fitted with upgraded Shtil Slbms, several of which were launched from her during her later operational Life. She was sponsored by the City of Tula, and is homeported in Gadzhiyevo."
