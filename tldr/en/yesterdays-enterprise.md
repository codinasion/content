It is the 15th Episode of the third Season originally broadcasting in Syndication in the Week of february 19 1990. Set in the 24th Century the Series follows the Adventures of the Starfleet Crew of the enterprise-d Star Fleet. In this Episode the Ship's Crew must decide whether to send the time-travelling Enterprise-C through a temporal Rift to its certain Destruction to prevent damaging Changes to their Timeline."
