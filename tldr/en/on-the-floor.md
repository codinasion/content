description: "On the Floor is a Song by american Singer Jennifer Lopez for her seventh Studio Album Love? . Featuring american Rapper Pitbull it was released by Island Records on 8 february 2011 as the Lead single from the Album. 'on the Floor' was written by Kinnda 'Kee' Hamid, Aj Junior, Teddy Sky, Bilal 'the Chef' Hajji, Pitbull, Gonzalo Hermosa, Ulises Hermosa, along with the Song's Producer Redone. It is a Pop Song combining techno latin Dance Pop and House Music with a common Time of 130 Beats per Minute. Lopez recorded a spanish Version of the Song ven a Bailar which includes additional lyrical Contributions from julio Reyes Copello and Jimena Romero."
