Eilish wrote the Song with her Producer her Brother Finneas O'Connell. When I was older an electronic Track with lullaby-inspired Instrumentation has lyrics heavily inspired by the Film's Plot. The Song was later included as a deluxe Track on Eilish's Debut Album when we all fall Awake where Do we go? ."
