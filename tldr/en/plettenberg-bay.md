short_summary: "Place in Western Cape, South Africa"
description: "Plettenberg Bay, nicknamed Plett, is the primary Town of the Bitou Local Municipality in the western Cape Province of South Africa. As of the 2001 Census there were 29149 People. It was originally named Bahia Formosa by early portuguese Explorers and lies on South Africa's Garden Route 210 Km from Port Elizabeth and 600 Km from Cape Town."
