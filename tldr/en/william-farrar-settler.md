description: "William Farrar was a Landowner and Politician in the Colonial Virginia. He was subscriber to the third Charter of the Virginia Company who immigrated from England to the Colony in 1618. After surviving the Jamestown Massacre of 1622, he moved to Jordan's Journey. In the following Year Farrar became involved in north America's first Breach of Promise Lawsuit when he proposed to cecily Jordan."
