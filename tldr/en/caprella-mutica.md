description: "Caprellamutica commonly known as japanese Skeleton Shrimp is a Species of Skeleton Shrimp. They are relatively large Caprellids with a maximum Length of 50mm. They are sexually dimorphic with the Males usually being much bigger than the Females. They are characterized by their 'hairy' first and second thoracic segments and the rows of spines on their bodies. Body Color ranges from green to red to blue, depending on the Environment. They are omnivorous highly adaptable opportunistic feeders. They provide valuable Food for fish Crabs and other larger Predators in Turn. They are usually found in dense Colonies attached to submerged manmade Structures floating Seaweed and other Organisms."
