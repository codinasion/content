description: "The Suwaki Gap also known as the Suwaki Corridor is a sparsely populated Area around the Border between Lithuania and Poland that centres on the shortest Route between Belarus and the russian Exclave of Kaliningrad Oblast on the polish Side of the Border. This choke Point was named after the polish Town of Suwaki and has become of great strategic and military Importance since Poland and the baltic States joined the north atlantic Treaty Organization."
