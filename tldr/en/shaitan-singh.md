short_summary: "Indian military officer and Param Vir Chakra recipient (1924–1962)"
description: "Major Shaitan Singh Bhati Pvc was an indian Army Officer and Recipient of India's highest military Decorations the Param Vir Chakra. Singh was born in Rajasthan. Singh joined the Jodhpur State Forces after his Graduation. He was transferred to the Kumaon Regiment after the princely Jodhpur was merged with India. He took Part in the Naga hills Operations and also in the indian Annexation of Goa in 1961."
