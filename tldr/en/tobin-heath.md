short_summary: "American soccer player"
description: "Tobin Powell Heath is an american Professional Soccer Player, Entrepreneur, and Artist. Playing primarily as a Forward and Midfielder for the United States national Team, she won Gold at the 2012 London Summer Olympics, 2015 Fifa Women's World Cup, and 2019 Fifa Women's World Cup as well as Silver at the 2011 Fifa Women's World Cup and Bronze at the 2020 Tokyo Summer Olympics. She was described as 'perhaps the USA's most skillful player' by the United States Soccer Federation, and was named U. S. Soccer Athlete of the Year in 2016 and U. S. Soccer Young Female Athlete of the Year in 2009."
