title: "Wine cellar"
short_summary: "Storage room for wine"
description: "A Wine Cellar is a Storage Room for Wine in Bottles or Barrels or more rarely in carboys Amphorae or Plastic Containers. Important Factors such as Temperature and Humidity are maintained in an active Wine Cellar by a Climate Control System. In Contrast passive Wine Cellars are not climate controlled and are usually built underground to reduce Temperature Swings. An above Ground Wine Cellar is often called a Wine Room while a small Wine Cellar is sometimes called a Wine Closet. The household department responsible for the storage, care and service of wine in a great mediaeval house was termed the buttery. Large Wine Cellars Date back over 3700 Years."
