description: "The paris Agreement commonly referred to as the Paris Climate Agreement or the paris Agreement is an international Treaty on Climate Change. Adopted in 2015, the Agreement covers climate Change Mitigation, Adaptation, and Finance. The Paris Agreement was negotiated by 196 Parties during the 2015 united Nations climate Change Conference near paris France. As of february 2023 195 Members of the un Framework Convention on Climate Change are Parties to the Agreement. Of the three unfccc Member States which have not ratified the Agreement the only major Emitter is iran. The united States pulled out of the Agreement in 2020 but rejoined in 2021."
