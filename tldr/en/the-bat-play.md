title: "The Bat (play)"
short_summary: "1920 mystery play"
description: "The Bat is a three-act Play by mary Roberts Rinehart and Avery Hopwood produced in 1920 by Lincoln Wagenhals and Collin Kemper. The Story combines Elements of Mystery and Comedy as cornelia Van Gorder and Guests spend a Stormy Night in her rented Summer Home looking for stolen Money they believe is hidden in the House while being stalked by a masked. The Identity of the Bat is revealed at the End of the Finale Act."
