description: "Samarium is a chemical Element ; it has symbol Sm and atomic Number 62. It is a moderately hard Silvery Metal that oxidizes slowly in Air. Being a typical Member of the lanthanide Series, Samarium usually has the Oxidation State +3. Compounds of Samarium are also known, most notably the Monoxide Smo, monochalcogenides Sms, Smse and Smte, as well as Samarium Iodide."
