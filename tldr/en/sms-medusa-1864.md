title: "SMS Medusa (1864)"
short_summary: "Screw corvette of the Prussian and German Imperial Navy"
description: "Sms Medusa was a Steam Corvette built in the 1860S for the prussian Navy. She was the second and final Member of the nymphe Class ordered as Part of an Expansion Program to counter the danish Navy over the disputed Ownership of Schleswig and Holstein. Medusa was laid down in February 1862, was launched in October 1864, and was completed in September 1865. She had one Sister Ship Nymphe and the Vessels were wooden Vessels armed with a Battery of sixteen Guns."
