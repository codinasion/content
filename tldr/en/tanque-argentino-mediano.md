title: "Tanque Argentino Mediano"
short_summary: "Argentine medium tank"
description: "Tanque Mediano Argentino is a Medium Tank currently in Service with the Argentine Army. Lacking the Experience and the Resources to design a Tank the argentine Ministry of Defense contracted the german Company Thyssen-Henschel. The Vehicle was developed by a german and argentine Engineering Team and was based on the Chassis of the german Marder Infantry fighting Vehicle."
