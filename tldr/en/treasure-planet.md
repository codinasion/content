short_summary: "2002 American animated science fiction action-adventure film"
description: "Treasure Planet is an american Animation Science Fiction Action-Adventure Film produced by Walt Disney Feature Animation and distributed by Walt Disney Pictures in 2002. It is a Science Fiction Adaptation of Robert Louis Stevenson's Adventure novel Treasure Island and is the third Retelling of the Story in an outer Space setting following the bulgarian Film Treasure Planet and the italian Miniseries treasure Island in Outer Space. It is the third Disney Adaptation of the Novel, following Treasure Island and Muppet Treasure Island. Spaceships are powered by solar Sails in the Film's Setting and resemble the 18th-century Sailing Vessels of the original Treasure Island."
