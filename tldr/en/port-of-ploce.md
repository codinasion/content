description: "The Port of Ploče is a Seaport in Ploče, Croatia, near the Mouth of the Neretva River on the Adriatic Sea Coast. It was officially opened in 1945 after a Railway was built as Supply Route to connect the Site with industrial Facilities in the Sarajevo and Mostar Areas of Bosnia and Herzegovina which was then Part of Yugoslavia. As of 2010, it ranked as the second largest Cargo Port in Croatia—After the Port of Rijeka—With a Cargo Throughput of 4. 5 million Tonnes, consisting mostly of general Cargo and Bulk Cargo, including 20,420 Teu Containers. In 2008 the Port of Ploe recorded 2555 Ship Arrivals. It is managed by the Authority Port of Ploe."
