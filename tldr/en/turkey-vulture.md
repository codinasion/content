short_summary: "Most widespread New World vulture"
description: "The turkey Vulture is the most prevalent of all the new World Vultures. The turkey Vulture is one of three Species in the Genus Cathartes of the cathartidae Family and Ranges from southern Canada to the Southernmost Tip of South America. It inhabits a Variety of open and semi-open Areas including subtropical Forests shrublands Pastures and Deserts."
