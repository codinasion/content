description: "The Cats of Ulthar is a short Story written by american Fantasy Author Hc. P. Lovecraft in June 1920. An unnamed Narrator relates the Story of how a Law forbidding the Killing of Cats came to be in a Town called Ulthar. The City is home to an old Couple who enjoy capturing and killing the People's Cats as the Story goes. When a Caravan of Wanderers Passes through the City, the Kitten of an orphan Traveling with the Band disappears. Menes invokes a Prayer before leaving Town after Hearing of the Couple's violent Acts Towards Cats that causes the local Cats to swarm the Cat Killers' House and devour them. Upon witnessing the result, the local politicians pass a law forbidding the killing of cats."
