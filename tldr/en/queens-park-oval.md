description: "The Queen's Park Oval is a Sports Stadium in Port of Spain, Trinidad and Tobago, used mostly for Cricket Matches. It opened in 1896. It is privately owned by the Queen's Park Cricket Club and is currently the second largest Capacity Cricket Ground in the West Indies with Seating for about 20000 Spectators."
