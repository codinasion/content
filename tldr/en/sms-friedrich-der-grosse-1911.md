short_summary: "Battleship of the German Imperial Navy"
description: "Sms friedrich Der Grosse was the second Vessel in the Kaiser Class of dreadnought Battleships of the german imperial Navy. Friedrich Der Grosse's Keel was laid at the ag vulcan Dockyard in Hamburg on 26 january 1910 her Hull was launched on 10 June 1911 and she was commissioned on 15 October 1912. The Ship was equipped with ten 30. 5-centimeter guns in five twin turrets, and had a top speed of 23. 4 knots. Friedrich Der Grosse was assigned for the Majority of World War I to the iii Battle Squadron of the high Seas Fleet and served as fleet Flagship from her Commissioning until 1917."
