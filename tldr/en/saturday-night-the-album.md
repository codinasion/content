title: "Saturday Night! – The Album"
short_summary: "1986 studio album by Schoolly D"
description: "Saturday Night! The Album is the second Studio Album of Hip Hop Artist Jesse weaver under the Alias schoolly d. The Album was recorded at Ins Studios in New York where Weaver created an Album of seven Tracks that included Rapping and Instrumentals that were both inspired by and sampled various Funk Musicians from the 1970s. The Album was released independently in 1986. Singles from the Album included the Tracks 'Saturday Night' and 'Dedication to all B-Boys'."
