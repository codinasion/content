title: "African-American art"
short_summary: "Visual arts of the people of African descent in the United States of America"
description: "African-american Art is a broad Term describing visual Art created by African-Americans. Their Range of Arts created over more than two Centuries are as varied as the Artists themselves. Some have used cultural Traditions of Africa and other Parts of the World as Inspiration. Others have found Inspiration in traditional african-american Plastic Art Forms including Basket weaving Pottery quilting Woodcarving and painting some of which are sometimes classified as Handicrafts or folk Art."
