description: "Nesses is the fourth Video Album by American Heavy Metal Band Slipknot. It was released by Roadrunner Records on September 28 2010. Double-disc Release contains a Recording of their Headlining Performance at the 2009 Download Festival along with a 45-minute behind the Scenes documentary from the Percussionist Shawn Crahan and four Music Videos from all Hope is gone. It is the first Release by the Band since the Death of the Band's former Bassist Paul Gray and is dedicated in his Memory. On 22 September 2010 the Film premiered in selected Theaters across the united States. The Screenings were free and there were free Giveaways. Reception was generally positive, with Artistdirect rewarding the Album a perfect five out of five Stars, and Blabbermouth. Net giving the Album eight and a Half out of ten Stars. The Album peaked at Number one on four Charts: the australian Top 40 Music Dvd Chart, the Finland Top 10 Music Dvds, the Uk Top 10 Music Dvd Chart, and the Us Top Music Video Chart."
