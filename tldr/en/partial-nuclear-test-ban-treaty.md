title: "Partial Nuclear Test Ban Treaty"
short_summary: "1963 international agreement"
description: "The 1963 Treaty banning nuclear Weapon Tests in the Atmosphere in Space and under Water that is formally known as the partial Test Ban Treaty prohibited all nuclear Weapon Test Detonations except those conducted Underground. It is also abbreviated as the Limited Test Ban Treaty and Nuclear Test Ban Treaty, though the Latter may also refer to the Comprehensive Nuclear-Test-Ban Treaty, which succeeded the Ptbt for ratifying Parties."
