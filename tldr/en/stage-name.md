description: "A Stage Name or professional Name is a Pseudonym used by Performers Authors and Entertainers such as Actors comedians Singers and Musicians. Such professional Aliases are adopted for a wide Variety of Reasons and may be similar or close to the Person's Birth Name. Some Performers choose to use their Stage Name as their legal Name though it's uncommon."
