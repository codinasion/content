description: "This is him - Senpai is an otokonoko - is a japanese Romance manga Series written and illustrated by Pom. It was serialized digitally through Line Manga from 2019 to 2021 as a weekly Series, and is collected in Tankōbon Print Volumes by Ichijinsha since 2021. The Story follows a Love Triangle between Makoto hanaoka a Crossdresser Saki aoi a bisexual younger Student and ryuji Taiga a Childhood Friend of Mako. Senpai is an Otokonoko received an official english Translation by Webtoon beginning on March 9, 2023. An anime Tv Series Adaptation produced by Project no. 9 is set to premiere in 2024 on Fuji Tv's noitamina Programming Block."
