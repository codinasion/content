short_summary: "2010 video game"
description: "Pixel Force left 4 dead is a fan-made 8-bit-styled Remake of Valve's first-person Shooter Game left 4 dead 2008. It was developed by independent Developer eric Ruth Games and published as Freeware on 4 january 2010. It was designer Eric Ruth's first Attempt at an 8-bit Demake, with the most difficult Part for Ruth being the Music Composition due to Left 4 Dead's Soundtrack Depth and the limited Audio that Ruth could work with. Pixel Force: left 4 dead allows Players to control one or more of four Survivors. It is an overhead Shooter similar to the Nes Games ikari Warriors and Fester Quest. It was the first of the Pixel Force Series, followed by Pixel Force: Halo and Pixel Force: Dj Hero."
