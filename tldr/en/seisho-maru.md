title: "Seisho Maru"
short_summary: "Cargo ship for Mitsui Bussan Kaisho (1918)"
description: "Seisho Maru was a Cargo Ship in military Service for mitsui bussan Kaisho that was sunk during World War Ii by an american. The Ship had been built shortly after the End of World War I as the Ss west Caruth a Cargo Ship for the united States shipping Board. Shortly after its Completion the Ship was inspected by the united States navy for possible Use as uss west Caruth but under this Name was neither taken into the navy nor commissioned. Before being sold to japanese Owners in 1928 she was also known as ss Exmoor and Ss Antonio Tripcovich."
