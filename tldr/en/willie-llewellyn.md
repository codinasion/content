short_summary: "British Lions & Wales international rugby union footballer"
description: "William Morris Llewellyn was a Welsh international rugby union player. He captained Wales in 1905 and London Welsh in 1902. He was a Member of the winning Welsh Team who beat the touring all Blacks in the famous Match of the Century in 1905. In 1904 Llewellyn toured to australasia with the british Isles and won three triple Crown Trophies. He played Club Rugby for many Teams usually for Llwynypia and Newport."
