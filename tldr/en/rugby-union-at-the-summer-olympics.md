description: "Rugby Union has been a Men's Medal Sport at the modern Summer Olympic Games and has been played at four of the first seven Competitions. The Sport commenced at the paris Games of 1900 where the Gold Medal was won by the Host Nation. It appeared Afterwards at the london Games in 1908 antwerp Games in 1920 and the paris Games in 1924."
