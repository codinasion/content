short_summary: "Water tower in Kolkata, India"
description: "The Tala Tank, also spelled Tallah Tank, is a Water Tower in Kolkata, West Bengal, India. Construction began in 1909 and in may 1911 it was inaugurated by Edward Norman baker the lieutenant Governor of Bengal. The Tank owned by the Kolkata municipal Corporation is fed by the palta Water Works near Barrackpore. More than 110 Years after Construction, the Tower remains the major Water Supplier to the City of Kolkata."
