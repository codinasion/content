description: "Sweet but Psycho is a Song by american Singer Ava Max released by atlantic Records on August 17 2018 as the Lead single from her Debut Studio Album Heaven Hell. The Song was written by Max, Madison Love, Tix, Cook Classics, and the Producer Cirkut. 'sweet but Psycho' is a Pop, Dance-Pop, Electropop, and Synth-Pop Song with Lyrics about the Perception of a young Woman. The Title of the Song was inspired by frequent Interactions between Max and her Parents. It received mixed to positive Reviews from Music Critics who praised its upbeat Sound and catchy Chorus but criticised its formulaic Production."
