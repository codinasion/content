title: "Sacred Cod"
short_summary: "Wooden effigy in Massachusetts, United States"
description: "The Sacred Cod is a four-foot-eleven-inch Carved-Wood Effigy of an Atlantic Codfish, 'painted to the Life', hanging in the House of Representatives Chamber of Boston's Massachusetts State House‍—‌' a Memorial of the Importance of the Cod-Fishery to the Welfare of this Commonwealth'. The Sacred Cod has gone through as many as three Incarnations over three Centuries: the first was lost in a 1747 Fire ; the second disappeared during the american Revolution ; and the third, installed in 1784, is the One seen in the House Chamber Today."
