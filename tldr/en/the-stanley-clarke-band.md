description: "The Stanley Clarke Band is an album by the Stanley Clarke Band led by jazz bassist Stanley Clarke. It was released by Heads Up Record in June 2010 and was produced by Clarke and Lenny White. The Band includes ruslan sirota on Keyboard Ronald Bruner Jr. On Drums and featured Performer Hiromi on Piano."
