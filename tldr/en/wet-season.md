title: "Wet season"
short_summary: "Yearly period of high rainfall"
description: "The Wet Season is the Time of Year when most of a Region's annual Average Rainfall occurs. Generally the Season lasts at least one Month. The Term green Season is also used by Tourist Authorities as a Euphemism. Areas with wet Seasons are distributed across Portions of the Tropics and Subtropics."
