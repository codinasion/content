title: "Rodrigues solitaire"
short_summary: "Extinct, flightless bird that was endemic to Rodrigues"
description: "The Rodrigues solitaire is an extinct flightless bird that was endemic to the island of Rodrigues, east of Madagascar in the Indian Ocean. Genetically within the Family of Pigeons and Doves, it was most closely related to the also extinct Dodo of the nearby Island Mauritius, the two forming the subfamily Raphinae. The Nicobar Pigeon is their closest Living genetic Relative."
