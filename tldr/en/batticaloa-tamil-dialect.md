description: "Batticaloa Tamil Dialect is shared between Tamils, Moors, Veddhas and portuguese Burghers in the Batticaloa-Amparai Region of the Eastern Province, Sri Lanka. The Tamil Dialect used by Residents in Trincomalee District has many Similarities with the Jaffna Tamil Dialect. According to kamil zvelebil a Linguist the Batticaloa Tamil Dialect is the most literary like of all spoken Dialects of Tamil and has preserved several Antique Features and remained more true to the literary Norm than any other Form of Tamil while developing some striking Innovations. Although Batticaloa Tamil has some very specific Features of the vocabulary it is classified with other sri lankan Tamil Dialects as it is related to them by characteristic Traits of its Phonology. It also retains Words that are unique to the present Day. Also, the Dialect has less Influence of Sanskrit."
