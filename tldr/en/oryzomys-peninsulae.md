description: "Oryzomys Peninsulae also known as the lower California Rice Rat is a Rodent from western Mexico. Restricted on the southern Tip of the Baja California Peninsula it is a Member of the Genus Oryzomys of the Cricetidae Family. Only about twenty Individuals collected around 1900 are known and subsequent Destruction of its Riverine Habitat may have driven the Species to extinction."
