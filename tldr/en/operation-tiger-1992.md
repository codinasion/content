title: "Operation Tiger (1992)"
short_summary: "Croatian Army (HV) offensive in areas of Croatia and Bosnia and Herzegovina near Dubrovnik"
description: "Operation Tiger was a croatian Army Offensive conducted between 1 and 13 July 1992 in Areas of Croatia and Bosnia and Herzegovina near Dubrovnik. It was designed to push the Army of republika Srpska from the City towards the Popovo Field and secure a Supply Route via Rijeka Dubrovaka which was gained in early June as the Siege of Dubrov. The Operation's Success was facilitated by the Establishment of the Hv's southern Front Command and the successful Conclusion of Operations against the Vrs in the neretva River Valley in May-June 1992 which ended with Operation Jackal."
