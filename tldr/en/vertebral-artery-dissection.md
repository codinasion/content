title: "Vertebral artery dissection"
short_summary: "Tear of the inner lining of the vertebral artery"
description: "Vertebral artery Dissection is a flap-like Tear in the inner Lining of the vertebral Artery located in the Neck and Supplies Blood to the Brain. After the Tear, Blood Enters the arterial Wall and forms a Blood Clot, thickening the Artery Wall and often impeding Blood Flow. The Symptoms of vertebral Artery Dissection include Head and Neck Pain and Intermittent or permanent Stroke Symptoms such as Difficulty Speaking, impaired Coordination, and visual Loss. It is usually diagnosed by a Contrast enhanced ct scan or mri scan."
