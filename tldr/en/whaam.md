description: "Whaam! Is a diptych Painting by american Artist Roy Lichtenstein of 1963. It is one of the best known Works of pop Art and among Lichtenstein's most important Paintings. Whaam! The Work was first exhibited at new York City's leo Castelli Gallery in 1963 and purchased by the Tate Gallery in 1966. It has been on permanent Display at Tate Modern since 2006."
