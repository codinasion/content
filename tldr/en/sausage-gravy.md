title: "Sausage gravy"
short_summary: "Breakfast dish from the Southern United States"
description: "Sausage Gravy is a traditional southern Breakfast Dish in the united States. After the Pork Sausage is cooked in a Pan and removed a Roux is formed by browning Flour in the remaining Fat. Milk and Seasonings such as Salt and Pepper are added to create a moderately thick Gravy to which cooked Sausage is added. Sometimes Ingredients such as cayenne Pepper or a Spicy Sausage are used to make a stronger Gravy."
