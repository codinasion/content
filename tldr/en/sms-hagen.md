title: "SMS Hagen"
short_summary: "Coastal defense ship of the German Imperial Navy"
description: "Sms Hagen was the final Vessel of the six siegfried Class of coastal Defence Ships built for the german imperial Navy. Her sister Ships were hildebrand Frithjof Heimdall and siegfried Beowulf. Hagen was built between 1891 and 1893 by the Kaiserliche Werft in Kiel armed with a main Battery of three 24-centimeter Guns. She served in the german Fleet throughout the 1890s and was rebuilt in 1900-1902. She served in the vi Battle Squadron after the Outbreak of World War I in august 1914 but did not see any Action. Hagen was demobilized in 1915 and then used as a Barracks Ship. She was finally sold for Scrap in 1919 and later dismantled."
