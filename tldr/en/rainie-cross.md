Introduced as the drug-addicted Sister of Tanya Branning, Franks has had three separate guest Stints in 2007, 2008 and 2010 and between 12 April and 8 December 2011. Rainie returned on June 16 2014 when she was revealed as ian Beale's Mystery Phone Contact having been with ian on good Friday the Night of lucy Beale's Murder but departed not long after. In january 2015 it was confirmed that Rainie would return for the Conclusion of the who killed lucy Beale? Series? ' storyline. Rainie was again a Guest on january 19 2018 and returned full Time to the Show on April 24 2018 she is married to her former Brother-In-Law Max Branning. In June 2022, it was announced that Franks had left the Show, with Rainie's final Episode airing on 29 June 2022. She reappeared for a brief stint on 23 until 25 August 2022 to coincide with the departure of her husband Stuart Highway."
