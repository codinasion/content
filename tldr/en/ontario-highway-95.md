short_summary: "Former Ontario provincial highway"
description: "King's Highway 95, commonly referred to as Highway 95, was a provincially maintained Highway in the canadian Province of Ontario on Wolfe Island. Together with Highway 96 the Routes were the only King's Highways that were not connected by a fixed Link to the Rest of the Network. At its southern End, the 11. 4-kilometre Route connected to New York State Route 12e via the private and seasonal Horne's Ferry. At its northern End it connected Marysville with highway96 a short Distance West of the Mv Wolfe Islander Iii Ferry to kingston. Today it is under the Jurisdiction of frontenac Islands township as frontenac County does not have a County Road System."
