short_summary: "Grape varietal"
description: "Thomcord is a Seedless table Grape Variety and a Hybrid of the popular Thompson Seedless or Sultanina Grape and the Concord Grape. Thomcord was developed by californian Grape Breeders working for the agricultural Research Service an Agency of the united States Department of Agriculture in 1983 as Part of a Test to better understand a new Seedless Grape Breeding."
