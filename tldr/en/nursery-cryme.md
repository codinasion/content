short_summary: "1971 studio album by Genesis"
description: "Nursery Cryme is the third Studio Album by the English Rock Band Genesis, released on 12 November 1971 on Charisma Records. It was their first to feature drummer/vocalist Phil Collins and Guitarist Steve Hackett. The Album received a mixed Response from Critics and was not initially a commercial Success ; it did not enter the Uk Chart until 1974, when it reached its Peak at no. 39. However, the Album was successful in Continental Europe, particularly Italy."
