description: "'out of Mind' is a Song by swedish recording Artist Tove Lo for her Debut extended Play, Truth Serum. It was written by Lo alongside Alx Reuterskiöld and produced by the Struts with Reuterskiöld. The Song was released as the Lead single from Truth Serum by universal Music on october 16 2013. It was also her third single Overall, after 'Love Ballad' and 'Habits'."
