title: "Washington Doctrine of Unstable Alliances"
short_summary: "American realist foreign policy doctrine"
description: "The Washington Doctrine of unstable Alliances sometimes called Caution against entangling Alliances was an early Realist Guide for us foreign Policy and the Nation's Interaction with other. According to Policy the united States should consider external Alliances as temporary Measures of Convenience and freely abandon them when the national Interest Dictates. The Policy has been cited as a rare Example of an explicit Endorsement of what is known in international Relations as Renversement Des alliances a State abandoning an Ally for an Alliance with a recent Enemy sometimes against the former Ally."
