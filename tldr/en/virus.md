title: "Virus"
short_summary: "Infectious agent that replicates in cells"
description: "A Virus is a submicroscopically infectious Agent that replicates only inside living Cells of an Organism. Viruses can infect all Life Forms from Animals and Plants to microorganisms including Bacteria and Archaea. Viruses are found in almost every Ecosystem on Earth and are the most numerous Type of biological Entity. Since Dmitri Ivanovsky's Article in 1892 describing a non-bacterial Pathogen infecting Tobacco Plants and the Discovery of the Tobacco Mosaic Virus by Martinus Beijerinck in 1898 more than 11000 of the Millions of Virus Species have been described in Detail. The Study of Viruses is known as Virology, a Subspeciality of Microbiology."
