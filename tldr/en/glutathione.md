title: "Glutathione"
short_summary: "Ubiquitous antioxidant compound in living organisms"
description: "Glutathione is an antioxidant in Plants, Animals, Fungi, and some Bacteria and Archaea. Glutathione is capable of preventing Damage caused by Sources such as reactive Oxygen Species free Radicals Peroxides lipid Peroxides and heavy Metals. It is a Tripeptide with a gamma-peptide Linkage between the Carboxyl Group of the glutamate Side Chain and Cysteine. The Carboxyl Group of the Cysteine Residue is attached by normal peptide Linkage to glycine."
