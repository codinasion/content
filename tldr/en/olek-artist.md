short_summary: "Polish-born artist, born 1978"
description: "Agata Oleksiak, known as Olek, is a polish Artist who is based in New York City. Their Works include Sculptures, Installations such as crocheted Bicycles, Inflatables, Performance Pieces, and fiber Art. They have covered Buildings, Sculptures, People, and an Apartment with Crochet and have exhibited in the United States, United Kingdom, Germany, Brazil, Turkey, France, Italy, Poland, and Costa Rica."
