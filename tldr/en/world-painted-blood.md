short_summary: "2009 studio album by Slayer"
description: "World painted Blood is the eleventh Studio Album by american Thrash Metal Band Slayer. It was released on 3 november 2009 on american Recordings and sony Music and was produced by Greg Fidelman and Executive produced by Rick Rubin. It is the Band's only Album produced by Greg Fidelman and the last with guitarist Jeff Hanneman and Drummer dave Lombardo. With much Anticipation for the Album after 2006's Christ Illusion, Members of Slayer began revealing Information about the Album Beginning in early 2009."
