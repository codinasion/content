description: "Sid Barnes was a key Member of donald Bradman's famous australian Cricket Team which toured England in 1948. The Team went undefeated in their 34 Matches this unprecedented Feat by a Test Side that was touring England earned them the Sobriquet the Invincibles. A right-handed Opening Batsman, Barnes was Part of Bradman's first-choice Team and played in four of the five Tests—He missed one Match due to injury—partnering the left-handed Arthur Morris."
