short_summary: "2008 video game"
description: "Saints row 2 is a mobile tie-in Action Game developed by swedish Studio G5 Entertainment and published by Thq Wireless to accompany the console Game Saints row 2. It was released in October 2008. The Game follows the Story of the Series where the Player Returns from Prison and Fights to protect and expand his Gang's Territory. The player can steal, carjack, mug, shoot, stab, and kill computer players. There are special mini Games for Robbery Theft and Carjacking. Completing Missions earns Money to be spent Towards replenished Supplies and Player Upgrades. Reviewers contended that the Game was too large for the small Screen, which exacerbated difficult Driving Controls."
