short_summary: "Northern Ireland footballer (1900–1973)"
description: "Thomas Milne Sloan was an irish professional Footballer who played at the Half back. He began his Career in northern Ireland with irish intermediate League Side Crusaders while also serving as a Policeman. He joined the Cardiff City Football League first Division Side in 1924 where he spent five Years and made more than 90 Appearances in all Competitions. He had his playing Time at the Club often limited due to Competition with Club Captain fred Keenor but he did play for the Side in the 1927 fa cup final as they defeated arsenal to become the only Team from outside England to win the Competition."
