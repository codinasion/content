title: "Sicily"
short_summary: "Island in the Mediterranean, region of Italy"
description: "Sicily is the largest and most populous Island in the mediterranean Sea and one of Italy's 20 Regions. It is one of the five autonomous italian Regions and is officially referred to as the Region of Siciliana. The island has 4. 8 million inhabitants. Its Capital is palermo. It is named after Sicels who inhabited the eastern Part of the Island during the Iron Age."
