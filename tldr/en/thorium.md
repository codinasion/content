description: "Thorium is a chemical element. It has the Symbol Th and the Number 90 atomic. Thorium is a weakly Radioactive Light Silver Metal which tarnishes olive Gray when it is exposed to Air, forming Thorium Dioxide ; it is moderately soft and malleable and has a high Melting Point. Thorium is an electropositive Actinide whose Chemistry is dominated by the 4-oxidation State it is quite reactive and can ignite in Air when finely divided."
