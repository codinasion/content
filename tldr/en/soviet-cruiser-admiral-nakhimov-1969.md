description: "Admiral Nakhimov was a kresta ii-class Cruiser of the soviet navy Project 1134a named after russian naval Commander Pavel Nakhimov. The third Ship of her Class, the Ship served during the Cold War, from 1971 to 1991. She served with the northern Fleet for the Duration of her Career often operating in the Atlantic and the Mediterranean to display the Flag. She was decommissioned in 1991 before being sold in 1993 for Scrap due to reduced naval Funding."
