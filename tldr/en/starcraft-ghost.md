title: "StarCraft: Ghost"
short_summary: "Cancelled video game"
description: "Starcraft Ghost is a stealth-action military Video Game developed by Blizzard Entertainment. It was intended to be part of Blizzard's StarCraft series and was announced in September 20, 2002. It was to be developed for the Nintendo Xbox and Playstation 2 Video Game Consoles by nihilistic Software. Several Delays in Development caused Blizzard to move back the Release Date and the Game has not materialized. Nihilistic Software ceded Development to Swingin' Ape Studios in 2004 before Blizzard bought the Company, and Plans for the Gamecube Version were canceled in 2005."
