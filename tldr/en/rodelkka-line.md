description: "The Rodelkka Line is a former Oslo-Transit Line in Norway. It was used from 1900 to 1961 for the Neighborhood of Rodelkka. After closing the southern Portion of the Line was designated as Part of the Sinsen Line. The Rodelkka Line was built in 1900 by Kristiania kommunale Sporveie and took over in 1905 by Kristiania Sporveisselskab. In 1924 Ownership was transferred to oslo sporveier. It was served by the Line 9 until 1949 when it was replaced by a Bus Service. In 1955 a new Route from carl Berners plass to rodelkka was constructed and the Line was served by Line 13 until 1961 when the Service was terminated. The Tracks were removed between 1962 and 1964."
