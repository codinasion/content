title: "Tokyo Game Show"
short_summary: "Video game convention"
description: "Tokyo Game Show commonly known as Tgs is a video Game Trade Fair and Convention held annually in September at the Makuhari Messe in Chiba Japan. It is presented by the Computer Entertainment Suppliers Association and Nikkei Business Publications inc. The main Focus of the Show is on japanese Games but some international Video Game Developers use it to showcase upcoming Release - related Hardware. The duration of the event is four days. The first two Days of the tokyo Game Show are only open to Industry Attendees and the Public can attend during the final two Days."
