short_summary: "Singaporean politician (1926–2023)"
description: "Richard Hu Tsu Tau was a singaporean Politician who served as Minister for Finance from 1985 to 2001. A Member of the governing People's Action Party, he was Member of Parliament for Kreta Ayer Smc between 1984 and 1997, and Kreta Ayer–Tanglin Grc between 1997 and 2001. Hu was Singapore's longest serving Finance Minister and gave a Record Number of 16 Budget Speeches. He served as Chairman of the monetary Authority of Singapore between 1985 and 1997."
