short_summary: "Korean-Canadian film director"
description: "Helen Lee is a korean-canadian Film Director. Born in Seoul, South Korea, she emigrated to Canada at the Age of four and grew up in Scarborough, Ontario. At a young Age she began studying Film Studies at the University of Toronto and later at the new York University. While at University she was influenced by Gender and Minority Theories as shown in her first Film the short Sally's Beauty Spot. She continued her Studies and produced two more Films before taking a five-year Hiatus in 1995 to live in Korea. After her Return, she released another short Film and her Feature Film Debut, the Art of Woo. She continues to produce Films though at a reduced Rate. Films often deal with Gender and racial Issues reflecting the State of east Asians in modern Society a common Theme of her Work is sexuality with several Films featuring interracial Relationships."
