description: "Robert Patrick 'Tate' Forcier is a former American Football Quarterback. He was a starting Quarterback for the 2009 Michigan Wolverines Football Team ahead of Denard Robinson, and Robinson's Backup for the 2010 Michigan Wolverines Football Team. He left the Program in january 2011 when Head Coach rich Rodriguez was replaced by brady Hoke. He missed the Gator Bowl on january 1 2011 after being ruled academically ineligible and was no longer enrolled in the University when the new Term began following the Semester Break. On 9 february 2011 Forcier announced his Transfer to miami University. He originally intended to redshirt the 2011 Season and Play for the Miami hurricanes Football Team in the 2012 and 2013 Seasons but instead transferred to san jose State University to play for the Spartans Football Team. He soon withdrew from the San Jose State Football Program. Then he attended training Camp with the Hamilton Tiger-Cats of the canadian Football League."
