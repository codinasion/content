description: "Kill Switch is the eleventh Episode of the fifth Season of the Science Fiction Tv Series the X-Files. It first premiered in the united States on Fox Network on february 15 1998. It was written by William Gibson and Tom Maddox and acted by Rob Bowman. The Episode is a Monster of the Week Story that is unrelated to the Series' larger Mythology. Kill Switch earned a nielsen Household Rating of 11. 1, being watched by 18. The initial Broadcast reached a Total of 4 million People. The Episode received mostly positive Reviews from Television Critics, with several complimenting Fox Mulder's virtual Experience. The episode's name has also been said to inspire the name for the American metalcore band Killswitch Engage."
