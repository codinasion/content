title: "Washington Bridge"
short_summary: "Bridge between Manhattan and the Bronx, New York"
description: "The Washington Bridge is a 2375 Foot long Bridge over the Harlem River in new York City between the Boroughs of Manhattan and the Bronx. The Crossing opened in 1888 Links 181st Street and amsterdam Avenue in Washington Heights Manhattan with University Avenue in morris Heights Bronx. It has six Lanes of Traffic as well as Sidewalks on both Sides. Ramps at either End of the Bridge connect trans-manhattan Expressway and the Cross Bronx Expressway."
