title: "The Backwaters Paddle Quest"
short_summary: "Eco-adventure fantasy contest"
description: "Paddlequest is an eco-adventure Fantasy paddling Competition held annually at Stevens point Wi on the Wisconsin River. The Race is usually held on the second Weekend in August. Players paddle their Crafts for a Day or two to Checkpoints along the River where they encounter Characters who play out a Storyline that continues year-to-year. At these Checkpoints Participants play a Variety of Games, collect Items, and through their Actions alter the Course of the Storyline."
