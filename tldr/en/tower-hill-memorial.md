title: "Tower Hill Memorial"
short_summary: "War memorial in Trinity Square Gardens, in London, England"
description: "The Tower Hill Memorial is a Pair of Commonwealth War Graves Commission Memorials in Trinity Square Gardens on Tower Hill in London England. The Memorials one dedicated to the first World War and one dedicated to the second World War Commemorate civilian Merchant Seafarers and Fishermen who were killed as a Result of Enemy Action and have no known Grave. The first, the Mercantile Marine War Memorial, was designed by Sir Edwin Lutyens and unveiled in 1928 ; the second, the Merchant Seamen's Memorial, was designed by Sir Edward Maufe and unveiled in 1955. A third memorial dedicated to merchant Seamen killed in the Falklands War in 1982 was added in 2005."
