title: "Romney Classical Institute"
short_summary: "School in Romney, Hampshire County, Virginia , United States"
description: "Romney classical Institute was a coeducational Collegiate preparatory School in Romney Virginia between 1846 and shortly after 1866. Romney had previously been served by Romney Academy, but by 1831 the school had outgrown its facilities. The Virginia general Assembly allowed the Romney literary Society through a Lottery to raise Funds for a new School. On December 12, 1846, the Assembly established the School and empowered the Society with its Operation."
