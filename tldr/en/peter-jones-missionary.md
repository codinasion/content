title: "Peter Jones (missionary)"
short_summary: "Anglo-Canadian missionary"
description: "Peter Jones was an Ojibwe Methodist Minister, Translator, Chief and Author from Burlington Heights, Upper Canada. His Ojibwa Name was Kahkewāquonāby, which means 'waving Feathers'. In Mohawk, he was called Desagondensta, meaning 'he stands People on their Feet'. His Mississaugas Band had been on the Verge of Destruction in his Youth. As a Preacher and a Chieftain, as a Role Model and as a Liaison to Governments, his Leadership helped his People survive Contact with Europeans."
