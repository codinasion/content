short_summary: "Hurricane season in the Atlantic Ocean"
description: "The 1993 Atlantic hurricane season was a below average Atlantic hurricane season that produced ten tropical cyclones, eight tropical storms, four hurricanes, and one major hurricane. It officially started on 1 June and ended on november 30 Dates which traditionally delimit the Period during which most tropical Cyclones Form in the atlantic Ocean. The first tropical Cyclone tropical Depression one developed on 31 may while the final Storm tropical Depression ten dissipated on September 30 well before the average Dissipation Date of the last tropical Cyclone of the Season."
