short_summary: "Film genre"
description: "A Superhero Film is a Film that focuses on Superheroes and their Actions. Superheroes are Individuals who generally possess superhuman Abilities and are dedicated to protecting the Public. The Films usually contain Action Adventure Fantasy or Science Fiction Elements. The first Film about a particular Character often focuses on the Hero's origin Story. It also frequently introduces the Hero's Nemesis."
