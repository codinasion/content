short_summary: "Archaeological site in Guatemala"
description: "Zaculeu or Saqulew is a pre-columbian Maya archaeological Site in the Highlands of western Guatemala, about 3. 7 km out of the modern City of Huehuetenango. Occupation at the Site Dates to the early classic Period of mesoamerican History. Zaculeu was the Capital of the postclassic Kingdom of Mam and was conquered by the Kiche Kingdom of Qumarkaj. It displays a Mixture of Mam and KʼIcheʼ Style Architecture."
