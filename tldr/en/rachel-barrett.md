short_summary: "Welsh suffragette and newspaper editor 1874–1953"
description: "Rachel Barrett was a welsh Suffragette and Newspaper Editor born in Carmarthen. Educated at the University College of Wales in Aberystwyth she became a Science Teacher, but quit her Job in 1906 on hearing Nellie Martel Speak of Women's Suffrage, joined the Women's Social and Political Union and moved to London. In 1907 she became a Wspu Organiser and after Christabel pankhurst fled to paris Barrett became the joint Organizer of the national Wspu Campaign. In 1912 despite no Background in Journalism she took over the new Newspaper the Suffragette. Barrett was arrested in 19131914 for Activities connected to the Suffrage Movement and spent some Time Incognito to avoid Re-Arrest."
