description: "The Phantom Tollbooth is a fantasy Adventure Novel by norton Juster with Illustrations by Jules feiffer first published in 1961. The Story follows a bored young Boy named Milo who unexpectedly receives a magic Tollbooth that transports him to the once prosperous but now troubled Kingdom of Wisdom. Along with a Dog named Tock and the Humbug, Milo goes on a Quest to the Castle in the Air seeking the Kingdom's two exiled Princesses, named Rhyme and Reason. As Milo learns valuable Lessons he finds a Love of learning in a Story full of Puns and Words such as exploring the literal Meanings of Idioms."
