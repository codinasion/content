title: "USS Louisiana (BB-19)"
short_summary: "Pre-dreadnought battleship of the United States Navy"
description: "Uss Louisiana was a Battleship from the connecticut Class of the us navy. She was the second Member of the Class of six pre-dreadnought Battleships and the third Ship carrying her Name. Louisiana was laid down in February 1903, launched in August 1904, and commissioned in June 1906. She was a 16,000-long-ton Battleship capable of 19 Knots. Her primary Armament consisted of four 12-inch Guns supported by a mixed secondary Battery of 7in and 8in Guns."
