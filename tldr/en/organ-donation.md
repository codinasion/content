title: "Organ donation"
short_summary: "Process of voluntarily giving away organs"
description: "Organ Donation is the Process where a Person authorizes an Organ of their own to be removed and transplanted legally to another Person either by Consent while the Donor is alive through a legal Authorization for deceased Donations made prior to Death or for deceased Donations by the Authorization of the legal Next of Kin."
