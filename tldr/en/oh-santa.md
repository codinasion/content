Carey wrote and produced the Song in Collaboration with Jermaine Dupri, Bryan-Michael Cox and Rapper Anduers it was released as the Lead single from the Album on October 1, 2010 by Island Def Jam Records. It is an up-tempo Rb Song about Carey making a Plea for Santa Claus to bring her Partner back to Time for the Christmas Holidays. It received a positive Response from Music Critics with many praising its Composition and Style."
