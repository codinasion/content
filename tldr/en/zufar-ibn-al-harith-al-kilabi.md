short_summary: "7th-century Muslim military commander and tribal chief"
description: "Abu al-hudhayl Zufar ibn al-harith Al-Kilabi was a Muslim Commander, a Chieftain of the Arab Tribe of Banu Amir, and the preeminent Leader of the Qays tribal–political Faction in the late 7th Century. During the first muslim civil War he commanded his Tribe in the Army of A'Isha against the Forces of Caliph Ali at the Battle of the Camel near Basra in 656. The following Year he relocated from Iraq to the Jazira and fought Ali at the Battle of Siffin under Muawiya Ibn Abi Sufy. During the second muslim civil War he served mu'awiya's son Caliph Yazid I leading Jund Qinnasrin's Troops against Anti-Umayya."
