description: "Wrestlemania Xxx was the 30th annual Wrestlemania professional Wrestling Pay-Per-View and livestreaming Event produced by Wwe. It took Place on 6 april 2014 at the mercedes-benz Superdome in new Orleans louisiana and was the first wwe Event Broadcast live on Ppv and livestreamed on Wwe's Subscription Streaming Service the Wwe Network which was launched in february. Wwe Hall of Famer Hulk Hogan served as the Host of the Event."
