It grows as a gnarled Tree to 7 Metres high having large green leaves up to 22cm long with Dentate Margins. The cylindrical yellow inflorescences, up to 13Â cm high, appear between November and May, attracting various species of honeyeaters, sunbirds, the sugar glider and a variety of insects. Flowers fall off the Ageing Spikes which swell and develop Follicles that each Contain up to two viable Seeds."
