title: "Pekarangan"
short_summary: "Type of home garden developed in Indonesia"
description: "Pekarangan is a Type of tropical Home Garden developed in Indonesia, mainly in Java. Pekarangans typically contain Plants, while some Possess Animals and Structures such as Pens and Bird Cages. The Gardens yield Food for Subsistence and Income, and Plants for ornamental Use. Along with their Subsistence and commercial Uses, they are used for social Interactions and Yield Sharing and provide Materials for cultural Ceremonies and religious Practices. Some Pekarangans are maintained and arranged spatially according to local Values. Home Gardens of this Type may have existed for several thousand Years but their first Mention is found in a javanese Chronicle written in 860 Ad. In 2010, around 103,000 square kilometers of Indonesian land were used for gardens of this sort."
