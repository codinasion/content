title: "Orion Nebula"
short_summary: "Diffuse nebula in the constellation Orion"
description: "The Orion Nebula is a Diffuse Nebula situated in the milky Way at the South of Orion's Belt in the Constellation of Orion and is known as the middle Star in the Sword of Orion. It is one of the brightest Nebulae and is visible to the naked Eye in the Night Sky with an apparent Magnitude of 4. 0. It is 134420 light Years away and the closest Region of massive Star Formation to earth. The M42 Nebula is estimated to be 24 light Years wide. It has a Mass of about 2000 Times the Mass of the Sun. Older texts frequently refer to the Orion Nebula as the Great Nebula in Orion or the Great Orion Nebula."
