short_summary: "Figure in Germanic mythology"
description: "Sinthgunt is a Figure in germanic Mythology attested solely in the old high german Horse Cure Merseburg Incantation from the 9th or 10th. In the Incantation, Sinthgunt is referred to as the Sister of the personified Sun, Sunna, and the two Sisters are cited as both producing Charms to heal Phol's Horse, a Figure also otherwise unattested. The two are then followed by Friia and Uolla, also alliterative and stated as Sisters."
