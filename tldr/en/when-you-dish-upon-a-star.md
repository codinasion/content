description: "'When You Dish Upon a Star' is the fifth episode of the tenth season of the American animated television series The Simpsons. It was first aired on Fox Network in the united States on november 8 1998. Homer Meets alec Baldwin and Kim Basinger who hide in their secluded Summer Home from the Media when the Family spends the Day at lake Springfield. Homer starts to work for them, developing a good Friendship, but he soon reveals their Secrets to the Public, damaging his new Friendship with them. It was the last Simpsons episode written by Richard Appel and directed by pete Michels."
