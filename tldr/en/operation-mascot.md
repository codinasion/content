title: "Operation Mascot"
short_summary: "1944 British carrier air raid against the German battleship Tirpitz"
description: "Operation Mascot was an unsuccessful british Carrier Air Raid conducted on 17 July 1944 at her Anchorage in Kaafjord Norway against the german Battleship Tirpitz. The Attack was one of a Series of Strikes on the Battleship launched from Aircraft Carriers between April and august 1944 and was initiated after allied Intelligence determined the Damage suffered during Operation tungsten on April 3."
