title: "Old Man of Hoy"
short_summary: "Sea stack in Orkney, Scotland"
description: "The Old Man of Hoy is a 449-foot Sea Stack on Hoy, Part of the Orkney archipelago off the north Coast of Scotland. Formed from old red Sandstone it is one of the tallest Stacks in the united Kingdom. The old Man is popular with Climbers and was first climbed in 1966. Created by Erosion of a Cliff by hydraulic Action some Time after 1750 the Cliff is not more than a few hundred Years old but may soon collapse into the Sea."
