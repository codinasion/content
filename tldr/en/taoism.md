Taoism emphasizes living in Harmony with the Taoâ€”Generally Understood as being the Impersonal, enigmatic Process of Transformation ultimately underlying Reality. The Concept originates from a chinese Word which has several related Meanings possible english Translations include'Way'Road' and'Technique'. Taoist Thought has informed the Development of various Practices within and beyond taoist Tradition including Forms of Meditation Astrology qigong Feng Shui and internal Alchemy. A common goal of Taoist practice is self-cultivation resulting in a deeper appreciation of the Tao, and thus a more harmonious existence. There are different Formulations of taoist Ethics but generally there is Emphasis on Virtues such as effortless Action Naturality or Spontaneity Simplicity and the three Treasures of Compassion Frugality and Humility. Many Taoist Terms lack simple Definitions and have been translated in several different Ways."
