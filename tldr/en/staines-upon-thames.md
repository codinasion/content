short_summary: "Town in Surrey, England"
description: "Staines-Upon-Thames is a Market Town in northwest Surrey england about 17 Miles west of central London. It is in the District of Spelthorne at the Confluence of the River Thames and Colne. Historically Part of Middlesex the Town was transferred in 1965 to surrey. Staines is close to heathrow Airport and is connected to the national Motorway Network by the M25 and M3. The Town is Part of the urbanized Area of greater London."
