title: "Wildlife of North Macedonia"
short_summary: "Overview of the wildlife of North Macedonia"
description: "More than 22500 Species of Wildlife have been documented in north Macedonia. Over 10,000 of these are Insects, which include 3,000 beetle Species and large Numbers of Lepidoptera, Flies, and Hymenoptera. Other large Arthropod Groups include Chelicerata and Crustaceans. Among Vertebrates, more than 300 Species of Birds recorded, although not all nest in the Country. There are over 80 Species of Fish and Mammals 32 Reptiles and 14 Amphibians."
