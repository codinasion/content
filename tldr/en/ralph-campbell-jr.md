He was a Member of the democratic Party and the first african american to hold executive Office in north Carolina. Campbell was born in Raleigh, North Carolina, and he attended St. Augustine's College. He graduated with a Degree in Business Administration in 1968 and served from 1971 to 1977 in the united States army Reserve. He worked various Government Jobs after leaving the Reserve before being elected in 1985 to the raleigh City Council."
