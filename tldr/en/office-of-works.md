short_summary: "Department of building and maintenance of the English royal household"
description: "The Office of Works was established in 1378 in the english Royal Household to supervise the Building and Maintenance of Royal Castles and Residences. In 1832 it became the Works Department Forces within the Office of Woods, Forests, Land Revenues, Works and Buildings. It was reorganized as a Government Department in 1851 and became Part of the Ministry of Works in 1940."
