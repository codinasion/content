short_summary: "Fish market in Seattle, Washington, U.S."
description: "Pike Place Fish Market is an open-air Fish Market at Pike Place Market in Seattle, Washington, United States, located at the Corner of Pike Street and Pike Place. It is known for its Tradition where Fishmongers throw the Fish purchased from Customers prior to packaging it. The fish Market was close to Bankruptcy in 1986 but after the Introduction of new Practices such as fish throwing Games and Customer Performances it received significant Publicity in national Media and on Television Shows. Pike Place fish Market is Today a world-renowned Tourist Destination attracting up to 10000 daily Visitors."
