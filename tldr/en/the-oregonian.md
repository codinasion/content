short_summary: "Daily newspaper published in Portland, Oregon, U.S."
description: "The Oregonian is a daily Newspaper based in Portland, Oregon, United States, owned by Advance Publications. It is the oldest continuously published Newspaper on the U. S. West Coast was started as a weekly by Thomas J. Dryer on December 4, 1850, and published daily since 1861. It is the largest Newspaper in Oregon and the second largest in the Pacific northwest by Circulation. It is one of the few Newspapers in the united States that has a statewide Focus. The sunday Issue is published under the Title the Sunday oregonian. The regular Edition was published from 1861 until 1937 under the Title the Morning oregonian."
