Tracking generally northward the Depression passed west of Jamaica. On June 12, the Depression peaked with Maximum sustained Winds of 35 Mph, never having reached tropical Storm Status. The following Day it reached Cuba where minimal Impacts were recorded. The Depression began leaking into the western atlantic ocean early on June 14 and then continued to the east Coast of Florida for a few Days parallel to the.
