description: "Theophylact or Theophylaktos was the eldest Son of Byzantine Emperor Michaeli Rangabe and Grandson on his Mother's Side of Nikephoros I. For the Duration of the Latter's Reign he was junior Co-Emperor with his Father and was tonsured castrated and exiled under the monastic Name Eustratius to plate Island."
