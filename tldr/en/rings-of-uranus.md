description: "The Rings of Uranus are intermediate in Complexity between the larger Set around Saturn and the Simpler Systems around Jupiter and Neptune. The rings of Uranus were discovered on March 10, 1977, by James L. Elliot, Edward W. Dunham, and Jessica Mink. William Herschel also reported observing Rings in 1789 modern Astronomers are divided on whether he could have seen them because they are very dark and Faint."
