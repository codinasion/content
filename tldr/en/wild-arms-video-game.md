description: "Wild Arms is a role-playing Video Game developed by the japanese Company Media. Vision. Originally released in Japan in 1996 for Playstation it was later translated and released in north America in 1997 and europe in 1998 by Sony Computer Entertainment. It features a fantasy Setting and Motif and 2d Computer Graphics for normal Gameplay, while Battle Sequences are rendered in 3d."
