short_summary: "Australian politician"
description: "Raymond James o'connor was an australian Politician who served as Premier of western Australia from january 25 1982 to february 25 1983. From 1959 to 1984 he was a Member of the Parliament of western Australia and a Minister at the Governments of david Brand and Charles Court. O'Connor was born in Perth and attended Schools in the Wheatbelt Towns of Narrogin and York as well as St Patrick's Boys' School in Perth, leaving School at the Age of 14. He competed in Athletics and played australian Rules Football as a Teenager and young Adult, including playing 14 Matches for East Perth in the western Australian National Football League. He served in the second australian imperial Force in new Britain and Bougainville during World War Ii."
