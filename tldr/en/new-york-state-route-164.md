description: "New York State Route 164 is a short State Highway located entirely in the Town of Patterson in northeastern Putnam County, New York, in the United States. It is a short Two-Lane Road that does not pass through any major populated Areas and Serves primarily as the Connector between Ny311 and Ny22. Ny164 also allows for a faster Passage from interstate84 via Ny311 to putnam Lake. The Route was originally designated as Ny312 during the 1930s and later became Part of Ny216. Ny164 was established on 1 january 1970 following the Truncation of Ny216 to its present eastern Terminus."
