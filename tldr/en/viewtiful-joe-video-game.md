short_summary: "2003 video game"
description: "Viewtiful Joe is a Side scrolling Beat'em up video Game developed for the Gamecube. It was originally released in 2003 as Part of the Capcom five under the Supervision of Director Hideki Kamiya and Producer Atsushi Inaba. The Game was later ported to the Playstation 2 by the same Design Team under the Name Clover Studio, subtitled in Japan Aratanaru Kibō. The Story of the Game Concerns joe an avid Moviegoer whose Girlfriend Silvia is kidnapped during a Movie featuring Joe's favorite Superhero Captain Blue. Joe is soon sent into Movieland where Silvia is taken by the villainous Group known as Jadow. After accepting a special V-Watch from Captain Blue, Joe transforms into the tokusatsu-style Persona 'viewtiful Joe' and sets out to rescue her."
