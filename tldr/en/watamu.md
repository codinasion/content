description: "Watamu is a small Town located about 105km North of Mombasa and about 15km South of Malindi on the indian Ocean Coast of Kenya. It lies on a small Headland between the blue Lagoon and Watamu Bay. Its main economic Activities are Tourism and Fishing. The Town has a Population of around 30000 in 2020 and is Part of Kilifi County."
