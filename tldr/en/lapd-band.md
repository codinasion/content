description: "L. A. P. D. Was an american Funk Metal Band founded in Bakersfield California in 1989. From 1989 to 1992, the Member Line-Up was James 'Munky' Shaffer, Reginald 'Fieldy' Arvizu, David Silveria and Richard Morrill. The Group has released one Studio Album, one extended Play, and one Compilation Album."
