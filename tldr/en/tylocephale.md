title: "Tylocephale"
short_summary: "Extinct genus of dinosaurs"
description: "Tylocephale is a Genus of pachycephalosaurid Dinosaur, a Group of dome-headed, herbivorous Ornithischians, that lived during the Late Campanian Stage of the Late Cretaceous in what is now Mongolia. It is known from a partial Skull and associated mandible which were unearthed by a polish-mongolian Expedition to the goyot Formation of the Gobi Desert in 1971. The Specimen was described as a new Genus and Species by polish Paleontologists teresa Maryaska and Halszka Osmlska in 1974."
