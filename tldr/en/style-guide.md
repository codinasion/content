title: "Style guide"
short_summary: "Standard for writing and design of documents"
description: "A Style Guide is a Set of Standards for the Writing, Formatting, and Design of Documents. A book-length Style Guide is often called a Style Manual or a manual of Style. A short style guide, typically ranging from several to several dozen pages, is often called a style sheet. The Standards described in a Style Guide are applicable for general Use or prescribed Use for an individual Publication particular Organisation or specific Field."
