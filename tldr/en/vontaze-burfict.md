description: "Vontaze Deleon Burfict Jr. Is an american former Professional Football Player who played Linebacker for eight Seasons in the National Football League, primarily with the Cincinnati Bengals. He played College Football for the Arizona State Sun Devils, earning first-team all-american Honors. Burfict was a projected top Pick in the 2012 Nfl Draft until an unimpressive Nfl Combine Performance and Character Concerns led him not to be selected. He was signed by the Bengals as an undrafted free Agent where he spent his first seven Seasons. He was a Member of the Oakland Raiders in his final Season. He is regarded as one of the most controversial Players of his Generation."
