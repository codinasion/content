short_summary: "Tributary of Kettle Creek in Clinton County, Pennsylvania"
description: "Twomile Run is a Tributary of kettle Creek in Clinton County Pennsylvania in the united States. It is 4. 5 miles long. Tributaries of the River include the two Mile run huling Branch and middle Branch. The Water runs through Leidy Township and Noyes Township. The Watershed of the Stream is approximately 9 square Miles. In the watershed Coal is mined. Most of the Acid Mine Drainage in kettle Creek comes from twomile Run."
