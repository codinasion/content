description: "The Playstation is a video Game Console developed and marketed by Sony Computer Entertainment. It was released in Japan on 3 December 1994, in North America on 9 September 1995, in Europe on 29 September 1995, and in Australia on 15 November 1995. As a fifth-generation Console, the Playstation primarily competed with the Nintendo 64 and the Sega Saturn."
