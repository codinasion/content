title: "Questprobe featuring The Hulk"
short_summary: "1984 video game"
description: "Questprobe featuring the Hulk is a 1984 graphic Adventure Video Game developed and released by adventure international in Collaboration with Marvel Comics. It is the first Entry in Questprobe, an intended Series of graphic Adventure Games that only released three Installments before the Developer's Bankruptcy. The Game's narrative follows the Marvel Superhero Hulk and his human alter-ego Bruce Banner who must discover the mysterious Crypt of the Chief Examiner. The Graphics and the Story Outline were created by Marvel Artists and Writers. Critical Reception was generally positive, with much of the Praise going to the Visuals. Reactions to the Gameplay were mixed, especially upon the Game's Budget Re-Release, by which Time it was considered dated."
