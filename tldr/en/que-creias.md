title: "¿Qué Creías?"
short_summary: "1992 single by Selena"
description: "'¿Qué Creías? Is a Song by the american Singer selena taken from her third Studio Album entre a Mi Mundo. It was written by Pete Astudillo and A. B. Quintanilla's Composition is a Result of a Challenge from a. B. to Astudillo to create a song before they arrived in Las Vegas from California. Astudillo Draws Inspiration from the Works of mexican Singer Juan Gabriel culminating in a Mariachi Ballad. Selena in describing the Song says that it embodies Women on a collective Scale. It is the Singer's Torch Song and the Lyrics elucidate Selena's inflexible Stance on refusing to absolve an unfaithful Partner. She directly addressed her former Lover who had taken her for granted by telling him of her Capability to exist without him. Critics acclaimed Selena's emotional vocal Range and her poetic Expression with a distinctly feminine Cadence."
