short_summary: "Indian film director (born 1969)"
description: "Paresh Mokashi is an indian Filmmaker, Producer, Actor and Theatre Director-Producer ; working predominantly in Marathi Cinema and Marathi Theatre. He started working as a Backstage Worker for Theatre and did few minor Roles for Plays as well as Films. Mokashi made his theatrical directorial Debut in 1999 with the marathi Drama Sangeet debuchya Mulee. He continued to work for the Theatre and made his directorial Debut for Cinema with the Marathi Film Harishchandrachi Factory in 2009. The Film depicts the Making of India's first full-length Feature Film, Raja Harishchandra, made by Dadasaheb Phalke. The Film was critically acclaimed and won several Awards. It was also selected as India's official Entry at the 82nd Academy Awards in the Category best foreign Language Film."
