title: "Sol, Arena y Mar"
short_summary: "1999 single by Luis Miguel"
description: "Sol Arena Y Mar is a Song written by arturo Perez Francisco Loyo and Salo Loyo and produced and performed by mexican Singer Luis Miguel. It is an uptempo Pop Song with Jazz Influences that Deals with a strained Relationship. The Lyrics were believed to have been inspired by Miguel's previous Relationship with Daisy Fuentes. It was released on 19 July 1999 as the Lead single from the Album Amarte Es un Placer."
