short_summary: "National Hockey League team in Quebec"
description: "The Montreal canadiens officially known as the Club de hockey Canadien and colloquially shortened to the Habs are a professional Ice Hockey Team based in Montreal. They compete in the National Hockey League as a member of the Atlantic Division of the Eastern Conference. Since 1996 the Canadiens play their Home Games at Bell Centre originally known as Molson Centre. The Team previously played at the Montreal Forum, which housed the Team for seven Decades and all but their first two Stanley Cup Championships."
