title: "Roller Soaker"
short_summary: "Amusement ride"
description: "Roller Soaker was a suspended Roller Coaster at Hersheypark in Hershey, Pennsylvania, United States. Manufactured by Setpoint Inc. The Roller Coaster was announced on 8 august 2001 and opened to the Public on 11 may 2002. Roller Soaker was located in the Boardwalk Section of the Park and Cost 7. 5 million to $8 million to construct. In December 2012 Hersheypark announced that the Roller Soaker would be removed to make Way for new Water Attractions in 2013. The Roller Coaster's Station was reused by Breakers Edge Water Coaster, a Water Coaster added in 2018."
