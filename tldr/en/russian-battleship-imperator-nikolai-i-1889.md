short_summary: "Russian Imperator Aleksandr II-class battleship"
description: "Imperator Nikolai I was a russian Imperator aleksandr ii-class Battleship built in the late 1880S for the baltic Fleet. She participated in the 1892 new York City Celebration of the 400th Anniversary of the Discovery of America. She was assigned to the Mediterranean Squadron and visited Toulon in October 1893. During the first sino-japanese War she sailed for the pacific Ocean and remained in the Pacific until late 1896 when she returned to the mediterranean Squadron and supported russian Interests during the Cretan Revolt. She returned to the Baltics in april 1898 and underwent a long Renovation which replaced all her Machinery before returning to the Mediterranean in 1901."
