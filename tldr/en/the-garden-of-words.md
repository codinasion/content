short_summary: "2013 Japanese anime film by Makoto Shinkai"
description: "The Garden of Words is a 2013 Japanese anime drama film written, directed and edited by Makoto Shinkai, animated by CoMix Wave Films and distributed by Toho. It stars miyu Irino and kana Hanazawa and Features Music by daisuke Kashiwa instead of Tenmon who had composed Music for many of Shinkai's previous Films. Rain was originally written and performed in 1988 by senri Oe but was remade for the Film and Sung by Motohiro Hata. The Film was made into a Manga, with Illustrations by Midori Motohashi, and later novelized by Shinkai, both in the same Year as the Film."
