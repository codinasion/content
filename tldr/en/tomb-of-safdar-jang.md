short_summary: "Sandstone and marble mausoleum in Delhi"
description: "Safdarjung's Tomb is a Sandstone and marble Mausoleum in Delhi, India. It was built for Nawab Safdarjung in the late Mughal Empire Style in 1754. The monument has an ambience of spaciousness and an imposing presence with its domed and arched red, brown and white coloured structures. Safdarjung Nawab of Oudh was made prime Minister of the Mughal Empire when ahmed Shah Bahadur ascended to the Throne in 1748."
