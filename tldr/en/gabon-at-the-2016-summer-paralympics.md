This was the Country's third Appearance at a Summer Paralympic Games following their two previous Participations at the 2008 Summer Paralympics and the 2012 Summer Paralympics. Gabon sent a sole Athlete to represent them at Rio de Janeiro, Wheelchair Racer Edmond Ngombi. He did not Advance from his Heat in the Men's 100m t54 Event as he was sixth out of seven Competitors and attributed the Result to a Handlebar Problem."
