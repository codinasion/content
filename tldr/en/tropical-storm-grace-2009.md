title: "Tropical Storm Grace (2009)"
short_summary: "Atlantic tropical storm in 2009"
description: "Tropical Storm Grace holds the Record for being the farthest Northeast forming tropical Cyclone in the atlantic Basin. The Seventh named Storm of the slightly below average atlantic Hurricane Season 2009 Grace formed on 4 October over the Azores from an extratropical Cyclone. It strengthened to attain maximum sustained Winds of 65mph and developed an eye-like Feature although cold Sea Surface Temperatures inhibited the Development of Thunderstorm Activity near the Center. The Storm lost its tropical Characteristics on 6 October and the Remnants of the Storm merged on 7 October with a separate System near the british Isles."
