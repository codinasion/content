description: "Pirates of the Caribbean at World's End is a 2007 american Epic Fantasy swashbuckling Movie directed by Gore Verbinski. The Film is produced by Jerry Bruckheimer and was written by ted Elliott and Terry Rossio. The Sequel to Pirates of the Caribbean: dead Man's Chest, it is the third Installment in the Pirates of the Caribbean Film Series. Set a few Months after Dead Man's Chest, the Story follows a desperate Quest to locate and rescue Captain Jack Sparrow, trapped on a Sea of Sand in Davy Jones' Locker, and convene the Brethren Court in a War against the East India Trading Company. In an uneasy Alliance will turner elizabeth Swann Hector Barbossa and the black Pearl's Crew Rescue Jack and prepare to fight Lord Cutler Beckett who controls davy Jones and the flying Dutchman."
