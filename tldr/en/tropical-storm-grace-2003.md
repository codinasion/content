title: "Tropical Storm Grace (2003)"
short_summary: "Atlantic tropical storm in 2003"
description: "Tropical Storm Grace was a weak tropical Storm that struck Texas in the atlantic Hurricane Season of 2003. The 11th tropical Depression and seventh tropical Storm of the Season Grace was also the weakest Storm of the Season. The Storm formed from a tropical Wave of a long Track in the western Gulf of Mexico on August 30. Grace remained disorganized throughout its Lifetime due to an upper-level low to its West. The weak Storm moved northwest and made Landfall on Southeast Texas. Grace weakened quickly over Land and dissipated on September 2 as it merged into a cold Front."
