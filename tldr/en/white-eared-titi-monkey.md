description: "The white-eared Titi Monkey also known as the Bolivian Titi or bolivian gray Titi, is a Species of Titi Monkey, a Type of New World Monkey, from eastern Bolivia and an Area of western Brazil. The Species has a Range that extends east from the Manique River in Beni Department Bolivia to southern Rondnia in Brazil. The southern End of its Range includes Forests around santa Cruz de la Sierra."
