description: "Tạ Phong Tần is a vietnamese Dissident Blogger. She was arrested in September 2011 on anti-state Propaganda Charges. On 30 July, her Mother immolated herself in Front of the Government Offices in Bạc Liêu Province in Protest of the Charges against her Daughter. On 24 September 2012, Tạ Phong Tần was sentenced to ten Years in Prison. In Response to her Arrest human Rights Watch Activists including the Un's State Department amnesty international and human Rights Watch protested her Actions."
