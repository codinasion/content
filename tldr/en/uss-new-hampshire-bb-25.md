title: "USS New Hampshire (BB-25)"
short_summary: "Pre-dreadnought battleship of the United States Navy"
description: "New Hampshire was the sixth and final Connecticut-Class Pre-Dreadnought Battleship, the last Vessel of that Type built for the United States Navy. She was equipped like most contemporary Battleships with an offensive Armament which consisted of four large-caliber 12-inch Guns and several medium-caliber 7 and 8-inch Guns. The Ship was laid down in May 1905, launched in June 1906, and commissioned in March 1908, a little over a Year after the revolutionary all-big-gun Hms Dreadnought rendered Ships like New Hampshire Obsolescent."
