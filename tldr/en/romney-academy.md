title: "Romney Academy"
short_summary: "School in Romney, Virginia , United States"
description: "Romney Academy was an educational institution for higher learning in Romney, Virginia. Romney Academy was first incorporated by the Virginia general Assembly on 11 january 1814 and was active until 1846 when it was reorganized as the Romney classical Institute. In Addition to the Romney classical Institute Romney Academy was also a forerunner Institution of Potomac Seminary. Romney Academy was one of the earliest Institutions for higher Education within the present Boundaries of the State of West Virginia."
