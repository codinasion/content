description: "The 2005 Football League two play-off final was an Association Football Match played on may 28 2005 between Southend united and lincoln City at Millennium Stadium Cardiff. The Match determined the fourth and final Team to win Promotion from Football League two english Football's fourth Tier to Football League one. The top three Teams of the 200405 Football League two Season gained automatic Promotion to league one while Teams placed in fourth to seventh Place in the Table took Part in play-off Semi-Finals Winners of these Semi-Finals competed for the final Place for the 200506 Season in. Southend united finished fourth while lincoln City finished sixth. They defeated northampton Town and macclesfield Town respectively in the Semi-Finals."
