The Book describes Khanna's Birth in Amritsar during the Partition of India in 1942, his 46-year-long Acting and political Career, his well-publicized Marriage to the Actor Dimple Kapadia, with whom he had Daughters Twinkle and Rinke, and his Death in 2012."
