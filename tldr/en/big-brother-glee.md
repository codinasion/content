description: "Big Brother is the fifteenth Episode and Spring Premiere of the third Season of the american musical Television Series Glee and the fifty-ninth overall. The Episode written by Michael Hitchcock and produced by eric Stoltz first aired on Fox in the united States on April 10 2012. It features the Introduction of special guest Star Matt Bomer as Blaine Anderson's Elder Brother Cooper and the Revelation of how badly injured Quinn Fabray had been in the Automobile Accident that had ended the previous Episode on my Way."
