Columbia Records released 'single Ladies' as a single on October 8, 2008, as a double a-side alongside 'if i were a Boy', showcasing the Contrast between Beyoncé and her aggressive Onstage Alter Ego Sasha Fierce. It explores the Unwillingness of Men to propose or commit. In the Song, the female Protagonist is in a Club to celebrate her single Status."
