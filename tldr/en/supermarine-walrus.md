title: "Supermarine Walrus"
short_summary: "British-designed single-engine amphibious maritime patrol aircraft"
description: "The Supermarine Walrus was a british amphibious single Engine Biplane designed by Supermarine R S. J. Mitchell at their Works at Woolston, Southampton. Primarily used as a Maritime Patrol Aircraft, it was the first british Squadron-Service Aircraft to incorporate an Undercarriage that was fully retractable, crew Accommodation that was enclosed, and a Fuselage completely made of Metal."
