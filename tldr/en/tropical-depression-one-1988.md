title: "Tropical Depression One (1988)"
short_summary: "Atlantic tropical depression in 1988"
description: "Tropical Depression one was the wettest tropical Cyclone in Cuba since Hurricane Flora in 1963. The first tropical Cyclone of the atlantic Hurricane Season of 1988 developed on 30 may from an Area of disturbed Weather in the northwestern Caribbean Sea. The tropical Depression headed Northeast to cuba making landfall in La Habana Province without intensifying. The Depression as it crossed Cuba became very disorganized as it entered the Straits of Florida and degenerated into an open Trough on June 2. Although only a tropical Depression, the System flooded central and western Cuba with over 40 Inches of Rain, causing 37 Fatalities, Damage to over 1,000 Houses, and the Evacuation of about 65,000 Residents."
