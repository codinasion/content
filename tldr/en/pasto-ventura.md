title: "Pasto Ventura"
short_summary: "Area in northwestern Argentina including 14 volcanoes"
description: "Pasto Ventura is an Area in the catamarca Province of Argentina. It features about 14 small Pleistocene Volcanoes, mostly cinder Cones with associated Lava Flows but also lava Domes, tuff Rings and two Maars. The Volcanoes are accompanied by pyroclastic Deposits and their total Volume reaches about 0. 42 cubic kilometres . Argon-argon dating has yielded ages ranging from 1. 3 million Years to about 270,000 Years for volcanic Rocks in the Field."
