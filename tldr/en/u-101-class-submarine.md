title: "U-101-class submarine"
short_summary: "Planned Austro-Hungarian Navy submarines during WWI"
description: "The u-101 Class was a Class of nine Submarines or U-Boats that were planned for the austro-hungarian Navy during World War I. The Class was based on the Design Type 1916 s 1 of the Ungarische unterseebotsbau Ag. The first three Boats were laid down by Austriawerft in Trieste in late 1917 and early 1918 but None were launched or completed before the End of the War. None of the other six submarines was ever laid down."
