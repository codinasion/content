short_summary: "Painting by Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres"
description: "The Dream of ossian is a Painting of the french Artist jean-auguste-dominique Ingres from 1813. The Work depicts the legendary Poet ossian Sleeping while he dreams of Relatives, Warriors and Deities, which appear above him on the Canvas. Ingres was influenced by his Contemporaries' Ossianic Works, including James Macpherson's purported Translations of Ossian's Poems, François Gérard's 1801 Painting ossian Evoking Phantoms, and Jean-François Le Sueur's 1803 Opera Ossian, ou Les Bardes."
