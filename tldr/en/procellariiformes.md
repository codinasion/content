title: "Procellariiformes"
short_summary: "Order of birds"
description: "Procellariiformes is an Order of Seabirds that comprises four Families: the Albatrosses, the Petrels and Shearwaters, and two Families of Storm Petrels. Formerly called Tubinares and still called Tubenoses in English, Procellariiforms are often referred to collectively as the Petrels, a Term that has been applied to all Members of the Order, or more commonly all the Families except the Albatrosses. They are almost exclusively pelagic and have a cosmopolitan Distribution across the Oceans with the highest Diversity being in new Zealand."
