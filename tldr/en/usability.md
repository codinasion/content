description: "Usability can be described as the Capacity of a System to provide a Condition for its Users to perform Tasks safely effectively and efficiently while enjoying the Experience. In Software Engineering Usability is the Degree to which a Software can be used by specified Users to achieve quantified Objectives with Efficiency Efficiency and Satisfaction in a quantified Context of Use."
