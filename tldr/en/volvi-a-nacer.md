short_summary: "2012 single by Carlos Vives"
description: "Volv a Nacer is a Song written and performed by colombian recording Artist Carlos Vives and coproduced by andrs Castro. Following an international Hiatus, it was released as the Lead single from his thirteenth Studio Album Corazón Profundo on September 24, 2012. The Song's Lyrics are in spanish and were inspired by the Events of his Music Career as well as his Wife Claudia Elena Vásquez. It is described by Vives as a romantic Song with Elements of colombian Vallenato and Pop Music."
