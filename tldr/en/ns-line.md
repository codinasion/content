short_summary: "Streetcar route in Portland, Oregon"
description: "The North South Line is a streetcar service of the Portland Streetcar system in Portland, Oregon, United States. Operated by Portland Streetcar, Inc. And Trimet, it travels approximately 4. 1 Miles per Direction from Northwest 23rd & Marshall to Southwest Lowell & Bond and serves 39 Stations. The Line Connects portland northwest District Pearl District Downtown Portland State University and the south Waterfront. It runs every day of the week between 15 and 18 hours per day and operates on headways of 15 to 20 minutes."
