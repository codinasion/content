title: "Siege of Melos"
short_summary: "Siege during Peloponnesian War (416 BC)"
description: "The Siege of Melos occurred during the peloponnesian War in 416 Bc which was fought between Athens and Sparta. Melos is an Island in the aegean Sea about 110 Km East of Mainland Greece. Though the Melians had ancestral Ties to sparta they were neutral during the War. In the Summer of 416bc Athens invaded Melos and demanded that the Melians surrender and pay Tribute to Athens or Face Annihilation. The Melians refused so the Athenians imposed Siege to their City. Melos surrendered in the Winter and the Athenians executed Melos' Men and enslaved Women and Children."
