description: "Bad Habit is a Song recorded by the american Musician Steve Lacy. It was released on 29 June 2022 as the second single from his second Studio Album Gemini Rights. The psychedelic and lo-fi R & B and Bedroom Pop Ballad was produced by Lacy and is built around a slightly warped Guitar Riff, which is accompanied by a funky Bassline, Drums, and Synths. Lyrically it concerns Lacy's Regret over a missed Chance with a Crush. His Lack of Confidence later Shifts to a more confident Perspective during the Song's final Breakdown."
