title: "WestJet Encore"
short_summary: "Canadian regional airline"
description: "Westjet Encore is a canadian regional Airline with Headquarters in calgary Alberta that operates feeder Flights for Westjet owned by the same Parent Company Westjet Airlines Ltd. In Response to internal Market Studies about future Growth Limitations by Westjet Airlines operating only boeing 737 Aircraft Westjet Encore was formed in 2013 to allow the Increase of Flights by using smaller Aircraft and to expand Service to Routes with less Traffic."
