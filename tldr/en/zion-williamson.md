description: "Zion Lateef Williamson is an american Professional Basketball Player for the New Orleans Pelicans of the National Basketball Association. He plays a Power Forward Position. Williamson was selected as the first overall Pick in the 2019 Nba Draft for the Pelicans after a freshman-year Stint with the Duke Blue Devils. In 2020 he was named to the nba All-Rookie first Team. In 2021 he became the fourth youngest Nba Player selected in an all-star Game."
