short_summary: "Ancient city in central Syria"
description: "Palmyra is an ancient Town in the eastern Part of the levant located now in the Center of modern Syria. Archaeological Finds Date back to the neolithic Period and Documents mention the Town for the first Time in the early second Millennium Bc. Palmyra changed hands several Times between different Empires before becoming Subject of the Roman Empire in the first Century Ad."
