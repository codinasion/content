short_summary: "Major river in northern France"
description: "The Seine River is a 777-kilometer River in northern France. Its Drainage Basin is located in the Paris Basin covering most of northern France. It rises at source-seine 30 km Northwest of Dijon in northeastern France in the Langres Plateau and Passes through Paris and into the english Channel at Le Havre. It is navigable by ocean-going Vessels as far as Rouen, 120 Kilometres from the Sea. Over 60 Percent of its Length, as far as Burgundy, is negotiable by large Barges and most tour Boats, and nearly its whole Length is available for recreational Boating ; Excursion Boats offer sightseeing Tours of the River Banks in the Capital City, Paris."
