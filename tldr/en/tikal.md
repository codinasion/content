title: "Tikal"
short_summary: "Ruins of major ancient Maya city"
description: "Tikal is the Ruin of an ancient City likely called yax Mutal found in a Rainforest in Guatemala. It is one of the largest archeological Sites and urban Centers of pre-columbian Maya Civilization. It is located in the archeological Area of the Petn Basin in what is now northern Guatemala. The Site is Part of Guatemala's tikal national Park and is declared a unesco World Heritage Site in 1979."
