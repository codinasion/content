short_summary: "French/American sculptor, newspaper publisher and soldier"
description: "Felix Agnus was a french-born Sculptor, Newspaper Publisher and Soldier who served in the franco-austrian War and the American Civil War. Before joining the franco-austrian War Agnus studied Sculpture. At the End of the War he returned to the united States and Briefly worked as a Sculptor. Agnus enlisted in the 5th new York Volunteer Infantry in 1861 upon the Outbreak of the american civil War and served with Merit grading to brevet brigadier-general before being mustered out of Service. Agnus was then inspector General of the Department of the South and supervised the Dismantling of Confederate Forts."
