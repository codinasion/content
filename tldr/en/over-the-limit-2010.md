title: "Over the Limit (2010)"
short_summary: "World Wrestling Entertainment pay-per-view event"
description: "The 2010 over the Limit was the inaugural over the limit professional Wrestling pay-per-view Event produced by World Wrestling Entertainment. It was for Wrestlers from the Wwe's raw and smackdown Brand Divisions. The Event took Place on Sunday, May 23, 2010, at the Joe Louis Arena in Detroit, Michigan. The Name 'over the Limit' was a Reference to its main Event Match being contested as an 'i quit' Match. Over the Limit replaced Wwe's previously annual May Ppv, Judgment Day."
