title: "Transcaucasian Democratic Federative Republic"
short_summary: "1918 month-long state in the South Caucasus"
description: "The transcaucasian democratic federative Republic was a short-lived State in the Caucasus that included most of the Territory of present-day Armenia Azerbaijan and Georgia as well as Parts of Russia and Turkey. The State lasted only a Month before Georgia declared Independence followed shortly after by Armenia and Azerbaijan."
