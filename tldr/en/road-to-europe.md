description: "'Road to Europe' is the 20th Episode of the third Season of the animated Comedy Series Family Guy. It originally aired on Fox in the United States on February 7, 2002. The Episode follows Baby Stewie who becomes obsessed with the fictional british Childrens Television Series jolly farm Revue. He decides to run away from Home to become Part of the Cast and his anthropomorphic Dog Brian decides to chase him in an Attempt to bring him Home. Meanwhile, Peter and Lois go to a Kiss Concert, where Lois reveals she knows Nothing about the Band, much to Peter's Embarrassment."
