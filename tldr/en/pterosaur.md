title: "Pterosaur"
short_summary: "Flying reptiles of the extinct clade or order Pterosauria"
description: "Pterosaurs are an extinct Clade of flying Reptiles in the Class Pterosauria. They existed during most of the mesozoic Period from the late Triassic to the End of the cretaceous. Pterosaurs are the earliest Vertebrate known to have evolved powered Flight. Their Wings were formed by a Membrane of Skin, Muscle, and other Tissues stretching from the Ankles to a dramatically lengthened fourth Finger."
