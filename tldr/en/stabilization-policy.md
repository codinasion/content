title: "Stabilization policy"
short_summary: "Policy intended to stabilize an economy or financial system"
description: "In Macroeconomics Stabilization Policy is a Package or Set of Measures introduced to stabilize a financial System or Economy. The Term can refer to Policies in two different Sets of Circumstances Credit Cycle Stabilization or Business Cycle Stabilization. In either Case it is a Form of discretionary Policy."
