short_summary: "2004 video game"
description: "Rallisport Challenge 2 is a Rally racing video Game developed by digital Illusions Ce and published in 2004 by microsoft Game Studios for the Xbox. Released as a Sequel to the 2002 Video Game Rallisport Challenge, the Game introduced several new Features, including a Career Mode, additional Cars, and track Types with new Environments, including Night Driving and Ice Racing. Digital Illusions ce developed Rallisport Challenge 2 as a completely new Approach to its Predecessor with the Developers aiming to create a more realistic and diverse Simulation of Rally racing by introducing added environmental Effects and a improved Car Damage and Handling Model."
