description: "Treacherous is a Song by american Singer-Songwriter Taylor Swift taken from her fourth Studio Album red. The Song was written with his Producer Dan Wilson. Blending Styles of Country and Pop, 'treacherous' is a slow-burning Ballad that builds up into a Finale. His Lyrics Tell of the Attempt by a Narrator to protect a Fragile and dangerous Relationship. A Demo of the Song was included in the track List of red's deluxe Edition."
