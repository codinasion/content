title: "Tropical Storm Alice (1953)"
short_summary: "Atlantic tropical storm in 1953"
description: "Tropical Storm Alice was the first tropical Storm in the atlantic Ocean to receive a Female Name. It was a rare tropical Cyclone Off-Season that hit central America in late may to early June 1953. Alice formed on May 25 in the western Caribbean, and executed a large Loop over Central America. It passed over western Cuba causing heavy Rainfall and possibly several Deaths as a Result of drowning. It then ran another Loop in the Gulf of Mexico reached a peak Intensity of 70mph and weakened before hitting the Florida Panhandle on June 6. Although heavy Rain occurred in Florida there was little Damage."
