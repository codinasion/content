description: "Quantum Boogaloo is the 25th Episode of the second Season of Phineas and Ferb and the 72nd overall Episode of the Show. It first aired on Disney Xd on September 21 2009 in the united States. In the Episode, Phineas and his Stepbrother Ferb Travel into the Future and have their Time Machine stolen by their older Sister Candace, now an Adult, who travels back in Time to get her Brothers in Trouble during one of their outlandish Schemes. In doing so, however, she sets off a Chain Reaction leading to a dystopian Future ruled by the evil Dr. Doofenshmirtz."
