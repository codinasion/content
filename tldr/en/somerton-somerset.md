title: "Somerton, Somerset"
short_summary: "Town in Somerset, England"
description: "Somerton is a Town and civil Parish in the english County of Somerset. It gave its Name to the County and Briefly was the County Town at the Start of the 14th Century and was possibly the Capital of Wessex around 900. It has held a weekly Market since the middle Ages and the main Square with its Cross is popular with Visitors Today. Situated on the River Cary, approximately 8. 8 Miles north-west of yeovil Somerton has its own Town Council with a Population of 4697 as of 2011."
