description: "Norwegian Wood otherwise known as norwegian Wood simply is a Song by the english Rock band the Beatles from their 1965 album Rubber Soul. It was written mainly by John Lennon with lyrical Contributions from paul Mccartney and is credited to the Lennon Mccartney songwriting Partnership. Influenced by the introspective Lyrics of Bob dylan the Song is considered a Milestone in the Development of the Beatles'Songwriters. The Track features a sitar Part, played by lead Guitarist George Harrison, that marked the first Appearance of the indian String Instrument on a western Rock Recording. The Song was a Number 1 Hit in Australia when released on a single there in 1966, coupled with 'Nowhere Man'."
