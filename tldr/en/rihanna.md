description: "Robyn Rihanna Fenty is a barbadian Singer, Businesswoman, Actress, and Songwriter. She is widely regarded as one of the most prominent recording Artists of the 21st Century. Signed with Def Jam Recordings in 2005, she gained mainstream Recognition following the Release of her first two Studio Albums, Music of the Sun and A Girl like Me, influenced by Caribbean Music, both peaking in the top Ten on the Us Billboard 200 Chart. Her third Album good Girl gone bad incorporated Elements of Dance Pop and established her as a major Icon in the Music Industry. Its Lead single, 'Umbrella' peaked the Us Billboard Hot 100, won her first Grammy Award, and ushered her into global Stardom."
