title: "Taylor–Burton Diamond"
short_summary: "Diamond weighing 68 carats (13.6 g)"
description: "Taylorburton Diamond weighing 68 Carats became famous in 1969 when it was purchased by the Actors elizabeth Taylor and Richard Burton. Burton had previously been the Underbidder when Jeweller Cartier bought the Diamond at Auction for 1050000 setting a Record Price for a publicly sold Jewel. Thousands of People in Chicago and new York queued to see the Diamond after its Sale in 1969. It was worn by Taylor Afterwards at the 40th Birthday of princess Grace of Monaco and the 42nd Academy Awards."
