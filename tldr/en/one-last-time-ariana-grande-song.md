description: "One last Time is a Song by the american Singer Ariana grande. It was released digitally by Republic Records on August 22, 2014 as the second promotional single from her second Studio Album, My Everything. The Song was sent on 10 february 2015 as the Fifth and final single from the Album to contemporary Radio. It was written by Carl Falk Savan Kotecha Giorgio Tuinfort Rami Yacoub and David Guetta. The Song was produced by the latter two, with Tuinfort serving as a Co-Producer and Ilya serving both as a Co-Producer and vocal Producer. A french Version of the Song featuring french Singer Kendji Girac, alternatively titled 'attends-moi', was released on February 16, 2015, in France, Belgium and Switzerland. It is also included on the deluxe Version of Girac's Debut Studio Album Kendji. An italian Version of the Song featuring italian Rapper Fedez was released on May 26, 2015, only in Italy."
