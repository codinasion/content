short_summary: "1995 video game"
description: "Atmosfear the Harbingers is an australian Video Board Game designed by Brett Clements and Phillip Tanner and published by Mattel as a major Update to the Series at. The Object of the Game is to collect six different coloured 'Keystones', face Player's worst Fear and thus beat the 'Gatekeeper'. Rather than play as a Soul Ranger each Player adopts the Persona of one of the Harbingers. The Game is set in a Place known as the other Side. The Gatekeeper is to ensure that the other Characters do not escape from the other Side. The Board is composed of a central Hub and six two-sided interchangeable Provinces which are matched together creating a hexagonal Shape. A Videotape is included in the Game and acts as a Clock. The Videotape stars Wenanty Nosul as the Gatekeeper."
