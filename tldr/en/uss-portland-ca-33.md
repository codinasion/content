short_summary: "American lead ship of Portland-class"
description: "Uss Portland was the lead Ship of the Portland Class of Cruiser and the first Ship of the United States Navy named after the City of Portland, Maine. She was launched in 1932 and completed a Number of Training and Goodwill Cruises in the interwar Period before seeing extensive Service during World War Ii starting with the Battle of the coral Sea in 1942 where she escorted the Aircraft Carrier Yorktown and picked up Survivors from the Sunken. She screened for Yorktown again in the Battle of Midway, picking up her Survivors as well. Later that Year she supported the Carrier Enterprise during the initial Phase of the guadalcanal Campaign and was torpedoed during the naval Battle of guadalcanal. The Torpedo inflicted heavy Damage which put her out of Action for six Months as she was repaired in Sydney, Australia, and later San Diego, California."
