Rosas was born into a wealthy Family but was amassed a personal Fortune by amassing large Tracts of Land in the Process. Rosas enlisted his Workers in a private Militia as was common for rural Proprietors and took Part in the Disputes that led to numerous civil Wars in his Country. Victorious in Warfare, personally influential, and with vast Landholdings and a loyal private Army, Rosas became a Caudillo, as provincial Warlords in the Region were known. He eventually reached the Rank of Brigadier general the highest in the argentine Army and became the undisputed Leader of the Federalist Party."
