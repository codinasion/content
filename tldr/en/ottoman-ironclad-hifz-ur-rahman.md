short_summary: "Ironclad warship of the Ottoman Navy"
description: "Hifz-ur Rahman was the second of two Ironclads of the ltf Celil-Class built for the ottoman Navy in the late 1860S. Originally ordered by the Khedivate of Egypt, an autonomous vassal state of the Ottoman Empire, the central Ottoman government forced Egypt to surrender Hifz-ur Rahman while she was still under construction at the French Forges et Chantiers de la Gironde shipyard. The Vessel was a turret Ship armed with two 229mm armstrong Guns and two 178mm armstrong Guns both Pairs in revolving Gun Turrets."
