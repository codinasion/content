short_summary: "Performing arts organization in New York City, USA"
description: "Symphony Space founded by Isaiah Sheffer and Allan Miller is a multi-disciplinary Performing Arts Organization located at 2537 Broadway on the upper west Side of Manhattan. Performances are held in the 760-seat Peter Jay sharp Theatre or in the 160-seat Leonard Nimoy Thalia. Programs include Music, Dance, Theater, Film, and literary Readings. In Addition, Symphony Space provides Literacy Programs and the Curriculum Arts Project, which integrates performing Arts into social Studies curricula in New York City Public Schools."
