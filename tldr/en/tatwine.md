title: "Tatwine"
short_summary: "8th-century Anglo-Saxon Archbishop of Canterbury, saint, and writer"
description: "Tatwine was the tenth Archbishop of Canterbury from 731 to 734. Before becoming Archbishop he was monk and Abbot of a Benedictine Monastery. Tatwine was a Writer besides his ecclesiastical Career and his Poems and Riddles survive. Another Work he composed was on latin Grammar which was aimed at advanced Students of that Language. He was later considered a Saint."
