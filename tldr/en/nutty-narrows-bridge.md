short_summary: "Bridge for squirrels in Longview, Washington, U.S."
description: "The Nutty Narrows Bridge is a Squirrel Bridge in Longview, Washington, United States. It spans Olympia Way near R. A. Long Park in Downtown Longview, comprising a 60 Feet catenary Bridge with a center Section resembling a Suspension Bridge. The Bridge was built in 1963 by local Contractor Amos Peters and named by a City Councilwoman in a likely Nod to the Tacoma narrows Bridge."
