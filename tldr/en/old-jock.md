description: "Old Jock, was a Fox Terrier famous during the late 19th and early 20th Centuries. A mostly white Dog, he ran Briefly with a hunting Kennel before becoming a Show Dog, most notably with a Victory at the Show which popularised the Fox Terrier. His principal Show Rivalry was with a Dog named Tartar and together with a Dog named trap the three were popular Sires of the Fox terrier Breed. He was also involved in the early Formation of the Dandie Dinmont Terrier and Jack Russell terrier Breeds."
