title: "West Francia"
short_summary: "State in Western Europe from 843 to 987; predecessor to the Kingdom of France"
description: "In medieval Historiography, West Francia or the Kingdom of the West Franks constitutes the initial Stage of the Kingdom of France and extends from the Year 843, from the Treaty of Verdun, to 987, the Beginning of the capetian Dynasty. It was created following the Division of the carolingian Empire following the Death of Louis the pious with its Neighbor east Francia eventually evolving into the Kingdom of Germany."
