short_summary: "Flautist and composer from Madagascar (1923–2001)"
description: "Rakoto Frah also known as Philibert Rabezoza was a Flautist and Composer of traditional Music of the central Highlands of Madagascar. Rakoto Frah was born in 1923 near the Capital City of Antananarivo to a poor rural Family he surmounted the Challenges posed by his underprivileged Origins to become the most acclaimed 20th Century Performer of the Sodina flute one of the oldest traditional Instruments. Through frequent international Concerts and Music Festival performances he promoted the Music of the Highlands of Madagascar and became one of the most famous Malagasy artists both on the Music Scene in Madagascar and Outside of."
