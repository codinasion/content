title: "Pallas's cat"
short_summary: "Small wild cat species (Otocolobus manul)"
description: "The Pallas Cat also known as the Manul is a small wild Cat with long and dense Light Grey Fur and rounded Ears that sit low on the Sides of the Head. Its Head-And-Body Length ranges from 46 to 65 Cm with a 21 to 31 Cm long bushy Tail. It is well camouflaged and Adapts to the cold continental Climate in its native Range which receives little Rainfall and experiences a wide Range of Temperatures."
