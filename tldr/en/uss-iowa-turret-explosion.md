title: "USS Iowa turret explosion"
short_summary: "1989 incident onboard USS Iowa (BB-61)"
description: "An Explosion occurred on April 19 1989 in the Number two 16-inch Gun Turret of the united States navy Battleship Ussiowa during a Fleet Exercise in the caribbean Sea near Puerto Rico. The Explosion at the Gun Room in the Center killed 47 of the Crew and severely damaged the Gunturret itself. Two major Investigations were undertaken into the Cause of the Explosion, one by the U. S. Navy and then one by the Government Accountability Office and Sandia National Laboratories. The Investigations produced conflicting Conclusions."
