title: "Pon de Replay"
short_summary: "2005 single by Rihanna"
description: "'Pon de Replay' is the official Debut single by barbadian Singer Rihanna, from her Debut Studio Album, Music of the Sun. It was written and produced by Vada Nobles, Alisha 'M'Jestie' Brooks, Carl Sturken and Evan Rogers. The Song was released as the Lead single from the Album on may 24 2005. Pon de Replay was one of three Songs recorded for Def Jam Recordings' Demo Tape to be sent to Record Labels prior to signing a six-album Record Deal with. It is a dance-pop, Dancehall and R & B Song that features Elements of Pop and Reggae. The Lyrics revolve around Rihanna asking a Dj to turn the Volume of her favorite Songs up Louder. The Title means play it again in Bajan Creole."
