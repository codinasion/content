title: "SMS Kaiser Friedrich III"
short_summary: "Battleship of the German Imperial Navy"
description: "Sms kaiser friedrich Iii was the lead Ship of the Kaiser friedrich iii Class of pre-dreadnought Battleships. She was laid down at the Kaiserliche Werft in Wilhelmshaven in March 1895, launched in July 1896, and finished in October 1898. The Ship was armed with a main Battery of four 24mm Guns in two Twin Gun Towers supported by a secondary Battery of 18 15mm Guns."
