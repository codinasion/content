title: "Progressive music"
short_summary: "Type of music that emphasizes expansion of form and stylistic variety"
description: "Progressive Music is Music which attempts to expand existing stylistic Boundaries associated with specific Genres of Music. The Word comes from the basic Concept of Progress which refers to Advancements through Accumulation and is often deployed in the Context of distinct Genres with progressive Rock being the most notable Example. Music considered progressive usually synthesizes Influences from various cultural Domains such as european Art Music celtic Folk west indian or african. It is rooted in the Idea of a cultural Alternative and can also be associated with Auteur-Stars and Concept Albums considered traditional Structures of the Music Industry."
