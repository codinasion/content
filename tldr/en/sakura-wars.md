short_summary: "Japanese media franchise"
description: "Sakura Wars is a Japanese steampunk media franchise created by Oji Hiroi and owned by Sega. It is focused around a series of cross-genre video games. The first Game in the Series was released in 1996 with five Sequels and numerous Spin-Off Titles since then. The Series—Set during a fictionalized Version of the Taishō period—depicts Groups of Women with magical Abilities using steam-powered Mecha to combat demonic Threats."
