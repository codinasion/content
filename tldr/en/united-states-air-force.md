short_summary: "Air service branch of the U.S. military"
description: "The united States Air Force is the Air Service of the united States armed Forces and is one of the eight uniformed military Forces of the united. Originally created as Part of the united States army signal Corps on August 1 1907 Usaf was established with the Enactment of the national Security Act of 1947 as a separate Branch of the united States armed Forces. It is the second youngest Branch of the united States armed Forces and fourth in the Order of Precedence. The united States air force Defines its core Missions as Air Supremacy global integrated Intelligence Surveillance and Reconnaissance rapid global Mobility global Strike and Command and Control."
