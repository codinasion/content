short_summary: "Species of lizard"
description: "The Crocodile Monitor also known as the papuan Monitor or Salvadori's Monitor is a Species of lizard endemic to new Guinea. It is the largest Monitor Lizard in new Guinea and one of the longest verified Lizards - up to 255 Cm. Its Tail is exceptionally long, with some Specimens having been claimed to exceed the Length of the Komodo Dragon, however less massive."
