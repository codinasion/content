description: "Pepsiman is an Action Video Game developed and published by Kid for the Playstation. It was released in Japan on 4 March 1999 and is based on the eponymous japanese Superhero Mascot for the american carbonated soda Pepsi. It focuses the Player on avoiding Obstacles by running, Dashing, and Jumping, while Pepsiman automatically runs forward through each of the Game's Stages."
