description: "The History of british political Parties also known as Politico's Guide to the History of british political Parties is a Reference Book about the political Parties in the united Kingdom. Written by David Boothroyd it was published by Politico Publishing Ltd in 2001 and distributed by international specialized Book Services in the united States. Boothroyd worked in parliamentary Monitoring Services as a Researcher at the Time of the Book's Publication. The Book contains Entries on over 250 uk political Parties that participated in parliamentary Elections. It is organized alphabetically by Entry with the Size of each Entry Relative to the History and Influence of the individual political Party. Boothroyd contains Information about the History and Election Statistics of each Party as well as a brief narrative. He focuses on the Conservative, Liberal, and Labour Parties ; the Parties with the most significant Histories in british Politics."
