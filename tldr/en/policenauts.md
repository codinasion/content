description: "Policenauts is a graphic Adventure Game developed and published by Konami. It was written and directed by Hideo Kojima and originally released in 1994 for the Pc-9821. A hard Science Fiction Story, Policenauts is set in the Mid 21st Century and follows Jonathan Ingram, an Astronaut recently recovered Floating in Space in Cryosleep after an Accident at a Space Colony sent him drifting into Space for 24 Years. Now a Detective in los Angeles ingram Travels to the Colony to investigate the Murder of his Ex-Wife and her Husband's Disappearance. As he begins his Investigation he begins uncovering an illegal Organ trafficking Ring."
