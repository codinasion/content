short_summary: "Church in Wales"
description: "St Ceinwen's Church, Cerrigceinwen, is a former parish Church in the Countryside of central Anglesey, north Wales. The current Building Dates from 1860 although the Site was used for Worship since at least the 7th Century. The Entrance reuses some old carved Gravestones one from the 9th to the 11th Centuries and another from the 12th Century. The Church grounds contain a well thought to have Healing Properties. The Church and the Well are both named after St Ceinwen, an early celtic Female Saint."
