title: "Hurricane Dean (1989)"
short_summary: "Category 2 Atlantic hurricane in 1989"
description: "Hurricane Dean was a strong tropical Cyclone that impacted the united States and atlantic Canada while remaining offshore in early August 1989. The fourth named Storm and second Hurricane of the atlantic Hurricane Season 1989 Dean formed on July 31 and reached tropical Storm Status the following Day east of the Leeward Islands. Dean brushed the northern leeward Islands as a Category 1 Hurricane on the Saffirsimpson Hurricane Scale bringing light Rain but producing no Damage before turning Northward and hitting Bermuda as a Category 2 Hurricane. It continued north before making Landfall in southeastern Newfoundland."
