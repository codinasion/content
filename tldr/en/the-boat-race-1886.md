title: "The Boat Race 1886"
short_summary: "Oxford versus Cambridge rowing race"
description: "The 43rd Boat Race took Place on April 3 1886. The Boat Race is an annual side-by-side rowing Race between Crews from the Universities of Cambridge and Oxford along the Thames. Repairs to hammersmith Bridge restricted the Crews to one Arch. Cambridge won the Race with two Thirds of a Length in a Time of 22 Minutes and 30 Seconds becoming the first Crew to come from Behind at Barnes Bridge to win."
