title: "San Juan Creek"
short_summary: "River in Orange County, California, United States"
description: "San Juan Creek also called the san Juan River is a 29-mile long Stream in Orange and riverside Counties draining a Watershed of 133. 9 square Miles. Its Mainstem starts in the southern Santa Ana Mountains in the Cleveland national Forest. It winds through san juan Canyon to the West and south and is joined by Arroyo Trabuco as it passes through san juan Capistrano. It flows into the pacific Ocean at doheny State Beach. San juan Canyon covers a major Part of California's State Route 74."
