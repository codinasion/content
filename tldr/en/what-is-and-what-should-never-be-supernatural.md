description: "What is and what should never be is the twentieth Episode of the second Season of the supernatural paranormal Drama - televised Series. It was originally broadcast on may 3 2007 on the Cw. The narrative follows Series Protagonist dean Winchester who finds himself after a Confrontation with a Djinn in an Alternate Reality. The Creature appears to have fulfilled Dean's greatest Wish: that his Mother had not been killed when he was a Child. Dean is happy in the new World until it becomes apparent that his previous Work as a Hunter of supernatural Creatures has been undone. At this Point he rejects the Alternate Reality and Attempts to find a Method to bring himself back."
