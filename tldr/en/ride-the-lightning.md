title: "Ride the Lightning"
short_summary: "1984 studio album by Metallica"
description: "Ride the Lightning is the second Studio Album released by the american heavy Metal Band Metallica by the independent Record Label Megaforce Records on July 27 1984. The album was recorded in three weeks with the producer Flemming Rasmussen at Sweet Silence Studios in Copenhagen, Denmark. The Artwork, based on a Concept by the Band, depicts an electric Chair being struck by lightning flowing from the Band Logo. The Title was taken from a Passage in Stephen King's novel the Stand in which a Character uses the Phrase to refer to Execution by an electric Chair."
