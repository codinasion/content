description: "The first Season of the american Television Series Star Trek Picard features the Character Jean-Luc Picard after he retired from Starfleet following the Destruction of the Planet Romulus. On his Family's Vineyard in 2399 Picard is drawn to a new Adventure when he is visited by the Daughter of android lieutenant Commander Data. The Season was produced by Cbs Television Studios in Association with Secret Hideout, Weed Road Pictures, Escapist Fare, and Roddenberry Entertainment, with Michael Chabon serving as Showrunner."
