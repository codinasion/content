description: "Viva El Príncipe is the thirteenth Studio Album by Mexican recording Artist cristian Castro, released on 30 November 2010 by Universal Music Latino. It is a tribute Album to Mexican Singer José José, Castro's musical Idol. The Album was produced by Rafael Pérez-Botija who also produced for José José. Viva El Prncipe covers twelve Songs by Jos Jos and includes a Poem recited by Castro's Idol. The Recording took Place in august 2010 at hit Factory Criteria in Miami Florida."
