title: "Pot Black"
short_summary: "Snooker tournament"
description: "Pot black was a Snooker Tournament in the Uk which was broadcast on Bbc. Each Match was contested over a single Frame where other Tournaments were significantly longer. The Event carried no ranking Points but played a significant Role in the Popularisation of the modern Snooker Game. The Event was first held in 1969 with a Field of eight Players and ran annually until 1986. The Event resurfaced for three Years in both 1991 and 2005. The Series was followed by Events for other Categories of Players, with a Juniors and Seniors Events, and a Celebrity Version held in 2006."
