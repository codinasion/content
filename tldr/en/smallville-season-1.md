description: "The first Season of Smallville an american Television Series developed by alfred Gough and Miles millar began circulating on the Wb Television Network on October 16 2001. The Series recounts the early Adventures of kryptonian Clark Kent as he adjusts to his developing Superpowers in the fictional Town of smallville Kansas during the Years before he becomes superman. The first Season consists of 21 Episodes and concluded its initial airing on may 21 2002. Regular cast Members during Season one are tom welling Kristin Kreuk Michael rosenbaum eric Johnson Sam Jones iii allison Mack Annette O'Toole and John Schneider."
