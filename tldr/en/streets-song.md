description: "Streets is a Song by american Rapper and Singer doja Cat from her second Studio Album hot Pink. She wrote it with David Sprecher and Lydia Asrat, alongside its Producers Dominique and Darius Logan. In 'Streets', an R & B Ballad with Elements of Trap Music, Doja Cat Sings and Raps about her Desire to return to a former romantic Partner. Some Critics who reviewed hot Pink praised the Song for showing her Versatility as a musical Artist."
