description: "'you're mine 'is a Song by american Singer and Songwriter Mariah Carey included on her fourteenth Studio Album, Me. I Am Mariah. . . The Elusive Chanteuse . It premiered on February 12, 2014, as the third single from the Album. It was written and produced by Carey with Rodney Jerkins. An Rb Song the Lyrics revolve around the Singer Reminiscing of a past Lover. Critical Response to the Song was positive: Carey's breathy vocal Style and Use of the whistle Register at the Climax earned Praise from Critics. It also drew several Comparisons to one of Carey's previous Singles, 'we belong together'."
